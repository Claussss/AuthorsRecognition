"Greetings,' she whispered as one figure separated from the rest and whipped out a short,  batonlike sword  from a  sheath she  wore slung  like a bow across her back. Cythen was  glad of the  sword beneath her  palms and of  the sturdy boots that let her spring  to her feet while  the advancing woman drew  a second sword
