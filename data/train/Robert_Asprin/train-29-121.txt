That would never do. A change in fortunes for Critias, the man facing all directions; and absolute hell for Straton, the man who had lost his way. The very plan was an indulgence approaching the sensual for a man who had restrained himself so long, so very long, and nightly prayed for his enemies, that they go on living. And it was so easy, for a man so like every other man in Sanctuary, to the eyes of the invaders.
