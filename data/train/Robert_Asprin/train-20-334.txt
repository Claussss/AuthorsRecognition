Her eyes  narrowed  and  he  flushed  a  little  beneath  the  acuity  of  her gaze. Deliberately he began to unhook his vest, waiting for her to speak again. "Something happened-" she said then. "Something's troubling you." He draped his vest across another chair and sat down in it with a sigh.
