Feltheryn headed for his dressing room, stage left, and prepared to put on his makeup. He did not need as much as he once had. Now the job was to make him seem young enough for the part of the king. Once it had been a task to make him seem old enough. He was part way through when he heard the voice of Hort outside his door, and with it that of Rounsnouf. "But you could wait," Hort said. "He will still be there later!"
