Mor-am would be at home, she had thought; and he was not. She  kept  walking  now,  casual in  the  mutter  of  thunder, the  before-storm movements of  the street  people, moving  because if  something had  gone wrong, nowhere was really safe. They hunted hawkmasks nowadays; and Eichan had cast them adrift. There was one last place to go and she went to it, toward Mama Becho's. The door still spilled light into the dark, where a few patrons sprawled,  drunk
