But they went in   a group to the  bar,  though one,  in a  black mantle,  with  iron  at chest  and head  and wrists,   pointed directly to him like a man sighting his arrow along an outstretched arm. The man  talked to  Two-Thumbs awhile,  took off  his helmet  with its horsehair crests  that seemed  blood-red, and  approached Hanse's  table alone.  A  shiver coursed the thief's flesh, from the top of his black thatch to his toetips.
