Strick laid down coins for two days  and three candles, and took a candle  and a silent  Avenestra, her  legs almost  functioning, upstairs.  He was  careful  to secure  the  door  and inspect  the  window.  He turned  to  the  girl slouching unprettily on the edge of the bed. "Avenestra, I want you to give me something." "Uh-huh. How you wan' it?" "No, I mean an object. Something of yours. A coin. Anything."
