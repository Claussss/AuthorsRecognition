She doffed her clothes and bathed in the river while Smhee unhobbled the  horses and took them some distance up to let them drink. When she came back, she  found him just returning with them. 'Before dusk comes, we'll have to move them down to a point opposite the  isle,' he said. 'And we'll saddle them, too.'
