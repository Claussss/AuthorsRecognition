If ever there was a night to be home in bed, this was it.... He dashed up the stairs where he had lingered so many times before, tripping now and again over some dislodged stone,  some crack that hadn't been there  when he was young. On the portico he paused to  get his breath and look back the way  he had come. Nothing coming, no one passing in the street....
