At dawn, they reached the faint abandoned trail Wess had brought them in on.  It led away from the road, directly up into the mountains. The trees, black beneath the slate-blue sky, closed in overhead. Wess felt as if she had fought her way out of a nightmare world into a world she knew and loved. She did not  yet feel free,  but she could  consider the possibility  of feeling free again. 'I'm here, love.'
