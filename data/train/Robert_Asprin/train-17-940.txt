Wriggly blood, Tempus  was in a  humour darker than  the drying clots,  fully as dark as the odd, round cloud coming fast from the northeast. Kadakithis's noble Rankan visage  was suffused with rage,  so that his skin  was darker than his pale hair: 'But whyt In  the name of all the gods, what did  the poor little creature ever do to you?  You owe me a eunuch, and an  explanation.' He tapped his lacquered nails on the chariot's bronze rim.
