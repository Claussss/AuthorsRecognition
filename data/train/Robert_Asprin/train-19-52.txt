"Strick said to tell ye a word," Skarth told him, and dropped his voice so that Shmurt leaned forward across the bar. "Boodoovagoolarunda," Skarth whispered. Shmurt smiled and shook his head- "Don't know where he gits them words! What d'you need?" Skarth told him. "She wants to stay here?" "You sure?" "Shmurt ..." Shmurt nodded hurriedly, raising both hands in a fending gesture, and soon they had Taya installed, happily or un-, in one of the rooms upstairs over the tavern.
