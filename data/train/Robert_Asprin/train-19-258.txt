Sashana took one long breath and her shuddering stopped. Her proud chin lifted, but still she looked to Myrtis for some council. The smile, and the nod, that Myrtis gave might have frozen even mighty Tempus with fear. "It were best," said Glisselrand, her rich voice suddenly an emblem for reason, "that none involved be recognized- Moreover, what is to stop Vomistritus from announcing himself to his tormentors and offering more money than any of us possess for his freedom?"
