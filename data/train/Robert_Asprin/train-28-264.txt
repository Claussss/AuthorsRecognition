'A hand here.' 'Damned Ilsig mud. She weighs ten times as much now.' 'Easy. A little more mud, a little  sooner won't affect the temple, but it's  an ill thought to rouse the Others.' Torchholder's calm voice quieted the others. The torches were re-lit. From her hideout, Illyra could see a mud-covered shroud on the bier.  Dubro had succeeded  somehow: she did  not allow herself  to think anything else.
