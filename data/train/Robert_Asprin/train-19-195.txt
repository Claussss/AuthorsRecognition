"Oh, I love this town.'" she cried, her eyes glittering with delight. The next morning the broadsides reappeared on Sanctuary's buildings, but with an improvement; they were put up with a different kind of glue. The glue that had stuck so tenaciously to Feltheryn's hands was nothing compared to the stuff that caused Lempchin to cry and babble as he returned to the theater from his rounds, his hands, face, and clothes plastered with pieces of paper bearing the offensive criticisms. He had become, in less than an hour, a miserable little walking billboard, and nothing they tried could make the stickum dissolve.
