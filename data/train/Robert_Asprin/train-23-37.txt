"You seem both upset and determined to express your annoyance by being rude. I find both positions difficult to understand." Dobbs started to protest, but the man at the desk continued. "First of all, when you ordered your materials Cash On Delivery, you accepted the risk of currency-exchange fluctuations. That is standard in any contract of that kind, but it's still good business. If you paid in advance and our ship was attacked and taken by pirates, you'd be out the full cost of the shipment. As it is, you have to pay only for goods delivered, even though occasionally you have to pay a premium."
