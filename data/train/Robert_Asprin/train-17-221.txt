They  held him  and caressed  him until  the racking  sobs ceased.  Ashamed,  he scrubbed away the  tears with the  palm of his  hand. Aristarchus stood  nearby, blinking his large green eyes. 'You must think me an awful fool, Aristarchus, a fool, and weak.'
