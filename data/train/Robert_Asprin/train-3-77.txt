The card she set down bore the images of storm and tornado. "This represents our determination to see this done. And this one is for our fear..." She  set another  card above  it, on  which a  triumvirate of  robed and  hooded figures stood pointing at a kneeling man. "Justice," came the whisper, and Gilla licked  suddenly  dry lips,  understanding  even without  explanation  that this represented the dead children for whom they sought revenge.
