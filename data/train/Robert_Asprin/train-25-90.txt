"Allow me to introduce myself. I am Mr. Yamada. " His failure to introduce his companions identified them as bodyguards. Almost as a reflex, the two mercenaries swept them with a cold, appraising glance, then returned their attention to Yamada. "Am I correct in assuming I am addressing Stephen Tidwell?" His eyes shifted. "Michael Clancy?" The two men nodded silently. For the time being, they were content to let him do the talking.
