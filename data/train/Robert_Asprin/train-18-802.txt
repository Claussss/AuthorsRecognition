She had nothing but her mind, and that was unfocused, chaotic as this place was chaotic: she was the worst  of helps for it, a raw  Power without a center of  her own, an existence without  a reason. It was the worst of places for her to come. The ground  quaked. Thunder  rolled and  a voice  pursued her  without words,  a shrieking shout that impelled the winds and stung with mortal cold.
