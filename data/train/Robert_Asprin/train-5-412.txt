Chapter Eighteen:    "Cast your fate to the winds."    -L.BERNSTEIN    THE table was waiting for us. There were only two chairs with chips stacked neatly in front of them.    I had a sudden moment of panic when I realized I didn't know which chair was facing south, but Aahz came to my rescue. Darting out of the crowd, he pulled out one chair and held it for me to sit in. To the crowd it looked like a polite gesture, but my friends knew I had come dangerously close to changing the rules I had labored so hard to memorize.
