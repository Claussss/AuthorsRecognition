Jubal was suddenly aware of how far astray his memories had led him. "You miss the  point," he said  brusquely. "The fault  was mine. It  was my open arrogance that brought attention of a sort I neither expected nor wanted. If you willingly lay your hand in  a trap, do you hate  the trap for snapping shut,  or curse your own stupidity for placing your hand in jeopardy?" "I should think you'd want to avenge yourself on the one who cost you so much."
