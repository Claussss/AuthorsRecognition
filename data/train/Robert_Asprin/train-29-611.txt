The wizard lowered his blades. A fine sweat sheened on his brow, and the glow of the oil lamps lent him a strange countenance. Still, the fear was unmistakable; Dayrne saw it in those dark. deep-set eyes. The prostitute cried piteously. "Help me'" she begged Dayrne. "Don't let him kill me, I'm with child!" Dayrne stayed by the door. He needed a moment to recover his strength and to think. For all the wizard's apparent power, he feared Dayrne. Why?
