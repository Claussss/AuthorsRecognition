'Oh,' he  said, and  paced. 'Oh  gods. Prince  -don't call  me friend! Don't let anyone else hear that!' The prince looked  very much as  if he wanted  to touch him,  and was sure  that Hanse would  shrink away.  'How lonely  we both  are, Hanse.  You won't have any friends, and I can't! I dare trust no one, and you who could trust - you  reject even an extended hand.' Hanse  was  almost  stricken.  Friends?  He  thought  of  Cudget,  dead  Cudget.
