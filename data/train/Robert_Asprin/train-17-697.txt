'If you have the courage, come now...  Do you think that you can find  the house of Enas Yorl?' Lalo cringed from his snarl of laughter,  but the sorcerer did not wait for  him to reply. He had  cast his cloak around  him and was lurching  towards the door, and this time the shape the cloak covered was hardly human at all.
