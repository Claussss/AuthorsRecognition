Abruptly perceiving himself through the woman's eyes, Lalo straightened, acutely aware of his stained tunic and frayed breeches, and the stubble on his chin. 'Why  do you  need a  painting?' she  asked scornfully.  'Isn't this  enough  to purchase the use of your own powers?'  From a bag suspended around her neck  she poured out a river  of moonlight which resolved  itself into a string  of pearls
