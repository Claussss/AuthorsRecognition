He would  have displayed each, slowly, in its turn, but his customer's eyes fell on a sea-green bolt and Kul realized it would be folly to test the priest's patience. 'Your  grace has  a fine  eye,' he  said instead,  unrolling a  half-length  and letting the priest examine the hand and transparency of the cloth.
