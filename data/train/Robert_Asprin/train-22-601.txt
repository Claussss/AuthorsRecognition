He broke off. 'Ah, here we are. As soon as you are safely inside - and of course we'll search the place and make sure there is no one lurking in a dark corner-' It was a  few periods later.  'Thank you,' said  a grateful Stulwig.  He watched them, then, go down the stairs. When Quag paused at the bottom, and looked  back questioningly, Stulwig dutifully closed and barricaded the door. And there he was.
