Both men were  thinking about the painter's unpredictable art when a warty, gray arm thrust between them. "Good beer.  Special beer  for the  gentlemen^" the  wall-eyed bouncer  with the garish orange  hair said  with a  smile that  revealed corroded,  and not  quite human, teeth.
