Stilcho slumped aside on his knees, rested his back against the door, his  heart hammering away in his chest. And Haught grinned with white teeth. The old stairs creaked under any step (they were set that way deliberately,  for more than  one Stepson  used the  mage-quarter stables  and the  room above)-and Straton trod them carelessly,  which was the best  way to come at  the man whose sorrel horse was stabled below.
