"Haught!" Moria cried, as the Stepson made a sound nothing human ought to  make. She jerked with both hands at Haught's shoulders. "Don't! Haught!" Haught stopped. He stood up, slowly, the globe still beneath his arm. And  Moria flinched  in the  first backward  step, then  stood her  ground, jaw   clenched, muscles shaking in the threat of this utter stranger who stared at her with eyes that held  nothing of  the Haught  she had  known.
