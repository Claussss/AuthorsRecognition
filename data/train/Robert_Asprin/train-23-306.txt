An insistent chiming interrupted his thoughts, and he looked to his console. The priority call light flashed red, drawing a frown to his face. There was supposed to be a ban on personal conferences for the duration of the meetings, sparing him the annoyance of captains "stumping" for support of their proposals. For a moment he considered ignoring the call, then he noticed it was coming from the Raven. Was there trouble among the captains? A duel?
