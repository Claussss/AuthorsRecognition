'How may we serve the magician Lythande?' The women on the couches and cushions were twittering at one another in surprise and dismay, and Lythande could almost hear their thoughts; None of  us has  been able  to attract  or seduce  the  great  magician, and this ragged street  wench has  caught  his eyes?  And,  being  women, Lythande  knew  they  could see  the unclouded beauty that shone through the girl's rags.
