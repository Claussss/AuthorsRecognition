"The roiling inside is bad enough, but when I break wind in public, particularly when a woman is examining my fruits..." Strick clamped both hands over his mouth to hold back all sound of laughter. The poor fellow was accustomed to his true affliction. But gas disturbed him; it was socially embarrassing! Strick rose and moved around the desk.
