Snegelringe stopped. The whole world seemed to stop for a moment as one of the ladies in the pudgy actor's retinue stepped forward. "Daphne!" Prince Kadakithis exclaimed. "My husband!" said Princess Daphne, and the look she gave him could have frozen the oceans all the way to the Beysa's homeland. "I heard that you had made a gracious contribution to the evening, so how could I do less?" She stepped past him and drew out a small velvet bag which she dropped on the table in front of Glisselrand with just enough force to indicate that it contained metal; from the sound of it, gold. Then she looked back at the prince.
