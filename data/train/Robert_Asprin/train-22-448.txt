Walegrin  waited  until he  was  alone on  the  filthy streets  before  changing direction and striding, shoulders set and fists balled, into the tangled streets of the Maze. The whores of  the Maze were  a special breed  unwelcomed in the  great pleasure houses beyond the city walls.
