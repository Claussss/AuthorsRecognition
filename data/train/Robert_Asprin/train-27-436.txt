She took his hands, drew him to sit, with her, on the floor; he knelt, carefully. 'No,' Haught said, quietly, a small, lost voice. 'No. Don't.' But Ischade had  no glance for  him. She began  to speak, whispering,  as if she shared secrets with the man. His lips began to move, mouthing words she spoke.
