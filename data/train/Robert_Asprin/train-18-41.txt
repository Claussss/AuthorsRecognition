Back and forth it rolled, first one way and then the other, making a sound  like chariot wheels upon the stone floor,  a sound which grew louder and  melded with the  thunder  outside and  the  renewed clatter  of  hailstones which  resembled horses' hooves, as if a team from heaven was thundering down the blackened sky.
