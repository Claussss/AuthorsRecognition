Shadowspawn paused on a ledge formed by a set-in second floor. "Here," he whispered, "you're too slow. Get on my back." Notable let him know he'd rather just do his own climbing, but he went along. Resentful, he sank his claws well in. Hanse didn't mind; that was the reason he had so recently acquired the padded vest-black. With Notable riding his back without the hint of a purr, Shadowspawn went on up and onto the roof.
