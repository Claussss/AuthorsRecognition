Rankan  expansion  when the  boy  was five.  Yet  he'd come  south  on Abarsis's venture, and stayed when Tempus inherited the band. When they crossed  the Street of  Shingles and headed  into Shambles Cross,  the pragmatic Janni spoke  a soldier's safe-conduct  prayer and touched  his warding charm. A confusion of turns within the ways high-grown with hovels which cut off view and sky, they heard commotion, shouting men and running feet.
