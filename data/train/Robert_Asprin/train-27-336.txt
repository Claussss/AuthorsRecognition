All  the while  the prince  sat in  his palace  and intrigued with thieves, invaders, all, a wiser fool than some; priests connived, gods  perished in this and other planes - and  Ranke, the  heart of  empire, was  in no  less disarray,  with every lord conniving and every priest conspiring.
