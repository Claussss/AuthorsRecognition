   For a moment, our path was blocked by a crowd listening to a young rabble-rouser who spoke to them from atop a jury-rigged platform. As near as I could make out, they were protesting the war.    "I tell you, the council is withholding information from us!"    A growl arose from the assemblage.    "As citizens of Ta-hoe, we have the right to know the facts about this war!"    The response was louder and more fevered.
