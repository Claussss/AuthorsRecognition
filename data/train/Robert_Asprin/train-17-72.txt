Chan blushed scarlet, embarrassed, flustered, and confused. Wess began to  think she knew what was going on, but she did not want to believe it. 'You are speaking to me, man,' Quartz said, using the word with as much contempt as he had put into 'woman'. 'I have  but one more question for you. You are  not ill-favoured, yet you cannot get someone to bed you for the joy of it. Does this mean you are diseased?'
