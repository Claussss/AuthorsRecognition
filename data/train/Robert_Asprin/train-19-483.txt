Crit didn't say anything. That scared him, and got his attention, when damned little else could. "I'll get up there," Strat said. "I'll do the damn duty.-Crit, I'm through, you hear me? I'm through with her, I'm not going back, I promise you." Crit still said not a thing. That scared Strat-more than any threats Crit could have made.
