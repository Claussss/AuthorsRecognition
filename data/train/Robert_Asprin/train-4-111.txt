  I wasn't sure, but I thought General Badaxe was stifling a laugh somewhere in the depths of his beard as his rival trudged out.   "How about you, General? Do you think your men could assist in passing word of my royal summons to the Great Skeeve?"   "That won't be necessary, your Majesty."   With sudden seriousness he approached me, laid a hand on my shoulder, and stared into my eyes.   "Lord Magician," he said, "the King would like to see you."
