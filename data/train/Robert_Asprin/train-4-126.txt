  Next, I was supposed to welcome her to Possiltum, but before I could get my mouth open, she came up with her own greeting.   "Sorry I didn't curtsey any lower, but I'm not wearing a thing under this dress. Rod, it's beastly hot here in the lowlands," she said, giving me a wide but thinlipped smile.   "Aahh....."
