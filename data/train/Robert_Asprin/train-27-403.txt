'Get him away now. I have further dealings with these here.' The nightmare  lasted. The  silence held,  that chill  quiet lying  over all the alley with its  sea of tents.  Not the look  of her eyes  that had wrought  this quiet, no, Mradhon reckoned, but some subtler spell. Or fear. Perhaps they  knew her. Perhaps here in Downwind she  was better understood than across the  river, for what she was, and what her visitations meant.
