"Do everything I tell you and you'll be here more than tonight, you'll be here many, many days and nights-do you like that idea? You won't have to steal again. You'll have everything you could want-does that appeal to you?" He could not believe this was happening. He only stared at her, with the soap in his hands, and said, "Are you a witch?" "Do you think so?-What's your name?" It was dangerous to answer that with witches. He had heard so. He looked into her eyes and found himself saying truthfully: "Taz. Taz Chandi."
