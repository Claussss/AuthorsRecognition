Samlor gripped the body of his victim beneath both armpits and rushed forward, using the corpse as a shield and a battering ram. His niece might still be inside the Vulgar Unicorn, but he couldn't find her in the darkness if she didn't-or couldn't-answer his call. Star was a level-headed girl who might have screamed but wouldn't have panicked to silence when Samlor shouted for her.
