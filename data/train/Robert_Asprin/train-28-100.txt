Under a nose keen as a bird's beak, his thin lips framed well-kept teeth  marred by a  chip off  the right  upper front  molar. He  was lean  and obviously  very strong, for he was turning over a chest that must weigh a hundred pounds and his biceps were scarcely bulging. 'Scroll?' he said softly, setting down the chest. 'What scroll?' It was very hard for  Jarveena to reply. She felt  her heart was going to  stop.
