"But these winter days are so short!" Jarveena cried. "That is precisely why you must go now. It lacks less than an hour of dawn. Be on your way! No, wait! There's one thing more." "Yes?"-as she turned to obey. "No need to bring your customary fee. Reserve that for my final on- slaught on your scars. It is enough that you have given me my greatest challenge in a hundred years of weary life, the first of all that holds out hope for me ... Begone!"
