Bercy said boldly, 'I would rather  belong to you, magician,' and flung  herself again on Lythande, her hands clutching and clinging, dragging the lean face down to hers. Lythande, who rarely  touched anything living, held her  gently, trying not to reveal consternation. 'Bercy, child, this is only a fancy. It will pass.' 'No,' she wept. 'I love you, I want only you!'
