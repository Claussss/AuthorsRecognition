They had not broken  Satan. They would kill  him before they could  strip him of his pride. But they had stripped him naked, and shackled him. And they had  hurt him. Streaks of silver-grey cut across  the red-gold fur on his shoulders.  They had beaten him. Wess clenched her fingers around the handle other knife. Bauchle Meyne picked up a long pole. He was not fool enough to get within  reach of Satan's talons.
