Sunshine glared  from the  whitewashed walls  around him,  flashed from polished swords  and daggers  displayed in  the armourers'  stalls, glittered  in  myriad points  of  light from  linked  mail. But  the  brilliance around  him  was less dazzling than the vistas  opening to Lalo's imagination  now. He would have  not merely a comfortable living, but riches; not only respect, but fame!
