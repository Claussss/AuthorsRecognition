Chenaya remained where she was as  the old woman disappeared to fetch  her wine. She took a  deep breath and  let it out.  Zip would turn  up, she had  no doubt. She'd spread enough wealth to insure  that; she'd killed his enemies, too.  He'd come all right, if only out of curiosity.
