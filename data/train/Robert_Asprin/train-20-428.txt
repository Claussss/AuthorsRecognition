Below  he felt a trembling and heard the hiss of many waters, and, just as the wooden  lid of the shaft was knocked aside,  the hollow boom of water forced  upward through too narrow a way. Something dark clung to the rim of the shaft, like a rat flooded from its  hole, then clambered the rest of the way  out once the fury of the waters  had passed.
