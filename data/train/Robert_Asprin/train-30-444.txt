"Master, please!" cried Lalo desperately, trying to let her know, without saying so, that he understood. "I know the danger of secrets-I have told you mine and I am in your power. But if there are  any in this city that you love, please  show me how to undo the evil I have done!" There  was  a  long  sigh.  The sense  of  danger  began  to  ease. Gilla  moved uncomfortably, and Lalo realized that she had been holding her breath too.
