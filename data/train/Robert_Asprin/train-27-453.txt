No, the almost-ghost  said, weeping, but  she compelled it.  The body grew  warm again. Moaned with pain. 'Help me,' she said, looking up at the others who sat huddled in the corner. It was Haught  who came. Even  Mor-am was too  afraid; but Haught  - who touched her, with his hands and  in a different way, like  the flickering of a fire.  He took Stilcho up; Mor-am helped, and Vis, and Moria last of all.
