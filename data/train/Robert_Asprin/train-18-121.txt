By then  Tempus and  Jihan were  running down  the hall,  toward secret passages Tempus knew like the back of  his sword-hand or Jihan's female mysteries,  which led to the lower chambers where,  near the dungeons, Niko and the  children-whom some said were more than that-were being kept. Ischade's Foalside house was more home than haunt, less forbidding than Roxane's to the south, but hardly an inviting place to visit.
