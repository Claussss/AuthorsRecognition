He dropped his sword, fell to his knees, beat at his face. But he was much too slow. The falcon, Reyk, climbed back into the sky, leaving the man's eyes in bloody ruin. He winged a tight  circle, then settled on his mistress's  arm. She sent him aloft once  more. "Can't carry you  and fight," she whispered  tersely.
