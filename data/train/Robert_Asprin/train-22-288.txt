'But the Raggah ... they will torture you!' 'No. This bag contains a small vial of poison. They will find only a corpse.  If they find me at all.' Masha burst into tears, but she took the jar, and after kissing Smhee again, she rode off, his horse trotting behind hers. At the top of the hill she stopped  to look behind at the  hut. Far off, coming  swiftly, was a dark  mass. The Raggah. She turned away and urged her horse into a gallop.
