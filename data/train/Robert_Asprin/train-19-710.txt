The woman's tears ceased; she turned pale enough to frighten the commander, There was no fine for women fainting on the wharf, but he had no desire to have his arms full of drooping femininity. To his immense relief she squared her shoulders and started breathing normally again. "Is it permitted to tie a cart here-by the cobblestones?" Walegrin nodded. "Then I shall carry my goods myself. I cannot risk my brother-in-law's cart. I do not have two soldats."
