He bent above this seeming corpse and picked it up. not even thinking of  broken bones, only struggling with limp weight;  the head lolled. It only had  one eye. Blood was everywhere. Haught met him, passing Ischade, got the other arm of this perhaps-living thing, and they took it to the door. Moria was there. Mor-am stood against the wall.
