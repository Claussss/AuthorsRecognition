"Can you tell?" asked Lalo, blessing the thought that had prompted him to bring his apprentice along. Darios frowned, and half closed his eyes. They all fell silent as a sphere of pale light appeared before him. "Lalo, watch and tell me if it gets brighter when I move around." Slowly Darios began to circumambulate the room. "What's that?" The sphere was blazing. Lalo pointed to the point of light that reflected it from the silver ball in the Beysa's hand.
