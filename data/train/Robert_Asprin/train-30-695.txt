But it was Haught stood in the hallway door, not Her, but only Haught,  standing there with that  doe-soft look in  his eyes, that  set to his  well-formed mouth that  betokened some  vague satisfaction.  A malicious  child's satisfaction  in startling them; a malicious child's innocence: she hoped it was nothing  darker. The door closed. No servant was in evidence. "New t-trick,"  Mor-am said.
