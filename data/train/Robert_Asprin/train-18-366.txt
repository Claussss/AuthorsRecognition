What are you doing? he asked her in absentia. Do you know what you're asking? And in the  gnawing doubt that  had been between  them at the  beginning and now again: Does it matter to you? The bay moved, and the alley passed in a blur of starlit cobbles, the glare of a lantern. Things passed in and out of focus. And in a profound effort he took the ring from off his finger and put it in  his pocket where it was only mildly euphoric.
