Harran lay there,  postponing getting up  into the cold  for just a  few seconds more. Since that day five years ago when the Rankans served the writ on Irik, he had  not been  inside Siveni's  temple. For  so long  now I've  been done   with temples. Going into one, now-and hers-do I truly want to reopen that old wound? He stared at the skinny, twitching  shape curled up in the ashes,  and wondered.
