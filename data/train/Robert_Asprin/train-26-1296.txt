Lalo  squinted into  the rain.  Wedemir must  be mistaken  -any Downwinder   not already drowned like a  rat in his hole  must have sought higher  ground by now. But there was certainly something moving there.... Something stirred in him like a flicker of flame. He moved toward the bridgehead and the  movement warmed  him so  that he  could go  faster. Wedemir  started to protest, then splashed after him.
