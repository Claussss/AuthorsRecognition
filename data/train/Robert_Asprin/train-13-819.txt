   The party came into a moderately large clearing, perhaps twenty meters across. The sandy soil was soft and loosely packed here. "Look!" said Qual, shining his light on a depression in the ground. It was an enormous footprint.    "Ghu almighty, what kind of critter made that?" said Asho. "It must be enormous..."    "That is game we hunt," said Qual. "The mark is fresh, so we are very up close to it. It went that away." He pointed to the left.
