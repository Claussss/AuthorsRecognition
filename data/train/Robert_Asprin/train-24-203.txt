I reached my decision. "Follow my lead," I beamed to my teammates. "Move as if we hadn't seen them, but ready your weapons." With that, I rose and began walking to the right, paralleling the tree line. Zur and Kor followed, ambling along with such exaggerated laxness that I feared it would betray our plan. Although Tzen favor a surprise attack, we are not a deceitful race. As such, I was afraid our clumsy theatrical efforts would be immediately transparent.
