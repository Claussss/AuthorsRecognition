He felt exhausted. He wondered how Darios's day had gone-Lalo's lips twitched as he visualized his apprentice trying to maintain his dignity in the Aphrodisia House. He would have to keep a straight face tonight when he asked him how the exorcism had gone. "Lalo ..." The croak of a call came from close behind him. Lalo stopped short in the street, then whirled, hand going to the hilt of his dagger as someone stumbled into him.
