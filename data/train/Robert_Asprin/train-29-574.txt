No more, though. He had killed that love when the Raggah kidnapped her. Kadakithis hadn't even bothered to look for her or to wonder about her fate. And when she did return-thanks to Chenaya-she had found him with another woman. Hardly a woman at all, but a fish-eyed carp. She didn't know if she hated him. But he had hurt her. She wanted to hurt him back. "Daphne!" Kadakithis exclaimed. "You look positively radiant."
