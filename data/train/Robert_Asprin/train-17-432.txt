Spurred by this new  irritation, Hort let the  stem of the boat  drift away from the dock as his father prepared to board. The petty gesture was in vain. The Old Man stepped into  the boat, stretching  his leg across  the water  with  no more thought than a  merchant gives his keys in their locks. 'Row that way,' came the order to his son.
