Only Tyr  delayed for a moment, staring at the  bay-then nipped it  neatly in the  left rear fetlock,  scrambled sideways to avoid the kick, and dove past Mriga into night. Ischade  also looked  at the  bay; then,  more wryly,  at her  yard's trees  and bushes, still full of green fire that burned but did not consume. She waved  the godfire out of existence and shut the door, thinking of old stories about hell.
