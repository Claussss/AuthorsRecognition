They'd had little enough  rest since leaving the  ruined mine with the  precious ore; and of the twenty-five men who had left Sanctuary only seven remained.  But Walegrin trusted his six stalwarts against four times that many hillmen. Walegrin's thoughts were stopped  by the warning cry  of a mountain hawk;  Malm, who had a shepherd's eye for ominous movements, had spotted the enemy.
