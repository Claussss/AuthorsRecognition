  I had my answer to that, but in a flash of wisdom kept it to myself. Instead, I said my goodbyes and left.   "From your expression, I take it that your interview with the Queen was less than a roaring success," Badaxe said dryly.   "Spare me the 'I told you so's,' General," I snarled.
