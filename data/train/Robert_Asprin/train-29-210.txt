"What is it?" Strat asked again. But Ischade did not answer him. She flung her cloak about her and walked out the door, which had a disconcerting way ofopeningjust when it had to. He was last out, and it shut behind them with a thump, as the gate swung open. Stilcho's horse shied and pulled at its tether. The bay simply stood. And when he got there, Ischade was holding the reins. "I'll ride behind," she said.
