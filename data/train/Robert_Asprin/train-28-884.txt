Lythande's rapier snicked from  its scabbard and thrust  at Rabben as if  of its 'Ha!  Do  you  think  Rabben  fights  street-brawls  with  the  sword  like  any mercenary?' Lythande's sword-tip  exploded in the  blue star-glow, and  became a shimmering snake, twisting back on itself to climb past the hilt, fangs dripping venom as it sought to coil  around Lythande's fist. Lythande's own star  blazed.
