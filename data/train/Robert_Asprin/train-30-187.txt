He'd pulled the barbed missile out; he didn't understand why it hurt worse  now, He  was  sick  to  his  stomach  and  only  intermittently  did  he  recall  his determination to get home. Home to his own safe haven, or home to Mama  Becho's, where someone would tend him, home to... anywhere where he could lie down, where the Beysibs or the Stepsons or the 3rd Commando or the army wouldn't find him.
