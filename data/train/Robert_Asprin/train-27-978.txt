Gilla. Her face  was puffy and  her eyes red  from weeping, and  as his gaze met hers they glistened with another tear. Without thinking, he reached up and brushed it from her cheek: then he stared at his hand, pallid and veined and thin. And now that awareness of the rest of  his body was returning, he realized that he felt curiously light, and his other hand clutched at the bedclothes as if to hold him there.
