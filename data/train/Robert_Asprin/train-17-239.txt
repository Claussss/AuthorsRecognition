Lythande made no reply. 'All I  know,' Wess  said, 'is  that anything  anyone does  might cause pain. To oneself, to  a friend.  But you  cannot do  nothing.' She  stood up. 'Come. Come sleep, with me and my friends. And then we'll go home.' Lythande stood up too. 'There's so much you don't know about me, little  sister. So much of it could hurt you.'
