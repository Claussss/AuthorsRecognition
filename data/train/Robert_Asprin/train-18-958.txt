"Find me  Tempus's secrets,"  a man's  voice with  strange, menacing inflections commanded. "If they hide the son from me, I'll have the father." The witch produced the globe from  wherever she had hidden it. Stilcho  clutched his sleeve where the  bloody cloth was hidden  and backed toward the  door. They didn't notice him  leaving-or perhaps they  did. They were  laughing, a laughter that rose in pitch until it blended with the maniacal whine of the globe itself.
