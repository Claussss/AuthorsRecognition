This individual held in his right  hand a scroll, partly unrolled. and  with his left he was tapping on the table. The proportions of his fingers were  abnormal, and one  or two  of them  seemed either  to lack,  or to  be overprovided  with, joints.  One of  his nails  sparked luridly,  but that  ceased after  a little.
