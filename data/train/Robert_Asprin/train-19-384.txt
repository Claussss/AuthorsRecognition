"But you grew up in Sanctuary!" said Joia. "You must know someone!" Valira frowned, remembering a little man with ginger hair whose painting had shown her her soul. And when his wife, Gilla, stayed here during the False Plague Riots a few seasons back, she had been kind. "You do know a mage!" exclaimed Joia, watching her. "Please help me, Valira-I'm afraid!"
