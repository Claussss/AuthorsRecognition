If only he could remember the Sigil on the outside of the door! That night, he had thought only of getting into the vault-he had been sure that his master was just behind him... . Darios takes a deep. shuddering breath and forces himself to stillness again. Are all the wizards in Sanctuary dead? He tries to use his inner vision, but he has not received the proper initiations to walk the Wizards' road. All that comes to him is the face ofRhian, gray eyes clear as rainwater, auburn hair taking fire from the setting sun... .
