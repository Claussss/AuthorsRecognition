Zalbar did not even bother to knock, but simply pushed his way through the  door of the  House of  Whips and  Chains. It  was his  first visit to this particular brothel  which catered  to tastes  bizarre even  for Sanctuary,  but his   anger outweighed his curiosity. When the madame rushed wide-eyed, to confront him,  he was brief and to the point. "You have a skull here as part of your decorations. I want it."
