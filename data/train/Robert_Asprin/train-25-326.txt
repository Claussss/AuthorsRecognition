"Don't move, either of you!" The men seemed not to hear him, continuing with their conversation. "I said, Don't move!" Still they ignored him. Worthington was starting to feel foolish, aware of the driver peering out the door behind him. He was about to repeat himself when one of the mercenaries noticed him. He tapped the other one on the arm, and the whole table craned their necks to look at the figure by the truck.
