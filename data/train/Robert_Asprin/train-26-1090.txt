"Madam, goddesses," said the Queen, "let us see where the little one leads us." Somehow or other the door was only a few steps away this time. Outside it  stood a great iron chariot with four coalblack chargers already harnessed, and Skotadi stood on  the driver's  side, holding  the reins.  They climbed  in and  Skotadi whipped up the horses. The chariot rolled  through the courtyard  and out the  gates in utter  silence.
