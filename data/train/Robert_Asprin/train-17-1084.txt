Tempus felt the god  rustling around in him,  the deep cavernous sensing  in his most private  skull that  told him  the deity  was going  to speak. Silently! he warned the god. They were uneasy with  each other, yet, like two lovers after  a trial separation. We  can take  her, mildly,  and then  she will  leave. You  cannot tolerate  her presence. Drive her off. I will help thee, spake Vashanka.
