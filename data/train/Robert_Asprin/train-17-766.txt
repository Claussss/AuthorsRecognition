A  ship  moved  majestically  across  the  harbour,  passed  the  lighthouse and disappeared around the point. Lalo had thought of shipping out on such a vessel, but he was  too unskilled for  a sailor, too  frail for a  common hand. Even the solace  of  the  taverns was  denied  to  him.
