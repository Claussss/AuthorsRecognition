  Killing the Queen would be one solution, but somehow I shrank from cold-blooded murder ... or hotblooded murder for that matter. No. What was needed was something to throw a scare into her. A big scare.   The answer walked past me before I recognized it. Fortunately, it was moving slowly, so I turned and caught up with it in just a few steps.   Answers come in many shapes and sizes. This one was in the form of a Deveel with a small tray display hung by a strap around his neck.
