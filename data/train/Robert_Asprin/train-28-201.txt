Molin Torchholder.  Illyra  recognized the  name.  He was  the  priestly temple builder within the Rankan prince's entourage. She had another friend and  patron living within his household.  Was this the woman  of Cappen Varra's idylls?  Had the minstrel finally overstepped himself? 'Why would the Rankan have his way with you?' she asked, prying gently. 'They have sought to build a temple for their gods.'
