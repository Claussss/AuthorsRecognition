Two Hell-Hounds trotted down the street below, paused momentarily at the  corner and gave a whistle which was answered from two streets away. Lalo wondered  what had happened to the mercenaries. Then  a shadow rose from the opposite  rooftop, glimmering like silver as it came into the full light of the moon.
