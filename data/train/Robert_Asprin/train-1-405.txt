The lanterns weren't an act of charity toward travelers. They were intended to drive lurking footpads into somebody else's doorway. Khamwas paused, then directed them up one arm of a five-way inter- section, past a patrol station. The gate to the internal courtyard was lighted by flaring sconces, and there was a squad on guard outside. An officer took a step into the street as if to halt the trio, but he changed his mind after a pause.
