The nose was misshapen now, as if some drunken potter had pressed too hard  into the clay. Lalo stared at his model  and threw down his pen. The face  before him bore no resemblance to the one he had drawn! 'Go away!' he said hoarsely. 'I can't do what you ask of me -1 can't do anything anymore ...' He began to shake his head and could not stop. 'You need a drink.' Pewter clinked against the tabletop.
