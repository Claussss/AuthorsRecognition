Tidwell bounded backward, away from his injured foe and backpedaled to the far end of the platform. As Aki moved to follow, he pegged the knife into the platform at his feet, dropped to one knee, and held his arms out from his body at shoulder height. "Aki! Stop!" Aki paused, puzzled. "Stop and listen!" Suspiciously, Aki retreated slowly to the far end of the platform, putting distance between himself and Tidwell, but he listened.
