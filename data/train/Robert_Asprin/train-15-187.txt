   Her eyes popped open. Standing over her, a look of concern on his face and a large tray balanced on his right hand, was a room service waiter.    "Nothing's wrong," she snapped, and began to rise to her feet, gathering her carefully ripped dress close around her.    "But, miss, you asked for help," the waiter said, a confused look on his face.    "Oh, shut up," she said and flounced away. The waiter stared after her for a moment, then shrugged and went about his business.
