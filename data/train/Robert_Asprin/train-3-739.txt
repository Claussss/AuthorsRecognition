Everywhere there was a wealth of grain and flowers and fruit, and dancing nymphs and gauzy drapery and ewers of outpoured  wine. The wood on which the mural  was painted was warped, and Shipri had wormholes in her, in embarrassing places. Harran sat down beside her for a moment. "Do you regret it?" Mriga looked at him out of big hazel eyes. "Me myself? Or she and I?"
