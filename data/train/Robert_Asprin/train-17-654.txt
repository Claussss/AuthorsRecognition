'You seek death here, thief!' a voice said, and light flooded the chamber. Hanse didn't  pause to  reply or  look to  see who  bore the  light. He  turned, plucking forth a guardless knife like a leaf of steel, and threw. Only then  did he really look at the man in  the doorway; throw once to disconcert, the  second time with aim.
