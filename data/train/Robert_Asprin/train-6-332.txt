   "Do you see those signal towers?" I asked, pointing at the distant structures.    Ajax rose to his feet and squinted in the indicated direction. "Sure can," he nodded.    "We ... um ... I was wondering," I explained, "can you use your bow to disrupt their signals?"    In response, Ajax drew an arrow from beneath his cloak, cocked it, and let fly before I could stop him.    The shaft disappeared in the direction of the nearest tower. With sinking heart, I strained my eyes trying to track its flight.
