Ignoring the stares, she pushed her blue wool cloak from her broad shoulders and shrugged her pack to the floor. The strong heavy scent  of beer and sizzling  meat made Wess's mouth  water. She sought out the man behind the bar. 'Citizen,' she said,  carefully pronouncing  the Sanctuary  language, the  trade tongue of all the continent, 'are you the proprietor? My friends and I, we  need a room for the night, and dinner.'
