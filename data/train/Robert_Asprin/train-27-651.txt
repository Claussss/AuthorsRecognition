'Good intentions will  not deceive him,  either.' Myrtis had  become kind-voiced again. 'Your need for vengeance will not make you a courtesan. There are  others here who can bell our cat.' 'No,' Cythen protested. 'He'll come here  again and make his mistake again,  and he might kill another of your courtesans.  Isn't it to your advantage to let  me risk my life rather than sacrificing one of those who belong to you?'
