Faster and faster moved the artist's  hand, covering the wall with a  scrollwork of figures that writhed one into another, contorting the outline that  contained them. Here was the  face of a woman  raped to death in  one of the upper  rooms, there the desperate clutch of a man robbed of the coppers that would have  saved his family.
