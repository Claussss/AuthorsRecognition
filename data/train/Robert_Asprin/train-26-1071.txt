They passed over a wide porch, floored in jet, and a doorway loomed  up before them,  containing great depths  of still, blackness, silent, cold. Against  that dark Siveni's  spearhead sizzled faint  and pitiful, the smoking wick of a lamp of lightnings, drowning in the immensity of night.
