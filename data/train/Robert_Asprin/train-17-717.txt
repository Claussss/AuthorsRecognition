'Give me  the power  you forced  on me  last night!'  Lalo's voice strengthened. 'Give me the power to paint the soul!' The laughter of  Enas Yorl began  as the whisper  in the sand  that precedes the simoom, but it grew until Lalo was physically buffeted by the waves of  pressure in the room. And then, after a little, there was silence again, and the sorcerer asked, 'Are you quite sure?' Lalo nodded once more.
