"If she waits much longer," the crusty woman admitted, "that path may well rise up to bite her feet. It is not to be denied."  "But she will deny it, amoushka"-a S'danzo diminutive for grand- mother or elder seeress. "She sees Trevya wherever she turns, but her heart only grows harder." The Termagant snorted. "She is a little fool who should by now know what happens when children get tangled up in the Sight and fate."
