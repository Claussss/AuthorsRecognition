He continued along the  Wideway, past the wharves  where the foreign ships  were berthed, pulling at their moorings  like a nobleman's horses tethered  outside a peasant's sty. Some of  the merchants had spread  out their wares on  the docks, and  Lalo  threaded  his  way  among  knots  of  people  bickering  over prices, exchanging insults and news  with equal good humour.
