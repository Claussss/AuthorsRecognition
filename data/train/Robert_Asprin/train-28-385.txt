'I don't know, I don't know,' Cappen groaned. 'I think  you can  go on  aways, whether  or no  things are  as the  witchmaster supposes. We hold where I come from that no man can flee his weird, so he may as well meet it in a way that'll leave  a good story. Besides, this may not be  our death-day; and I doubt yon dragons  are unkillable, but it could be  fun finding out; and chiefly, I was much taken with your girl. Not many like her, my friend.
