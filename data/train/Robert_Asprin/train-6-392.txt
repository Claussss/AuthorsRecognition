   "Mornin', youngster," the bowman hailed, emerging from the bush. "Think I got us a place all picked out."    "Hi, Ajax," I replied. "Say... um... when you get a minute, I'd like to talk to you about last night."    "Think nothin' of it," Ajax assured me with a grin. "I've plum fergot about it already."    There was a glint in his eye that contradicted his words, but if he was willing to pretend nothing had happened, I'd go along with it for now.
