"I've got to get out of this  damned town," Hanse muttered in a voice  wavery as the sand-viper, and  went out the  window. He had  to drag himself  back up that fulvistone wall on one silken rope so that he could go down another-all the  way across  the palace  grounds and  wall and  the Processional  to where  Kama  and company would have made the arrow-end of the line fast.
