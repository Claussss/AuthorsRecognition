In some part he hoped Crit would give up finally, let him alone, simply let him coast into oblivion. Or hit him and give him cause-some cause, any cause to fight for- But Crit, who had killed more men than anyone could remember, some of them piece by piece and slowly, looked at him as if he was feeling that kind of pain himself-as if someone hurt him and made him crazy, and he loved that someone too much to do what he would do to anyone else in the wide world who crossed him as far as Strat had.
