            For a minute I thought he was going to ruin everything, but then he turned and slunk to Aahz's side and stood there regarding me with mournful blue eyes.             Everything             "Well, good-bye. Good luck!" I called, and trudged slowly back over the knoll, hopefully a picture of abject misery.             Once out of sight, however, I turned and began to sprint as fast as I could in a wide circle around the inn.
