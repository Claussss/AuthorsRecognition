A second call led him down a  passageway too narrow to be called an  alley, much less a street. Moving with stealth and caution, Walegrin eased around  forgotten doorways suspecting ambush with every step. Only a third call and the appearance of a familiar face in the shadows quickened his pace. 'Malm, what is  it?' he asked,  stepping over some  soft, stinking mass  without looking down. 'See for yourself.'
