'Because it isn't my business to  deny what you wish known about  yourself. Even to my oldest friend, my first lover.' Lythande  stared up  at the  curved surface  of the  inside of  the sphere.  The tension eased in the set of her shoulders, the expression on her face. 'Thank you, little sister,' she said, her voice full of relief. 'I did not  know if my identity were safe with you.
