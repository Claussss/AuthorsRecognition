'Father\I am busy\' He made a flinging gesture and disappeared. Mizraith made the same gesture and the boy came back. 'We can do this all night. Or you can talk to me.' Noticeably less rampant. 'This is unforgiveable.' He raised his arm to make  the pass again;  then checked  it as  Mizraith did  the same.  'Clothe me.'  A brick disappeared, and Marype was wearing a tunic of woven gold. 'Tell me you are not in the thrall of Markmor.'
