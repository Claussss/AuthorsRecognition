That was right before Hanse had left  town, in a hurry. Apparently he had  taken with him the eldest daughter of the murdered S'danzo, Moon-flower. Next morning, Notable was gone, too.  Just short of frantic, Ahdio searched  and asked; put out the word. Notable was gone without a trace. At least it was  hard to imagine  such a  fighter's having  been snatched  and used  to fill someone's hungry belly. Ahdio swallowed hard, then turned up his mug.
