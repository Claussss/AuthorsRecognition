"Men call me venal, yet they do not know, the depths of my depravity and desire ..." Feltheryn began. "Yes, that one!" Sashana cut him off. "Can you imagine it? And he delivered it badly! Well, of course I took my cue and went to the table, but Vomistritus is not that much an imbecile. There were no knives. The whole repast was finger-food, and none of it very good-looking, either." "He might at least have had a decent meal prepared," said Glisselrand.
