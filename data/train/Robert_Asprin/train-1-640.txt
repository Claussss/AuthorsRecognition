Always a curl to her voice. Everything was a question that needed- no, demanded-an answer. But this time it would not work. "There's herbs left from Masha zil-Ineel-from Herself's congestion- that could be brewed up?" "I'm fine, Suyan," Illyra said at last. "I slept fine. The baby didn't bother me. You didn't bother me. And I don't need any herbs- just ..." She inhaled a pause and wondered what she did need. "I'm going uptown today. What I need is a change of scenery."
