Smhee finally relieved her bursting question. 'They look like the bengil crab of  Sharranpip. They live in that hole but  they must catch fish in the river.' 'What is that to us?' 'I think the pool  must be an entrance  to a cave. Or  caves. The crabs are  not water-breathers.' 'Are they dangerous?' 'Only when in water. On land they'll  either run or, if cornered, try to  defend themselves. They aren't poisonous, but their claws are very powerful.'
