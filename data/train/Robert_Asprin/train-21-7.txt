Still smiling, I picked up the goblet of wine I had brought with me for moral support and started to take a sip. "Oh, Aahz isn't so bad." For a startled moment, I thought Gleep had answered me. Then I realized the voice had come from behind me, not from my pet. A quick glance over my shoulder confirmed my worst fears. My partner, green scales, pointed teeth and all, was leaning against the wall not ten feet from where I stood, and had apparently been listening to my whole oration.
