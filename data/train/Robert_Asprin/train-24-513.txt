"Still has not departed... Attack Alert, groups from North and Southeast closing." "Attack Alert!" I relayed, but it was unnecessary. The sounds of the hot-beams were deadly soft as the other two teams opened fire on the advancing Enemy. The sound was soon lost in the shriek of dying Leapers. "Zur," I beamed, "the hot-beams are effective against the Ants?" "Most satisfactory, Commander," came the reply.
