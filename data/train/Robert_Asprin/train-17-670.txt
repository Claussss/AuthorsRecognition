somewhere near, he  began to fear  that he would  never see Tempus,  and then of course he did see him. Healed and scarless. Hanse went home and threw up. He traded a few things for more  strong drink, and he got drunk and  stayed that way  for a  while. He  just didn't  feel like  stealing, or  facing Tempus,   or Kadakithis either. He did dream,  of two gods and a  girl of sixteen or so.  Ils and Shalpa and Mignureal. And quicklime.
