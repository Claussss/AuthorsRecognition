The  double clang  of gratings  behind him  was not  really unexpected.  Samlor waited, poised behind  his knife point,  but no one  came down the  stone boring from either direction. No one and  no thing. Samlor resumed walking, the  tunnel curving and perhaps descending slightly with each step. The stone was  beginning to vibrate, a tremor that was too faint to be music.
