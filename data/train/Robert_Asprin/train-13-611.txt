   Ernie, who had an excellent idea what was likely to happen if he made the wrong move, grinned broadly. He intended to be very careful not to do anything that the bodyguard might decide to interpret as unfriendly.    "It looks like I'm on a hot streak today," he said. "Been cleaning up over at the roulette table all morning."    "Good for you," said Victor Phule. "The owners don't know it, but they're giving money away hand over fist.
