Illyra jumped at  the sound. Only  two of the  candles remained lit;  she had no idea how long she had slept, only that her home quivered with shadows and a man, as tall as Dubro but of cadaverous thinness, stood within the knotted rope. 'Who are you? What do you want?' She flattened against the back of the chair. 'Since you do not recognize me, then say, I have been looking for you.' The  man gestured.
