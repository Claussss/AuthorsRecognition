When all was prepared,  Lythande entered the darkened  room. There was no  light but the light of the Blue Star. The girl lay on a bed, stretching up her arms to the magician with exalted abandon. 'Come to me, come to me, my love!' 'Soon,' said Lythande, sitting beside  her, stroking her hair with  a tenderness even Myrtis  would never  have guessed.  'I will  sing to  you a love-song of my people, far away.'
