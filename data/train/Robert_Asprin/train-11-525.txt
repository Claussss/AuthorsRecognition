   WE needed to score some points, and to do that, we needed the ball.    That thought was foremost in my mind as we lined up again. One way or another, we were going to get that ball.    When the whistle sounded, I was ready for it. Reaching out with my mind, I brought the ball winging to my grasp. Before our team could form up around me, however, the whistle sounded again and the Jahk in the striped tunic came trotting toward us waving his arms.
