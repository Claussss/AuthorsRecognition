But Tempus had  no more time  for watching birds  or taking chances;  he heard a dagger fall  from the  bolthole's height  as he  waded amid  the coils-first  to Stealth, who still fought  gamely though ichor had  burned one eye shut  and his limbs were bound with writhing snake. Pitting all his strength against the  failing power of the snake- now  shrinking but perhaps not fast enough-the Riddler struggled.
