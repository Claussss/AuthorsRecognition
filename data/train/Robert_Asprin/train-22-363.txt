The knife-point clinked  on the far  capstone. Samlor squirmed  a little nearer, prayed to Heqt, and thrust outwards  with his left hand. The stone  swung aside. Breathable air flooded the Cirdonian with the rush of organ music. Too relieved to be concerned at what besides air might wait beyond the  opening, Samlor struggled  out.
