No ordinary doxy,  this: a  courtesan of  quality strayed  from some rendezvous, an opportunity some fickle god had tossed into his path or him into hers. 'Well,' he said, and flung wide his arms, leaned from one side of the way to the other to block her attempt to walk around him ... a little fun, he reckoned.
