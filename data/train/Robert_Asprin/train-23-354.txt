He suddenly noticed that Whitey was shrinking on the viewscreen. Not that the reception was bad, but rather that she seemed to be sagging... folding in on herself. "Are you all right?" he asked, suddenly solicitous. "I didn't mean to shout at you. It's just that things have been pretty rough at this end." Whitey shook her head, but this time she didn't raise her eyes. "That's all right. It's what you're saying, not how you're saying it that's made up my mind."
