A short time later, Hanse responded to Strick's request to visit. He met the young Lady Esaria on her way out. Neither recognized the other because neither knew the other. Somewhere, the goddess Eshi smiled. "Hanse," Strick said without any preamble at all, "a man needs your help. A client needs a service only you can perform." Hanse put on his face of sweet innocence. "I can't imagine what you mean."
