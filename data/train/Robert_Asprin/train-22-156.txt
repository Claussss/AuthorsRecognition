'Over there is the formation known as the  ape's head,  and at  the other  end is  that which  the natives call the dragon's tail.' On the  edge of  the isle  grew some  trees, and  in the  waters by  it were the ubiquitous tall reeds. There was no sight or sound of life on it. Even the birds seemed to shun it.
