   With a quick brush of my mind, I altered Gus's appearance. Now they had a full-grown dragon hovering over their midst. The effect was spectacular.    I, however, did not allow myself the luxury of watching. I had learned something in this brief exchange, and I wanted to try it out.    I dissolved my Imp army, then reformed them, not in front of the troops, but in their midst!    This threw the formations into total disorder. As the soldiers struck or threw at the phantom figures, more often than not they struck their comrades instead.
