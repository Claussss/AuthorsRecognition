The procession continued on towards the canopy. The rain had stopped completely. A sliver of moonlight showed  through the dispersing clouds. Torchholder  loudly hailed  the  break in  the  clouds as  an  omen of  the  forgiving, sanctifying, presence of  Vashanka and  began the  ritual.
