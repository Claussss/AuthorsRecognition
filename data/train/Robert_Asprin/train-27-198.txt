Then she  sent her  servant forth,  unwitting, the  extra satisfaction - gleaned from knowing  that his  spirit knew,  and deep  within him  cried and  struggled giving the whole endeavour spice. Jagat's men would see him to the road out near the Stepsons' barracks; they took his sagging weight in brawny arms. And Roxane, for a time, was free  to quit this scrofulous town and wend  her way
