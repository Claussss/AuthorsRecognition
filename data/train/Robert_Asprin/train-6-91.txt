   Following his gaze, I saw the general speaking urgently with the king. The king listened for a moment, then shrugged and said something to the magician.    Whatever he said, the magician didn't like it. Drawing himself up haughtily, he turned to leave, only to be called back by the king. Pointing to the clouds, the king said a few more words and leaned back. The magician hesitated, then shrugged, and began gesturing and chanting once more.
