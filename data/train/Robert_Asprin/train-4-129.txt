  "How's that?"   "You've got a wee bit of trouble coming your way, at least, according to the gentleman I met on the road. Here he comes now. Bye."   Queen Hemlock had already disappeared, vanishing into the depths of the palace like a puff of smoke. Instead, I found myself focusing on the man who had stepped from the carriage and was currently trudging up the stairs toward my throne. I observed that he had the same weasel features and manners of J. R. Grimble.
