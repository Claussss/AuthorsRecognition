Have you had anything to complain of about my work so far?" "Nothing," said Molin. He sounded as if he would rather have had complaints. "And have you not been checking  the actual building against the plans  each day and each night?  And have you  or your spies  found one stone  out of place,  or anything not just as it should be?" Molin Torchholder stared at her. "Then let me do my work and take  my wage in peace." She looked at him  merrily.
