Where the Stepsons kept their horses in a ramshackle stable. That association was what gnawed at Zip. He poured blood from his own veins over these ancient stones, hoping for an Ilsigi god. Even an Ilsigi devil would do-something of Sanctuary's own people and not the occupation forces. And something, finally, finally glowed within the crevices of the stones -glowed and winked out again. "What do you want?" he cried, kneeling on the dirty cobbles, pounding his fists on his knees. "What do you want me to do?"
