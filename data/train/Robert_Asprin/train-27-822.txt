The tunnel was  as good a  hiding place as could be found; and if the guard had been placed in the temple, it was at  least probable that Star would be brought to it by her captors. Samlor slipped  back the  way he  had come.  He set  the tip  of the  Beysib bow between the edge  of the trap  door and its  jamb.
