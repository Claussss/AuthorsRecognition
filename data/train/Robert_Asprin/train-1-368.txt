"Now the others," chirped a little voice. "Do not kill a snake and leave its tail!" The caravan master was on his knees. He did not recall closing his eyes, but he opened them now. The man with the staff was on his feet again and straightening his disordered cape. The manikin was back on his shoulder, strutting proudly with hands on hips. "You," said Samlor very distinctly. "Shit it in or you'll join 'em."
