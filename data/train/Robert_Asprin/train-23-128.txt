Tambu's eyes wandered to the third ship, floating silently next to their recent opponent. Having now seen how fast a ship could heal itself after an attack, he could appreciate anew the extent of the attack which had wrecked such havoc as to leave a ship gaping open like that. "We got him!" Puck's awe-filled voice came over the intercom. "Keep your guns on him!" Tambu snapped. "We don't know if he has any more surprises up his sleeve."
