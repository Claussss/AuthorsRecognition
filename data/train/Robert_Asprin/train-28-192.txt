'If you can't wait, I'll see you now,' she yelled. 'You will?' Illyra untied the rope and lifted the hanging cloth to let the woman enter.  She had surrounded herself with  a shapeless, plain shawl;  her face was veiled  and shadowed  by a  corner of  the shawl  wound around  her head.  The stranger  was certainly not someone who came to the S'danzo of the bazaar often. Illyra retied the rope after seating her patron on one side of the velvet-covered table.
