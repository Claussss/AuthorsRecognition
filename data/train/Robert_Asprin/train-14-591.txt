   "Captain Jester, I assume? I'm Major Matthew O'Donnel."    Startled at being greeted by name, Phule nonetheless managed a snappy salute.    "Welcome to Haskin's Planet, Major."    O'Donnel neither returned the salute nor offered to shake hands.    "Yeah. I'm sure," he said with a tight humorless smile. "Look, Captain, I imagine you're about as happy to see us as we are to be here. Now, is there somewhere we can talk? Somewhere air-conditioned, if possible. I'd like to get this foolishness settled as fast as possible."
