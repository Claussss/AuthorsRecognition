Lalo surfaced, groaning, from  a dream in which  he had been taken  prisoner and dragged through the night  until... Something hit him  hard in the ribs  and  he opened his eyes. It was morning, and it had not been a dream. He saw flaking white-washed  walls, and splintered crates and  furniture heaped on the  bare earth of the  floor. It was not a prison  then.
