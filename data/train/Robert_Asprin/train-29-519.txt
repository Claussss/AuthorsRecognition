"Come with me," he said again. His voice was the high wind, and when he spoke no more she still heard his words. He was the sound of the night. She looked into his eyes. Hand in hand, they stepped from Sabellia's garden niche and onto the pathway. Out of respect for the silence that shrouded the park the gravel refused to crunch beneath their tread. Unable to help herself, Tiana smiled. The moonlight continued to shine on the small bust in the Promise of Heaven.
