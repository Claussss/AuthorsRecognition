Almost gently, then, his fingers touched the sleeve and, through it, the arm  of the man. 'Cappen Varra,' Stulwig said. The young man with the long black  hair that rested on his shoulders turned  his head.  The tone  ofStulwig's voice  was evidently  not threatening;  for Cappen merely paused without tensing. Nor did he make a quick reach of the hand towards the blade at his side. But it took several moments before he seemed to realize who his interceptor was.
