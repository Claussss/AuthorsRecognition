Fighting off his apprehensions, the reporter seated himself at the console. "I am addressing Tambu?" he asked with an ease he did not truly feel. "That is correct, Mr. Erickson. I notice you've brought a Tri-D recorder with you. As I will not be meeting you face to face, it is unnecessary. The console at which you are seated is recording our conversation. You will be supplied with a copy. Visually, there will be nothing to record."
