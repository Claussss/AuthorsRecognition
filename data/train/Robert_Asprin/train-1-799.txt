Teretaff smiled, then laughed, and embarrassed himself when laughter became sobs. In manner womanly, his daughter Jileel went to embrace him- Uncomfortable, Hanse began backing. "I have to go now." He swallowed. "Got an appointment, you know." "Young man." Hanse swallowed again. "Name's Hanse, ma'am." "Hanse, then. And I am called the Termagant. You know that I am the senior amoushem; first among the S'danzo with the Ability. Moonflower liked you. I know, and Mignureal ... well. I admit that I never had much-I never had any use for you. That has changed. You may consider me friend. Hanse.
