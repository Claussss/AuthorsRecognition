Narvy was flailing both arms, to one  of which clung a chomping cat anchored  by twenty or so claws and an unknown number of needly teeth. "Get him offf meee!" poor Narvy shrieked. "Are you daft or jesting, man? I'm not wearing mailed gloves!" Screaming enough for six, Narvy wheeled and limp-dashed out the open doorway  in the wake of his friend- who was already out of sight. "Sweetboy! Let's have a drink!"
