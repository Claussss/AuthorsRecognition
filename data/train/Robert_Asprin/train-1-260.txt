"Agreed," Kama said with a ghost of her father's smile. Then they climbed the steps and Crit put his back against the stone, crossbow ready, attempting to cover every avenue of attack while Kama tried key after key and cursed like a Nisibisi freeman. Finally she said, "No luck. Nothing works." And slumped against the doorjamb. They looked at each other too long, and Crit had to look away. It was in that silence that they heard something move inside, behind the stout wood of the door.
