Bush was silent for a few moments, his eyes not meeting Pete's glare. Finally he sighed. "You're right, Pete. I should have leveled with you sooner." He opened a drawer on his desk and withdrew a sheath of papers, tossing them on the desk in front of Pete. "Here, look at these." Pete picked up the sheets and started leafing through them. They were photocopies of the rough drafts of some documents. Crossed-out paragraphs and note-filled margins abounded. Whatever they were, they were a long way from presentation state.
