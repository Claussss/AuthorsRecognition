"The little dog too?" said the ferryman. "Dogs go for half fare." Tyr stood on her hind legs to give  the ferryman the coin, then sat down on  the boat's middle seat, grinning, and barked, thumping her tail on the gunwale. "Why, thank you, missy,  that's a kindness and  so I shall," said  the ferryman, hastily pocketing the  second half of  Tyr's coin, which  he had bitten  in two.
