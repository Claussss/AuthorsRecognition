"A bird who flies to the nest of another," chirped the manikin senten- tiously, "will lose a feather." "What in hell is that?" asked the caravan master deliberately, pointing at the manikin with his right index finger. The bodkin-bladed push dag- ger paralleled the gesturing finger as if by chance.
