   Around me the empty-eyed cow skulls started to hum faintly      and vibrate a little, filling the room with a noise that ate at my      very soul.    "Whatever we're going to do," Tanda said, her hands over      her ears, "let's do it fast."    Again I stuffed the map in my pouch and, with my hands over my ears as well, I headed through the middle      of thousands of humming skulls toward the secret panel in the far wall.
