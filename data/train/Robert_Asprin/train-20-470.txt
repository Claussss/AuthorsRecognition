Dawn's  first light  pierced the  shadows and  sent the  denizens of  the  night scurrying.  The  streets of  Sanctuary  were almost  quiet.  Flocks of  seabirds wheeled silently  over the  town, swooping  suddenly as,  one after another, the houses opened  their doors  to jettison  nightslops into  the street.
