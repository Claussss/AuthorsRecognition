Still again Hanse swallowed. It was his way not to act honored, but he could not escape the feeling that this was like being acknowledged friend by the Prince-Governor, as he had been. Suddenly his stance changed, and his grin was the old cocky one. "My occupation hasn't changed. Termagant." She blinked. "I do not hear you. A friend entrusted a bag of money to you for her father, and you brought it this long way."
