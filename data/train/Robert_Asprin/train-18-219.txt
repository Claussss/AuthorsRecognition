He tottered on the edge, reached up hands cold as clay and held to Haught as  to his last and only hope. There is something that shines and I see it, dead man. It beckons the powerful with an irresistible lust. And she dares not. The dust shines and shimmers and falls everywhere and she dares not gather  that power up. She seals up the ways. She burns it with fire. Nisi power. She loathes it and desires it.
