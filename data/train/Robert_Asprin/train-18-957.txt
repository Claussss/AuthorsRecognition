Strat blinked and groaned. Stilcho hoped he'd understood. There wasn't time  for more. The door was opening. He prayed he wouldn't have to watch. "I said the table," Haught said in his soft, malice-laden voice. Stilcho shrugged  and thought,  carefully, about  being dead.  But Haught had no energy for the likes of him, not with Roxane-Stilcho's empty eye saw Roxane, not Tasfalen-hovering behind him and Strat helpless at his feet.
