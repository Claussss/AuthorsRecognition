'They will most likely  follow the same path  we did, since they  are carrying a body also,' she  explained as she  laid the ropes  across the mud,  burying them slightly. 'We will trip them here.' 'And I will switch our corpse for the girl?' They said nothing more as each crouched in a mud-hole waiting, hoping, that  the procession would pass between them. The luck promised in her dream held.
