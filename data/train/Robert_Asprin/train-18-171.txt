Thus she didn't see the snake until it reared, fangs wide and gaping, and struck like lightning, biting Arton on the arm. Then, wide awake with  two terrified babes to  hold, one wounded and  screaming, the other howling just as loudly, she cowered. To reach her sword or freeze the  snake, arching high above the bed and  glaring fire-eyed down upon her, she'd have to put down one or both children.
