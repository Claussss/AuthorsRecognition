Haught, not reserved, not holding  so much of himself  back: this man was  fever mad, and  it was  all going  to happen  right here  in the  drawing-room for the servants and all to gawk at if she did not prevent him.... "Upstairs," she murmured, fending off his hands from her. "Upstairs."
