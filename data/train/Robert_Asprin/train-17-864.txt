With an  unpleasant smile  the man-god  strode to  the table  where he  ripped a mouthful of bread from the loaf,  drained the beaker of salted water  and lifted the crude sword. He shifted it once or twice in his hand, his fingers  adjusting to  its awkward  balance. With  the same  smile still  on his  lips he  advanced towards the terrified men in white.
