Scavenger's Island-pirate haven. A place which made Sanctuary at its worst seem serene and orderly by comparison. Scourge of the seas, Harrier of the coast, and, also, a place which had generally regarded Sanctuary as a poor relation and left it alone. But Sanctuary wasn't poor any longer. "How does this tie to the missing men?" Hakiem asked, completely sober now. Hort shrugged. "Some go willingly as recruits, the rest as galley slaves."
