The homely woman blinked. She looked at Hanse. "But why-whatever have you done to offend a ho'house madam, Hanse?" But Shadowspawn was staring at Throde. His face registered astonishment, or perhaps it was revelation. Whatever it was, his eyes showed that he had gone back inside himself, where he was deep in cerebration, and calculation, and machination. Abruptly he rose and left. The other three stared at the doorway through which he had departed their company. Ahdio gave his head a shake.
