Now, he gazed upon the palace itself;  the palace of the golden prince sent  out from Ranke  to (pretend  to) govern  Sanctuary. The  thief smiled,  but with his mouth closed.  Here there  were tigers  in the  form of  guards, and young teeth would flash even in this most wan of moonlight. That precaution was merely  part of his competence.
