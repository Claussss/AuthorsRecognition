There was  silver in  the long  hair; lamplight  limned lean  cheeks and a high, narrow brow where the identifying blue star glowed. Lalo looked away, ashamed to meet the wizard's gaze. How the Adept must despise him, as  he would have sneered at a beggar  who stole his paints  and tried  to paint  the Prince.  But a  beggar would only have made himself ridiculous. Lalo's ignorant misuse of power might doom them all.
