He started to go through the curtains for a bow, .if not to receive applause then to gauge the danger, but Glisselrand took his arm and stopped him. "I think not," she said, and he saw that there were lines in her face that age had not put there. "What's wrong?" he asked. "I don't quite know," she replied, "but I think we shall find out. The Prince and the Beysa have sent word that they are coming backstage. Let's get to the green room."
