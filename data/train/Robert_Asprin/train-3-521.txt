But the revenant  merely lifted a  hand and touched  his face, lover's  gesture. "Dust," it said, which was its only  word; daily Haught swept up the dust  which infiltrated the house, and sifted it  for the dust of magics which  might linger in it, the remnant of the Globe of  Power; with that dust he made a potion,  and dutifully he infused it into this creature, stealing only a little for  himself.
