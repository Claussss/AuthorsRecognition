Hanse wanted the drink. Mradhon Vis turned the comer, none following, stopped against an alley wall  and let the tremors pass from his limbs. Ugly, that back there. Corpses, he had seen - had created his share, in and out of mercenary service. He had no wish to take on useless trouble ... not now, not with gold in his boot and a real prospect of more.
