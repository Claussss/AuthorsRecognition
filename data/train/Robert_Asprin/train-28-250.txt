'But not like that!' 'It is the way of men who have been hung.' They walked  back towards  the charnel-house  where Sanctuary's  undertakers and embalmers held sway. There, for five  copper coins, they found a man  to prepare the body. For another  coin he would have  rented them a cart  and his son as  a digger to take the unfortunate ex-thief to the common field outside the Gate  of Triumph for proper burial.
