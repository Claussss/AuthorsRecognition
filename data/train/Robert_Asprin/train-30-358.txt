"Now, you go lie down on your bed and stay there until Vanda brings your  sister Latilla home." She gripped his small shoulder, propelled him into the children's room, and shut the door behind him with a bang that shook the floor. Wedemir slowly set  his last basket  on the kitchen  table, watching his  mother with an apprehension that belied  the broad shoulders and sturdily  muscled arms he had gotten working the caravans.
