"Strick, I swear: no one else could." Strick sat staring at him in silence until Hanse had to exert his strength to keep from looking down. The expression of wide-eyed innocence that had long served him well with others didn't work with this man. This maker of spells was different. Strick was like ... like no one. At last Hanse asked, "What's the second favor?" "Don't ever come in that way again."
