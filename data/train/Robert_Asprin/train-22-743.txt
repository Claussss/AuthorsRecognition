'Hear me, Vashanka! I have had enough! Get this sidewalk stand out of here!' There was  no answer.  Within, everything  was dim  as dusk,  dim as  the pit of unknowingness which spawned day and night and endless striving. There were no weapons here for him to see, no counter, no proprietor, no rack of armaments pulsing and humming expectantly. But then, he already had his. One  to a customer was the rule: one body; one mind; one swing through life.
