'I'll  find it,' he said, and, less than confident of the situation at hand: 'Are you sure you don't want me to stay about?' Black brows  drew together,  a frown  uncommonly grim.  'I have  no doubts to my safety. - Ah, your name, sir. When I pay, I like to know that.'
