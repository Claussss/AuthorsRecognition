Shortly after noon next  day, Hanse had a  talk with babbly old  Hakiem, who had lately done much babbling  about what a fine  fellow His handsome Highness  was, and how he had even spoken with Hakiem, giving him two pieces of good silver  as well! Today Hakiem listened to Hanse,  and he swallowed often. What could  he do save agree?
