But Bakarat's mind was by no means as sluggish as his wobbly gait. The Toad was known as the most successful merchant and entrepreneur in Sanctuary. Though all found the vision of his person revolting, none could afford to offend the wealthy merchant. The Toad had not attained his exalted economical status by entirely honest means, either. Next to the legendary Jubal, he ran one of Sanctuary's most sophisticated and complex information and crime networks. In fact, it was rumored that the only reason Jubal had not eliminated this potential rival was the fact that Bakarat paid him well to turn a blind eye to his clandestine endeavors.
