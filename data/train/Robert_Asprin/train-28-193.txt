A woman of means who wishes to be mysterious. That shawl might be plain, but  it is too good for someone  as poor as she pretends  to be. She wears silk  beneath it, and smells of roses, though she  has tried to remove perfumes. No doubt  she has gold, not silver or copper. 'Would you  not be  more comfortable  removing your  shawl? It  is quite warm in here,' Illyra said, after studying the woman.
