  "My stock! My tent! Who's going to pay for this?"   "That's Hoozit," Massha retorted, "and I'm certainly not going to pay. I don't have any money."   "No money?" the proprietor gasped.   "No. I just came in here to get out of the rain."   "Rain? Rain? But it isn't raining!"   "It isn't?" my apprentice blinked. "Then, goodbye."   With that she ambled off, making a hole in yet another tent side as she went.
