He did not try it. He looked back again, cast the hood back from a face fire had touched. His eyes were dilated, wild. 'Why do  you come?'  she asked,  intrigued, despite  a life  that had long since lacked variety. In the casual matter of the door she had dropped pretences  that she wore like robes;  he knew, must know,  that he was in  deadly jeopardy. 'Who sent you?' He seemed the sort not to plan, but to do what others planned.
