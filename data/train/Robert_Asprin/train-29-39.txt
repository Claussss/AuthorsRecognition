"And no one else suspects that we're being harvested by pirates?" Again Hakiem had to shake his head. Sanctuary had always been downtrodden-a home to thieves, not the target of pirates. Old habits died hard, indeed. "The Old Man," Hort continued, speaking of his father, "says you can always trust kings and princes to build their walls in the wrong place." I suppose you can, Hakiem agreed in silence.
