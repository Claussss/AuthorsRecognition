Walegrin said, offering his hand. "Anything in my power, but first we talk to Tempus-and don't tell him we've made an agreement." The Prince led the way along unfamiliar corridors. They were in the private part of the palace and the  surroundings, though crude by capital  standards, dazzled Walegrin. He bumped into the Prince when the latter stopped by a closed door. "Now, don't forget-we haven't agreed to anything. No, wait-give me your sword."
