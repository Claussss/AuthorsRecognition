At Lalo's exclamation, the Hell-Hound's pitted-leather face became, if possible, a richer shade of terra cotta, and  his eyes slid away from the painter's  gaze. Lalo followed the  look to the  doorway, and suddenly  began to understand  what power had brought him here, though he was as far as ever from comprehending why.
