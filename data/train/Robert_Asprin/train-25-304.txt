"I still haven't seen the buses." Clancy scowled through the dust and bug-caked windshield of the truck. "Don't worry, they'll be-there they are!" The buses were rounding the curve ahead, bearing down on them with the leisurely pace characteristic of this country. Tidwell watched the vehicle occupants as they passed, craning his neck to see around the driver. The bus passengers smiled and waved joyously, but Tidwell noticed none of them took pictures.
