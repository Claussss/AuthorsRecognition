Instead of  replying to  this insolence,  Jubal studied  the ex-storyteller  for several moments in silence. "What distresses you, old one?" he said finally. "I dealt fairly with your  fish eyed  companion,  even  to  the  point  of  admitting  my  own  weaknesses   and limitations. Still your words and stance reek of disapproval, as they have since you first entered the room. Have I done or said something to offend you?"
