   For several moments, Phule and Beeker stared silently at the place in the room it had occupied. Finally the commander cleared his throat.    "How in the world did someone that ignorant and naive get to be a multimillionaire?"    "Not to belabor the obvious, sir," Becker said softly, "I believe he inherited it."    Phule wrinkled his nose in disgust. While he had borrowed seed money from his munitions-baron father, he had long since paid it all back, with interest, and considered his wealth to be self-made. As such, he had little tolerance for those who inherited their wealth, and none at all for those who were foolish with what money they had.
