   "The one on the far end ... in the white shirt."    Stilman followed her eyes. "So?"    "Look at his arms," Maxine instructed.    The man's shirt was of very fine cotton, and his arms were clearly visible, though it took a moment to realize that it was his arms one was seeing. Adorning the arms, from shoulder to wrist, were colorful swirls of tattoos, so vivid that, to a casual glance, they almost seemed to be a paisley pattern on an undergarment.
