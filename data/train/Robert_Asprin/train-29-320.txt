The Beysa laughed. "Master Snegelringe," she said, "your imitation of the prince was most enlightening. Only less so than the reason for it which we have just had revealed. But perhaps you might choose another model for the performance you will give tomorrow night." "Unless," said Feltheryn, the plot of the play before him coming clear, "Your Highness would consent to see it in another light!" The Prince and the Beysa turned to him and Glisselrand clutched his hand.
