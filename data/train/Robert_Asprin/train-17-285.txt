'EnasYorl.' He dropped his hand from the blind man's arm. 'Him. For what?' 'He wants to talk to you. You come - recommended. And you'll be paid.' Hanse took in his breath and fingered his coin, looked down at it a space, found it new minted and  heavy silver, and reckoned  uneasily in what quarters  he was recommended.
