I barely turned around from shutting the door when Bunny charged across the room, slamming into me with a huge hug that took my breath away ... literally. "I was so worried about you!" she said, her voice muffled by my chest. "Ahh ... ack!" That last comment was mine. Actually, it wasn't so much a comment as a noise I made while trying to force some air into my lungs. This proved easier said than done ... and it wasn't all that easy to say!
