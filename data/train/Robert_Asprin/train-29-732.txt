Vanda and Rhian were on either side of Darios now. "Darios-my poor darling! You look like one of your own spirits!" Rhian drew his arm across her shoulder. "Starved-" whispered the mageling, "but even before that ... wasn't handsome. A spell, Rhian ... to make you think so. Forgive me!" "You silly boy!" Rhian shook her head. "Do you think it mattered?" "We'll take you home and let my mother's cooking put some flesh on your bones!" said Vanda, taking his other arm.
