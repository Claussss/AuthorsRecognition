And within the hour, in a dark  recess of the house with the basilisks  prowling the halls  unchecked, something  gibbered within  a pile  of midnight robes, and with  keen sense  of beauty  imprisoned in  that moaning  heap, longed   towards Darous, who saw nothing, sensed the  essence of this change and kept  himself to other halls.
