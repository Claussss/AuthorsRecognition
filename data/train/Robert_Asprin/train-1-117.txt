"Kindness?" Cade asked. "Or are you looking for something, boy?" "Neither, who knows. Just asked." The boy's voice turned hard. "Sorry I bothered you, pud," and he moved away, not quite showing his back to Cade. "Wait!" Cade said. "Wait." He moved to catch up to the boy, but the youth kept his distance. "Who are you?" "What's it to you?" The boy crouched a bit, his body tense. Not wor- ried yet, but definitely wary. Cade threw the boy a silver piece which the lad caught deftly.
