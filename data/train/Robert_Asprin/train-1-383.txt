"My sister, though," he added, embarrassed himself for reasons the other man should not be able to fathom, "that was more her line." To the extent that anything besides sex was Samlane's line. "I see," said Khamwas, and he continued to glance down at the child even as he returned to the earlier question. "I don't know Setios at all," he explained, "but I know-I've been told by, well-" He shrugged. Samlor nodded grimly; but if this fellow called himself scholar rather than wizard, he at least recognized that the latter was a term of reproach to decent men.
