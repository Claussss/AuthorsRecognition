She spat again, but this time for  Mama Becho's sake the wad landed squarely  on Zip's  boot. "I'm  everything that  Tempus is,  lover," she  said,  grim-voiced, mocking his trepidation. "And more. You  don't believe that yet, but you  will." She turned her back to him, went to the serving board. To Mama she said, "Got  a pair of dice?" The old woman reached up onto a shelf and found a pair of yellowed ivory  cubes.
