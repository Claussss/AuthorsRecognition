"It's in. Come take a cup of something cold with me." She stepped  down from  the stone,  wondering about  the odd  tone in his voice, schooling herself to show no reaction. Carelessly she walked in front of him  to the tent he'd had set  up at the site, so  that he could watch the  workers, and her, in comfort. She  flung one flap on  its door aside. Silk,  she thought. And not because it makes the best tents, either.
