"Anything else, my boy? Scandals, magic, two-headed calves?" Hort relaxed and began one of many tales, about a love charm gone remarkably awry. It was nearly dawn when Hakiem made his way out of the Maze to West Gate Street. He'd stayed out later than planned, drunk more than he should, and could practically feel his plump bed beneath his cheeks. A group of tired guards hailed him as he came through the gate, then looked the other way as he took a candle from the rack and slipped into the backways.
