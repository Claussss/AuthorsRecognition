Lalo's eyes narrowed. He drew his brush through the light ochre and began. A half  hour went  by, and  an hour.  Lalo worked  steadily without really being conscious either of the  passage of time or  of what he was  doing. Zanderei was light and shadow,  color and texture  and line-a problem  in interpretation.
