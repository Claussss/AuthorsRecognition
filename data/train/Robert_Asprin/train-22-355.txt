The Cirdonian  left both  suits of  armour in  the room.  They had not preserved other wearers. Wizard mail and its  tricks were for those who could  control it, and Samlor was all too conscious of his own humanity. The passageway bent, then  formed a tee with  a narrow corridor a  hundred paces long. The corridor was closed at either end by living rock. Its far wall was, by contrast, artificial -  basalt hexagons a  little more than  a foot in  diameter
