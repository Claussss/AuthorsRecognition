   "Working?" Snipe stared at the group. "You'd better be working. This isn't a leisure club, you know."    "Man's a genius," muttered somebody just out of Snape's direct line of sight.    Snipe decided to ignore the sally, which after all might be interpreted as a compliment of sorts. "Exactly what sort of work are you doing?" he asked.    A young, round-faced legionnaire with old-fashioned eyeglasses answered him. "That is an excellent question, Lieutenant. Perhaps if we all inquire carefully, we will learn the answer."
