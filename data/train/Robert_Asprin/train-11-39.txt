   He began moving back and forth through the room, gathering items from our possessions.    "First," he announced, "here's some money of your own for the trip. You probably won't need it, but you always walk a little taller with money in your pouch."    So saying, he counted out twenty gold pieces into my hands. Realizing I had hired a team of demons to fight a war for five gold pieces, he was giving me a veritable fortune!
