"She  wants  you-not  at the  uptown  house  tomorrow. Stay  away.  Come  to the riverhouse. After midnight." He closed his hand on the ring. A shudder ran through him with a reaction he had no wish to betray to the slave's amusement. He kept his face cold and his  voice steady. "I'll be there," he said. "I'll tell her  that," Haught said  with uncommon civility,  and whisked himself around the comer again.
