Unicorn, of course-the soul of the Vulgar Unicorn.... Lalo's hand jerked and stopped. He shuddered as unwelcome knowledge flooded  in. Here in this booth  a man had died  not long ago-his lifeblood  flowing from the stroke of a deftly-placed  blade. He had struggled,  and blood had splashed  the wall-that  smear Lalo  had assumed  was soot  before. Without  his volition  the charcoal swept around it, incorporating it as a blacker shadow within the whole.
