'Don't let the fish-eyes gobble you up!' he replied. Gilla snorted. 'In broad daylight? I'd like to see them try! Besides, our  Vanda says they're only people like ourselves, for all their funny looks, and  serving that Lady Kurrekai,  she should know.  Will you trust  Bazaar tales or  your own daughter's word?' She  backed out of  the doorway, hoisted  the child on  to one broad haunch, and scooped up the market basket.
