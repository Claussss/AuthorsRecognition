Come ahead, something said. come ahead, come ahead- He took the first step, he took the second, not really wanting it, but he felt his hand brush the gate, felt it leave his hand- He walked the path and ducked aside into the weeds and into the hedges, where a crack in the shutters gave forth a seam of light into the brush; he worked his way most carefully into this hedge against the house and rose up beneath the window, carefully, carefully to peer into the crack-
