   "Tanda!" I crowed with delight, forcing my eyes back to her face.    "Do you mind coming down?" she called. "I can't come up."    I considered swooping down on her dramatically, but decided against it. I'm still not all that good at flying, and the effect would be lost completely if I crashed into her.    Instead, I settled for lowering myself gently to the ground a few paces from her.    "Gee, Tanda, I... slack!"
