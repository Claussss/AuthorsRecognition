Chapter Six:    "That's entertainment!"    -VLAD THE IMPALER    CROSSING my arms, I moved toward the pavilion, keeping my pace slow and measured.    Aahz had insisted I practice this walk. He said it would make me look confident and self-possessed. Now that I was actually appearing before a king, I found I was using the walk, not as a show of arrogance, but to hide the weakness in my legs.    "Well?" the general rumbled, looming before me. "I asked you a question! Where did you get that pike? You'd best answer before I grow angry!"
