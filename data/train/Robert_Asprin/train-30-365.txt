For  one astounding  moment struck  speechless, Gilla  stared at  him. Then  she snatched and threw a  wooden spoon from the  pot on the stove.  "No, don't thank me, for I'm sorry I did it now!" A colander followed the spoon. She reached  for the copper kettle and Lalo ducked as Wedemir got to his feet, protesting.
