She was real.  She was waiting  for him. It  was wonderful, all  of it with her. Even his wondering, Is she Eshi?, did not inhibit him or her or his enjoyment or hers. What matter whether she was the Esaria she appeared to be or the  goddess; she was higher than he could have aspired, and the experience was supernal. He deduced  that it  really was  Esaria, not  Eshi (in  his dream, of course, he reminded himself) because surely Eshi wouldn't have been eating so much garlic.
