The firewall, around him hot and snapping, gave matters the immediacy of battle, the plain-and-simple truth of life and death. The fire was just a little out of control, and his horse had to leap hot flames. Within, sod  was beginning  to smoke  and combust,  sparks flew,  men yelled and squirted water  on themselves  and their  mounts as  they let  fly with  flaming arrows and urged skittish horses toward Roxane's front door.
