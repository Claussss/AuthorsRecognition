"But  you-" Wonder  startled words  from his  lips and  the star  on  Lythande's forehead blazed suddenly. Lalo's sensitized nerves felt the throb of power,  and abruptly he recognized his  danger. He squeezed shut  his eyes. Powers he  might have, but chance  memory told him  that only another  wizard could survive  open revelation of the secret of a wearer of the Blue Star. "I see," came the Adept's voice, soft, terrible.
