Briefly closing his eyes  while he shook his  head, Ahdio filled another  goblet with that  best of  wines and  topped off  the mug  for Wints,  the head  having subsided.  He  headed for  the  table against  the  wall, his  scintillant  coat jingling softly.  Just as  he passed  a regular  named or  rather called Weasel, Ahdio heard his loud conversation topper: "In a pig's ass!" "Someone call for  my special sausage?"  Ahdio called en  passant, and went  on,
