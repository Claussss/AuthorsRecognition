Wedemir couldn't understand Walegrin's muttering. He lowered his hand from his face. "Wha ... ? Ough! Gods-" He hastily re-covered his mouth and lurched toward the archway, where he could be heard heaving. An anger approaching mindless fury kept Walegrin breathing. He strode across the yard to the reeking midden. He kicked aside a bit of straw. His worst suspicions rolled into the sunlight. Filling his lungs with the foul air, Walegrin bellowed her name.
