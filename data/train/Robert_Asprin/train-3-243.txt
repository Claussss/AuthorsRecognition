"Just your permission will  be enough, Randal. Let's  not bring any powers  into this whose response we can't foresee, let alone control." The woman  was walking  alone in  the garden  while, within  the manse beyond, a civilized uptown party was under way. Her hair was blond and curly, bound up  in the fashion noblewomen  in the capital  had adopted this  season: held in  place with little golden pins hafted with likenesses of Rankan gods.
