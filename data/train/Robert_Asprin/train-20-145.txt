"Come backstairs," Mama would say when the money ran out. "Let's talk about it." Grinning all the while. He knew the  look. Like Elid's.  Like-He drank to  take a taste  from his mouth, made  the  drink small,  because  his life  was  measured in  such  sips of  his resources. He hated, gods, he hated. Hated women, hated the bloodsucking lot  of them, in whose eyes there was darkness that drank and drank forever.
