There was a sigh from behind them. The two men jerked around and saw that the ornamental pond was lifting onto one end. The water, which had dampened Samlor's boot a moment before, did not spill though it hung on edge in the air. There was a ladder leading down into the opening the pond had cov- ered. "Collector, you called him," said the caravan master grimly as he watched the reflection in the vertical sheet of water.
