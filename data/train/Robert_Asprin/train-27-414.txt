'G-get him  in,' Mor-am  said, and  coming to  the edge,  Mradhon took  the limp weight  all to  his side,  going into   water to  the knee  to reach  the  boat, staggering as he  flung the body  down. The boat  hardly rocked. He  gripped the side of it, stood there, uselessly,  to steady it. Haught crouched on  the muddy shore, head down, breathing in great gulps.
