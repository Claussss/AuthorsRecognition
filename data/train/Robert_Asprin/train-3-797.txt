"Ah, yes. Another question I wish you would answer, with your obvious expertise. What other jobs have you done?" Better ones  than you're  doing now,  Siveni thought  as she  lifted the cup and smelled, very deep in the bouquet of  the wine, an herb she recognized. She  had invented it; and this was one use for it that she had never approved. "Stibium," she  said,  answering  his  question   and  naming  the  drug,  both   at  once.
