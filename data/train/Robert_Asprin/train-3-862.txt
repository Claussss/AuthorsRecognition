The diagram  was a triangle  within a hexagon  within a circle, and other lesser figures were traced in the apertures. At each point  of the triangle they  stood, each with  a cup and  a small round  loaf of bread  in front of them-  the cup washed  in wine and  upended, the bread  baked in a fire struck by the same  flints that ground its  grain.
