'Stay sober enough to do it yourself, why don't you?' Mradhon said. The cup came  down on the  tabletop. Moria stood  up; the wine  spilled over the scarred surface, dripping off the edge. But Haught thrust himself into Mradhon's way in his own temper. Something seized up in him when he did; his gut knotted. Ex-slave that he was, his nerves did not forget. Old reflexes. 'Don't talk to her that way.'
