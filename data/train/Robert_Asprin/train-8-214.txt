   "I have come as a peace emissary," I sez, "in an effort to reach an equitable settlement of our differences."    The Deveel simply makes a rude noise at this, which I magnanimously ignore as I continue.    "I would suggest you meet our offer with equal enthusiasm for peace ... seein' as how continued hostilities between us will doubtless result in my colleagues and me trashin' this fine establishment of yours ..."
