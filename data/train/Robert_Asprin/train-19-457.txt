"Watch out!" He stopped short and the guard behind him bumped into him, swearing. "He's got a knife!" "Who? Where?" Swords flared from their sheaths and Lalo was thrust hard against the wall. "You fools, jumping at shadows! There's nobody here!" Lalo blinked. There was no one now, but he had seen something, or why should he suddenly think of a man who had been dead for years? "They're all jumping at shadows here, if you ask me," muttered one of the men as they started forward again.
