Her talent fed; and the stripping of a soul from a body was likely a  negligible thing for  Haught these  days. Stilcho  felt the  chill that  came when Haught's substance passed between him and Ischade.  "Don't-I tried to reason with him.  I tried to tell him he's dead. He's not listening.
