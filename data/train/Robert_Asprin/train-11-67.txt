   Of all the things that had flashed across my mind when Tananda warned me that the Jahks were weird, the one thing that had not occurred to me was that they were blue-and-yellow-paper freaks.    "Get off the float!"    This last was shouted at us from someone in the crowd.    "I beg your pardon," I shouted back.    "The float! Get off of it!"    "C'mon, handsome," Tananda hissed, hooking my elbow in hers.
