"Is she any good in bed?" Terci  said with a wink... a gesture Monkel  had never been sure how to interpret. "You'll have to bring her down here some night. We'd all like to meet her." "Fool," Haron scoffed, dealing the  speaker a good-natured cuff. "Can't  you see anything? She  was just  here. That  little guard  with the  big tits. It was as clear as seabirds circling over a school of feeding fish."
