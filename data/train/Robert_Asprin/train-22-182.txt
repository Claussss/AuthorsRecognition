Presently, something  scuttled with  a clatter  across the  solid rock  from the shore. She jumped but uttered no exclamation. The thing looked like a spider  in the  dark, an  enormous one,  larger than  the one  they'd killed.  It paid  no attention to them or perhaps it wasn't at all aware of them. It leaped into  the pool and disappeared. Smhee said, 'Let's get behind that boulder.' When they were in back of it, she said, 'What's going on?'
