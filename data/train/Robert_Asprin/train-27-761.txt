'Yes?' grunted a voice from above. 'Tarragon,' Samlor whispered. If the  password had been changed, the  next sound would be steel grating through his ribs. The door flopped open. A pair of men reached down and heaved Samlor inside  with scant ceremony. Both of them were masked, as was the third man in the room.  The third was the obvious leader, seated  behind the oil lamp and the  account books on a desk. The men who held Samlor were bravos; more perhaps than their  muscles
