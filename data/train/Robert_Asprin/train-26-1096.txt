On the far shore, in  the streets among  the shanties and  rotting houses, milled  dark crowds of the dead, but none of them used the bridge. Tyr galloped up the curved upstroke of the bridge and skidded and galumphed  and almost fell down the down-stroke  of it, yapping crazily. The  chariot followed.
