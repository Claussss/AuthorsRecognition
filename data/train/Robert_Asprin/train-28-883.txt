Lythande maintained  an impassive  face; but  whatever came,  Rabben must not be allowed to pursue that  line. 'You may couple  like an animal in  the streets of Sanctuary, Rabben, but I do not. Will you yield her up, or fight?' 'Perhaps I should  yield her to  you; this is  unheard of, that  Lythande should fight in the streets over a woman! You see, I know your habits well, Lythande!' Damnation of Vashanka! Now indeed I shall have to fight for the girl!
