The door swung open. Smhee bounded in,  the dead man's  spear in his  hands. Masha, following,  saw a large room the  air of which  was green and  reeking of incense.  The walls were covered with tapestries, and the  heavy dark furniture was ornately  carved with demons' heads. They paused to listen  and heard nothing except a faint  burbling 'Get the bodies in  quickly!' Smhee said, and  they dragged the corpses  inside.
