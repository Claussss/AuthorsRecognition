"Oh, I can guess where he'll go," Stilcho said. Stilcho laughed and grinned. He had a  patched eye and was missing one tooth  on the  side, but  in the  dark when   the scars  showed less  there was  a  ruined handsomeness about him. An elegance. He snatched the skin from Haught and hurled it, spattering the cobbles. "Run!" he yelled at Haught, and laughed aloud. "Stilcho, damn you!" "Try!" Stilcho  yelled.
