Unfortunately, the  wily old  crown-thief had  possessed the  foresight to dress some luckless  fool in  his raiments  while he  saw to  business elsewhere.  Her attack had been successful; she'd just aimed at the wrong man. Still, there was merit to the Riddler's idea, and a plan had come to her in  the night, like  a dream,  like the  voice of  Sa-vankala himself  guiding her.  She opened her eyes, glanced at the sun thoughtfully, and resumed her combing.
