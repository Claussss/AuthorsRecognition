The Sikkintair devoured Lalo's flesh and scoured his bones until the wind harped through his rib cage and drummed out a rhythm with the long bones of his thighs. His clever painter's hands,  stripped of the muscle  that had made their  magic, rattled like winter-bared twigs against the sky.
