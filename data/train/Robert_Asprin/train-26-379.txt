The following night,  Ahdio and Throde  worked alone. Once  again Ahdio made  an announcement, sadly: his  woman was gone.  That brought groans  and embarrassed, chastened faces and  expressions of sympathy.  It was the  first quiet night  at Sly's Place in anyone's memory.
