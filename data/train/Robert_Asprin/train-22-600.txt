'The city is being punished for its sinfulness.' Stulwig automatically spoke the standard explanation  given by  the priests  of Ils. 'When we  have learned  our lesson, and paid our penalty, the invader will be impelled to depart.' 'When I left the palace,' said Quag,  'there was no sign of the prince's  slaves packing his goods.' Shrugging. 'Such a departure for such a reason is  difficult for me to envision, and I suggest you build no hopes on it.'
