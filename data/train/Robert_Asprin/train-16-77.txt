   Phule paused for a moment, and, as he had expected, the now-traditional voice from the back of the room piped in.    "What's the good news?"    "The good news," he responded, working to keep his voice and face deadpan, "is that what we are being assigned to guard is the Fat Chance Casino on Lorelei, which I think you'll agree is a step up from standing duty in a swamp. To quote Headquarters directly, it's `easy duty in paradise.'"
