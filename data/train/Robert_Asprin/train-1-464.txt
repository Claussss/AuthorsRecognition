Star nodded, her face scrunched up on the verge of tears. The drifting glows dwindled noticeably. "Everything's going to be fine, dearest," the caravan master said, giv- ing the child an affectionate pat as he rose. It bothered him to have to scare his niece in order to get her to obey- while she remembered-but she scared him every time she did something innocently dangerous, like opening the ivory box. Better she be fright- ened than that she swing from his arm, trussed like a hog.
