She set it before Strick.  Taking it in both hands,  he rose and came around  to the seated goldsmith. Gonfred accepted  it and looked questioning; he  was still holding, barely. "Let the breath out," he was told. "Drink,  and try to do it in such a  way that it all goes down at a gulp."
