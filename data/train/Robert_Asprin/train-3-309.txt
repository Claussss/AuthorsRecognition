She came  up to  within an  arm's length  of the  Tros and  it backed a pace: It remembered the way she used to curry it until its hide showed bare of hair. He slipped off its back as her throaty voice, arch and full of childish  vanity, said, "You wished to see me, Tempus?  I can't imagine why. I did not  invite you to my wedding."
