"What were you doing here today?" The questioner's voice was calm, as if nothing had happened. "I was supposed to shoot at the Senator." "Were you supposed to hit him?" "No." The man was swaying slightly from the pain in his arm. "Who hired you?" The man shook his head. The Oriental's fist lashed out again. "The Senator!" The man screamed. A murmur ran through the crowd. The Senator stepped hurriedly to the front of the platform.
