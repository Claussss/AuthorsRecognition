Melilot being rich, he could afford locks on his doors; he had given her a heavy bronze key which she had concealed in her writing case. She fumbled it into  the lock, but before she could turn it  the door swung wide and she stepped  forward as though impelled by another person's will. This was the street  - or rather alley.  This was the door  with its overhanging porch. Outside everything was right.
