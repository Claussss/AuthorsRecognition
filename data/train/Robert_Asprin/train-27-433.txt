the fire in the fireplace sank lower. He turned towards Ischade, saw her let  go a breath. Her face - he had never seen it angry; and saw it now. But she walked to a table, quietly poured wine, a rich, rich red. She turned  up other cups, two, four, the sixth.  She filled only the one. 'Make  yourselves at home,' she said. 'Food, if  you wish it. Drink. It  will be safe for you.  I say that it is.'
