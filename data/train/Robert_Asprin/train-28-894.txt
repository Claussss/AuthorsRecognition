'Bercy, have you eaten today?' 'No, master.' Lythande summoned the  huge eunuch Jiro,  whose business it  was to conduct  the favoured customers  to the  chambers of  their chosen  women, and  throw out the drunks and  abusive customers  into the  street. He  came -  huge-bellied, naked except for a skimpy loincloth and a dozen  rings in his ear - he had once  had a lover who was an earring-seller and had used him to display her wares.
