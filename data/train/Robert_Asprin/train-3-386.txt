She took another deep breath and held it. What was that odor? She glanced at the doorway Mama  Becho had  gone through.  An old,  worn blanket  hung across it; a thin, tenuous smoke wafted around the edges. Krrf smoke. She wet her lips slyly and wondered how Gestus and Dismas were faring.
