"My earrings," she murmured, hurrying back to the little table beside the bed. A moment later: "Darling? Didn't I put my earrings right here? They're-they're gone!" "Muss've dropped 'em on th' floor," he said without concern, and yawned again. Watching her, smiling a little, remembering. Watching her go to her knees beside the bed in her search was fun, and he entertained a little fantasy about that.
