"My greater  problems, I  was told,  lie within  my past.  You'll have to let me struggle with them on my own." They rode  away from  the temple  in silence,  Walegrin keeping  his mare a good distance behind the gelding.  He bit his lip,  scratched himself and gave  every indication  of  reaching an  unpleasant  decision before  trotting  the mare  to Molin's side. "You should go to Illyra," he stated sullenly. "Heaven's forfend-why?"
