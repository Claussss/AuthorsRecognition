Radishes and  cucumbers are  your enemies,  Jakob. Mind  now, there  is always a trade, a  Price beyond  this sack,  and over  that I  have no  control. If it is unbearable, return." Jakob arose, made his request and heard it granted, and traced out the lines  of the other man's face with his fingers. He departed with his sack, now empty.
