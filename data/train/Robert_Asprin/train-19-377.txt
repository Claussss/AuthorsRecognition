"You're not Aglon!" "Aglon's dead! You little bitch, have you had so many men you can't even remember?" "Ricio ..." The name ended in a little catch of the breath. The girl pushed herself onto one elbow, brushing tumbled auburn curls away from her eyes. "Thank the gods! I thought Aglon's ghost was ... after me! I was so afraid." She reached out to him, but he shrugged away her hand. He was very young, and the welts where she had scratched him were already rising red on his chest.
