On the  wooden peg  over her  bed hung her cloak, jerkin and breeches, and  also her precious writing-case, just  as though she herself had replaced them on retiring. Seizing the  case, she  opened the  compartment where  she hid  such things, and triumphantly produced the gold she had accepted from the commander - but not the silver he had allotted to herself.
