Strat blinked  in the  sunlight and  rode past  the Blue  line checkpoint in the morning-the bay's shod hooves ringing  hollow on the cobbles beside  the bridge. The misnamed White Foal flowed murkily by, with its scarce traffic on dark-brown water; a skiff or two; a scruffy little barge. The scarred end-posts stood innocent in the sun.
