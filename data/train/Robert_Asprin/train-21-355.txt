For a mad moment, I thought she was going to protest my leaving. If she had, I'm not sure the strength of my resolve would have been sufficient to get me out the door. She started to say something, then stopped and drew a deep breath instead. "Good night, Skeeve," she said finally. "Come and see me again sometime ... soon." To say the least, there were many distracting thoughts dancing in my head as I made my way back to my room.
