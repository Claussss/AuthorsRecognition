But a touch  brushed his arm, a touch where no sound  had been; and Stilcho gasped and spun,  and all but took that fatal fall-except for the hand that closed on his arm and kept him from headlong flight. "Does the river draw you?" Haught asked. "The place ef one's death-has a hold on a soul. I'd avoid the water, Stilcho."
