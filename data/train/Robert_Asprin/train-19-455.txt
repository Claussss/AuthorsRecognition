"I want to bring my apprentice with me too-" Lalo turned, satchel in hand, and Darios stood up eagerly. "Got no orders-" the guard began. "Oh, what does it matter!" exclaimed one of the other men. "He said to get the limner quickly. Can't hurt to take two!" They moved quickly through the streets of the city. Even in the Maze folk got out of the way of a well-armed troop who seemed to know their business. Lalo had never gone across town so fast. But it was only as the guards conducted him up the broad staircase towards the royal suites instead of downward to the Hall of Justice lockup that he realized how great his fear had been.
