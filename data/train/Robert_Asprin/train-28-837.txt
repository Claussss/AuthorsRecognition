'I feel as  young as I  look,' she whispered  to the unnoticing  rooms. 'I could satisfy every man in this  house if I took the  notion to, or if anyone  of them had half the magnificence of my Zaibar.' Myrtis turned back to an empty room and went to sleep alone.
