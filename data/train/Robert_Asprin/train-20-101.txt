That function, too, had increased five-fold. Seizing the wall with one hand, Jubal  drew his feet under him then pushed  with his legs. Standing up had once seemed so simple; nothing he ever thought  about. Now sweat popped out on his brow and his vision blurred. He kept pushing; by now agony  was as  familiar as  the Lizerene's  face. Slowly,  his hands  scrabbling against the walls, he rose until his weight was on his feet.
