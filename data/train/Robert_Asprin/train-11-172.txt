Chapter Nine:    "War may be Hell. .. but it's good for business!"    -THE ASSOCIATION FOR MERCHANTS, MANUFACTURERS, AND MORTICIANS    TA-HOE was a beehive of activity when we arrived. Preparations for the upcoming war were in full swing, and everybody was doing something. Surprisingly enough, most of the preparations were of a non-military nature.    "What is all this?" I asked our native guide.    "I told you," he explained. "We're getting ready for a war with Veygus."
