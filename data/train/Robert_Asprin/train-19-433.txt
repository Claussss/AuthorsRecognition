"Let us see if this improves the atmosphere-" He handed the pictures to Myrtis. "But that's not what you saw!" objected Darios. "No, but when Madam Myrtis gives these sketches to her girls, perhaps this is what they will see-and believe-and believing, make it so," answered Lalo, remembering what Molin Torchholder had asked him to do. "I only wish I knew what it was that suddenly gave their fears such power!"
