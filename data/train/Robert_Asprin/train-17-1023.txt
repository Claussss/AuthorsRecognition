It was all. It was nearly the  end of Zaibar the Hell Hound's entire  career; he almost struck  his commander-in-chief.  But he  refrained, though  he could  not utter even a civil goodbye. He went to his billet and he went into the town, and he worked wrath out of himself, as best he could. The dregs he washed away  with drink, and after that he went  to visit Myrtis, the whoremistress of  Aphrodisia
