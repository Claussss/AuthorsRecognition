"Traditionally, Japan has produced the finest fighting men in the world. The Samurai, the Ninjas, are all legendary for their prowess in battle." There was no reaction from the force. Mentally he braced himself. Here we go! "Also, traditionally, they have had the worst armies!" The force stiffened without moving. Their faces remained immobile. "The armies were unsuccessful because they fought as individuals, not as a team. As martial artists, you train the muscles of your body, the limbs of your body, to work together, to support each other. It would be unthinkable to attempt to fight if your arms and legs were allowed to move in uncontrolled random motions."
