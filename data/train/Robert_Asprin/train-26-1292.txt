Stepson, four burly men  from the 3rd Commando,  and a couple of  scrawny youths from Zip's collection of toughs were trying to build a bulwark. It was a  motley construction, cobbled together with wood from the market pens nearby, logs  from half-drowned woods upriver, and anything else they could carry away.
