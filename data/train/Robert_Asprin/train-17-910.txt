Even incomplete, the walls which barred  the folk from  spit and  lighthouse grinned  with a  death's-head smirk  towards the town, enclosing granaries and stables and newly whiled barracks and a spring for fresh water: if War came hither, Tempus proposed to make Him welcome for a  long and arduous siege.
