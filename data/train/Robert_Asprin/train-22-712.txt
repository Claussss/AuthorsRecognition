When he had been a student of philosophy and Cime had been a maiden, he had learned  the axiom that  Mind is unlimited  and self-controlled, but  all other things are  connected; that nothing  is completely separated  off from any other thing, nor are things divided one from the other, except Mind. The sorcerers put it  another way: they called  the consciousness of all  things into service, according to the laws of magic.
