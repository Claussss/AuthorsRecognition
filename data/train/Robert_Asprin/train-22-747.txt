So many years, and you yet seek self-delusion? 'Definitely. As do You,  if You think to  gather worshippers in this  fashion! 0 Berserker God, You cannot roast their mages before them: they are all  dependent on  sorcery. You  cannot terrify  them thus,  and expect  them to  come to  You. Weapons will not woo them; they are not men of the armies. They are thieves, and pirates, and prostitutes! You have gone too far, and not far enough!'
