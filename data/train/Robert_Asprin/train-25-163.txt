All movement froze at the sound of Ivan's voice. "What'd you say, Mister?" "Ivan, for God's sake..." "I said, 'No!"' He began to move toward the mugger. "All my life I have been ordered around!" "Stand back!" "Ivan! Don't!" Fred's mind was racing. He had to do something quickly. "You have no right to..." The gun exploded in a flash of light, the report deafening in the night. Ivan lurched backward. Shit! Fred threw his wallet at the mugger's face. The boy instinctively flinched away, raising his hands, and Fred was on him.
