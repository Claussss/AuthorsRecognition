Balustrus was polishing the freshly ground blade when Walegrin entered the  hot, dusty shed.  The bronze  man's tunic  was filthy  with sweat  and dust  from the grinding wheel. His mottled skin glistened more brightly than the metal. "She's a  beauty, isn't  she?" he  said, giving  the blade  to Walegrin while he sought his crutches. Fine, wavy lines of black alternated with thicker bands of a more silvery metal.
