"I still think I should go with you," Targ said aloud, though he did not look at Cade. "No." The only light in the room came from the single lantern lying between them. Cade stared at the large shadow Targ cast on the wall behind him, like a giant leaning over to listen to their conversation. "You must get the other three. All must die tonight." "They're expecting me to be there. The deal is with me. If they see you, they'll know what's up."
