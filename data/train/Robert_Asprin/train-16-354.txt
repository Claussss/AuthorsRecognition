   "Please ... have a seat," Maxine said to the slender, youthful Oriental as Stilman ushered him into the suite. "So nice of you to accept my invitation."    The man's face was impassive, but there was anger in his voice and movements.    "I wasn't aware I had a choice," he said, sinking into the offered chair.    Maxine raised her eyebrows in mock surprise.    "Mr. Stilman," she said, "didn't you make it clear that I was extending an invitation to our guest?"
