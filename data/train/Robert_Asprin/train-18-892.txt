"You've a bit to leam," Tasfalen said. "Most of all, a sense of perspective. But I'm willing to take an apprentice." From Haught, a long silence: then, quietly: "Is it mistress or master?" Tasfalen's right eyebrow jerked in wrath. Then a grin spread over his face. "Oh, I  like you  well, upstart.  I do  like you."  The pottery  globe vanished  from his/her hands. "First lesson: don't leave a thing like that in reach."
