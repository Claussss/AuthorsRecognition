His eye fell on a magician, and he wondered about the star tattooed on the man's forehead. Then, too,  he noted that  the mage was  engaged in a  heated argument with a brightly  garbed young bravo  who displayed numerous  knives, their hilts protruding from arm-sheath, sash, and boot top in ominous warning. 'That's Lythande,' Mungo  informed him, noting  his interest. 'He's  a fraud. If you're looking for a magician, there are better to be had ... cheaper.'
