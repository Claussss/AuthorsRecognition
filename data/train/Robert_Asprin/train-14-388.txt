   "Mind if I join you?"    Super Gnat looked up from her breakfast to find the company commander standing over her table. With a shrug, she waved him into the facing chair.    The smallest member of the company was not unattractive, though no one would call her beautiful. An obvious band of freckles across her cheekbones and nose combined with her heart-shaped face and short brown hair to give an impression of a pixie-a robust young farm pixie, not the cuter, more sophisticated Tinker Bell variety.
