Shadows poured down the riverside, shadows marched from the ravaged barracks  in Downwind, and  ignored the  barriers the  Beggar-king and  his kind had erected. Ignored the PFLS  and its flung  stones and its  naphtha-bottles and the  fires: that demi-legion had  seen the fires  of hell and  were not impressed.
