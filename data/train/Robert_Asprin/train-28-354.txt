'Your story's worth a couple more drinks,' One-Thumb invited. 'Another evening,' Hanse demurred. 'Right now I need a whore. Don't forget those ten royals, singer.' He left, stiff-legged. 'Well,' said the innkeeper after a silence, 'what do you make of this latest?' Cappen suppressed a shiver. His palms  were cold. 'I don't know, save  that what we confront is not of our kind.' 'You told me once you've got a charm against magic.'
