"Tell her to come out here," Sarah answered. "Out here? But it's dark. Cade says-" "Never mind what Cade says," she interrupted. "Tell the Lady Marissa to come out here." He shrugged and did as he was told. Marissa came out moments later, holding a lantern and a goblet of wine. She handed Sarah the drink. "I thought you could use it," Marissa said in her soft voice. Sarah smiled. Marissa was so thoughtful. At first Sarah had been put off by the other's title and light, Rankan good looks. Now she wondered if she could have made it this far without her friend.
