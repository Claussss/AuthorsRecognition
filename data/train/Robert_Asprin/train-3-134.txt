Lalo jerked around, flinched as he saw what had answered them and then  stumbled over his own feet, trying to get  between it and Gilla. But she had  always been broadly built and big-boned, and she gripped his arm and stayed beside him. The Thing  that had  spoken looked  on his  confusion and  laughed. Lalo stared, realizing in horror  that it was  female, wrapped in  scorched robes from  which pale smoke  rose in  ghostly trails,  with singed  hair that  lifted as the wind
