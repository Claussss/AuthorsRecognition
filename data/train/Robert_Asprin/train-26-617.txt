Unexpectedly, she heard  voices behind her.  She ducked into  the nearest cranny and crouched behind a barrel. Slops, to judge by the odor. She held her nose and waited. A ragtag squad of men passed without noticing her. Most appeared to wear swords, though  a few  carried only  clubs. There  was nothing disciplined about them.
