   "You misunderstand me." Massha waved, fanning the air with a massive hand. "What I meant was, take off your disguises."    "Our disguises?" I blurted, swallowing hard.    In reply, she held aloft her left hand, the index finger extended for us to see. The third-no, it was the fourth-ring was blinking a brilliant purple.    "This little toy says you're not only magicians, you're disguised," she grinned. "Now, I'm as sociable as the next person but I like to see who I'm doing business with. In fact, I insist!"
