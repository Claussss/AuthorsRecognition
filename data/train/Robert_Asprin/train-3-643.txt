Two red slits appeared in that dark, and the same glow  limned the flare of  humanish nostrils and  the seam of  a humanish mouth,  as if there were fire inside an utterly dark face. It smiled at him. My worshiper, it said. And whispered other things, about power, and how it had been shut in hell  until it gained its freedom. The pain ebbed down. But not the cold.
