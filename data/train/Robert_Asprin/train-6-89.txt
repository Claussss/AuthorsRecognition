   There was a sharp nudge in my ribs.    "Did you see that?" Aahz whispered urgently.    "See what? "I asked.    "The king's advisors. A general and a chancellor unless I miss my guess. Did you see the gold medallion on the general?"    "I saw his axe!" I whispered back. The light in the courtyard suddenly dimmed. Looking up, I saw a mass of clouds forming overhead, blotting out the sun.    "Weather control," Aahz murmured half to himself. "Not bad."
