"Upstairs-Cythen's  room,"  Zump said  as  soon as  he'd  crossed the  barracks' threshold. Every one of the half-dozen men in the room was watching him. But  at least they  weren't thinking  about plague  or imperial  barges. Walegrin forced himself to walk slowly as he climbed the half-flight of stairs to where  Cythen, the only woman billeted with the regular garrison, slept. Thrush and Cythen stood guard outside the open door.
