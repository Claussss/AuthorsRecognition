'You're astute, you little  weasel!' he said in  a tone of grudging  admiration. 'Are you quick-witted enough to know precisely why neither he nor I - nor you!  can read that writing?' Jarveena swallowed hard. 'There's a spell on it,' she offered after a pause. 'Yes! Yes, there is! Better than any code or cipher. Except for the eyes of  the intended recipient, it will never read the same way twice.'
