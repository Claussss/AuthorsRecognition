"Ischade, dammit! You want that damn girl, you get out here, fast!'" Stilcho rode in behind and slid down, ran up to the gate and got it open -him, it did not shock. For him, Ischade's door opened, and Ischade came out and stood on her porch, waiting. "Come on," Stilcho said nervously, and grabbed Strat by the arm. He needed no pull. He all but beat Stilcho to the porch steps; and held Stilcho's distance from her, who stood cloaked and dark and ominously frowning.
