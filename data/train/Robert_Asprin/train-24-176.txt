"Zur...Zur...Zur...Zur..." I beamed desperately into the cavern behind me. It took a distressingly long time to arouse him. "Zur here!" came the weak response finally. "Trouble on the forest line...Possibly our scouts... Going to check it... Rouse the others and stand by... As I beamed the last part of my orders, I was on my feet and running. As I plunged down the slope of the first hill, another scream split the air. I redoubled my speed, laboring uphill, then plunging into the next valley.
