'The  damned workmen  left the  hauling ropes  strewn about,'  a mud-splattered priest exclaimed as he scrambled out of the knee-deep mud-hole. 'And the girl?' Molin continued. 'Thrown over there, from the look of it.' Lifting his robes in one hand, Molin Torchholder led the acolytes and priests to the indicated mud-pit.
