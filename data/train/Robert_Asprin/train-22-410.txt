Samlor  lost his  footing in  the remains  of Regli's  coachman. An  earth-shock pitched him  forwards against  the door  panel. It  was unlocked.  The Cirdonian lunged out into the  street as the shattered  dome followed its pinnacle  into a cavern that gaped with a sound like the lowest note of an organ played by gods.
