"This is a glue shop. If you leave with one of my Jars anyone who sees you will see why you have come and notice nothing else." Her veiled face whitened. "I hadn't thought of that." "By the way, this variety is made especially for porcelain and ceramics. It does wonders on broken dishes." After she had hurried away, the clay jar held where it could be seen, Sambar came through the curtained doorway. "Master, why do you always insist that the pickup be dead? Wouldn't they pay more if you did it for them?"
