He bent down and peered at the  stone floor just beyond the doorway. He  turned. 'Here. Your young  eyes are better  than my old  ones. Can you  see a thread  or anything like it raised above the floor just beyond the door?' She said, 'No, I can't.' 'Nevertheless.' He threw his  torch through the  doorway. At his  order, she got,  down with her cheek against the stone and looked against the flame.
