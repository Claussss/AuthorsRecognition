Feltheryn's stentorian tones rang out with remarkable resonance for a monarch as enfeebled as he was supposed to be. "It's little I ask, and am well content with less. Three masters-pain, time, and the royalty in the blood- Have taught me patience-" The stage shuddered as something large and heavy hit the floor. Feltheryn broke off and turned. "Patience!" he roared. "Gods give me patience-I have to work with fools!"
