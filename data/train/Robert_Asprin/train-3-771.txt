And there was the wall. As an exercise in logistics alone it was trouble enough. First the plans, argued over for weeks, changed, changed  again, changed back; then ordering  the stone, and having it quarried; then hiring  people enough to move such weights,  others to  work  on  the  roughed-out   stones,  trimming  them  to  size.   Overseers, stonemasons, mortarers, caterers, spies to make sure everything was  working....
