   "Why, I'd be delighted," said the general, rising to extend a hand. "There's nothing closer to my heart than the welfare of the retired officers. I'll tell my adjutant to make it a firm date."    "Oh, I'm so pleased," said Mrs. Biffwycke-Snerty.    "Now we have something for all our clients.
