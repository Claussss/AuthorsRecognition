"But the smoke flare..." Realization struck him like a slap in the face. It was the mercenaries. They had given him a fake radio call and a fake smoke flare. He suddenly was aware of his men moving. They were abandoning their equipment and walking back toward the base. Their eyes were glazed and some of them were crying. He knew he should call to them, order them, console them. He knew that he should, but he couldn't.
