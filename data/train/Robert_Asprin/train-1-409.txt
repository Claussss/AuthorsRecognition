"Is it going to rain?" Star asked sleepily from the cradle of Samlor's arm. The caravan master glanced at the sky. There were stars, but a scud of high clouds blocked and cleared streaks across them at rapid intervals. There was an edge in the air which might well be harbinger of a storm poised to sweep from the hills to the west of town and wash the air at least briefly clean. "Perhaps, dearest," the Cirdonian said. "But we'll be all right."
