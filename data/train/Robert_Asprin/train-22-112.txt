Ten minutes or so later, the fever began to go down. An hour later, according to the sandglass, she was given another spoonful. By dawn, she seemed to be rid  of it, and she was sleeping peacefully. Meantime, Masha and Smhee talked in low tones. Wallu had gone to bed, but not to sleep,  shortly  before  sunrise.  Eevroen had  not  appeared.  Probably  he was sleeping off his liquor in an empty crate on the wharf or in some doorway. Masha
