Perhaps I am dead, he thought then.  Poor Cilia will grieve for me, hut  she has her hoard, and the  older children are earning  money of their own.  She will do better without me  than I would  if she had  left me alone  ... The thought  was bitter, and he found himself weeping as he stumbled along.
