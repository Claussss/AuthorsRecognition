Cappen  followed   him  artlessly,   down  the   length  of  the Serpentine, and into territory  of the city at  large - where the  law came, and where a  wanted thief was less   than safe. The   houses and shops   here were more  sturdy, and finally magnificent, and those latter existed behind walls, and most with   bars on  the windows.  Walkers  grew  scarce for  a  time,  and Cappen  hung  further
