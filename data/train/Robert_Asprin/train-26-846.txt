Haught's fingers bit painfully into his arm. "You talk to them, I say." No  more remarks  about his  mother. Stilcho  turned his  head with   deliberate slowness and looked at the gathering menace. No one alive was on the street  but Haught. And himself. And  many of these were  Roxane's. Many were not-just  lost souls left  unattended and  lately, in  the lamentable  condition of  Sanctuary, without compulsion to go back to rest.
