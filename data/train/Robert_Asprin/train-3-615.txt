"Something's  wrong," he  said. In  that inner  eye, soiled,  filmy shapes  went streaming  like  smoke  through  the  gates,  the  gates-the  fires,  the   lost reaches.... "A lot of  people just died." He  swallowed hard, tried to  calm his shaking, tried to  get back the  sight of Moria  across the table,  and not that black vision where Something waited, where by the riverside-in the woods- "Stilcho!"
