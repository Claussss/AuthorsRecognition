Crit asked, as Tempus  was leaving the dark  and comforting common room  for the last time, whether any children's bodies  had been found - three girls  and boys still were missing; one young corpse had turned up cold in Shambles Cross. 'No,' Tempus said, and thought no more about it. 'Life to you. Critias.' 'And to you, Riddler. And everlasting glory.' Outside, Jihan was waiting on one Tros horse, the other's reins in her hand.
