   "Why, of course not," said Lola, looking at the card. "Master at arms-that sounds exciting. Was the ship attacked?"    "I could do without that kind of excitement, ma'am," said the officer with a low chuckle. "Master at arms is just an old-fashioned title for a ship's security officer. On a ship like this, that's a part-time job. I earn my keep by being purser, and second engineer in a real pinch."
