But worst of all, to  Mriga's thought, were those many,  many dead who had  never even lived enough  to burn a little, who had given up both sin and passion as useless. They walked dully past the flaming damned, and past goddesses, and neither hellfire nor the cold  clean light of Siveni's spear found anything in their eyes at all. She soon enough found worse.
