Ischade  looked  down  silently  at what  the  light,  the  silver morning,  the irresistible joy beating in the air, had made of her. Long she looked down,  and lifting her hands, gazed into them as if into a mirror. Finally she lowered them and said, calm as ever, "I prefer my way." Mriga looked a long moment at her. "Yes. Anyway, thank you," she said.
