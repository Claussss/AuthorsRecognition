'Hakiem?' Hort bristled. 'He's a beggar.' 'He lives here. He's  a good storyteller; his  wealth's his pride. Whatever  you do, wherever you go -  take your pride. Be good  with yourself and you'll be  at home with the best of'em. Take my gift, son; it's only advice, but you'll be the poorer without  it.' He  tossed the  gold coin  to the  sand at  Hort's feet and stalked off. Hort retrieved the coin and stared at the Old Man's back as he marched away.
