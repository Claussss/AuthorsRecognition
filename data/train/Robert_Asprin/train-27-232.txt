Because of his skin and his accent Tempus almost  took him prisoner, thinking to give him to Straton, for whom all manner of men  bared their souls,  but he  marshalled his  anger and  sent the  young man away with a pocket full of  soldats and instructions  to convey Jubal's  message to Critias.
