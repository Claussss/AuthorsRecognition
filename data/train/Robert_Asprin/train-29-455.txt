The silver hair was out of sight, but not gone. Cholly could feel it. Zandulas was shouting, "Did you see that?" By the last lap Borak was ahead of Atticus by half a length. Crispen had gotten tangled in Magyar's wreck and lost too much time to make it up. "Collect my winnings," he told Zandulas. "Why? Where're you going?" "Must be the Red Gold. I'm not feeling so good," Cholly lied. He could hear the crowd shouting Borak's name as he hurried down the steps. A knife darted at him but was deflected by the iron and leather vest he wore. He was lucky, and knew it.
