   I was loving it.    I had never had a large crowd make a fuss over me before. It was nice.    "Heads up, kid," Aahz murmured, nudging me in the ribs. "Here comes the reception committee."    Sure enough, there was another procession emerging from the main gates of the palace. It was smaller than ours, but made up for what it lacked in numbers with the prestige of its members.    The king was front and center, flanked closely by Grimble and Badaxe. The chancellor was beaming with undisguised delight. The general, on the other hand, looked positively grim.
