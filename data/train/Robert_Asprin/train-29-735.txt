Lalo began to laugh, because of the swift toss of Rhian's bright head, and the look on Wedemir's face-and for sheer simple joy because he had been healed. "I still love you, lambkin-" Lalo put his arm around Latilla, who sniffed and turned her face away. "You love Mama better ..." she mumbled. Lalo sighed, aware that there was a part of his daughter that wished he were still blind. But it would do no good to tell her so.
