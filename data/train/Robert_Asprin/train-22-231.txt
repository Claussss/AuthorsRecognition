He strode to the pool in which  were set the wheels. The wheels went  around and around impelled by the downpouring water. Masha counted aloud. Twelve. 'A magical number,' Smhee said. They were set in rows  of threes. At one end  of the axle of each  were attached some gears which in  turn were fixed to  a shaft that ran  into a box under  the wheel. Smhee reached out to the nearest wheel from the pool edge and stopped it.
