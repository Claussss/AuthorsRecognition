"Your Majesty," I said, entering the room. I looked about quickly as she was shutting the door, and, when she turned, gestured toward a chair. "Please, if you could take a seat?" She raised a questioning eyebrow at me, but took the indicated seat without argument. "What's this all about, Skeeve?" she said. "You look so solemn." There was no way of stalling further, so I plunged in. "I wanted to let you know that I've made my decision regarding marrying you," I said.
