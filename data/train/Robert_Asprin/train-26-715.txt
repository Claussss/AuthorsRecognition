That thin thread grew  strongest when he looked  closest into her eyes,  when he shared her  bed and  each morning  died for  it and  came back  from hell again, because the thread was always there. It  was all he had of pleasure. It  was all he had of life. He knew what hell was, being too frequently a visitor; and  when he went down again the  souls of his dead would  cling to him and clamor  at him
