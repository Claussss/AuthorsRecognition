   "Ah! A Tah-bul for one!"    Actually, I had been preparing to beat a retreat, but this guy wasn't about to leave me that choice.  Chairs and tables seemed to part in his path as he swept off through the diners like a sailing ship through algae, drawing me along in his wake. Heads turned and murmurs started as we passed. If they were trying to figure out where they had seen me before, it could take a lot of talking.
