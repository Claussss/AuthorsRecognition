Now ... now while the noise of the creaking stairs gave him sound to rely on  in tracking them - he had  his chance, and took it,  a path he had marked  out that afternoon. He carefully  set his hands  on a barrel,  levered himself up  into a tuck and  sought the  next level  of debris,  noiselessly, one  after the other, holding his breath as one foothold rocked and the next proved stable.
