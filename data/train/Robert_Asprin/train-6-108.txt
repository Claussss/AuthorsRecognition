   "I am the magician known as Skeeve," I began grandly. "I have come in response to a gracious invitation from His Most Noble Majesty, Rodrick the Fifth."    I paused and inclined my head slightly to the king who smiled and nodded in return.    "I have come to determine for myself if I should consider accepting a position at the court of Possiltum."    The phrasing of that last part had been chosen very carefully by Aahz. It was designed to display my confidence by implying the choice was mine rather than theirs.
