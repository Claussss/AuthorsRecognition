  We barely made it to our appointed places ahead of the Queen's procession. The throne of Possiltum had been temporarily moved to a position just inside the doors to the palace, and only by sprinting through the corridors with undignified abandon were Badaxe and I able to reach our respective positions before the portals were thrown open.   "Remind me to have a word with you about the efficiency of your army's early warning system," I said to the General as I sank into my seat.
