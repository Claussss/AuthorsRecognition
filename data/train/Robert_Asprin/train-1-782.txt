"Ah, the earring, and thus a bit of blood!" "Wh-what?" The wizened physician's voice quavered. Strick released those frighteningly bony shoulders and sat beside the man who looked far too old for the age he claimed. The spellmaker would have bet that before this malignant spell the physician had looked fifteen years younger. "How did you lose your earring?" "Late one night about two months ago I was set upon by footpads and -by the gods! This began about then! I have lost very much weight in these past two months, Strick, and of course strength as well."
