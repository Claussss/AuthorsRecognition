"Ah, locked up tight," he said, and Linza with her arm around him squeezed his waist and made a sound somewhere between a giggle and a chuckle and a hiccup, and Tarkle congratulated himself on his good fortune and again counted himself really lucky to have her here with him. He got the door unlocked and, with a sweeping gesture, pushed it open and swung his arm wide in welcome, Linza started into the moonlit room, and shivered against him.
