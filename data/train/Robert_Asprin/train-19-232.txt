Feltheryn smiled. "I see you are angling for more pomp and nocking," he said; and Glisselrand grinned. "It couldn't hurt, my dear," she said. Rehearsals continued, the costumes and sets were finished, and in no time at all it was opening night. Lowan Vigeles and Rosanda elected the royal box on the second night, Molin Torchholder accompanied the prince and the Beysa for the first night, and everything went as smoothly as melon with custard. In fact, by the end of the first act the impossible seemed to be taking place.
