There he shot off the horse in a dismount which was a fall  disguised as a vault, slapped the beast  smartly away, telling it hissingly  to go home, and  slipped inside with such  relief as his favourite knife must feel when he sheathed it. 'One-Thumb,' Hanse called out, making for the bar, 'what is going on out there?'
