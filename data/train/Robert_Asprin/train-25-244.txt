Clancy was smiling at him. "Steve, you're a real son-of-a-bitch." Tidwell shrugged modestly, and they settled back to wait. They didn't have to wait long. The next team came into sight, jogging along the trail in a loose group. The leader, a girl in her late teens that Clancy was spending most of his off-hours with, spotted the two sitting on the edge of the cliff. She smiled and waved at them. They smiled and waved back at her. They were still smiling when the ambush opened up.
