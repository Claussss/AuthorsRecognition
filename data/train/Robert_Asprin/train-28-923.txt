At last she lay  all but unconscious, satiated;  and Lythande watched in  agony. When she opened her eyes again, Lythande was looking down at her, sorrowfully. Bercy stretched up  languid arms. 'Truly,  my beloved, you  have loved me  as no woman has ever been loved before.'
