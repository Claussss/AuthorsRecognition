The Beysib had come. Hanse went  down to  the dock  that day  and looked  at the  ships as  they came closer, and  closer, and  he pondered  and considered.  Then he  went back up to Eaglenest where he had consorted with gods and fought with a god. They were  not there. Only the ruins were there. And the well. Hanse sighed.
