But, unfortunately, emperors have a way of dying and empires do change hands. The new Rankan Emperor, Theron, though a brilliant military strategist, had little appreciation for the finer points of culture and etiquette. His taste ran more towards large quantities of ale than the refined delicacies of vintage wine. And Kranderon, though farsighted in business ventures and moneymaking opportunities, was shortsighted in the political and military arena.
