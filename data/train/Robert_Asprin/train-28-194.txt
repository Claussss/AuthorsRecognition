'I'd prefer not to.' A difficult one, Illyra thought. The woman's hand emerged from the shawl to drop three old Ilsig gold coins on to the velvet. The hand was white, smooth, and youthful. The Ilsig coins were  rare now that  the Rankan  empire controlled  Sanctuary. The  woman and her questions were a welcome relief from Illyra's own thoughts. 'Well, then, what is your name?' 'I'd prefer not to say.'
