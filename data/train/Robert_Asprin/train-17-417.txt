It tempted. He was beautiful, this  moment, this one moment, and the  nights and the years were long. And then the other chance occurred to her and she shivered, who had not shivered in years. 'No. No. Maybe you're set  to die, but I'm not. No. Oppose  two curses the like of ours - half the city could go in that shock, not to mention you  and me. The chance of that, the merest chance - No. I'm not done living...'
