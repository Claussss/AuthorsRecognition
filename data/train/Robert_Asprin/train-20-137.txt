Night after  night he  spent here,  and many  of his  days. He lodged across the alley, in  space Mama  Becho rented  for more  than it  was worth-excepting  her assurance that it would stay inviolate, that the meager furnishings would always be there, that there would never be some sly opening of the door when he was out or while he was  asleep. Tygoth made his  rounds of Mama's properties  all night
