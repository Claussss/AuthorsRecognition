Not long ago, this would have made me nervous. My recent excursion to Limbo, however, had gotten me a bit more used to notoriety. The next time they glanced over, I looked directly back at them, then gave a brief, polite nod of acknowledgment with my head. This, of course, caused another hurried huddle and burst of giggles. "What are you smiling at?" Aahz said as he set my wine in front of me and slid onto the bench across the table, cradling his own outsized drink.
