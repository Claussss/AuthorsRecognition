A dog barked. Strat looked over the rail and down, and saw a black cur in the shadow down there, next the bay horse- Someone sneezed, and instantly where the dog had been was Randal, in a night-robe and bare feet, wiping his nose. "Damned allergies," Randal said. "I thought-" "Thought what?" Strat came hastily down the steps, no little annoyed for the public scene. "Thought-I smelled an associate of yours about you."
