She waited. When she could bear the waiting no longer, she demanded. "Tell me'" "I shall not. This only will I say: His punishment is just." "I don't understand!" "Better you should not. Better that no one should. Had I known what a burden of knowledge I was taking on-no! Condemning myself to!-I'd never have set out to offer help." Guessing at the meaning behind the words, Jarveena bit her lip. Tears sprang unbidden to her eyes, and yet were welcome, for they disguised the ghastly form that Enas Yorl was melting into.
