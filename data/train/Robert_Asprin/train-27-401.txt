He looked past her, to the wretch on the floor, to what this ragged, awful crowd had left of a man. He had seen corpses, of various kinds. This one looked  worse than most and might  still be alive, which  daunted him more than  death. But it was a question of downhill. He  walked in, among the beggar-horde, among  ragged men and women. Gods! there was a child, feral, with a rat's sharp, frozen  grin.
