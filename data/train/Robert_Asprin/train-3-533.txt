Offended, he was less storm and more man, a decent man whose self-respect was in pawn: whose thought now was indeed for  the lives and the souls he had  proposed himself to buy. He was two men; or man and something much less reasonable. "I'll see you home," he said, like some spurned swain to the miller's  daughter.
