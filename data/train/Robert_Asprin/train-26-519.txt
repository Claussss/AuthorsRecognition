The first rider jerked sharply on the reins, threw a leg over the mount's  head, and jumped to the ground. The second rider slid backward over the damp, lathered rump, stumbled, then sagged to the pavement. A hood was flung back; a pommel smashed against the unyielding barriers. A voice called out full  of desperation  and anger.  "Father! Let  us in!  Dayrne-anyone "Chenaya!" The  second rider  rose to  a timid  crouch and  drew a small dagger.
