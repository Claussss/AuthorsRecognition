She did not  charge a great  deal of money  for her Seeings,  this S'danzo named Moonflower. She Saw danger  and felicities to come,  pain and pleasure to  come, and she Saw linkages. She had Seen enough once to let Hanse know that he was involved in a very  large plot emanating from Ranke itself;  a treacherous governor's concubine had  quite charmed Hanse and, with a treacherous Hell-Hound, aided him into the palace  one
