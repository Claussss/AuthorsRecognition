He paused to wet his lips. "What is more, even if I could, with this many people present, simple time parameters dictate that not everyone who wants to speak would be able to." Mentally he crossed his fingers. "It is therefore my decision," he announced, "that for the duration of this discussion, I will not recognize speakers from the floor.
