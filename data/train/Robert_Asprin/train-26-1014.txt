"What about Tyr?" said Siveni. "She'll ride this  soul," said Ischade.  Her hand had  fallen on the  ram's head again. It looked up  at her, and up,  and helplessly, up; and  Ischade swung the sickle. In the unlight of the dark lantern, the ram's eyes blazed horribly, then emptied, and the black blood gushed out on the altar's white stone. "Now,"  said Ischade, a slow warm smile in her voice, and reached out to the ewe.
