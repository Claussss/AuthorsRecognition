'There's a spider's web just ahead. Between those two bushes. Be careful of  it. But look out  for other dangers,  since one will  be obvious enough  to distract your attention from others. And don't forget that the thorns of these bushes are probably poisonous.' In the dim moonlight she saw the web. It was huge, as wide as the stretch of her arms. She thought, if it's so big, what about its spinner?
