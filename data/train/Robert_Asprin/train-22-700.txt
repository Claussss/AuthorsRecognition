'I have to report to Kitt - to K-adakithis.' 'Then report that I am handling it.' Behind the magician, he could see the ranks whispering. Thirty men, the archmage had brought. Too many. 'You and I have more in common than in dispute, Tempus. Let us join forces.' 'I would sooner bed an Ilsig matron.' 'Well, I am going in there.' The archmage shook his head and the cowl fell back. He was pretty, ageless, a blond. 'With or without you.'
