And, hearing the pad of feet and the rustle of clothing,  and seeing the  torches, hastily locked  up and joined  the throngs that were streaming in one direction: towards the temple of Ils. From his open shutter Stulwig could see the temple with its gilded dome. All the portions  that he  could see  were lit  up, and  the light  was visible  through numerous glass reflectors.
