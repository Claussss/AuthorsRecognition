That  saved it.  That Her  was Roxane.  The fiend  knew instinctively  what  she wanted. It proposed treachery (which was its fiendish part) and hoped for  mercy (which was its human vulnerability). FIND, she told it. And the  orange-haired fiend leapt up and gibbered  with that hope for  mercy. It  went loping  and shambling  off shattering  boxes and  wine bottles and scaring hell out of a sleeping drunk behind the Unicorn.
