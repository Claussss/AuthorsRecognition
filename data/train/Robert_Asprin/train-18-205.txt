For it seemed, as the house  collapsed, that the sky itself caught  fire. Demons of colored light slunk through that wider blaze and slipped away. Wings of lightning beat against the firmament where a rising sun was dwarfed  to dullness by their light. And down from purple lightning and clouds that came together, combusting to form a great cat-thing with hell-red eyes who swiped at it as it came, flew an eagle.
