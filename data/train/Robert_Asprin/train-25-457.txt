"Is this the way it's going to be?" Stills shrugged and smiled and left without answering. The room lapsed into silence as Mausier sat staring into space. Suddenly, he felt a hand on his shoulder. It was Weaver. "Cheer up, Mr. Mausier." His voice was sympathetic. "It could be worse. You're a valuable man. Just play ball and they'll take care of you. You know, 'go along, get along.'" Mausier didn't respond. He just kept thinking about the gun in his bedroom closet.
