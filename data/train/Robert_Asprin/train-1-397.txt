The words faded as the glow in Star's hand shrank to a point and disappeared. "I was ready." said the caravan master slowly, "to find a guide in there." He did not gesture toward the tavern. He was speaking to himself, not to the pair of living humans with him in the alleyway. They stared at Samlor, his niece, and the stranger, as they would have stared at a pet lion who suddenly began to act oddly.
