I need you. She was saying. Come to me... . "I hear her too," Moria whispered. "Oh, gods, no-don't go!" Haught, the ex-slave, the dancer, the mage-stirred in his sleep too, in a barred boarded house-stirred at the side of the creature with whom he shared his exile-got up from bed and walked to those windows, feeling -something finally, be it only the threat of Hell and death.
