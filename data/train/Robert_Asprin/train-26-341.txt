"Going to have to get  you a club to wear  in your belt, or brass  knuckles. But forget the apology-I saw  it all. Not your  fault at all. Here.  First one's for you. Next one's  for me. Going  to be an  edgy night, Throde.  Who the blazes is that woman?" Throde had no answer. He served the veiled lady's table. She had two glasses  of wine only, without ever showing her face; her companion put away several  beers.
