They expected the  dreaded mage to  walk in at  any time, but  he still had  not appeared when they shut the door. Smhee whispered, 'Anyone coming by will notice that there is no guard.' They entered the next  room cautiously. This was  even larger and was  obviously the bedroom. The bed was huge and  round and on a platform with three  steps. It was covered with a rich scarlet material brocaded in gold.
