Siveni's plan and Mriga's had been  to take Harran straight back to  heaven with them, to her tall, fair temple-house in the country beyond the world's time. But they had dallied too long in the mortal world, while Harran got his bearings and got  used to  his new  body... and  then one  night had  awakened to  find  that heaven's gate was shut on them, and no way back. They were marooned.... So Siveni walked the mortal world without her armor, without her army-conquering
