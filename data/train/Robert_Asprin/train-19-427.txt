"Hey!" cried one of the others, licking his wet hand, "he's got brandy in here!" As Wedemir got to his feet he heard the click of dice across the floor. "Six and six," said Ottar, reaching for the silver ball. "No!" shrieked Ricio. "You barbarian swine!" Wedemir took another step towards him, and then everything changed. The room was filled with pale-haired northerners, waving bloody knives; Wedemir smelled smoke. He started to turn, saw Ricio's knife flash. Instinct took over and his callused fist connected with the boy's jaw.
