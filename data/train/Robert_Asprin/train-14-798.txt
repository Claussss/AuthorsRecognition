   Joshua had moved to the window overlooking the shuttle pads and was staring at something outside the line of vision of the other officers.    "General," he said without turning away from his post, "I think you should look at this."    The sound was from a full dozen hover cycles, whose Legionnaire riders kept revving the engines noisily despite their slow pace. What was even more attention-getting, however, was the procession they were escorting.
