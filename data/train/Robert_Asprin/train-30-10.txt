Privately he suspected that his elevated status was not so much a compliment  to him as  it was  the Beysa's  way of  keeping her  own clanfolk  in line,  but he reveled in the attention while he had it. The  next  petitioners  were  ushered in  and,  dutifully,  Hakiem  directed his attention to the problems at hand. He did not know the three Beysib in the group save they weren't clan Burek aristocrats and therefore must be Setmur fishermen.
