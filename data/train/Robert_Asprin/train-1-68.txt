Things were a lot more complicated than they had appeared on the surface. Terrel must have been much more involved in the PFLS than Sarah thought. He had been killed, tortured because of this. Somehow, Terrel had crossed someone in a major way. Damn them all! Cade threw the whetstone across the courtyard, against the far wall. Damn. Why hadn't he come to me? And that was what kept eating at him, demanding an answer. Why hadn't Terrel asked Cade for help? He knew what his younger brother was, what he did. Cade had always protected Terrel, but this time Terrel had chosen to do it on his own. And he'd paid the price. Whom had he crossed and how?
