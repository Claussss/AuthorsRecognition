He improved that with another wish.... At about dusk he departed, a bit weak in the legs but happy (he'd had to  resort to a wish to get her  to leave him alone and go  away), for he had thought of  a wonderful  mission  for himself:  Hanse  Godslayer. Along  the  way his  stomach rumbled. He wished he had an apple, so the first vendor he passed called  "Hey!" and tossed him a beauty.
