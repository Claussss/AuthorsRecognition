Regli's house had been built for room-by-room defence in the days when  bandits or a  mob would burst  into a dwelling  and strip it,  in despite of anything the government might attempt. The midwife stood, stiff and dour, with her back to the door. Regli ignored  her and slashed  at the  wall again.  'In the  year I've  known her,  Samlane hasn't mentioned her brother a dozen times -and each of those was a curse!' he said.
