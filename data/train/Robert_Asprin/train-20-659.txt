He does not look his age, or tend  to think of it. The years are his,  mandated. Only a very special kind of enemy  could defeat him, and those were few  and far between. Simple death, morbidity or the  spells of his brothers were like  gnats he kept  away by  the perfume  of his  sweat: merely  the proper diet, herbs and spells and  consummated will,  had long  ago vanquished  them as  far as  he was
