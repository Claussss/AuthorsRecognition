   "That was nice, Captain," the actress said after the youth had departed. "Would it be a horrible imposition to ask if I could give you a kiss before you left?"    "Tiffany," Phule said with mock solemnity, "it would be a pleasure."    The phone rang on the bedside table.    "Damn!" the actress snarled, then caught herself and smiled again.
