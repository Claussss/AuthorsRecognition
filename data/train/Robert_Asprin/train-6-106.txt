   The general flushed and started to pick up the pike. Then the chancellor snickered, and the general spun around to glare at him. The chancellor smirked openly and whispered something to the king, who tried to suppress a smile at his words.    The general turned to me again, ignoring the pike, and glared down from his full height.    "Who are you?" he asked in a tone which implied my name would be immediately moved to the head of the list for public execution.
