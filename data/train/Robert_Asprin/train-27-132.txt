Crit snatched the feathered parchment from him, held it squinting in the torch's light. 'That's right, that's what it says here.' He rubbed his jaw; then stepped forward. The slave flinched, his handsome face turned away. Crit pulled out  the bolts that  held him  pinned, grunting;  no blood  followed; Straton's  quarrels penetrated clothing only; the  slave crouched down, unscathed  but incapacitated
