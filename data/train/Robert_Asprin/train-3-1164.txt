Hurriedly Gonfred knocked back the contents of the goblet. He gasped some  more, watching the other man return to his chair behind the cloth-draped desk. "Your hiccups  are gone,  Gonfred my  friend. There  is always  a trade, a Price beyond this silver, over which I have no control. If it is unbearable, return." Gonfred sat staring. His hiccoughs  were gone. "Thank you, Spellmasier!"  He was at the door when  he turned, paced back  to the desk, and  retrieved both silver
