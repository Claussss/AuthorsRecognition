  Robert Asprin   Stealers' Sky Dramatis Personae The Townspeople ABOHORR-One-thumbed proprietor of the Vulgar Unicorn, now owned by Strick. SILKY-A barmaid at the Vulgar Unicorn. AHDIOVIZUN; AHDIOMER VIZ; AHDIO-Proprietor of Sly's Place, a legendary dive within the Maze. THRODE-An employee at Sly's Place. CLEYA; JODEERA-The woman Ahdio loves, and who works for him at Sly's Place. Since she is far too beautiful to travel safely through the Maze, Ahdio has arranged for her to be protected by a disguise of ugliness. OULEH-A doubly endowed denizen ofSIy's Place.
