"Hold the house!" Feltheryn instructed. "Rounsnouf has fled, and I must chase him!" "To the Vulgar Unicorn?" Snegelringe inquired. "If so, I'll have the hide of the barkeep. I paid him to be sure the curtain was on time!" He went back into his dressing room, wiped the makeup from his face with a wet towel, then pulled on a tunic. Just to be sure he would be taken seriously he added the belt with the King's sword. He threw a short cloak over the tunic against the chill, then he left the theater. No matter that the sword was cheap iron, a hand on the hilt was all it usually took!
