He shivered, there in the  dark. There was the taste  of blood in his mouth.  He leaned there against the  wall, jolted as the  bay took another kick  to let him know its opinion of this dark stable. He suspected. He suspected himself-is this a sane man? He had to go-there. To the river. To find out. Not by dark, not during her  hour but by his; by the daylight, when he might have his wits about him.
