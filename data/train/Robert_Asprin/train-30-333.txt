Chenaya threw herself into the fight. The force of blows and blocks shivered  up her arm. She filled her other fist with one of her daggers; when one of her foes ventured too  close, she  shoved it  through his  sternum. It  came free  with a slick, sucking noise as she kicked him away. Sweat ran down her face; blood slicked the palm of her right glove. She  whirled into the midst of  the three remaining attackers,  raking the edge of  her sword
