The Hell-Hound shook his head again, remembering with no small measure of disbelief the last briefing he had attended. The big news of the briefing was that Theron was recalling the Rankan 3rd Commando and the remaining elements of the Stepsons back to the capital "for reassignment to assist in suppressing the civil disorder within the Empire."
