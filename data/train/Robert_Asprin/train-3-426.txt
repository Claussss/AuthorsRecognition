"Let's go,"  she called,  tapping her  friend on  the arm  in comradely fashion. There was more than a hint of glee in her voice. Dismas led her down the Wideway, up the Street of Smells and along a narrow road she didn't know. The road rutted  out; they were in undergrowth denser  than any she'd imagined this side of the White Foal. They stopped in a wide ditch. "There," he whispered.
