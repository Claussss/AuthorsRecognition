She set them on the counter with a rude grunt. Chenaya crooked a finger at  Zip. "Roll 'em," she ordered. "High number wins." He paused, studying  her, their gazes  locked in a  game of dare  and challenge. Finally, he  swept up  the cubes  and tossed  them. "Eleven," Chanaya announced. "Not bad." Then, she rolled them.
