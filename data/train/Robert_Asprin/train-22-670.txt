'Back, back, back,' he  counselled the horse, who  twitched its ears and  turned its head around reproachfully, but obeyed him. He heard titters among the eunuchs, another behind in the crowd. He swung  round in his saddle. 'Hakiem,  if I hear any  stories about me I  do not like, I  will know whose tongue to hang on my belt.'
