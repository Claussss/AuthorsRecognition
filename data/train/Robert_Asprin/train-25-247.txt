They were listening intently, soaking up everything he said. "Okay, we've held up training enough. Report to the firing range after dinner for an extra hour's penalty tour." The teams laughed as they resumed their training. Sending them to the firing range for a penalty tour was like sending a kid to Disneyland. Ever since the new weapons had arrived, the teams had to be driven away from the ranges. They even had to take head count at meals to be sure teams didn't skip eating to sneak out to the range for extra practice.
