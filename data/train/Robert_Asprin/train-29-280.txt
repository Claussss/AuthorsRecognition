"I am an old man, in case you have not noticed," he said with dignity. "My body does not move as quickly as it once did, nor do my guts digest as well. I will pay you as if the whole cup were wine." That settled the matter and the barkeep gave him what he wanted. "I will pay for something else as well," he said after he had taken a drink. "Something within your proper duties but which might be distasteful without the gold."
