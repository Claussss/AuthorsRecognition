Before the  golden-haired prince could  express his surprise or pleasure he was politely, but firmly, shoved to one side. "It is time." The Beysa came forward onto a  cloth-of-gold carpet laid between the alcove  and the altar. Her first steps were tentative; she tottered between the outstretched arms of her waiting-women.
