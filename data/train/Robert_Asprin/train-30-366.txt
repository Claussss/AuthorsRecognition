"You've a goddess to sleep with? Worm! Then go to her-we'll do fine without  you here!" Gilla exclaimed. The copper pot hurtled toward Lalo like a sunwheel, struck, and clattered to the floor. He straightened, holding his arm. "I will go-" He fought his voice  steady. "I should have left long ago.  I could have been the greatest artist in the Empire if you hadn't tied me here-I   still could-by the Thousand Eyes of  Ils you do not know  what I can do!" he  went on.
