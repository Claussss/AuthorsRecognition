I thought about this for some time. "I must admit," I said at last, "I had never given serious consideration to outliving my usefulness." "I would not concern myself with the problem," replied Zur, "were I you, Commander. In many ways, you have changed much more readily than I." "Explain?" I requested. "The change has been obvious, Commander," Zur asserted. "Whether your rise in rank has been because of your change, or you have changed to fit the rank is irrelevant. The change is there."
