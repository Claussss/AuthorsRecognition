He step-clonked his way to the counter. "Are you Ahdio?" The mailed man grinned. "Uh-huh. What do you want me to call you tonight-Notable?" Under the hat a black mustache moved, but was too full to allow teeth to flash in a rueful smile. "You know everything, don't you? You really a sorcerer, Ahdio?" Ahdio set a hamlike hand to his chain-scintillant chest and took on a sweet and innocent look-as much of one as that slab of face was capable. "Me? Are you a member of the Hell-Hounds?"
