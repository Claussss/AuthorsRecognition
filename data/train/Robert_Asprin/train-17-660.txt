'Thales, there's only  me and I  can't carry you.  I freed another  and he can't help. What shall I do?' It took Tempus a long while to make him understand, trying to form words without a  tongue, and  once Kurd  moved. Hanse  turned to  see the  other freed  wretch fleeing past the  vivisectionist. Hanse threatened  and Kurd froze.  He held the lantern in a quivering hand at the end of a wavering arm.
