'Thou shalt  have a  sword,' a  voice said  sonorously inside  his head,  a lion within the shadowed corridors of his mind, ';/ thou free'st my valued and  loyal ally. Aye, and a fine sheath for it, as well. In silver!' Hanse stopped. He was still and dark as the shadow of a tree or a wall of stone. He was good at it; six minutes ago four cautious people had passed close  enough to touch him, and never knew he was there.
