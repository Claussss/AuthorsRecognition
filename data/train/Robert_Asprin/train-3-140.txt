"Thank the gods!" said Illyra. In  the flickering light of the lamps  she looked worn and hollow-eyed. "I thought you didn't  believe in them," muttered  Gilla. She was still  holding onto Lalo's hand. Carefully she opened her  fingers, and set it on his lap  with the other.  He was  still unconscious,  but his  breathing had  quickened. In  a moment, she thought, he will waken, and what then?                        |
