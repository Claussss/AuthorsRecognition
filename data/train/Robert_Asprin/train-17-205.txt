'Over there.'  She pointed  to a  shadowed recess  beyond the  tent. Aristarchus started for it, still holding her horse's bridle. The animals stepped delicately over broken equipment and abandoned clothing. Quartz and Chan  ran from the  shadowed side of  the tent. Quartz  was laughing. Through  the  chaos  she saw  Wess,  tagged  Chan on  the  shoulder  to get  his attention, and changed direction to hurry towards Wess.
