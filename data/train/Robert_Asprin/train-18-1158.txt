"Strat, damn  you to  hell," Crit  whispered, shaking  his head  to clear  it of horse-thoughts and horse-needs  and horse-loyalties. This  wasn't even a  living horse, just a ghost, something Ischade had conjured from a dead animal. But the thing kept coming, head high, feet carefully placed to avoid stepping on its dangling bridle reins. Bridle reins? Had they been there before? He didn't think so.
