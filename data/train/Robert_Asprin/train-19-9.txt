The Rankans living in Sanctuary CHENAYA; DAUGHTER OF THE SUN-.4 beautiful and powerful young woman, the prince's cousin, who is fated never to lose a fight. In her arrogance and innocence she made more enemies in Sanctuary than even fate could handle and has left town until her reputation repairs itself. DAYRNE-Her companion and trainer. LEYN, OUIJEN, DISMAS, AND GESTUS-Her friends and gladiators at her father's school.
