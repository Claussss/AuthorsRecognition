With a curse that  made the ranks clap  their hands to their  helmeted ears, the archmage swished into the street, across  it, and through the door marked  'Men' without another word. It was his motioned command which made the troops follow. A waitress Tempus knew came out when the gibbous moon was high, to ask him if he was hungry.
