He  covered the  youth's nakedness with his mantle, taking the gory hand from the broken haft, letting it fasten on his own. Then  the  blue-grey eyes  of  Abarsis opened  in  a face  pale  with pain,  and something else: 'I am not frightened, with you and the god beside me.'
