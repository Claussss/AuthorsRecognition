She  tightened her  lips as  if she   would spit  at him.  It occurred  to  her. Childhood  reflex.  Then  her eyes  fixed  on  a move  behind  his  shoulder. On Tasfalen, who had stood listless till  then; now Tasfalen's head lifted and  the eyes  focused sharp;  the chest  gave with  a wider  breath and  the whole  body straightened.
