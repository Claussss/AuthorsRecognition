His candle flickered.  He glanced up  and saw a  beardless fat man  in an ornate formal  robe,  scarcely  normal  dress  for  a  visit  to  the  Vulgar  Unicorn. 'Greetings,' the person said. His voice was like a child's. Cappen squinted through the gloom. 'I don't believe I know you,' he replied. 'No, but you will come to believe it, oh, yes, you will.' The fat man sat  down.
