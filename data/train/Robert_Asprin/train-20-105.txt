Turn, cut.  Move. Block,  turn, block,  cut! Finally  he stopped,  the raindrops collecting in the wrinkles of his forehead. Slow-all of it. Slow. To the untrained eye his swordwork might seem smooth and expert, but he knew  he had a mere fraction of his old speed. He made to test his suspicions; he stooped and picked up two clods of dirt with his left hand and tossed them into the air. He swung at  them with his  improvised weapon.
