Horses clattered towards her and she spun, afraid of being run down. Aristarchus brought them  to a  halt and  flung her  the second  horse's reins.  It was  the skewbald stallion from Satan's cart, the one with the wild blue eye. It  smelled the blood on her and snorted and reared. Somehow she kept hold of the reins.
