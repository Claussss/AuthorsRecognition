Her hand and arm reached out. Gently,  she took hold of the third pouch;  tugged at it. Stulwig let go. He watched numbly as she turned and went rapidly down the stairway. Moments later there was  a flare of light  as the bottom door  opened  and shut. Just before it  closed he had a glimpse  of the alley that was  there, and of her turning to go left.
