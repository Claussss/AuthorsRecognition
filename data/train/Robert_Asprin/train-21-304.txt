Finally he nodded to himself. "You look okay," he declared. "Shouldn't I?" I said, innocently. "I heard you had quite a time last night," he explained, shooting me another sidelong glance. "Thought I'd better look you up and survey the damage. I'll admit you seem to have weathered the storm well enough. Resilience of youth, I guess."
