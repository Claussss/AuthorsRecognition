He paused again. Suddenly, there was a soldier standing ten feet from him. He hadn't seen him stand up or step out of the bushes; it was as if he had sprung from the earth itself. "It's about time. Want a smoke?" "You wanted to talk, so talk." The man sounded annoyed. Tidwell grinned to himself-probably upset that his crack team had been discovered. "I've got a message for you. We're asking you once politely to withdraw your men."
