He was sweating and he  was thirsty and he was  nauseated. There was a red  film before his eyes that made it hard to tell which comer he was on. If he was lost in  Downwind, he was nearly dead:  he knew those streets like  he knew the  tunnels, the  sewers... the  sewers. If  he could  find a rat-hole, he could curl up in one;  he didn't want to die  in public. That thought, and  that alone, kept him on  his feet just long  enough for him to  stumble into Ratfall,
