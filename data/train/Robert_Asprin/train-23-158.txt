Ramona was wide-eyed now. "But-if that's true-" she stammered. "... You could have bluffed your way out." Tambu finished the thought for her. "It's a little late for that now, don't you think?" The girl stared wordlessly for a few moments, then threw back her head and laughed. "Ramona, the crafty pirate," she declared, shaking her head. "Trapped by blind luck and her own big mouth. Forgive me, but if I don't laugh, I might start crying."
