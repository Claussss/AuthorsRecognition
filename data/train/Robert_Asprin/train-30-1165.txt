Monkel surpressed a start as Haron hawked noisily and spat on the floor. Of  all the local customs, this was the hardest  for him to accept. Among the Beysib,  a woman's saliva was more often than not poisonous. "That's a  lot of  bird dung.  Old Man,"  she announced.  "Just goes to show how little you know about what a woman looks for in a man. Ignore these  wharf-rats, Monkel. Tell me, what does she think?"
