"What is it, love?" Ramona called, stretching sleepily. The figure at the console made no move to respond or to acknowledge her question. "Tambu? Hey!" Concerned now, she crawled to the foot of the bed and rose, moving to his side. "Tambu?" she asked again, touching his shoulder lightly. He turned his head and focused on her as if seeing her for the first time. "Oh! Sorry, love. Did I wake you?" "What is it?" she pressed, ignoring his question. "Is something wrong?"
