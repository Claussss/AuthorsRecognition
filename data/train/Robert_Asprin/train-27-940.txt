'As you served Enas Yorl and the priests of Savankala, so now, my son, you shall serve Me,' said the Voice of Ils. Before him  Lalo saw  a white  canvas, and  brushes that  surpassed his own as a Downwinder's donkey is surpassed by a horse of Tros, and a palette with pigments for whose secret the colour-grinders of Sanctuary would have given their  souls. Lalo's right hand prickled  with power that built,  built - it must  be grounded
