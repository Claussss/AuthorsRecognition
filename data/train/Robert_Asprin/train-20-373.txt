"Yes, clasp your hands-your head is very well as it is. You must relax, sir, and think how near your business is to its conclusion..."Lalo poured thinner  into the cup and dipped his brush. "Yes," Zanderei echoed softly. "I am almost done. A week or less will show me if I have accomplished all I  was sent to do. The  conflict draws very close to  us now." His thin lips curved in the faintest of smiles.
