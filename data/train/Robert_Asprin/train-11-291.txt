   "Good enough, kid," Aahz growled, clapping me on the shoulder. "Let's go!"    With that, he plunged headlong into the crowd, clearing a path for me to emerge on the street behind the Trophy procession. Clearing paths through moveable objects, like people, is one of the things Aahz does best.    "Make way!" he bawled. "One side! Make way!"    Close behind him, I added my bellow to the din.    "Ta-hoers!" I called. "At the South wall! Tahoers!"
