Heavy steps came to it; someone opened  the peephole and looked out and shut  it She knocked  a second  time. And  heard a  higher voice  than belonged with that tread, before the bar thumped back and the door opened inward. The S'danzo Illyra stood to meet her, and that shadow to the side was Dubro, was a very distraught Dubro; and Illyra's face was tearstreaked. The S'danzo wrapped her fringed shawl about her as at-some ill wind sweeping through her door.
