The sword was metal again but twisted and useless, in the shape of the snake  it had been, coiling back toward the scabbard. Enraged, Lythande jerked free of the twisted metal, sent a spitting rain  of fire in Rabben's direction. Quickly  the huge  adept covered  himself in  fog, and  the fire-spray  extinguished itself.
