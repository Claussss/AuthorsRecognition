"I take it you haven't seen this." She gestured at the wall over the washstand. I squinted slightly and focused my still-bleary eyes on the spot she was indicating. Written on the wall, in bright red lipstick, was a note. Sorry to go, but I didn't want to wake you. Last night was magic. You're as good as your rep. Let me know when you want to play some more. I found myself smirking as I read the note.
