He watched as the board lit up again. This time the lights were all amber-all but one. The Scorpion would never call in again. "Tambu out." The fleet secure, Tambu slumped back in his chair as the enormity of his deed washed over him. Gone! All of them. Ramona, Egor, A.C., Jelly...all of them wiped out when he pressed a single button on his console. In his shock-dulled mind, he realized he had lost his personal battle. When pressured, it was Tambu who controlled his actions, and Tambu had ended his last hope of retirement. He couldn't leave the fleet now. With the captains gone, there would be no one to pass control to. He would have to stay on, working with the new captains, reorganizing...
