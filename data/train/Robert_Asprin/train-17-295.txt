Women  of quality  he had  seen tended to nutter the eyes;  this one  stared eye to  eye, and  he  found himself inclined  to break  the contact, to look  down or elsewhere.  She  extended her  hand, close to  touching  him, a move he thought might be an invitation to take liberties of his own. And then  she drew  the hand  back and  the moment  passed.
