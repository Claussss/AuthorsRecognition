Shaking his head, Throde went through the door into the taproom-the inn  proper. Ahdio sighed. "Sure nice to have company," he  muttered, and Sweet-boy looked up and  belched. Ahdio gave him a look. "Here! Cats do not belch, Tige. Maybe you should consider giving up strong drink." The final word brought the cat to attention, and to its mug. It peered within as if myopic, looked pointedly up at its human, twitched its stub and said "Mraw?"
