Writhing under the cross-examination, Monkel deliberately avoided looking at the other Setmur  clansmen in  the room.  He knew  they would  be staring  at him in amazement and/or  disgust. Sex  was a  private subject  among the Beysib, seldom discussed and never bantered about publicly. The Old Man eyed Monkel in quiet speculation. "A guard from the royal clan Burek?" he said. Monkel nodded silently.
