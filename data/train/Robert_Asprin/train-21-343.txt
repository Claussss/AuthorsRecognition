I paused for a few moments, then rapped lightly on her door before I lost my nerve. Truth to tell, I was half hoping she was out or asleep, which would let me off my self-imposed hook. "Who is it?" So much for half-hopes. Maybe next time I should try a whole one. "It's me, Bunny. Skeeve." "What do you want?" "I'd like to talk to you, if it's all right." There was a silence that lasted just long enough for me to both get my hopes up, and to start seriously worrying.
