"This is no place for you." Raif bit his lip, darting glimpses at the bodies around him. He slowly sheathed his knife. "You said you would teach me to be a warrior," he said. He gestured at the dead mercenary. "I've seen death before. Cade." Cade's eyes went dark. He grabbed the boy and pushed him to the ground by the corpse of the Beast. Grabbing the old man's collar, he pulled the corpse up to face the boy.
