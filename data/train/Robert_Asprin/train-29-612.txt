"Don't just stand there like a worthless eunuch!" the whore shouted when her rescuer didn't move. "He's going to-" The wizard frowned and touched her temple with one finger. Her head sagged back before she could say another word. Her eyes fluttered shut. She sighed, then went limp. But almost instantly, her lids snapped open again. She screamed and cowered away from the wizard's hand as far as her bonds allowed.
