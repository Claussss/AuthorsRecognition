Here in the  chapel of the  Mother, flickering candles  struck sparks of  colour from the  mosaics and  one scarcely  noticed the  rough repairs where Vashanka's thunderbolt had cracked the  wall. Gilla huddled in  the shadows while the  blue robed priestesses passed back and forth before the marble image of the  Goddess, continuing their song. Whatever men destroy is for Thy mending, 			Forever feeding from Thy fruitful breast;
