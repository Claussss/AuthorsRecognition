Crit did the same for Randal. Neither asked if the task was done. All could see the globe, spinning  brighter, whirling larger, consuming the lesser flame of burning wood and stone and thatch and blazing like a star. The horses were glad to be reined back; the heat was singeing. You couldn't hear a word or even the trumpets of  mounts who hated fire as they reared  and walked backwards on hind legs.
