"No doubt she's wonderful," he mumbled as he chewed. "Just not that wonderful!" Thus it came as a surprise when, on the fourth morning after the play had opened. Lady Sashana arrived at the theater with her bodyguard servants and declared her intention to kill the Emperor's cousin, and the price be damned. "My poor child!"
