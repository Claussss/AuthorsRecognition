The man  on the  horse who  could be  seen within  the cloud  waved: a  flash of scarlet glove,  a swirl  of burgundy  cloak. Behind  his tasselled  steed he led another,  and it  was this  second grey  horse who  again challenged  the  other stallions on the beach,  its eyes full of  fire.
