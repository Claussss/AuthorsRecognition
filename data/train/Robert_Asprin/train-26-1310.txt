Holding them as  if they might  bite, he came  back to the  little group in  the center of the room and squatted down. "Do you like pretty pictures? What do you like- butterflies?" A swift stroke  of the chalk  laid the  sweep of  a red  wing; another  suggested the long body and bright eyes. Lightning flared in the window, blinding him. When Lalo could see again  Arton's chubby hand was rubbing the picture away.
