'Oh.' And Mignureal looked upon him, head to one side. Moonflower saw the look. 'You will go to bed and tomorrow you will tell me what you were doing abroad  so late, Mignue. You will not come near Hanse again, do you understand?' 'Oh,  mother.'  Mignureal met  the  level gaze  only  briefly. 'Yes,  mother.  I understand.' And she went to bed.
