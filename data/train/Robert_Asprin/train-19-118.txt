"No one does. Termagant, believe me." "I promise not to try to leam more than you want me to know. Will you come and see me, Hanse?" "Not tonight!?" "No, no, not tonight, Hanse. At a reasonable time of day when this night's work is done and you can come and visit me in the next day or three, will you do so?" "Termagant, I will." "Good," she said, with an exuberant nod. "Then when you come to see me, Hanse, bring me this." Her long-fingered hands came out from within her cloak, and in an instant she had draped a piece of cord over his neck. Something thumped his chest and he looked down.
