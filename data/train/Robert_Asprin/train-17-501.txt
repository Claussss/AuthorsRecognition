'That's where they'll beach it! Come on!' He led their rush down the dock. He  heard rather than saw the net scoop up  its prey; a cheer  went up from  the small boats.  He was waiting  waist-deep in the water when the Old Man's boat  finally reached the shallows. Grabbing on  to the cleats, Hort dragged the boat to the beach as if it were a toy while his  father sagged wearily between the oars.
