"That's all right," Haught said. "You've told me." He still had his hand on  the Stepson's  ankle,  and closed  it  down till  his  fingers went  white.  "Hello, Straton's eyes opened.  He made a  small move to  lift his head  from the wadded cloak, and perhaps he saw Haught, before the pain got him and twisted his  face. "Oh, damn," he said, letting his head back, "damn." "Damned for sure," Haught said. "How does it feel, Rankan?"
