She blushed hotly. No doubt he'd seen  a lot more than her back. And  she'd been in such a rush to get away with  Daxus she'd forgotten to pull on more than  the cloak. She released the chain and  hurriedly dressed. But it irritated her  that she hadn't noticed Dayrne, and she mentioned it.
