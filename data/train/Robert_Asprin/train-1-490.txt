"Are you all right?"called Khamwas. The question impressed Samlor, for it sounded sincere-and in similar circumstances, he himself would have been worried more about his own situation than that of his companions. "We're going to get drenched when we leave here," the caravan master said. "Leaving'll still feel good. Any luck yourself?" The Napatan grimaced. "The room's empty," he said. "The brazier's as clean as if it was never used. I'm not sure it's here at all."
