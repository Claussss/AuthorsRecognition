The vivid images of  that past disaster faded.  Stulwig sank back and  down onto the sheep fur. Covered himself. Listened in the continuing dark to the sound  of wind against a corner of his greenhouse. It was a strong wind; he could feel the bedroom tremble.  Moments later,  he was  still awake  when he  heard a  faraway muffled cry - someone being murdered out there in the Maze? Oddly, that was  the final steadying  thought.
