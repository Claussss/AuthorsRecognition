A man who loved his son, and who had warned him; at the same time he understood young idealists, and was proud of him. "Be sensible," he had told his son. "Trade is the way to power." And his son Beruth: "Trade! When the Rankene pigs tax us to the bone and confiscate our shipments!" "Did I say, compliance?" he had said. "Did I say, stupidity?" Tapping the side of his head. "Brains, young hothead. Trade is an art of the mind. Trade is an art of compromise-"
