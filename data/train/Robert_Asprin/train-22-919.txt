'So you said in Ranke. I still find it offensive, without redeeming merit.' 'Without redeeming...' Kurd shrieked, then words failed him. His lips tightened, he seized Zalbar by the arm and began pulling him towards the house. 'Come  with me now,' he instructed. 'Let  me show you my work  and explain what I am  doing. Perhaps then you will be able to grasp the importance of my studies.' In his career Zalbar had faced  death in many guises and done  it unflinchingly.
