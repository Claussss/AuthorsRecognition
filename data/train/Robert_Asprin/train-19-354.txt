"First," Mariat continued, "allow me to make two special introductions." As she spoke, a young boy in his early teens entered the room. He brought with him a clean slate upon which he began to draw a map. "This is my grandson Keldrick, who has recently proved himself man enough to take part in this assembly." The merchants shifted uncomfortably, not quite understanding what she meant. Bakarat was becoming more and more agitated as the scene developed according to Mariat's plan.
