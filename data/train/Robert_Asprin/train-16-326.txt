   "That will be five dollars, sir," the cocktail waitress said, interrupting Lobo's thoughts as she delivered his drink.    The opportunity was too good to let pass.    "What do you mean, five dollars?" he snarled, raising his voice. "I thought drinks were free in these casinos."    Though she was small, easily as small as the uniformed Legionnaire accompanying the monster, the cocktail waitress held her ground, apparently used to dealing with loud drunks.
