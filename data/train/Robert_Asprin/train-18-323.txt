"Good night and good sleep, young sir." Young sir. The peephole  closed and a tight  small smile came to  the ex-slave's face; a fox's smile. He stepped briskly off the porch with a light swirl of  his russet cloak and a wink of his sword-hilt in the starlight. "Gods," Stilcho said, "the ring- the ring, man-" "Ah," Haught said, pressing a hand to his breast. "Damn. I forgot it." He looked back at the door. "I can't call them back-that wouldn't impress them at all."
