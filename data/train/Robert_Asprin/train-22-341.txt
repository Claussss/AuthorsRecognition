The room was huge and empty, pierced by a score of doorways. Each but the one at which Samlor stood and one other was closed by an iron grate. Samlor touched but did not draw  his double-edged dagger. 'I'll play your  silly game,' he whispered. Taking short  steps, he walked around the  circumference of the room and out the other open door. Another empty passage stretched beyond it. Licking his lips again, Samlor followed the new tunnel.
