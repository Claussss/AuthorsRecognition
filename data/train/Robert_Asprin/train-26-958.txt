"Stilcho you fool, shut that, the  wind's like knives," said another voice  from beyond  the  door. And  there  came a  smaller,  slimmer man,  who  wore a  cold composure exactly the opposite of  Stilcho's desolation; but under it,  ghost to its solidity, dwelt the same impression of unrelenting fear.
