"Who are you?" "Tell the Beysa. Tell  the Beysa mine is  the house with the  red door, downhill from here. My name is Moria. Say  to the Beysa that there are ways  to safeguard her people. And ways to pass any door." It came out in a rush and was done.  She did not know what she had said, except that the two Beysib stared at her and the tall woman's necklace had risen up to stare too, quite unpleasantly.
