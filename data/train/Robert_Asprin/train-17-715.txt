Shaking with fatigue and loss, Lalo sat  down on the floor. He heard the  rustle of the  sorcerer's robes  as he  moved to  embrace his  lady, and after a little while the sound  of the painting  being removed and  her footsteps going  to the door. Then Lalo and Enas Yorl were alone. 'Well  ...  it  is done  ..  .'The  sorcerer's voice  was  fleshless,  like wind whispering through dry leaves. 'Will you take your payment now?'
