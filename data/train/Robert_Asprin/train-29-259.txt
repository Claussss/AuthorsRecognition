He fell back laughing and his legs stretched out so that his feet, too small for his body, wiggled in the air- "It sounds as if he has to use the outhouse!" Feltheryn held back his own laugh. Taken out of context, the little comic was right. "Come, Rounsnouf," Feltheryn said, offering his hand and helping the little man up. "I think that we had best to bed, else wake the house." "Let me see ..." the comic said as he gained his feet and shook himself. "The boy walked thus ..."
