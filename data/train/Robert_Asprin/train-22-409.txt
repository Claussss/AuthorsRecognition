Samlor staggered to his feet, choking on  dust and knowing that if he was  lucky he was about to die. Heqt's gilded bronze head, backed by the limestone spire, plunged down from  the ceiling.  It struck  Dyareela's upturned  face like  a two-hundred-ton  crossbow bolt. The  floor beneath  disintegrated. The  limestone column  scarcely slowed, hurtling out of sight as the earth itself shuddered to the impact.
