The Stepson looked up. A shadow stood  there in the lightnings, in the rain,  an unlikely cloaked shape, a darkness by the bridge. When the lightning next flashed it was  gone. Fire danced on the water, full  of tricks and shadows on this side of  the bank. The blaze might have taken  all of Downwind, but for the rain. It was dying even now.
