   At that moment the guy came out of the back room carrying      two plates. With a smile and a flourish he slid them in front of us.      Vegetables. Asparagus, carrots, celery, a few sliced toma     toes, and part of a cucumber, artfully arranged on a bed of what looked like grass.    "Wonderful," Glenda said, smiling at the man with her big     gest and most alluring smile. "I hope we can find a way to repay you for this feast."
