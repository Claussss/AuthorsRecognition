"You must be the only one in the known dimensions who doesn't know that song" she said. "Are you sure you're Skeeve? The Great Skeeve?" "Well ... yes. Do we know each other?" Realizing how stupid the question was, I hastened to modify it before she could answer. "No. I'm sure I would have remembered if we had met before." For some reason, my clumsy recovery seemed to please her. "That's sweet," she said, floating forward to run a soft hand along my cheek, light as a butterfly's touch. "No. I haven't had the pleasure. We have a mutual acquaintance, though. Do you remember a Djin named Kalvin?"
