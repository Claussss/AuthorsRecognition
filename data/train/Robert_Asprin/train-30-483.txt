"Nhrm," she said. It was as close as Mriga ever got to the word. From under  the tangle of matted, curly hair, from  out of the bland, barren face,  eyes flashed briefly  up at  Harran-empty, but  very much  alive. There  was no  intelligence there, but there was passion. Mriga loved knives better than anything.
