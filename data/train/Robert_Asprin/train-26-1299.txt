And as if  the will that  had held it  steady had been  suddenly distracted, the wind  disintegrated  into  a  thousand whirling  eddies.  The  river,  no longer thwarted, raced through its narrow channel bare inches below the roadbed of  the bridge  and across  Sanctuary's harbor  in a  great surge  that lifted  anchored vessels to the limits of their moorings and then passed onward out to sea.
