It seemed, sometimes, that one more soul laying down its life for him and one more burden laid upon him would surpass his capacity and he would crack apart into the desiccated dust he doubtless was. Crossing  the whitewashed  court, passing  the stables,  his Tros  horses  stuck steel-gray muzzles over their half-doors  and whickered.
