The white-haired man in green linen said, "I haven't seen you lately. I trust you have something of interest for me?" "Maybe. I found this medallion in this morning's goods. Can you decipher the writing?" The little man's bushy eyebrows raised. His sallow face turned ashen. His gnarled hands trembled, dropping the bauble onto the counter as if it had suddenly become hot. After a moment he said, "Do me a favor, Cholly. Go. Get that thing out of here. Please."
