They had planned this carefully. The  gates to the palace were barred  at night; only a handful of guards bothered to patrol the grounds. No one was admitted  at night except with the Prince's permission. But she and Dayme had found away. Another wall rose around  the granaries themselves. It  was to the west  side of this wall that they  ran. Dayme* unslung the  pack, removed a grapple  and rope.
