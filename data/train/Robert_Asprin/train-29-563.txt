"Doesn't that scare away your customers?" he wondered, amused. "Easy enough to hide it in this darkness," she answered, grinning weakly. "But it's always within reach." They stepped out of the foliage and onto the walkway. Once more, Dayme caught her hand, raised it to his lips, and kissed it. "I'll try to help," he promised again before he turned away. He glanced over his shoulder, but she didn't follow. When he turned a second time she was gone. Asphodel knew the park far better than he did.
