His guide held out a dingy bandage as well. 'For your eyes. For your own safety. The house  has ...  protections. If  I told  you only  to shut  your eyes, you'd forget at the worst moment. And my master wants you whole.' Hanse stared  at the  offered rag,  liking all  of this  less and less; and very softly he  drew the dagger from  his arm sheath and  extended the blade  towards the guide's face.
