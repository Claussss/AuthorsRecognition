'Demands to see me?' Myrtis laid  down the cheese knife. 'A soldier  has nothing that "demands" to see me at the front door. At this hour, soldiers are less  use than tradesmen. Send him around to the back.' The girl ran  back up the  stairs. Myrtis finished  spreading her cheese  on the bread. She had eaten half of it when  a tall man cast a shadow over her  private dining alcove. 'You are blocking my sunlight, young man,' she said without looking up.
