   I saw all this at a glance. I also saw something else.    "Wait a minute, Ajax," I ordered. "Look at that!"    The various missiles loosed by the center line were falling to earth in the massed formations of the troops still awaiting commands. Needless to say, this was not well received, particularly as they were still unable to see the actual target of their advance force. To them, it must have appeared that by some magik or demonic possession, their allies had suddenly turned and fired on them.
