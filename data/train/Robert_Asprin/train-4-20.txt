  "Good afternoon. Lord Magician. May I come in?"   "Certainly, Your Majesty," I said, standing aside. "I never refuse a fifth."   The King frowned.   "Is that a joke? If so, I don't get the point."   "Neither do I," I admitted calmly. "It's something Aahz my apprentice says."   "Ah, yes. Your apprentice. Is he about?"   Rodrick swept majestically into the room, peering curiously into the comers as if he expected Aahz to spring forth from the walls.
