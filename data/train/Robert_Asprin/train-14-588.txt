   There was a heartbeat before recognition sank in, and then a buzz began to ripple through the formation.    "Sir!" came Lieutenant Armstrong's urgent whisper. "Do you know who they are?"    "I know, Lieutenant."    "Those are the Red Eagles!"    "I said I know, Lieutenant!"    "But, sir..."    "Company... atten-hut!"    Phule bellowed out the command as much to stop the conversation as to present a proper military picture. Mostly, however, he wanted time to try to collect his own thoughts.
