There  was nothing  in her  stomach but  bile. She  crawled to  the stream   and scrubbed her hands, then her face, with sand and water. She stood up again.  Her friends were staring at her, shocked. 'There was someone,' she said. 'Bauchle Meyne. But I killed him.' 'Ah,' Quartz said. 'You've given me  another gift,' Satan  said. 'Now I  don't have to  go back and kill him myself.' 'Shut up, Satan, she's never killed anyone before.'
