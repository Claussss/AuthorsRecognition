The window grate might have withstood a battering ram. The screw jack, butted against the stone sash, exerted its pressure sideways across the bars and their frame. The grating crumbled as the Cirdonian inexora- bly levered the jack through it, ignoring what the effort was doing to his fatigued muscles. With the last of his strength, Samlor lurched through the opening he had just torn and sprawled onto the floor of the room beyond.
