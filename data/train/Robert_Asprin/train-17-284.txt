The blind man would  have taken him out  the front, among the  others, following the crowd. Hanse  pulled him instead  to another door,  out into the  back alley where few had  gone and those  already vanished. 'Now,'  Hanse said, taking  the blind man by the arm and backing him against the wall. 'Who?'
