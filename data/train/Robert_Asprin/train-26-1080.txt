"Why would I,  after I went  to such trouble  to come here?"  said the Queen. "I gave up  being spring's  goddess in  favor of  something more worthwhile. Shipri handles spring now." She was still  a moment. "Besides, even Death needs  love," said the Queen at last. Mriga could think of nothing to say. "So." She looked down at them, grave, patient. "Choose. Will you pay the  price? And which of you?"
