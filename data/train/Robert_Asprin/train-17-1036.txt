'With your blessing and the god's, I am fearless. And you will be close by, busy attacking Black Jubal's fortress. While  you arc arresting  the slavemaster  for his  treasonous   spying, whosoever   will  make  good  the   woman's escape.  I understand your thought;  I have arranged  for  the retrieval  of her  weapons.' Tempus chuckled. 'I  hardly know what to say.' 'Say you look kindly upon me, that I am more than a bad memory to you.'
