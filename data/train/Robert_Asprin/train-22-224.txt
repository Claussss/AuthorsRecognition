The stone, however,  looked solid. When  they were halfway  down the bore,  they were blasted with a tremendous growling and roaring. 'Lions?' Masha said. 'No. Bears.' At the opposite end were two gigantic animals, their eyes gleaming redly in  the light, their fangs a dull white. The two  intruders advanced  after waiting  for the  bears to  charge.
