   "Here, fella. Want a treat?"    This last was uttered by a sleazy-looking Deveel as he held out a hand with a lump of some unidentifiable substance in it.    I realized with a guilty start that I had overindulged, sinking too far into my thoughts to maintain awareness of my surroundings. After the unkind thoughts I had entertained about Nunzio's attention span, this was an inexcusable lapse on my part. Ignoring the offered gift, I raised my head and cast about desperately to reassess the situation.
