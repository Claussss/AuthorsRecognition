"I own it, Shadowspawn: you are good and you are a natural. Now. Want to work  a bit from the saddle?" His enthusiasm  showed in his face and added  bright color to his voice. The one  called Stealth  waited a  moment; the  one called  Shadowspawn did  not embellish on that  word which, when  spoken flat and  unadorned, was one  of the four or five harshest and most unwelcome words in any language.
