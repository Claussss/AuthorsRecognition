"Yeah, I caught that. Incidentally, did I show you the results from the firing range today?" "Spare me." But Clancy was on his feet halfway to his case. "They were firing Springfields today," he called back over his shoulder. "The old bolt-action jobs. Range at five hundred meters." Tidwell sighed.
