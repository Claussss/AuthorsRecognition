And as  he ran,  the blood began to course  freely  through  his  limbs  once  more,  and  he  shed  middle-age  and awkwardness as he had shed his ruined smock, and his fear. Lalo leaped over a  handcart that had been  abandoned in the road  and paused to send it spinning broadsides. That would  not long delay them, but he  could hear mercenaries laying bets on a dogfight in the next street. Laughing like the  boy
