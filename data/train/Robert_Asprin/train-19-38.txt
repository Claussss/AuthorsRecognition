"Glass," Skarth said in instant appraisal, and Strick laughed. "True. But it's also today's message token. Hand it to Abohorr and ask him what you want to know. By tonight either he or Ahdio will know where Tarkle stays." On the way out of Strick's, Skarth offered the ridiculously disguised girl his hand. She shrank away.
