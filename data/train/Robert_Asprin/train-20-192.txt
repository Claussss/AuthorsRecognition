"You didn't have to give our names," Moria said. She trembled with rage, whether at Eichan or at her brother. "Any objection if we settle that bastard outright?" "And  leave  questions  unanswered?"  Eichan flung  on  the  cloak.  He towered, difficult to conceal if one suspected  it was Eichan. "No. We can't  afford that now. You've cost us a safe hole. You live in it. And watch yourselves." "There'll be watchers," Moria said, hoping that there would.
