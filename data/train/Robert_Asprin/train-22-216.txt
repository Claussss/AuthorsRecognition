Their many eyes were huge and purplish and cold. They scuttled forwards,  waving the foremost pair of  legs, then backed away  as Smhee waved his  torch at them. Masha walked half-turned away from him so that she could use the brand to  scare away any attack from the rear or side. Suddenly, something leaped from the edge of the darkness and soared towards her. She thrust the brand at it. But the creature seemed to go through the torch.
