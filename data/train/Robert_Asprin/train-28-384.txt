Drawing a seat opposite, he sat down, laid red-furred right shank across left knee, and said gently, 'Tell me.' When  it had  spilled from  Cappen, he  was a  long span  quiet. On  the walls shimmered his weapons, among pretty  pictures that his housemates had  selected. At last he asked low, 'Have you quit?'
