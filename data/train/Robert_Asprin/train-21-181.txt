The General's brow darkened noticeably. "If you're referring to her less than slender appearance, or perhaps the difference in our age ..."he began in a deep growl. "No, you misunderstand me," I said hastily, cutting him off ... though once he mentioned them, both points were worth reflecting on. "I meant, why should you want to speak to me about such a matter?" "Oh. That." For the moment, at least, Badaxe seemed mollified. I mentally made a note to table any discussion of the two points he had raised until another time.
