She paused and looked down at the run-down farmhouse which would soon be refurbished to be their home on the winery, in addition to the uptown mansion Lord Torchholder had arranged for them. "Children, this is home, where we will spend our vintage years. This is where we build anew, from the ground up." It was an entirely new kind of day dawning in Sanctuary. A day of hope.
