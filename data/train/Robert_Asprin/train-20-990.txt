She was gone in the morning, and he lay smiling, thinking about his dream. Lying on his back, he rolled his head. He could see cloak, tunic, and wine-sack from here. That brought him wide awake, and sent  his hand  swinging down  beside the  pallet to  check his buskins. The silver coins  were still  there. Hanse  demonstrated the  cliche of sitting bolt upright. Hurling back the spread, he inspected his bed. That required no effort.
