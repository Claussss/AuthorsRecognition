"If I'm so smart why am I here? Why am I locked in this place with gold I  can't even steal? Why do I take orders from a-" His finger touched her  lips but the silence  was hers, sudden and  prudent. She caught the shadow  in his eyes,  that perpetually evaded,  darted, shifted in  a slave's nowhere privacy-he had turned that apparent shyness to furtive  purpose. Or had always had it. "She's calling in the debt," she said, "isn't She?"
