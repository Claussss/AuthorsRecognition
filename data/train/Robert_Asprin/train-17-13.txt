'What about the wine?' One-Thumb called angrily. 'You haven't paid yet.' Hakiem's response was habitual: 'Pay? I  didn't order it. It was you  who filled the cup. Pay for it  yourself.' He regretted the words  immediately. One-Thumb's treatment of drinkers who refused to  pay was legendary throughout the Maze.  To his surprise, then, it was One-Thumb who gave ground. 'Well, all right,' the big man grumbled. 'Just don't make a habit of it.'
