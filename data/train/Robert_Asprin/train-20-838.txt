He did not stop for  arms or horses, when he  found Niko and Janni, but  set off through the raging din toward the Avenue of Temples, where the child the man and god had begotten upon the First Consort was kept. Handsigns got  them through  until speech  was useful,  when they  had run  west through the lawns and alleys, coming to Vashanka's temple grounds from the back.
