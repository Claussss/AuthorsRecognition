Without blinking, she pressed into his hands that which she carried, and  turned and ran in a  butterfly flurry of yellow  skirts and streaming blue-black  hair. Hanse stood, stupidly staring after her until she rounded a corner and was  gone down another street. Then he looked down at his gift.
