   Having run out of things to say, I finished with a half hearted shrug. Strangely enough, after bearing my soul and clearing my mind of the things which had been troubling me, I felt worlds better.    "Are you through?"    Kalvin was still hovering patiently with his arms folded.  Perhaps it was my imagination, but there seemed to be a terse edge to his voice.    "I guess so. Sorry for running on like that."
