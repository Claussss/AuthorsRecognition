Shaking his head, Tempus  took the amulet Abarsis  held out to him.  'Come then. Stepson, we will see what part of your glorious expectations we can fulfil.' It  was said,  ever after,  that the  Storm God  took part  in the  sack of  the slaver's estate. Lightning  crawled along the  gatehouses of its  defensive wall and rolled in balls through the inner  court and turned the oaken gates to  ash.
