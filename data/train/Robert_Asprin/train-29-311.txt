"How shall I call you then? Like some great bird, that though she be my slave can yet take wing? Like some famed horse, that though I hold the reins can race the wind? I call you love, and hold you in my arms, and yet you overpower me. I call you wife, and you must call me lord; and yet I worship at your shrine!" He ceased to exist as a separate person and became the tragic king, a man doomed by circumstance to destroy all that he loved in life, rescued from the ultimate humiliation only by the intervention of supernatural forces at the end: forces beyond his comprehension.
