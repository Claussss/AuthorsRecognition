The S'danzo ILLYRA-Half-blood S'danzo seeress with True Sight. Wounded by PELS in the False Plague Riots. DUBRO-Bazaar blacksmith and husband to Illyra. TREVYA-A crippled foundling presented to Illyra by Walegrin. MOONFLOWER-Seeress of considerable talent and greater bulk mistakenly slain by Beysib bodyguards. MIGNUREAL; MIGNUE-Her daughter, who loved Hanse and went north with him, but now dwells alone in Firaqa.
