But wherever he went, however far he circled this place, the brook reappeared in its meanderings. He knew what it was, and that if there was a place where it did not exist, then it would be very bad news indeed. It ran  slower than  it had,  and more  shallow. Now  and again some dead branch floated down it, which presaged something. He was afraid to guess. "Come in," Crit said. "Keep your hands in sight."
