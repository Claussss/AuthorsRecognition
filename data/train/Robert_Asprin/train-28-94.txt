'By that, I take it you serve a priestess of Argash?' he said eventually. It was a logical  deduction. On the twenty-foot-high  fane of that divinity  his most devoted followers volunteered,  when life wearied them,  to be hung up  and fast unto death. But Jarveena shook her head violently. 'N-no, sir! Dyareela!'  naming a goddess banned these thirty years owing to  the bloodthirstiness of her votaries.
