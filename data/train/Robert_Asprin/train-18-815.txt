Damn her. Damn, damn, and damn her to bloody hell. Am I alive? Am I like that poor sod Stilcho, alive-dead, killed and brought back out of hell, o gods- A door opened downstairs; wind sucked in a chill gust from the window. "Ischade," he yelled,  and flung himself  past Tasfalen's corpse,  out the door, toward the stairs. He caught himself at the top, looking down on Moria in a torn and muddy gown, on Stilcho standing there ghastly as the truth in that bedroom.
