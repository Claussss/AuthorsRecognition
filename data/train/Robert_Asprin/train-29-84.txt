"Not too choosy, if your friends include the likes of Tempus Thales," Jubal retorted, his voice hardening. "And as for being your own man ..." He lifted the iron from the brazier. "... I fear that came to a halt when the slavers took you. You're mine now. Bought and paid for." Saliman expected Hanse to flinch, but the thief was uncowed. Though his eyes followed the iron, his voice was firm and confident.
