"The sky," Khamwas said hoarsely. "Look." Samlor drew the long dagger from his belt and lifted Star to his chest with his free arm. The semicircle of demons waited, crouching slightly, with their spindly, steelstrong arms interlocked. They were close enough that if one of the creatures leaned forward, it could rip the caravan master's face away in its pointed teeth. "Look!" Khamwas screamed, and even so his voice was smothered by a sound like the scream of a giant snake.
