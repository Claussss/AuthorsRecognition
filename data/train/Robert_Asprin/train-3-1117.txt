"Huh! Think you're that good? You give me someth'." He handed her a silver coin. "That's yours. I want nothing fork." She stared at it, held  it up closer, stared, and  slid off the bed. Sitting  on the floor, she wept for the next ten or so minutes. When at last she looked  up, he bade her use his kerchief. She did. He repeated his request. She stared, head on one side. At last, wriggling loosely, she gave him her broad black belt.
