   A sudden fear clutched at my heart. "C'mon, Tanda," I growled and started off.    By the time we reached the tree line the whole team was assembled there, talking excitedly among themselves.    "What is it?" I barked, joining them.    The group fell silent, avoiding my eyes. Brockhurst lifted a hand and pointed across the meadow.    There, silhouetted against a huge bonfire was Aahz, hanging by his neck from a crude gallows. His body was limp and lifeless as he rotated slowly at the end of the rope. At his feet, a group of soldiers were gathered to witness the spectacle.
