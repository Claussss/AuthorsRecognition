"You go to hell!" he screamed and shut the gunslit. The mercenary on the shore turned to the reporters and shrugged. "You'd better get your heads down." With that, he triggered the remote control detonator switch on his control box, and the frogmen-planted charges removed the three boats from the scene. The mercenary doubled over, gasping from the agony of his wounds. The dark African sky growled a response as lightning danced in the distance. He glanced up at it through a pink veil of pain. Damn Africa! He should have never agreed to this transfer.
