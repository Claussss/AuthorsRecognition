When they got their groaning selves together after a minute or so, their intended prey had vanished, seemingly into the shadows. "Damned old faker!" Ahaz whimpered. "What a mean trick! And us just boys, too!" That was when Hakky kicked him in his other leg. And cried out at the pain his violent movement sent sizzling into his bruised and swelling genitals. A few minutes later the damned old faker used his staff to poke aside several of the thirty-one dangling strands of Syrese rope hanging before the entry to that low dive called Sly's Place. He step-clonked in, glanced around at a fine big crowd of drinkers and babblers, and step-clonked down the single step into the noisy, odoriferous main room.
