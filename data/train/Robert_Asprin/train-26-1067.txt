The south gate looked much as it  did in Sanctuary, and made it plain  that some passions had not entirely died out  here; the posts were splashed with  PFLS and gang graffiti. But  there were no  guards, no Stepsons,  nothing but iron  gates that stood  open. The  great courtyard  inside was  drowned in  shadow, and  the wailings of hell seemed subdued here.
