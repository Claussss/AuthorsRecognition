"What's the matter, Stepson, Tempus take your balls upcountry?" Vis shouted from a safe distance as Crit mounted up. He got off one quarrel, but his aim was half-hearted. It smashed harm- lessly against the brick beside Vis's head. And then there was Kama to deal with, slouched in her saddle, frown- ing. He said, "We have to report this to Torchholder. I need you. Let's go." She reined her horse after his, either unwilling to dispute his statement or unable.
