The youth's  eyes remained  untouched by  the burn-scars,  dark-lashed and  dark browed eyes. Haunted, yes; long habituated to terrors. The commander indicated a chair and  the one-time hawkmask limped to it  and sat down, staring at him from those dark eyes. The nose was broken, scarred across the bridge; the fine mouth remained intact,  but twitched at times with an uncontrollable  tic that might be fear -  not enviable
