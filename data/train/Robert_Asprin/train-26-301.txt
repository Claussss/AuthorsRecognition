He drew two cups  of beer and unwrapped  the brineless sausage Ivalia  had given him. Sweetboy watched as if entranced, ears on the move. Ahdio had treacherously saved back the six-inch length of sausage about the thickness of Throde's staff. Now the big man gave  it to Sweetboy all at  once, as reward. Along with  a full Sweetboy immediately proved that he was a cat who loved beer, not an  alcoholic.
