She laughed  softly and  drew her  sword. She  had fought  four men  once in the arena. Now there were five. The result would be the same, but the game might  be more interesting. "Try to make it a good contest," she taunted them, beckoning. The nearest man rushed, stabbed at her belly. Chenaya sidestepped, kicked him in the groin as  her sword came  up to deflect  the blow another  man aimed at  her
