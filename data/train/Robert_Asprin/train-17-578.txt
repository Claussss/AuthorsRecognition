Kadakithis walked across the rich carpet  of his privatemost chamber and sat  on the edge of  the peacock spread  of his bed.  He gestured. 'Do  take that divan, Hanse, or those cushions as it pleases you.' Hanse nodded his  thanks. He sank  among the cushions,  curbing a grin  at their luxury.
