Neither was she. It was obvious that the keen eyes of Randal were reading her soul. She trembled, went up on tiptoes, and eyed the doors through which the others had gone. Still time to run. "Well, Merricat, how was your trip to the first plane?" said Randal gently as at last he came toward her. He knew her name! She could hardly believe it.
