He hefted his spear  and drew his sword.  Abruptly he laughed. 'Whatever  comes, 'twill not be dull!' Stepping high over the threshold, Cappen went forward. It felt like walking through any door, save that he entered a mild summer's day. After Jamie had  followed, he saw  that the vista  in the parchment  was that on which he  had just  turned his  back: a  veiled mass,  a pillar,  stars above  a nighted  city.
