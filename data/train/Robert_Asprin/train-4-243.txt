Chapter Fourteen:    "Some farewells are easier than others."   -P. MARLOWE    "So, where do you go from here?" Tananda asked.   She and Chumly were helping me pack. We had all agreed that having incurred the combined wrath of the King and Queen, it would be wisest to delay my departure as little as possible. Massha was off seeing to Gleep and Buttercup as well as saying her goodbyes to Badaxe.   "I don't really know," I admitted. "I was serious when I said I had accumulated enough wealth for a while. I'll probably hole up someplace and practice my magic for a while... maybe at that inn Aahz and I used to use as a home base."
