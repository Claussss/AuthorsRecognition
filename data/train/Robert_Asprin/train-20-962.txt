And of course  now he knew  who his greeter  was. It was  not possible, but then none of it was. "Whom shall I be to your eyes tonight, Son of Shadow?" Hanse  replied  with  surely  a  great  stroke  of  genius,  and  made  the most brilliantly diplomatic utterance of his life.
