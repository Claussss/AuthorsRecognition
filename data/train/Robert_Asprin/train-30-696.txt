He was  well-dressed, was Haught,  like themselves; wore  a russet tunic  and black  cloak, fine  boots, and  a sword  like a  gentleman. He brought a cup to the table and wine poured with a whisper into the gold cup.  He lifted it and drank. "Well?" said Mor-am. "Well, do you just walk in and serve yourself?"
