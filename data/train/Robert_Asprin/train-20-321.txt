For  a moment  the east  wind brought him the  rank, fuggy smell  of the Zoo  Gardens, then it  shifted and he felt on his face the cool breath of the sea. He halted just outside the Gate and  with a sigh reversed his cloak so  that its dull inner  lining concealed  his festival  clothes.
