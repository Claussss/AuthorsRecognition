At the door he stopped, blinking. He  looked back with a frown. Only now  did he remember the look Mignureal  had given him just  two hours ago, and  her strange words. They meant nothing  and connected to nothing.  'Oh, Hanse,' she had  said with a strange intensity  on her girlish face.  'Hanse - take the  crossed brown pot with you.' 'With me where?' But she had to flee, for her glowering mother was calling.
