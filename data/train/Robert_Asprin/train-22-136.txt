They ate  some dried  meat and  bread and  fruit and  then mounted  again. After watering the horses and themselves at the river, they rode at a canter for three more hours. And  then Smhee pulled  up on the  reins. He pointed  at the trees a quarter-mile inland. Beyond, rearing high, were the towering cliffs on the other side of the river.
