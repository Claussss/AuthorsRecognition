Hanse was meanwhile presenting the father of Mignureal and Jileel with a bag containing fine and far too valuable pearls which he had not stolen. He strongly suggested that Teretaff cause the pearls to be made into ear-drops for his several daughters, and "bury the rest under the floor or someplace." He left without TeretafTs knowing of the sack of gold pieces Hanse had secreted in his shop-home, for safekeeping.
