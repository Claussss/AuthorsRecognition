The phalanx marched straight from  the palace to the new-built  platform. Anyone shortsighted enough to sit in that path had to snatch up their things and  hurry out of the way. The route cleared as swiftly as water parting around a stone. Wess stood impulsively, about to sprint across the parade route to try once more
