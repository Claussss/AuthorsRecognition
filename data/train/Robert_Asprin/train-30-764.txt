Ischade  drifted wraithlike before him, shadow-black against the shadow of the brush  up-terrace, till she was lost in it. He struggled the harder, heard Stilcho laboring  behind like death upon his track. Lightning cracked. He  crested the slope  and Ischade was  there, at his  elbow, seizing on his arm. "Snakes," she reminded him. "Go softly."
