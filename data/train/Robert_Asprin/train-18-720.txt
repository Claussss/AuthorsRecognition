"Oh, damn!" She flung open the  casement and leaned out, her corset-hard  middle leant across the sill and the compression  of her ribs all but choking the  wind out of her as she set her palms on the rough stone. Cold wind stung her face and her exposed  front and  blew her  hair. Loose  ribbons hit  her in the face. "Go away!" she cried. "Hasn't my doorkeeper told you? Go away!"
