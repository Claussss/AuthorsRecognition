  Her words thudded at me like a padded hammer. I    had wanted very badly to hear that she was innocent and    that it had all been a mistake. I mean, she was so pretty,    so sweet, I would have bet my life that she was innocent,    yet here she was openly admitting her guilt to me.   "But why?" I managed at last. "I mean, how did you    get involved in swindling people to begin with?"   Her soft shoulders rose and fell in a helpless shrug.
