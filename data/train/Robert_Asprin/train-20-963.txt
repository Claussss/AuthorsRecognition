"The thrice-beauteous face  of the Lady  Eshi from the  statue in the  temple of Eshi Radiant," he said- And She was, smiling delightedly, ever so pleased. She embraced him with  warmth and Hanse nearly collapsed. Her hand clasping  his with  warmth, she  led him  into that  ruined and murkily shadowed once-luxury manse  ...  and it  was  again! Everywhere  candles  sprang into  lambence,  with  constant  flashes  and  continuing  unnatural brightness.
