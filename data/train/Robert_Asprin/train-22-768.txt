The bracelet  remained cold  in his  palm and  when it  moved he jerked his hand instinctively. Fast as  he was he  was only human,  not a striking  serpent; the bracelet, suddenly become a living snake, drove its fangs into the meaty part of his hand that  was the inner  part of his  thumb.
