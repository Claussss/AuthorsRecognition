studying Sanctuary. Sanctuary is a seaport, and  its name goes back to  a time when it provided  the only armed haven along an important  caravan route. But the long war  ended, the caravans  abandoned that  route for  a shorter  one, and  Sanctuary declined  in status -  but not  in population,  because for  every honest  person who left to pursue a normal life elsewhere, a rogue drifted in to pursue his normal life.
