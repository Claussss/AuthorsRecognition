Thus Cappen was acutely  aware of the smells of beer - old in the rushes underfoot, fresh where a trio of men  had settled down to guzzle - and of spitted meat, wafting from the kitchen. One-Thumb  approached, a  shadowy hulk  save for  highlights on  his bald  pate. 'Sit,' he grunted. 'Eat. Drink.' He carried a great tankard and a plate  bearing a slab of roast beef on bread.
