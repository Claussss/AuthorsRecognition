Someone knocked at the  outside door;  he heard  that door  groan open,  heard the burr of quiet voices as his  own guards passed  someone through. The  matter reached his  door then, a second, louder rap. 'Mor-am, sir.' The door opened,  and his guard let in  the one he had sent  for, this wreckage of a man. Handsome once  ... at least they said that he  had been.
