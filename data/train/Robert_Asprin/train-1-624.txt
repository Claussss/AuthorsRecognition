"Klikitagh!" she whispered, dropping on one knee beside the ... corpse? It was indeed. No pulse was to be felt. A rime of frost had formed upon its hair, its beard, its hands , . . Slowly she straightened, gazing down in wonder. "So your journey ended here, in Sanctuary," she murmured. "Well, death was what you most desired. And ..." A thought occurred, as wonderful as it was terrifying. "If I'm to believe what Enas Yorl asserts-and who but him should I believe in such a matter?-it follows that the worst crime in the history of the world has been committed. It was yours, my Klikitagh. And yours alone."
