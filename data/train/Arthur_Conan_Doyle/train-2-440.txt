The Baron looked at his watch and gave a guttural exclamation of
disappointment. “Well, I really can wait no longer. You can imagine that things are
moving at present in Carlton Terrace and that we have all to be at our
posts. I had hoped to be able to bring news of your great coup. Did
Altamont name no hour?” Von Bork pushed over a telegram. Will come without fail to-night and bring new sparking plugs.—ALTAMONT.
