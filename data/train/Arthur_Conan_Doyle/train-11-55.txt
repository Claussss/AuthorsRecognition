“Then he may have left the house as well.” “I believe that he did.” “He has abandoned me to my fate. Etienne, Etienne, they are coming!” Afar off I heard those fateful steps and the jingle of distant keys.
What were they coming for now, since there were no other prisoners to
drag to judgment? It could only be to carry out the sentence upon my
darling. I stood between her and the door, with the strength of a lion in my
limbs. I would tear the house down before they should touch her.
