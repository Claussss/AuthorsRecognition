“Another glass, Watson!” said Mr. Sherlock Holmes as he extended the
bottle of Imperial Tokay. The thickset chauffeur, who had seated himself by the table, pushed
forward his glass with some eagerness. “It is a good wine, Holmes.” “A remarkable wine, Watson. Our friend upon the sofa has assured me
that it is from Franz Josef’s special cellar at the Schoenbrunn Palace.
Might I trouble you to open the window, for chloroform vapour does not
help the palate.”
