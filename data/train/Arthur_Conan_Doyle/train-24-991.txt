“A cow!” said Aylward. “Say rather ten acres and a homestead on the
banks of Avon.” “Say you so? Then, by our Lady! here is for yonder one in the red
jerkin!” He was about to push recklessly forward into the open, when Sir Nigel
himself darted in front of him, with his hand upon his breast. “Back!” said he. “Our time is not yet come, and we must lie here until
evening. Throw off your jacks and headpieces, least their eyes catch the
shine, and tether the horses among the rocks.”
