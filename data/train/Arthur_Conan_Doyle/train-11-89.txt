Marshal Lannes rose from his knees and shook me by the hand. “You are a brave man, sir,” said he. “I have a present to make to you,”
 he added, handing me a very tiny glass tube. “It has been specially
prepared by Dr. Fardet. At the supreme moment you have but to put it to
your lips and you will be dead in an instant.” This was a cheerful beginning. I will confess to you, my friends, that a
cold chill passed up my back and my hair rose upon my head.
