      “The brute!” cried Holmes. “Here, Lestrade, your brandy-bottle!
      Put her in the chair! She has fainted from ill-usage and
      exhaustion.”       She opened her eyes again.       “Is he safe?” she asked. “Has he escaped?”       “He cannot escape us, madam.”       “No, no, I did not mean my husband. Sir Henry? Is he safe?”       “Yes.”       “And the hound?”       “It is dead.”       She gave a long sigh of satisfaction.
