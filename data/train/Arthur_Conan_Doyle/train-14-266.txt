      “There it is!” I cried.       “No, no, sir, it is nothing—nothing at all!” the butler broke in;
      “I assure you, sir—”       “Move your light across the window, Watson!” cried the baronet.
      “See, the other moves also! Now, you rascal, do you deny that it
      is a signal? Come, speak up! Who is your confederate out yonder,
      and what is this conspiracy that is going on?”       The man’s face became openly defiant. “It is my business, and not
      yours. I will not tell.”
