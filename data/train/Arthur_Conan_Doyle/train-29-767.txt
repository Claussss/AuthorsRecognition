
    “‘JEPHRO RUCASTLE.’ 
“That is the letter which I have just received, Mr. Holmes, and my mind
is made up that I will accept it. I thought, however, that before
taking the final step I should like to submit the whole matter to your
consideration.” “Well, Miss Hunter, if your mind is made up, that settles the
question,” said Holmes, smiling. “But you would not advise me to refuse?” “I confess that it is not the situation which I should like to see a
sister of mine apply for.”
