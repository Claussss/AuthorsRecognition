The same terrible silence fell upon the assembly, and the same twelve
pairs of merciless eyes were bent upon my face. Again it was the
grey-beard who spoke. “He is out of his order. There are two names upon our list before him.” “He escaped from our hands and burst into the room.” “Let him await his turn. Take him down to the wooden cell.” “If he resist us, your Excellency?” “Bury your knives in his body. The tribunal will uphold you. Remove him
until we have dealt with the others.”
