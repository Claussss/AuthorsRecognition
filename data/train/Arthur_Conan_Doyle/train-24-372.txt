“Nay, I cannot say,” said Alleyne, with a wild throb of sudden hope in
his heart. “I have thought so, and yet I cannot name the man. Indeed, save myself,
and Walter Ford, and you, who are half a clerk, and Father Christopher
of the Priory, and Bertrand the page, who is there whom she sees?” “I cannot tell,” quoth Alleyne shortly; and the two squires rode on
again, each intent upon his own thoughts.
