      The Duke’s beard had turned more aggressively red than ever
      against his ghastly white face.       “Where is he?” he gasped.       “He is, or was last night, at the Fighting Cock Inn, about two
      miles from your park gate.”       The Duke fell back in his chair.       “And whom do you accuse?”       Sherlock Holmes’s answer was an astounding one. He stepped
      swiftly forward and touched the Duke upon the shoulder.
