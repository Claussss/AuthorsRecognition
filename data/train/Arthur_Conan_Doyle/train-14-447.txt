      “Are you armed, Lestrade?”       The little detective smiled. “As long as I have my trousers I
      have a hip-pocket, and as long as I have my hip-pocket I have
      something in it.”       “Good! My friend and I are also ready for emergencies.”       “You’re mighty close about this affair, Mr. Holmes. What’s the
      game now?”       “A waiting game.”       “My word, it does not seem a very cheerful place,” said the
