“They are the enemy.” “Where is Grouchy?” “He is behind. They have passed him.” “Then how can I go back? If I go forward I may see him yet. I must obey
my orders and find him whereever he is.” The man reflected for an instant. “Quick! quick!” he cried, seizing my bridle. “Do what I say and you may
yet escape. They have not observed you yet. Come with me and I will hide
you until they pass.” Behind his house there was a low stable, and into this he thrust
Violette. Then he half led and half dragged me into the kitchen of the
inn. It was a bare, brick-floored room. A stout, red-faced woman was
cooking cutlets at the fire.
