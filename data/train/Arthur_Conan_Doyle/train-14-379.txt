      “Hush!” he whispered. “Hush!”       The cry had been loud on account of its vehemence, but it had
      pealed out from somewhere far off on the shadowy plain. Now it
      burst upon our ears, nearer, louder, more urgent than before.       “Where is it?” Holmes whispered; and I knew from the thrill of
