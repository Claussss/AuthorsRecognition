“What do you wish me to do in exchange?” “If you would die easy I ask you to give me truthful answers to the
questions which I ask.” A sudden thought flashed through my mind. “You wish to kill me,” said I; “it cannot matter to you how I die. If
I answer your questions, will you let me choose the manner of my own
death?” “Yes, I will,” said he, “so long as it is before midnight to-night.” “Swear it!” I cried.
