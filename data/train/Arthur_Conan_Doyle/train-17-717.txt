He went alone, at his own request, for he explained that any
unnecessary footmarks might make his task more difficult. For an hour
or more he was at work, returning at last with his feet heavy with snow
and his features as inscrutable as ever. “I think that I have seen now all that there is to see, Mr. Holder,”
said he; “I can serve you best by returning to my rooms.” “But the gems, Mr. Holmes. Where are they?”
