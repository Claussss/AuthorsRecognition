‘Alongside,’ replied Saxon; ‘my friend joins with me in hoping that you,
Captain Pogram, will have a pleasant and profitable voyage.’ ‘I am cursedly beholden to him,’ said the captain, with a flourish of
his three-cornered hat. ‘Also that you will reach Barbadoes in safety.’ ‘Little doubt of that!’ quoth the captain. ‘And that you will dispose of your wares in a manner which will repay
you for your charity and humanity.’
