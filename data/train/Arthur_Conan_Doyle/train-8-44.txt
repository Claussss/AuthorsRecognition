"Very interesting--very interesting, indeed!" said Holmes, peering at
this sinister relic.  "Anything more?" In silence Baynes led the way to the sink and held forward his candle.
The limbs and body of some large, white bird, torn savagely to pieces
with the feathers still on, were littered all over it.  Holmes pointed
to the wattles on the severed head. "A white cock," said he.  "Most interesting!  It is really a very
curious case."
