‘Illegitimate son,’ I remarked. ‘That may or may not be. There are those who say that Lucy Walters was
a lawful wife. Bastard or no, he holds the sound principles of the true
Church, and he is beloved by the people. Let him appear in the West, and
soldiers will rise up like the flowers in the spring time.’ He paused, and led me away to the farther end of the yard, for the
workmen had begun to arrive and to cluster round the dipping trough.
