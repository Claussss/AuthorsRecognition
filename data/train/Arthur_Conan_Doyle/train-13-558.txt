      Holmes looked at Blessington in his questioning way and shook his
      head.       “I cannot possibly advise you if you try to deceive me,” said he.       “But I have told you everything.”       Holmes turned on his heel with a gesture of disgust. “Good-night,
      Dr. Trevelyan,” said he.       “And no advice for me?” cried Blessington, in a breaking voice.       “My advice to you, sir, is to speak the truth.”
