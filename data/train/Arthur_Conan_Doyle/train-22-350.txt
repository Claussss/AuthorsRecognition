“Get what?” McMurdo whispered to his neighbour. “The business end of a buckshot cartridge!” cried the man with a loud
laugh. “What think you of our ways, Brother?” McMurdo's criminal soul seemed to have already absorbed the spirit of
the vile association of which he was now a member. “I like it well,”
 said he. “'Tis a proper place for a lad of mettle.” Several of those who sat around heard his words and applauded them.
