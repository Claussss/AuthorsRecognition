  "Stewart ran after us for more than a mile, and I could not
  help laughing as I looked back at last and saw him on a hill,
  holding his hand to his side, and nearly burst with running.   "Ballantrae turned to me with a face all wrinkled up, and his
  teeth all showing in his mouth.... He said no word, but his
  whole appearance was a kind of dreadful question.   "Look at him, if you doubt; look at him, grinning and gulping,
  a detected thief.
