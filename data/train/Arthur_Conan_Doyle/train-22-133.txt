It was spoken with fervour and feeling, and yet Inspector MacDonald
could not dismiss the subject. “You are aware,” said he, “that the dead man's wedding ring has been
taken from his finger?” “So it appears,” said Barker. “What do you mean by 'appears'? You know it as a fact.” The man seemed confused and undecided. “When I said 'appears' I meant
that it was conceivable that he had himself taken off the ring.”
