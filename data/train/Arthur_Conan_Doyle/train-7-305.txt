But the Colonel looked up again, and again he cried out in his agitation
and surprise. “I never saw anything plainer in my life,” he groaned. “It is on
the point of rock on our right front,--poor old Stuart with my red
cummerbund round his head just the same as we left him.” The ladies had followed the direction of the Colonel's frightened gaze,
and in an instant they were all as amazed as he. [Illustration: On this pinnacle stood a motionless figure p242]
