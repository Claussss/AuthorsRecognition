“How can you sing such things?” he cried. “You, too, an old man who
should be an example to others.” The wayfarers all gazed in the utmost astonishment at the interruption. “By the holy Dicon of Hampole! our silent clerk has found his tongue,”
 said one of the woodmen. “What is amiss with the song then? How has it
offended your babyship?” “A milder and better mannered song hath never been heard within these
walls,” cried another. “What sort of talk is this for a public inn?”
