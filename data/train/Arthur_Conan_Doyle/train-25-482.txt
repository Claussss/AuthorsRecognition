"No use, young fellah," said Lord John, with his finger on the chart.
"Seventeen from the right and second from the left.  This is the cave
sure enough." I looked at the mark to which his finger pointed, and I gave a sudden
cry of joy. "I believe I have it!  Follow me!  Follow me!" I hurried back along the way we had come, my torch in my hand.  "Here,"
said I, pointing to some matches upon the ground, "is where we lit up."
