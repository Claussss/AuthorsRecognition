He smiled, and said that he could not tell. "But surely, my friend," said I, "you can tell me what is ready?" "Even that I cannot do," he answered; "but I doubt not that the landlord
can inform us." On this he rang the bell, and a fellow answered, to whom
I put the same question. "What would you have?" he asked. I thought of the master, and I ordered a cold leg of pork to be washed
down with tea and beer.
