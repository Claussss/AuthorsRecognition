As he swung the door back I slipped behind it, but as luck would have
it he shut it again behind him, and there we were left standing face to
face. Never have I seen a more astonished young man. “A French officer!” he gasped. “Hush!” said I, “hush! Not a word above a whisper.” I had drawn my sword. “I am not a combatant,” he said; “I am a doctor. Why do you threaten me
with your sword? I am not armed.”
