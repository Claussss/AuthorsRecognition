“Why, where's Hector?” asked Mr. McIntyre, blinking round from side to
side. “He's not come, father. How could you expect him to come on such a night
as this? Why, there must be two feet of snow in the glebe field.” “Not come, eh?” croaked the old man, throwing himself down upon the
sofa. “Well, well, it only wants him and his father to throw us over,
and the thing will be complete.” “How can you even hint at such a thing, father?” cried Laura
indignantly. “They have been as true as steel. What would they think if
they heard you.”
