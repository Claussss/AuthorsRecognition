“You are certainly joking, Holmes.” “Not in the least. Is it possible that even now, when I give you these
results, you are unable to see how they are attained?” “I have no doubt that I am very stupid, but I must confess that I am
unable to follow you. For example, how did you deduce that this man was
intellectual?” For answer Holmes clapped the hat upon his head. It came right over the
forehead and settled upon the bridge of his nose. “It is a question of
cubic capacity,” said he; “a man with so large a brain must have
something in it.”
