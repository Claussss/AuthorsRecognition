The Marshal's revolver fell from his hand, and he dropped his arms to
his sides, a beaten man. "Maybe some of you gentlemen can tell me what this means," said he,
looking helplessly at the committee. Peter Stulpnagel took a step forward. "I'll tell you all about it," said he. "You seem to be the only person who knows anything." "I AM the only person who knows anything.  I should have warned these
gentlemen; but, as they would not listen to me, I have allowed them to
learn by experience.  What you have done with your electricity is that
you have increased this man's vitality until he can defy death for
centuries."
