‘You see, friends,’ said Monmouth, turning lack-lustre eyes from one
leader to another, ‘Providence would seem to be against us. Some new
mishap is ever at our heels.’ ‘Not Providence, your Majesty, but our own negligence,’ cried Saxon
boldly. ‘Had we advanced on Bristol last night, we might have been on
the right side of the ramparts by now.’ ‘But we had no thought that the enemy’s foot was so near!’ exclaimed
Wade.
