      “Let me see,” said Holmes, seating himself on Staunton’s bed.
      “You are the day porter, are you not?”       “Yes, sir, I go off duty at eleven.”       “The night porter saw nothing, I suppose?”       “No, sir, one theatre party came in late. No one else.”       “Were you on duty all day yesterday?”       “Yes, sir.”       “Did you take any messages to Mr. Staunton?”       “Yes, sir, one telegram.”
