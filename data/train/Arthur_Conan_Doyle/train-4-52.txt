‘Heart o’ grace!’ ejaculated Reuben in loose lipped astonishment. ‘The
murdering villains!’ ‘I would to the Lord that King’s ship would snap them up!’ cried I
savagely, for the attack was so unprovoked that it stirred my bile.
‘What could the rogues have meant? They are surely drunk or mad!’ ‘Pull at the anchor, man, pull at the anchor!’ my companion shouted,
springing up from the seat. ‘I understand it! Pull at the anchor!’
