“I thought it was father,” said she with a pleasing little touch of a
German accent. “Did you come to see him? He is down town. I expect him
back every minute.” McMurdo continued to gaze at her in open admiration until her eyes
dropped in confusion before this masterful visitor. “No, miss,” he said at last, “I'm in no hurry to see him. But your house
was recommended to me for board. I thought it might suit me--and now I
know it will.”
