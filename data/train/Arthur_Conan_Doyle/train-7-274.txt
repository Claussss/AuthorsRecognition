“And the others?” The dragoman shrugged his shoulders. “Well,” said the Colonel, “if only one of us is to escape, I think you
fellows will agree with me that it ought to be Belmont, since he is the
married man.” “Yes, yes, let it be Monsieur Belmont,” cried Fardet. “I think so also,” said Stephens. But the Irishman would not hear of it. “No, no, share and share alike,” he cried. “All sink or all swim, and
