The Colonel could not follow it all, “That is excellent,” said he. “But
what are we to do about the three ladies?” The black soldier shrugged his shoulders. “Mefeesh!” said he. “One of them is old, and in any case there are
plenty more women if we get back to Egypt. These will not come to any
hurt, but they will be placed in the harem of the Khalija.” “What you say is nonsense,” said the Colonel, sternly. “We shall take
our women with us, or we shall not go at all.”
