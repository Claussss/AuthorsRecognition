“It is over the fifth!” cried a Gascon loudly, and a comrade came
running with waving arms to say that the bolt had pitched eight paces
beyond the mark of the five hundred. “Which weapon hath the vantage now?” cried the Brabanter, strutting
proudly about with shouldered arbalest, amid the applause of his
companions. “You can overshoot me,” said Johnston gently. “Or any other man who ever bent a long-bow,” cried his victorious
adversary.
