      “It is Selden, the man who escaped from Princetown.”       Stapleton turned a ghastly face upon us, but by a supreme effort
      he had overcome his amazement and his disappointment. He looked
      sharply from Holmes to me. “Dear me! What a very shocking affair!
      How did he die?”       “He appears to have broken his neck by falling over these rocks.
