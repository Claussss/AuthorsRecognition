"You knew it, and never told us!" "Don't be harsh with me, Mr. Stanniford, sir! Make allowance for a man
who has had a hard part to play." "My head is swimming round. I cannot grasp it!" He staggered up, and
helped himself from the brandy bottle. "These letters to my mother and
to myself--were they forgeries?" "No, sir; your father wrote them and addressed them, and left them in my
keeping to be posted. I have followed his instructions to the very
letter in all things. He was my master, and I have obeyed him."
