“This is Tilford,” said Chandos. “Yonder is the house of the Lorings.” The King's expectations had been aroused and his face showed his
disappointment. “Is this the sport that you have promised us, Sir John? How can you make
good your words?” “I will make them good, my liege.” “Where then is the sport?” On the high crown of the bridge a rider in armor was seated, lance in
hand, upon a great yellow steed. Chandos touched the King's arm and
pointed. “That is the sport,” said he.
