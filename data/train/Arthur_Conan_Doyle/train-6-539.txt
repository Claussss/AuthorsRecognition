“Give yourself up, fair sir!” said he. “Never to fishermen and to archers! I am a gentleman of coat-armor. Kill
me!” “I also am a gentleman of coat-armor. I promise you quarter.” “Then, sir, I surrender myself to you.” The dagger tinkled down upon the deck. Seamen and archers ran forward,
to find Nigel half senseless upon his face. They drew him off, and a few
deft blows struck off the helmet of his enemy. A head, sharp-featured,
freckled and foxy-red, disclosed itself beneath it. Nigel raised himself
on his elbow for an instant.
