[Illustration: Fell suddenly upon his face p97] “What's that, Cochrane?” asked Belmont. “Why is he making an exhibition
of himself?” “As far as I can understand, it is all up with us,” the Colonel
answered. “But this is absurd,” cried the Frenchman, excitedly; “why should these
people wish any harm to me? I have never injured them. On the other
hand, I have always been their friend. If I could but speak to them, I
would make them comprehend. Hola, dragoman, Mansoor!”
