"What says the Spanish poet Calderon?" said I. "I doubt not that you
have read the couplet which has been thus Englished:     'Oh, maiden, may I humbly pray
    That I may help you on your way.'" The woman blushed, but said nothing. "Where," I asked, "are the Romany chals and the Romany chis?" She turned her head away and was silent. "Though I am a gorgio," said I, "I know something of the Romany lil,"
