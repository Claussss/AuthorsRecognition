He took a slip of paper from the side pocket of his coat, but, instead
of handing it to the young lady, he remained staring at it with the
utmost astonishment upon his face. “Well, I never!” he exclaimed. “Look here, Robert; what do you call
this?” “Hold it to the light. Why, it's a fifty-pound Bank of England note.
Nothing remarkable about it that I can see.” “On the contrary. It's the queerest thing that ever happened to me. I
can't make head or tail of it.”
