"Come into the house," said Holmes, in his crisp, firm way. "Yes, do!" pleaded Thaddeus Sholto.  "I really do not feel equal to
giving directions." We all followed him into the housekeeper's room, which stood upon the
left-hand side of the passage.  The old woman was pacing up and down
with a scared look and restless picking fingers, but the sight of Miss
Morstan appeared to have a soothing effect upon her.
