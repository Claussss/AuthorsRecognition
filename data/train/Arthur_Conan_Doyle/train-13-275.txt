      Hall Pycroft been doing at Mawson’s? What should we do, Mr.
      Holmes? Tell me what to do.”       “We must wire to Mawson’s.”       “They shut at twelve on Saturdays.”       “Never mind. There may be some door-keeper or attendant—”       “Ah yes, they keep a permanent guard there on account of the
