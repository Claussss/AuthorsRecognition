"You would murder me?" Bellingham had half risen, and his face was the colour of putty. "And for what?" "To stop your mischief.  One minute has gone." "But what have I done?" "I know and you know." "This is mere bullying." "Two minutes are gone." "But you must give reasons.  You are a madman--a dangerous madman.  Why
should I destroy my own property?  It is a valuable mummy." "You must cut it up, and you must burn it."
