‘What brutes!’ my companion ejaculated; ‘what shall we do, Micah?’ ‘They have clearly been laid on Saxon’s trail,’ said I. ‘We must follow
them up, or they will be too many for him. Can you hear anything of our
pursuers?’ ‘They have given up the chase, then, and let the dogs loose as a last
resource. Doubtless the creatures are trained to return to the town. But
we must push on, Reuben, if we are to help our companion.’
