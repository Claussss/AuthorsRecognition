“Colonel Etienne Gerard,” said he, “I have always heard that you are a
very gallant and enterprising officer.” It was not for me to confirm such a report, and yet it would be folly to
deny it, so I clinked my spurs together and saluted. “You are also an excellent rider.” I admitted it. “And the best swordsman in the six brigades of light cavalry.” Massena was famous for the accuracy of his information.
