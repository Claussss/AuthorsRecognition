“Four hundred paces and a score,” cried Black Simon. “I' faith, it is a
very long flight. Yet wood and steel may do more than flesh and blood.” The Brabanter stepped forward with a smile of conscious triumph, and
loosed the cord of his weapon. A shout burst from his comrades as they
watched the swift and lofty flight of the heavy bolt. “Over the fourth!” groaned Aylward. “By my hilt! I think that it is well
up to the fifth.”
