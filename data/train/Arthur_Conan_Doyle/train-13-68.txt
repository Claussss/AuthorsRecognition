      “You have a few sheep in the paddock,” he said. “Who attends to
      them?”       “I do, sir.”       “Have you noticed anything amiss with them of late?”       “Well, sir, not of much account; but three of them have gone
      lame, sir.”       I could see that Holmes was extremely pleased, for he chuckled
      and rubbed his hands together.       “A long shot, Watson; a very long shot,” said he, pinching my
      arm. “Gregory, let me recommend to your attention this singular
      epidemic among the sheep. Drive on, coachman!”
