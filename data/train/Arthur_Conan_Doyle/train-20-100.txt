      “Well, Mr. Holmes, have you proved us to be wrong yet? Have you
      found your tramp?” he cried.       “I have formed no conclusion whatever,” my companion answered.       “But we formed ours yesterday, and now it proves to be correct,
      so you must acknowledge that we have been a little in front of
      you this time, Mr. Holmes.”       “You certainly have the air of something unusual having
      occurred,” said Holmes.
