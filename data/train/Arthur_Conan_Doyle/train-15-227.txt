"But why to me?" I asked, in bewilderment. "Why of all the human race to
me?" "Because you were in the fit mental state to receive the impression.
Because you chanced to own the mirror which gave the impression." "The mirror! You think, then, that it was Mary's mirror--that it stood
in the room where the deed was done?" "I am convinced that it was Mary's mirror. She had been Queen of France.
Her personal property would be stamped with the Royal arms. What you
took to be three spear-heads were really the lilies of France."
