      “What did he get?”       “The man lived and he got off with a year. I have no doubt he is
      out now, but he has not dared to show his nose here. We have a
      cousin of his here, and I daresay he could tell you where he is.”       “No, no,” cried Holmes, “not a word to the cousin—not a word, I

      with it, the more important it seems to grow. When you referred
