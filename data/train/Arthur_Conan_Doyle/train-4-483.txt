‘Is it so very large, then?’ she cried, with pretty wonder. ‘But new
louses are building in Taunton, outside the old walls, and beyond
Shuttern, and some even at the other side of the river. Perhaps in time
it may be as large.’ ‘If all the folks in Taunton were to be added to London,’ said Sir
Gervas, ‘no one there would observe that there had been any increase.’ ‘Nay, there you are laughing at me. That is against all reason,’ cried
the country maiden.
