      “I can hardly put my foot to the ground.”       “Don’t put it to the ground.”       “But I can’t walk.”       “Well, then hop.”       Mr. Reuben Hayes’s manner was far from gracious, but Holmes took
      it with admirable good-humour.       “Look here, my man,” said he. “This is really rather an awkward
      fix for me. I don’t mind how I get on.”       “Neither do I,” said the morose landlord.
