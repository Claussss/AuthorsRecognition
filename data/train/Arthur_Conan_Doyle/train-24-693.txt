“What then would you do, John?” asked several. “There are many things which might be done,” said the forester
thoughtfully. “Methinks that I would begin by breaking my spear.” “So they all strive to do.” “Nay, but not upon another man's shield. I would break it over my own
knee.” “And what the better for that, old beef and bones?” asked Black Simon. “So I would turn what is but a lady's bodkin of a weapon into a very
handsome club.”
