      “He handed me the very paper which I have here, Watson, and this
      is the strange catechism to which each Musgrave had to submit
      when he came to man’s estate. I will read you the questions and
      answers as they stand.       “‘Whose was it?’       “‘His who is gone.’       “‘Who shall have it?’       “‘He who will come.’       “‘Where was the sun?’       “‘Over the oak.’       “‘Where was the shadow?’
