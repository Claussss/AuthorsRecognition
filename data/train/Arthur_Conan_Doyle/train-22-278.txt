“Frighten!” McMurdo was on fire in a moment. “Ah, yes, my friend! You need not be ashamed to be frightened of him. It
is Teddy Baldwin.” “And who the devil is he?” “He is a boss of Scowrers.” “Scowrers! I've heard of them before. It's Scowrers here and Scowrers
there, and always in a whisper! What are you all afraid of? Who are the
Scowrers?” The boarding-house keeper instinctively sank his voice, as everyone did
who talked about that terrible society. “The Scowrers,” said he, “are
the Eminent Order of Freemen!”
