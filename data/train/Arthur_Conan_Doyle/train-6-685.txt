“Mon Dieu!” he cried. “Who are you and whence come you at this hour of
the night? Is this the way to break into our royal presence?” Simon approached up one side of the table and Aylward up the other. When
they were close to the King, the man-at-arms plucked a torch from its
socket and held it to his own face. The King staggered back with a cry,
as he gazed at that grim visage. “Le diable noir!” he cried. “Simon, the Englishman! What make you here?”
