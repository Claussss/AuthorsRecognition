‘Why, this one is riddled like a sieve,’ he cried. ‘What do you load
your petronels with, good Master Clarke?’ ‘With two leaden slugs.’ ‘Yet two leaden slugs have made a score of holes at the least! And of
all things in this world, here is the neck of a bottle stuck in the
brute’s hide!’ ‘Good heavens!’ I exclaimed. ‘I remember. My dear mother packed a bottle
of Daffy’s elixir in the barrel of my pistol.’
