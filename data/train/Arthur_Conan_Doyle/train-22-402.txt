So determined was the appearance of the captain that both McMurdo and
his boss were forced to accept the situation. The latter managed to have
a few whispered words with the prisoner before they parted. “What about--” he jerked his thumb upward to signify the coining plant. “All right,” whispered McMurdo, who had devised a safe hiding place
under the floor. “I'll bid you good-bye,” said the Boss, shaking hands. “I'll see Reilly
the lawyer and take the defense upon myself. Take my word for it that
they won't be able to hold you.”
