“Give yourself time, good Master Wintersole, give yourself time!” said
Chandos in a soothing voice. “The papers!” gasped the little man. “Oh, my Lord Chandos, the papers--” “What of the papers, my worthy sir?” “I swear by our good patron Saint Leonard, it is no fault of mine! I
had locked them in my coffer. But the lock was forced and the coffer
rifled.” A shadow of anger passed over the soldier's keen face.
