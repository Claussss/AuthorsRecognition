‘“Fifth. Me to keep her in due repair, dry-dock her at intervals,
and see that she hath her allowance of coats of paint, streamers, and
bunting, as befits a saucy pleasure boat.”’ ‘“Sixth. Me to take no other craft in tow, and if any be now attached,
to cut their hawsers.”’ ‘“Seventh. Me to revictual her day by day.”’ ‘“Eighth. Should she chance to spring a leak, or be blown on her beam
ends by the winds of misfortune, to stand by her and see her pumped out
or righted.”’
