‘This exceedeth the limitations of your privileges,’ said Saxon, drawing
a pistol from his belt and cocking it. ‘If you say another word to
seduce these people from their allegiance, I fire.’ ‘Hope not to benefit Monmouth,’ cried the young officer, disregarding
the threat, and still addressing his words to the peasants. ‘The whole
royal army is drawing round him and--’ ‘Have a care!’ shouted our leader, in a deep harsh voice.
