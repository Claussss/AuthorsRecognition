“How is it with you, lady?” he asked at last, in a tremulous voice. Her eyes remained fixed intently upon the wall, and there was a long
pause ere she answered him. Her voice, too, which had been so clear
and ringing, was now low and muffled as that of one who speaks from a
distance. “All is very well with me, Bertrand,” said she. “The blessed hour of
sight has come round to me again.” “I could see it come! I could see it come!” he exclaimed, passing his
fingers through his hair with the same perplexed expression as before.
