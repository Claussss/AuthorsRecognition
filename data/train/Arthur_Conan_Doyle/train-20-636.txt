      “One moment,” said Holmes. “Where does this path lead to?”       “To the road.”       “How long is it?”       “A hundred yards or so.”       “At the point where the path passes through the gate, you could
      surely pick up the tracks?”       “Unfortunately, the path was tiled at that point.”       “Well, on the road itself?”       “No, it was all trodden into mire.”       “Tut-tut! Well, then, these tracks upon the grass, were they
      coming or going?”
