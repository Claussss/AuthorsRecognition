“The Coroner: Did your father make any statement to you before he died? “Witness: He mumbled a few words, but I could only catch some allusion
to a rat. “The Coroner: What did you understand by that? “Witness: It conveyed no meaning to me. I thought that he was
delirious. “The Coroner: What was the point upon which you and your father had
this final quarrel? “Witness: I should prefer not to answer.
