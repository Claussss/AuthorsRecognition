‘Good Heavens, Reuben!’ I cried in amazement, ‘what brings you all this
way from home?’ ‘The very same cause which brings you, Micah, and also Don Decimo Saxon,
late of the Solent, whom methinks I see in the shadow behind you. How
fares it, oh illustrious one?’ ‘It is you, then, young cock of the woods!’ growled Saxon, in no very
overjoyed voice. ‘No less a person,’ said Reuben. ‘And now, my gay cavalieros, round with
your horses and trot on your way, for there is no time to be lost. We
ought all to be at Taunton to-morrow.’
