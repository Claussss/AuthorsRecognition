“Then we shall still hold Gascony and Guienne?” cried Sir Nigel. The lady shook her head. “French land, French blood, French speech,” she
answered. “They are French, and France shall have them.” “But not Bordeaux?” cried Sir Nigel excitedly. “Bordeaux also is for France.” “But Calais?” “Calais too.” “Woe worth me then, and ill hail to these evil words! If Bordeaux and
Calais be gone, then what is left for England?”
