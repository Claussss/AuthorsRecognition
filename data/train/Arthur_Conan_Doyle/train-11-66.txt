“What is this?” he asked. “How come you to have lost your ear?” I shook off my weakness, and pressing my handkerchief to my wound I rose
from my couch, the debonair colonel of Hussars. “My injury, sir, is nothing. With your permission we will not allude to
a matter so trifling and so personal.” But Lucia had burst through from her cell and was pouring out the whole
story while she clasped Lorenzo's arm.
