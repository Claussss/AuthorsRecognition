“Grapple my ship with that,” he cried, pointing to the crippled
Spaniard, “for I would have possession of her!” But already the breeze had carried them past it, and a dozen Spanish
ships were bearing down full upon them. “We cannot win back to her, lest we show our flank to these others,”
 said the shipman. “Let her go her way!” cried the knights. “You shall have better than
her.” “By Saint George! you speak the truth,” said the King, “for she is ours
when we have time to take her. These also seem very worthy ships which
are drawing up to us, and I pray you, master-shipman, that you will have
a tilt with the nearest.”
