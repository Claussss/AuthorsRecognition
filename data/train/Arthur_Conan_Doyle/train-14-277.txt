      “What do they call this sound?” he asked.       “Who?”       “The folk on the countryside.”       “Oh, they are ignorant people. Why should you mind what they call
      it?”       “Tell me, Watson. What do they say of it?”       I hesitated but could not escape the question.       “They say it is the cry of the Hound of the Baskervilles.”       He groaned and was silent for a few moments.       “A hound it was,” he said at last, “but it seemed to come from
      miles away, over yonder, I think.”
