      “Surely that must be the same Johnson whom I used to know,” said
      Holmes to the porter. “A lawyer, is he not, grey-headed, and
      walks with a limp?”       “No, sir, this is Mr. Johnson, the coal-owner, a very active
      gentleman, not older than yourself.”       “Surely you are mistaken about his trade?”       “No, sir! he has used this hotel for many years, and he is very
      well known to us.”
