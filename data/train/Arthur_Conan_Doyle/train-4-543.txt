‘If the West can give eight thousand, how many can all the counties of
England afford?’ I asked. ‘Is not that the fairer way to look at it?’ ‘Monmouth’s popularity lies mostly in the West,’ Saxon answered. ‘It
was the memory of that which prompted him to raise his standard in these
counties.’ ‘His standards, rather,’ quoth Reuben. ‘Why, it looks as though they had
hung their linen up to dry all down the line.’
