      “‘And under,’ he cried. ‘You have omitted the “and under.”’       “I had thought that it meant that we were to dig, but now, of
      course, I saw at once that I was wrong. ‘There is a cellar under
      this then?’ I cried.       “‘Yes, and as old as the house. Down here, through this door.’       “We went down a winding stone stair, and my companion, striking a
      match, lit a large lantern which stood on a barrel in the corner.
      In an instant it was obvious that we had at last come upon the
      true place, and that we had not been the only people to visit the
      spot recently.
