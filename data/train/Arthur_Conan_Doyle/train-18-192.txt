“You were certainly born under a lucky star,” I observed, with a smile. “That's a matter of opinion,” he answered, shaking his head. “Death
has no terrors for me, if it will but come in some familiar form, but I
confess that the anticipation of some strange, some preternatural form
of death is very terrible and unnerving.” “You mean,” said I, rather puzzled at his remark, “that you would prefer
