      “I think that fortune is very kind to us.”       “You mean that the person will come again?”       “It is very probable. He came expecting to find the door open. He
      tried to get in with the blade of a very small penknife. He could
      not manage it. What would he do?”       “Come again next night with a more useful tool.”       “So I should say. It will be our fault if we are not there to
      receive him. Meanwhile, let me see the inside of the cabin.”
