"Was any name in the mouths of the soldiers?" he asked in a meaning
voice. There was a hush for the answer. The sigh of the wind among the pines
and the low lapping of the river swelled out louder in the silence.
Balbus looked hard at his commander. "Two names were whispered from rank to rank," said he. "One was Ascenius
Pollio, the General. The other was----" The fiery Sulpicius sprang to his feet waving a glowing brand above his
head.
