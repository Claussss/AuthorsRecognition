“Where is the meet?” said the officer, and I thought that he was
hungering for his bifstek. But the other answered him that it was near
Altara, so I saw that it was a place of which he spoke. “You are late, Sir George,” said the orderly. “Yes, I had a court-martial. Has Sir Stapleton Cotton gone?” At this moment a window opened, and a handsome young man in a very
splendid uniform looked out of it.
