“Awake, Watson?” he asked. “Game for a morning drive?” “Certainly.” “Then dress. No one is stirring yet, but I know where the stable-boy

he spoke, his eyes twinkled, and he seemed a different man to the
sombre thinker of the previous night. As I dressed I glanced at my watch. It was no wonder that no one was
stirring. It was twenty-five minutes past four. I had hardly finished
when Holmes returned with the news that the boy was putting in the
