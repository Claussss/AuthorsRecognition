'Yes, it was by their light, and that of the lady's bedroom candle, that
the burglars saw their way about.' 'And what did they take?' 'Well, they did not take much--only half a dozen articles of plate off
the sideboard. Lady Brackenstall thinks that they were themselves so
disturbed by the death of Sir Eustace that they did not ransack the
house, as they would otherwise have done.' 'No doubt that is true, and yet they drank some wine, I understand.'
