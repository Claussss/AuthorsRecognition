“What’s this?” he cried in a high, screaming voice. “What is the
meaning of this intrusion? Didn’t I send you word that I would see you
to-morrow morning?” “I am sorry,” said I, “but the matter cannot be delayed. Mr. Sherlock
Holmes—” The mention of my friend’s name had an extraordinary effect upon the
little man. The look of anger passed in an instant from his face. His
features became tense and alert.
