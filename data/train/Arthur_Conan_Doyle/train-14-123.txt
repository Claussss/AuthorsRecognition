      “But, surely, you said that it was a new brown boot?”       “So it was, sir. And now it’s an old black one.”       “What! you don’t mean to say—?”       “That’s just what I do mean to say. I only had three pairs in the
      world—the new brown, the old black, and the patent leathers,

      it? Speak out, man, and don’t stand staring!”
