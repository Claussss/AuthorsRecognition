The man was silent. “The password, fellow!” The cold points of two daggers pricked his throat; but still he would
not speak. “Where is the blind man?” asked Nigel. “Here, Andreas, you can have him
and do what you will with him.” “Nay, nay,” the priest whimpered. “Keep him off me. Save me from blind
Andreas! I will tell you everything.” “The password then, this instant?” “It is 'Benedicite!'” “We have the password, Simon,” cried Nigel. “Come then, let us on to the
farther end. These peasants will guard the priest, and they will remain
here lest we wish to send a message.”
