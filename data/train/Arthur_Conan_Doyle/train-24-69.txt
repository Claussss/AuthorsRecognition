“Well played, sweet poppet!” cried the younger. “Hast a rare touch on
the strings.” “How knew you the tune?” asked the other. “I knew it not. I did but follow the notes I heard.” Both opened their eyes at this, and stared at Alleyne with as much
amazement as he had shown at them. “You have a fine trick of ear then,” said one. “We have long wished to
meet such a man. Wilt join us and jog on to Ringwood? Thy duties shall
be light, and thou shalt have two-pence a day and meat for supper every
night.”
