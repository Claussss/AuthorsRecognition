‘Nay, you had best leave it alone,’ I answered. ‘Say you so? Well, mayhap you are right. Throw off your moorings, then,
and clap on sail, for we must go.’ ‘But I am not concerned,’ said I. ‘Not concerned! Not--’ he was too much overcome to go on, and could
but look at me with a face full of reproach. ‘I thought better of you,
Micah. Would you let this crazy old hulk go into action, and not stand
by to fire a broadside?’
