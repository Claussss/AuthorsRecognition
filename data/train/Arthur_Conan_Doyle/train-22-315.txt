The bartender stood abashed. “I'm sorry, Councillor, but it's Ted
Baldwin. He says he must see you this very minute.” The message was unnecessary; for the set, cruel face of the man himself
was looking over the servant's shoulder. He pushed the bartender out and
closed the door on him. “So,” said he with a furious glance at McMurdo, “you got here first, did
you? I've a word to say to you, Councillor, about this man.”
