   “Laura S. McIntyre.” She had hardly sealed her letter before her father and Robert returned.
She closed the door behind them, and made them a little curtsey. “I await my family's congratulations,” she said, with her head in the
air. “Mr. Raffles Haw has been here, and he has asked me to be his
wife.” “The deuce he did!” cried the old man. “And you said--?” “I am to see him again.” “And you will say--?”
