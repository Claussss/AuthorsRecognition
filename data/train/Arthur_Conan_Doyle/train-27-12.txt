       *       *       *       *       * What Biel wants to know is, 'Why didn't I press home the charge against
the Bronckhorst brute, and have him run in?' What Mrs. Strickland wants to know is, 'How _did_ my husband bring such
a lovely, lovely Waler from your station? I know _all_ his money
affairs; and I'm _certain_ he didn't _buy_ it.' What I want to know is, 'How do women like Mrs. Bronckhorst come to
marry men like Bronckhorst?'
