The country detective chuckled. “I thought I had squeezed all the juice out of it, but I see there was

note except that there was something on hand, and that a woman, as
usual was at the bottom of it.” Mr. Scott Eccles had fidgeted in his seat during this conversation. “I am glad you found the note, since it corroborates my story,” said
