      “I am going to force my way in and see for myself who is in the
      house. I wish you both to be there as witnesses.”       “You are quite determined to do this, in spite of your wife’s
      warning that it is better that you should not solve the mystery?”       “Yes, I am determined.”       “Well, I think that you are in the right. Any truth is better
      than indefinite doubt. We had better go up at once. Of course,
      legally, we are putting ourselves hopelessly in the wrong; but I
      think that it is worth it.”
