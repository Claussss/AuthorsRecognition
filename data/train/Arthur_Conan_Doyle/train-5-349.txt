"You, have been a good wife to me, Clara," said he. She said nothing.  She did not attempt to improve upon her advantage.
A less consummate general might have tried to do so, and ruined all.
She stood silent and submissive, noting the quick play of thought which
peeped from his eyes and lip.  There was a sparkle in the one and a
twitch of amusement in the other, as he at last glanced up at her. "Clara," said he, "deny it if you can!  You have ordered the trousseau."
