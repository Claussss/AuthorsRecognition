      “I must apologise for calling so late,” said he, “and I must
      further beg you to be so unconventional as to allow me to leave
      your house presently by scrambling over your back garden wall.”       “But what does it all mean?” I asked.       He held out his hand, and I saw in the light of the lamp that two
      of his knuckles were burst and bleeding.       “It is not an airy nothing, you see,” said he, smiling. “On the
      contrary, it is solid enough for a man to break his hand over. Is
      Mrs. Watson in?”
