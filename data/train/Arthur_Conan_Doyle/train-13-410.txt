      “What an extraordinary assortment!” I exclaimed.       “Oh, the fellows evidently grabbed hold of everything they could
      get.”       Holmes grunted from the sofa.       “The county police ought to make something of that,” said he;
      “why, it is surely obvious that—”       But I held up a warning finger.       “You are here for a rest, my dear fellow. For Heaven’s sake don’t
      get started on a new problem when your nerves are all in shreds.”
