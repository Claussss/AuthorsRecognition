“FRED PORLOCK.” Holmes sat for some little time twisting this letter between his
fingers, and frowning, as he stared into the fire. “After all,” he said at last, “there may be nothing in it. It may be
only his guilty conscience. Knowing himself to be a traitor, he may have
read the accusation in the other's eyes.” “The other being, I presume, Professor Moriarty.” “No less! When any of that party talk about 'He' you know whom they
mean. There is one predominant 'He' for all of them.”
