‘I’ve had a new coat o’ paint all over,’ said he, with a wink.
‘Carramba! the old ship is water-tight yet. What would ye say, now, were
I about to sling my hawser over a little scow, and take her in tow?’ ‘A cow!’ I cried. ‘A cow! what d’ye take me for? A wench, man, and as tight a little craft
as ever sailed into the port of wedlock.’ ‘I have heard no better news for many a long day,’ said I; ‘I did not
even know that you were betrothed. When thou is the wedding to be?’
