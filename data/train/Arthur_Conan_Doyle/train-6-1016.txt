“King John!” he cried. A shout of joy rose from the warriors around him. “The King of France!
The King of France a prisoner!” they cried in an ecstasy. “Nay, nay, fair sirs, let him not hear that we rejoice! Let no word
bring pain to his soul!” Running forward the Prince clasped the French
King by the two hands. “Most welcome, sire!” he cried. “Indeed it is good for us that so
gallant a knight should stay with us for some short time, since the
chance of war has so ordered it. Wine there! Bring wine for the King!”
