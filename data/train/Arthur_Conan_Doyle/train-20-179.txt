      “But how came the window to be shut and fastened?”       “The woman’s first instinct would be to shut and fasten the
      window. But, halloa! What is this?”       It was a lady’s hand-bag which stood upon the study table—a trim
      little handbag of crocodile-skin and silver. Holmes opened it and
      turned the contents out. There were twenty fifty-pound notes of
      the Bank of England, held together by an india-rubber
      band—nothing else.
