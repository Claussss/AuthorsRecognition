"Leave it to me," said the brown watchful face above her. "Must it be--death?" "There is no real safety outside. But if your heart is too merciful,
then by the loss of sight and speech----" She saw in her mind the white-hot iron approaching those glorious eyes,
and she shuddered at the thought. "No, no! Better death than that!" "Let it be death then. You are wise, great Empress, for there only is
real safety and assurance of silence."
