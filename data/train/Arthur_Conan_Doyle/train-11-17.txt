“Have you got him?” asked a voice, in Italian. My monster gave a laugh and kicked the sack in which I lay. “Here he is,” said he. “They are waiting.” He added something which I could not understand. “Take him, then,” said my captor. He raised me in his arms, ascended
some steps, and I was thrown down upon a hard floor. A moment later the
bars creaked and the key whined once more. I was a prisoner inside a
house.
