“But if that were true, father, the gold must go back again.” “So it does, Robert, but a little at a time. Ha, ha! I've had my eyes
open, you see. Every night it goes down in a small cart, and is sent on
to London by the 7.40. Not in bars this time, but done up in iron-bound
chests. I've seen them, boy, and I've had this hand upon them.” “Well,” said the young man thoughtfully, “maybe you are right. It is
possible that you are right.”
