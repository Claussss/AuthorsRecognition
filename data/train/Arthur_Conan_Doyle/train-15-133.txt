"Are you Datus the scavenger?" asked the Patrician. The man drew himself up proudly. "Yes," said he, "I am Datus." "Did you do this injury to my statue?" "Yes, I did." There was an uncompromising boldness in the man's reply which compelled
respect. The wrath of his master became tinged with interest. "Why did you do this?" he asked. "Because it was my duty." "Why, then, was it your duty to destroy your master's property?"
