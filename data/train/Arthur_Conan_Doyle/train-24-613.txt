“Keep your tongue still, or it will be the worse for you,” shouted the
most drunken of the archers. “Who are you to spoil sport?” “A raw squire, new landed,” said another. “By St. Thomas of Kent! we are
at the beck of our master, but we are not to be ordered by every babe
whose mother hath sent him as far as Aquitaine.” “Oh, gentlemen,” cried the girl in broken French, “for dear Christ's
