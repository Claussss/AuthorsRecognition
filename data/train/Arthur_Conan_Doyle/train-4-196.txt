‘We belong to the Duke of Beaufort’s household,’ said Saxon. ‘Indeed! I thought ye might belong to Portman’s yellow regiment of
militia. I trust that the Duke will muster every man he can, and make
play until the royal forces come up.’ ‘How many will Churchill bring?’ asked my companion carelessly. ‘Eight hundred horse at the most, but my Lord Feversham will follow
after with close on four thousand foot.’
