“Have you heard from Hector?” “Oh, yes. He went off all right last Wednesday from Spithead, and he
will write from Madeira. But you generally have later news at Elmdene
than I have.” “I don't know whether Laura has heard. Have you been up to see the new
comer?” “Yes; I have just left him.” “Is he a married man--this Mr. Raffles Haw?” “No, he is a bachelor. He does not seem to have any relations either, as
far as I could learn. He lives alone, amid his huge staff of servants.
It is a most remarkable establishment. It made me think of the Arabian
Nights.”
