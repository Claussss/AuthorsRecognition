They examined the old worn stone. There was a large deeply-cut "VV" upon
the top of it. "What does 'VV' stand for?" asked Brown. "Naebody kens," the guide answered. "_Valeria Victrix_," said the lady softly. Her face was paler than ever,
her eyes far away, as one who peers down the dim aisles of overarching
centuries. "What's that?" asked her husband sharply. She started as one who wakes from sleep. "What were we talking about?"
she asked.
