“Now, off you go, and come back with a better report next time.” He waved his hand, and they scampered away downstairs like so many rats,
and we heard their shrill voices next moment in the street. “There’s more work to be got out of one of those little beggars than
out of a dozen of the force,” Holmes remarked. “The mere sight of an
official-looking person seals men’s lips. These youngsters, however, go
everywhere and hear everything. They are as sharp as needles, too; all
they want is organisation.”
