“Forty,” said the mate. “Sixty, if he is a day,” remarked Captain Meadows. “Why, I have heard
him talk quite familiarly of the first Afghan war. He was a man then,
and that is close on forty years ago.” “Wonderful!” I ejaculated. “His skin is as smooth and his eyes are as
clear as mine are. He is the superior priest of the three, no doubt.” “The inferior,” said the captain confidently. “That is why he does all
the talking for them. Their minds are too elevated to descend to mere
worldly chatter.”
