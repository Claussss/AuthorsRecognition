The Colonel sat with a very stiff back and the blank face of a man who
is not quite sure whether he is being complimented or insulted. “You can do the talking yourself if you like,” said he at last. “I
should be very glad to be relieved of it.” “I think that I am best fitted for it, since I am equally interested in
all creeds. When I ask for information, it is because in verity I desire
it, and not because I am playing a part.”
