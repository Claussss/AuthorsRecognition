“Not a word,” the general answered listlessly. “I thought the noise of the wind would prevent you hearing the signal
guns. She came ashore in the bay the night before last--a great barque
from India.” “From India!” ejaculated the general. “Yes. Her crew were saved, fortunately, and have all been sent on to
Glasgow.” “All sent on!” cried the general, with a face as bloodless as a corpse. “All except three rather strange characters who claim to be Buddhist
priests. They have decided to remain for a few days upon the coast.”
