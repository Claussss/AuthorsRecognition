“I will follow your Excellency,” said I. “There is a small engagement
which detains me.” He opened his eyes. “At this hour?” “Yes, sir,” I answered. “My fellow-officers, whom I never saw until last
night, will not be content unless they catch another glimpse of me the
first thing this morning.” “Au revoir, then,” said Marshal Lannes, as he passed upon his way. I hurried through the shattered door of the convent.
