“That's all right, Colonel,” said Belmont. “But for our own sakes we
ought to know what the chief has said.” “For my part I'll have nothing to do with the blackguard.” “I think that that is going too far. We are bound to hear what he has to

he had to bite his lip to keep down a bitter answer. He walked slowly
