‘But what can the well-filled purse do for them?’ said I. ‘What will
the few score pieces which these bloodthirsty wretches have filched from
this poor creature avail them when their own hour of death comes round?’ ‘True,’ said Saxon dryly; ‘they may, however, prove useful in the
meantime. This you say is Bishopstoke. What are the lights over yonder?’ ‘They come, I think, from Bishop’s Waltham,’ I answered.
