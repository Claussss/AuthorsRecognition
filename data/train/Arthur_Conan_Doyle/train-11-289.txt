“Hang it all, Jinny, give the fellow fair play,” cried Lord Dacre. “He
stood my fire like a man, and I won't see him interfered with. Whatever
happens I can't get worse than I deserve.” But already there had passed between me and the lady a quick glance of
the eyes which told her everything. Her hands slipped from my arm. “I leave my husband's life and my own
happiness to Colonel Gerard,” said she.
