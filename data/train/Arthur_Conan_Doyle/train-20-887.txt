      “I give you five minutes, Mr. Holmes.”       “One is enough, Lady Hilda. I know of your visit to Eduardo
      Lucas, of your giving him this document, of your ingenious return
      to the room last night, and of the manner in which you took the
      letter from the hiding-place under the carpet.”       She stared at him with an ashen face and gulped twice before she
