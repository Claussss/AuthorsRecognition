‘Father,’ said I, when I returned home, ‘I am ready to go where you
will.’ ‘May the Lord be glorified!’ he cried solemnly. ‘May He watch over
your young life, and keep your heart steadfast to the cause which is
assuredly His!’ And so, my dear grandsons, the great resolution was taken, and I found
myself committed to one side in the national quarrel. Chapter VII. Of the Horseman who rode from the West
