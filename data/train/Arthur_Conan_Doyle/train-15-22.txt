"You and your fellows," said the Roman. Clearer still grew the future, and there was terror in the eyes of the
spokesmen as they faced it. "But, your excellency, if the legions should go at once, we should have
the wild Scots at York, and the Northmen in the Thames within the month.
We can build ourselves up under your shield, and in a few years it would
be easier for us; but not now, your excellency, not now."
