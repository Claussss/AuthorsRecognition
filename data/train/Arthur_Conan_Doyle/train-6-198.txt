“What ails you, fair dame?” he asked. “Is there any small matter in
which I may stand your friend, or is it possible that anyone hath so
hard a heart as to do you an injury.” She rose and turned upon him a face full of hope and entreaty. “Oh,
save my poor, poor father!” she cried. “Have you perchance seen the
way-wardens? They passed us, and I fear they are beyond reach.” “Yes, they have ridden onward, but we may serve as well.”
