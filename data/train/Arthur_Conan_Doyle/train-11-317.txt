“And now to business,” said I, when he had done coughing and hawking.
“What is your name?” “Alexis Barakoff.” “Your rank and regiment?” “Captain of the Dragoons of Grodno.” “What is this note which you were carrying?” “It is a line which I had written to my sweetheart.” “Whose name,” said I, examining the address, “is the Hetman Platoff.
Come, come, sir, this is an important military document, which you are
carrying from one general to another. Tell me this instant what it is.”
