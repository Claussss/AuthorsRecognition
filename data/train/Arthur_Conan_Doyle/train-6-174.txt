“And how won you the horse?” “It was given me by the monks of Waverley.” “This is wonderful. Pardieu! I should have expected, from what I had
seen, that they would have given you little save their malediction.” “They had no use for the horse, and they gave it to me.” “Then we have only to find some one who has no use for a suit of armor
and will give it to you. Yet I trust that you will think better of it
and let me, since that good lady proves that I am your kinsman, fit you
for the wars.”
