He appeared presently, looking a little irritable at being disturbed in
his slumbers. “I made my report at the office,” he said. Holmes took a half-sovereign from his pocket and played with it
pensively. “We thought that we should like to hear it all from your own
lips,” he said. “I shall be most happy to tell you anything I can,” the constable
answered with his eyes upon the little golden disk. “Just let us hear it all in your own way as it occurred.”
