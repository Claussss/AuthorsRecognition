‘Have you the news from a trusty source?’ asked Decimus Saxon, after a
long silence. ‘It is beyond all doubt or question,’ Master Stephen Timewell answered.
‘Yet I can well understand your surprise, for the Duke had trusty
councillors with him. There was Sir Patrick Hume of Polwarth--’ ‘All talk and no fight,’ said Saxon. ‘And Richard Rumbold.’ ‘All fight and no talk,’ quoth our companion. ‘He should, methinks, have
rendered a better account of himself.’
