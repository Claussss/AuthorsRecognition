“Did you see no one?” “No. I heard Mrs. Douglas coming down the stair behind me, and I rushed
out to prevent her from seeing this dreadful sight. Mrs. Allen, the
housekeeper, came and took her away. Ames had arrived, and we ran back
into the room once more.” “But surely I have heard that the drawbridge is kept up all night.” “Yes, it was up until I lowered it.” “Then how could any murderer have got away? It is out of the question!
Mr. Douglas must have shot himself.”
