“Now, your questions!” said I. “You swear in turn to answer them truly?” “I do, upon the honour of a gentleman and a soldier.” It was, as you perceive, a terrible thing that I promised, but what was
it compared to what I might gain by compliance? “This is a very fair and a very interesting bargain,” said he, taking a
note-book from his pocket. “Would you kindly turn your gaze toward the French camp?”
