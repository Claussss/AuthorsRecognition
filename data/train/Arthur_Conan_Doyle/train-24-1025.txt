“And mine also is very old,” answered the squire. “And the Lady Maude is our single child. All our name and lands centre
upon her.” “Alas! that I should say it, but I also am now the only Edricson.” “And why have I not heard this from you before, Alleyne? In sooth, I
think that you have used me ill.” “Nay, my fair lord, say not so; for I know not whether your daughter
loves me, and there is no pledge between us.”
