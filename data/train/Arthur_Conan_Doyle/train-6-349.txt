“You know Paul de la Fosse of Shalford?” “You mean the small man with the twisted back?” “He is no smaller than yourself, Nigel, and as to his back there are
many folk that I know who would be glad to have his face.” “Nay, I am no judge of that, and I spoke out of no discourtesy. What of
the man?” “He has flouted me, Nigel, and I would have revenge.” “What--on that poor twisted creature?” “I tell you that he has flouted me!”
