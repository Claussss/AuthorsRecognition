“When you are fit to ride, you will come back with me to my people.” “Alas! I feared that you would say so. Had I taken you, Nigel--that is
your name, is it not?--had I taken you, I would not have acted thus.” “How then would you have ordered things?” asked Nigel, much taken with
the frank and debonair manner of his captive. “I would not have taken advantage of such a mischance as has befallen me
which has put me in your power. I would give you a sword and beat you
in fair fight, so that I might send you to give greeting to my dear lady
and show her the deeds which I do for her fair sake.”
