With a few words of grateful acknowledgment of our hospitality, he drew
a chair up to the fire and warmed his great, brown hands before the
blaze. “What d'ye think now, Captain Meadows?” he asked presently, glancing up
at his superior officer. “Didn't I warn you what would be the upshot of
having those niggers on board the _Belinda_?” The captain leant back in his chair and laughed heartily. “Didn't I tell you?” he cried, appealing to us. “Didn't I tell you?”
