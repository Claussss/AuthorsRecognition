“You'll kill the man,” said he. “Drop it!” Baldwin looked at him in amazement. “Curse you!” he cried. “Who are you
to interfere--you that are new to the lodge? Stand back!” He raised his
stick; but McMurdo had whipped his pistol out of his pocket. “Stand back yourself!” he cried. “I'll blow your face in if you lay a
hand on me. As to the lodge, wasn't it the order of the Bodymaster that
the man was not to be killed--and what are you doing but killing him?”
