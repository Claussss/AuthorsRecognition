He had seized Ettie's white hand between his own strong brown ones. “Say that you will be mine, and we will face it out together!” “Not here?” “Yes, here.” “No, no, Jack!” His arms were round her now. “It could not be here.
Could you take me away?” A struggle passed for a moment over McMurdo's face; but it ended by
setting like granite. “No, here,” he said. “I'll hold you against the
world, Ettie, right here where we are!”
