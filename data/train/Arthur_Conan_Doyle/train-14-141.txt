      We had risen to depart when Baskerville gave a cry of triumph,
      and diving into one of the corners of the room he drew a brown
      boot from under a cabinet.       “My missing boot!” he cried.       “May all our difficulties vanish as easily!” said Sherlock
      Holmes.       “But it is a very singular thing,” Dr. Mortimer remarked. “I
      searched this room carefully before lunch.”
