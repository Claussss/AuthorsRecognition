Holmes chuckled and rubbed his hands. “Great!” he said. “Great! Tell me,
Friend MacDonald, this pleasing and touching interview was, I suppose,
in the professor's study?” “That's so.” “A fine room, is it not?” “Very fine--very handsome indeed, Mr. Holmes.” “You sat in front of his writing desk?” “Sun in your eyes and his face in the shadow?” “Well, it was evening; but I mind that the lamp was turned on my face.”
