      Holmes shrugged his shoulders.       “One cannot always have the success for which one hopes. An
      investigator needs facts and not legends or rumours. It has not
      been a satisfactory case.”       My friend spoke in his frankest and most unconcerned manner.
      Stapleton still looked hard at him. Then he turned to me.       “I would suggest carrying this poor fellow to my house, but it
      would give my sister such a fright that I do not feel justified
      in doing it. I think that if we put something over his face he
      will be safe until morning.”
