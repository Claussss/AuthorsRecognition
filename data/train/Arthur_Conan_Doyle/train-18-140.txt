The tramp drew himself up to his full height and raised his right hand
with the palm forward in a military salute. “I can take you on as gardener and get rid of the fellow I have got.
As to brandy, you shall have an allowance and no more. We are not deep
drinkers at the Hall.” “Don't you take opium, or brandy, or nothing yourself, sir?” asked
