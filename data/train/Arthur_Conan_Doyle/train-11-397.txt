“We shall beat them, Gneisenau; the Duke and I will grind them to powder
between us. Push on, I say! The whole war will be ended in one blow.
Bring Pirsch up, and we can throw sixty thousand men into the scale
while Thielmann holds Grouchy beyond Wavre.” Gneisenau shrugged his shoulders, but at that instant an orderly
appeared at the door. “An aide-de-camp from the Duke of Wellington,” said he. “Ha, ha!” cried the old man; “let us hear what he has to say!”
