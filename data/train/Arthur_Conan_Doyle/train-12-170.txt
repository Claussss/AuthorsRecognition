Laura resumed her seat with a flush upon her cheeks and a quickening of
the breath. She turned her face away and gazed into the fire; but there
was a sparkle in her eyes which was not caught from the leaping flames. “Do you remember the first time that we met, Miss McIntyre?” he
asked, standing on the rug and looking down at her dark hair, and the
beautifully feminine curve of her ivory neck. “As if it were yesterday,” she answered in her sweet mellow tones.
