“Do you go out much in society?” “Arthur does. Mary and I stay at home. We neither of us care for it.” “That is unusual in a young girl.” “She is of a quiet nature. Besides, she is not so very young. She is
four-and-twenty.” “This matter, from what you say, seems to have been a shock to her
also.” “Terrible! She is even more affected than I.” “You have neither of you any doubt as to your son’s guilt?”
