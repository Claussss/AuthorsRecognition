"By all means.  Let our young friend give it a name," said Challenger. "Then," said I, blushing, I dare say, as I said it, "let it be named
Lake Gladys." "Don't you think the Central Lake would be more descriptive?" remarked
Summerlee. "I should prefer Lake Gladys." Challenger looked at me sympathetically, and shook his great head in
mock disapproval.  "Boys will be boys," said he.  "Lake Gladys let it
be."
