“Indeed, your high and mighty grace,” sneered one of the yeomen, “have
you in sooth so ordained?” “By the Virgin!” said a second, “I think that you may both chance to
find yourselves upon the road before long.” “And so belabored as to be scarce able to crawl along it,” cried a
third. “Nay, I shall go! I shall go!” said Alleyne hurriedly, as Hordle John
began to slowly roll up his sleeve, and bare an arm like a leg of
mutton. “I would not have you brawl about me.”
