‘Forty of them desire to plead guilty to the charge of taking up arms
against the King,’ replied our barrister. ‘Ah!’ roared the Judge. ‘Was ever such unparalleled impudence? Was there
ever such brazen effrontery? Guilty, quotha! Have they expressed their
repentance for this sin against a most kind and long-suffering monarch!
Put down those words on the record, clerk!’ ‘They have refused to express repentance, your Lordship!’ replied the
counsel for the defence.
