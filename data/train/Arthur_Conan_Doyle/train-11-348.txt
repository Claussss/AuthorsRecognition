Then at last my heart leaped within me, for I heard a light step in the
passage. An instant later the key turned, the door opened, and Sophie
was in the room. “Monsieur--” she cried. “Etienne,” said I. “Nothing will change you,” said she. “But is it possible that you do not
hate me? Have you forgiven me the trick which I played you?” “What trick?” I asked. “Good heavens! Is it possible that even now you have not understood it?
You have asked me to translate the despatch. I have told you that it
meant, 'If the French come to Minsk all is lost.'”
