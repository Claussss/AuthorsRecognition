‘There, at least, your time was well employed,’ said I; ‘you could
not hearken to the grand thoughts or lofty words of Shakespeare or of
Massinger without feeling some image of them in your own soul.’ Sir Gervas chuckled quietly. ‘You are as fresh to me, Micah, as this
sweet country air,’ said he. ‘Know, thou dear babe, that it was not to
see the play that we frequented the playhouse.’ ‘Then why, in Heaven’s name?’ I asked.
