At last my breath had returned to me. “Madame,” I said, as I raised my hat, “do I intrude, or is there any
possible way in which I can be of service to you?” But neither of them minded me any more than if I had been a fly who
buzzed between them. Their eyes were locked together. “I'll have my rights, I tell you. I've waited long enough.” “There's no use bullying, George.” “Do you give in?” “No, never!”
