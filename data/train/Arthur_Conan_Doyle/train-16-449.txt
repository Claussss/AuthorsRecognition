It was an exceedingly rude speech, and he was ashamed of it, the instant
after he had made it. The lady however, simply raised her eyebrows and
smiled. "It seems to me that you are begging the question," said she. "Of
course, if it makes women masculine that _would_ be a considerable
deterioration." It was a neat little counter, and Doctor Ripley, like a pinked fencer,
bowed his acknowledgment. "I must go," said he.
