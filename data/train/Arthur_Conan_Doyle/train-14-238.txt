      “Did the boy deliver it into your own hands?” asked Sir Henry.       Barrymore looked surprised, and considered for a little time.       “No,” said he, “I was in the box-room at the time, and my wife
      brought it up to me.”       “Did you answer it yourself?”       “No; I told my wife what to answer and she went down to write
      it.”       In the evening he recurred to the subject of his own accord.
