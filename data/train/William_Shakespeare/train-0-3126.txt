Shout within Sola, sola! 4.2
Enter Dull, Holofernes the pedant, and Nathaniel the curate 
NATHANIEL Very reverend sport, truly, and done in the testimony of a good conscience. HOLOFERNES The deer was, as you know—sanguis—in blood, ripe as the pomewater who now hangeth like a jewel in the ear of caelo, the sky, the welkin, the heaven, and anon falleth like a crab on the face of terra, the soil, the land, the earth.
