SIR JOHN Give me my rapier, boy. DOLL TEARSHEET I pray thee, Jack, I pray thee, do not draw. SIR JOHN (taking his rapier and speaking to Pistol) Get you downstairs. Sir John, Bardolph, and Pistol brawl MISTRESS QUICKLY Here’s a goodly tumult! I’ll forswear keeping house afore I’ll be in these tirrits and frights!
