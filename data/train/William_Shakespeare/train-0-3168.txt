Saint Cupid, then, and, soldiers, to the field! Advance your standards, and upon them, lords.
Pell-mell, down with them; but be first advised
In conflict that you get the sun of them. LONGUEVILLE Now to plain dealing. Lay these glozes by.
Shall we resolve to woo these girls of France? And win them, too! Therefore let us devise
Some entertainment for them in their tents.
