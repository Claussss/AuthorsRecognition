THIRD COUNTRYMAN (to Arcite) Thou wilt not go along? ARCITE Not yet, sir. FOURTH COUNTRYMAN Well, sir, Take your own time. (To the others) Come, boys. FIRST COUNTRYMAN My mind misgives me—This fellow has a vengeance trick o’th’ hip:
Mark how his body’s made for’t. SECOND COUNTRYMAN I’ll be hanged though If he dare venture; hang him, plum porridge!
He wrestle? He roast eggs! Come, let’s be gone, lads.
