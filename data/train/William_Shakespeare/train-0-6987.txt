OLIVIA Who has done this, Sir Andrew? SIR ANDREW The Count’s gentleman, one Cesario. We took him for a coward, but he’s the very devil incardinate. ORSINO My gentleman, Cesario? SIR ANDREW ’Od’s lifelings, here he is. (To Viola) You broke my head for nothing, and that that I did I was set on to do’t by Sir Toby.
