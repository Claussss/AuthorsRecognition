Exeunt Dogberry and Verges. The Watch sit Enter Borachio and Conrad BORACHIO What, Conrad! aside) Peace, stir not. BORACHIO Conrad, I say. CONRAD Here, man, I am at thy elbow. BORACHIO Mass, an my elbow itched, I thought there would a scab follow. CONRAD I will owe thee an answer for that. And now, forward with thy tale.
