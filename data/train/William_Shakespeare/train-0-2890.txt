DROMIO OF SYRACUSE (within) Let him walk from whence he came, lest he catch cold on’s feet. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Who talks within there ? Ho, open the door! DROMIO OF SYRACUSE (within the Phoenix) Right, sir, I’ll tell you when, an you’ll tell me wherefore. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Wherefore? For my dinner—I have not dined today.
