SIR JOHN I think I am as like to ride the mare, if I have any vantage of ground to get up. LORD CHIEF JUSTICE How comes this, Sir John ? Fie, what man of good temper would endure this tempest of exclamation? Are you not ashamed, to enforce a poor widow to so rough a course to come by her own? SIR JOHN (to the Hostess) What is the gross sum that I owe thee?
