PAINTER The gods preserve ye! Well fare you, gentleman. Give me your hand.
We must needs dine together. (To Jeweller) Sir, your jewel
Hath suffered under praise. JEWELLER What, my lord, dispraise? A mere satiety of commendations.
If I should pay you for’t as ’tis extolled
It would unclew me quite. JEWELLER My lord, ’tis rated
