CHIEF WATCHMAN A great suspicion. Stay the friar, too. Enter the Prince with others What misadventure is so early up,
That calls our person from our morning rest? Enter Capulet and his Wife What should it be that is so shrieked abroad? CAPULET’S WIFE O, the people in the street cry ‘Romeo’,
Some ‘Juliet’, and some ‘Paris’, and all run
With open outcry toward our monument.
