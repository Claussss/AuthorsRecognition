LAERTES A Norman was’t? KING CLAUDIUS A Norman. Upon my life, Lamord. KING CLAUDIUS The very same. I know him well. He is the brooch indeed,
And gem, of all the nation. KING CLAUDIUS He made confession of you, And gave you such a masterly report
For art and exercise in your defence,
And for your rapier most especially,
That he cried out ‘twould be a sight indeed
If one could match you. Sir, this report of his
Did Hamlet so envenom with his envy
That he could nothing do but wish and beg
Your sudden coming o’er to play with him.
