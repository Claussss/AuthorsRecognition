PRINCE HARRY Content, and the argument shall be thy running away. SIR JOHN Ah, no more of that, Hal, an thou lovest me. Enter Hostess HOSTESS O Jesu, my lord the Prince! PRINCE HARRY How now, my lady the Hostess, what sayst thou to me? HOSTESS Marry, my lord, there is a nobleman of the court at door would speak with you. He says he comes from your father.
