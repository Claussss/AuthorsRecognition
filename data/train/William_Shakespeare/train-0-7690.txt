Good madam, sit. In sooth, you shall sit here. LADY MAYORESS Good madam, pardon me, it may not be. In troth, I’ll have it so. I’ll sit here by ye.
Good ladies, sit.—More stools here, ho! LADY MAYORESS It is your favour, madam, makes me thus
Presume above my merit. LADY MORE When we come to you, Then shall you rule us as we rule you here.
