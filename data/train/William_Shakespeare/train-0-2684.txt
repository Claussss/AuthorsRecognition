COUNTESS OF SALISBURY O, perjured beauty! More corrupted judge!
When to the great star chamber o‘er our heads
The universal sessions calls to ’count
This packing evil, we both shall tremble for it. KING EDWARD What says my fair love? Is she resolute? COUNTESS OF SALISBURY Resolved to be dissolved, and therefore this:
Keep but thy word, great King, and I am thine.
Stand where thou dost—I’ll part a little from thee—
