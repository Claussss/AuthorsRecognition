APEMANTUS Right, if doing nothing be death by th’ law. How likest thou this picture, Apemantus? APEMANTUS The best for the innocence. Wrought he not well that painted it? APEMANTUS He wrought better that made the painter, and yet he’s but a filthy piece of work. PAINTER You’re a dog. APEMANTUS Thy mother’s of my generation. What’s she, if I be a dog?
