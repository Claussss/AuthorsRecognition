Now, good sir,
You know me, do you not? For a worthy lady, And one who much I honour. PAULINA Pray you then, Conduct me to the Queen. I may not, madam. To the contrary
I have express commandment. Here’s ado, To lock up honesty and honour from
Th’access of gentle visitors. Is’t lawful, pray you,
To see her women? Any of them? Emilia?
JAILER So please you, madam,
To put apart these your attendants,
Shall bring Emilia forth.
