LANCELOT Indeed, the short and the long is, I serve the Jew, and have a desire as my father shall specify. GOBBO (to Bassiano) His master and he, saving your worship’s reverence, are scarce cater-cousins. LANCELOT (to Bassiano) To be brief, the very truth is that the Jew, having done me wrong, doth cause me, as my father—being, I hope, an old man—shall frutify unto you.
