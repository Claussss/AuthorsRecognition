CAIUS Mockvater? Vat is dat? HOST Mockwater, in our English tongue, is valour, bully. CAIUS By Gar, then I have as much mockvater as de Englishman. Scurvy jack-dog priest! By Gar, me vill cut his ears. HOST He will clapper-claw thee tightly, bully. CAIUS Clapper-de-claw? Vat is dat? HOST That is, he will make thee amends.
