Enter Doctor Caius CAIUS Ver is Mistress Page? By Gar, I am cozened! I ha’ married un gar a boy, un paysan, by Gar. A boy! It is not Anne Page, by Gar. I am cozened. PAGE Why, did you take her in green? CAIUS Ay, be Gar, and ’tis a boy. Be Gar, I’ll raise all Windsor. FORD This is strange. Who hath got the right Anne?
