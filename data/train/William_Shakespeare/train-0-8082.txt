ESCALUS Slander to th’ state! Away with him to prison. What can you vouch against him, Signor Lucio?
Is this the man that you did tell us of? LUCIO ’Tis he, my lord.—Come hither, goodman Bald-pate. Do you know me? DUKE I remember you, sir, by the sound of your voice. I met you at the prison, in the absence of the Duke.
