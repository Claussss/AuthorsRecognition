Enter Keeper to Richard, with meat KEEPER (to Groom) Fellow, give place. Here is no longer stay. RICHARD (to Groom) If thou love me, ’tis time thou wert away. What my tongue dares not, that my heart shall say. My lord, will’t please you to fall to? Taste of it first, as thou art wont to do. My lord, I dare not. Sir Piers of Exton,
Who lately came from the King, commands the contrary.
