CRESSIDA Excuse me. PANDARUS He is elder. CRESSIDA Pardon me, pardon me. PANDARUS Th‘other’s not come to’t. You shall tell me another tale when th’other’s come to’t. Hector shall not have his will this year. He shall not need it if he have his own. PANDARUS Nor his qualities. CRESSIDA No matter. PANDARUS Nor his beauty.
