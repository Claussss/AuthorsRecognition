TIMON (to the Lords) You do yourselves Much wrong, you bate too much of your own merits.
(To Second Lord) Here, my lord, a trifle of our love. SECOND LORD With more than common thanks I will receive it. O, he’s the very soul of bounty! TIMON (to First Lord) And now I remember, my lord, you gave good words the other day of a bay courser I rode on. ’Tis yours, because you liked it.
