JULIET Speak’st thou from thy heart? And from my soul, too, else beshrew them both. JULIET Amen. NURSE What? Well, thou hast comforted me marvellous much.
Go in; and tell my lady I am gone,
Having displeased my father, to Laurence’ cell
To make confession and to be absolved. Marry, I will; and this is wisely done. Exit
