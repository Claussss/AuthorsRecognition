TIMON The best and truest, For here it sleeps and does no hired harm. APEMANTUS Where liest a-nights, Timon? TIMON Under that’s above me. Where feed’st thou a-days, Apemantus? APEMANTUS Where my stomach finds meat; or rather, where I eat it. TIMON Would poison were obedient, and knew my mind! APEMANTUS Where wouldst thou send it?
