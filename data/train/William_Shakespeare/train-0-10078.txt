Sc. 21 Enter Helicanus above; below, enterone of Tyre, the other of Mytilene SAILOR OF TYRE (to Sailor of Mytilene) Lord Helicanus can resolve you, sir.
(To Helicanus) There is a barge put off from Mytilene.
In it, Lysimachus, the governor,
Who craves to come aboard. What is your will? That he have his.
