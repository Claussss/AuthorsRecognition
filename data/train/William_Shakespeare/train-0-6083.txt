BRUTUS Good night, good brother. TITINIUS and MESSALA Good night, Lord Brutus. BRUTUS Farewell, every one. Exeunt Cassius, Titinius, and Messala Give me the gown. He puts on the gown
Where is thy instrument? Here in the tent. BRUTUS What, thou speak’t drowsily. Poor knave, I blame thee not; thou art o’erwatched.
Call Claudio and some other of my men.
I’ll have them sleep on cushions in my tent.
