Here comes the King, The Queen, the courtiers—who is that they follow,
And with such maimed rites? This doth betoken
The corpse they follow did with desp‘rate hand
Fordo it own life. ’Twas of some estate.
Couch we a while, and mark. LAERTES What ceremony else? HAMLET (aside to Horatio) That is Laertes, a very noble youth. Mark.
