If sad and merry madness equal be. Enter Malvolio, cross-gartered and wearing yellow stockings, with Maria How now, Malvolio? MALVOLIO Sweet lady, ho, ho! Smil’st thou? I sent for thee upon a sad occasion. MALVOLIO Sad, lady? I could be sad. This does make some obstruction in the blood, this cross-gartering, but what of that? If it please the eye of one, it is with me as the very true sonnet is, ‘Please one, and please all’.
