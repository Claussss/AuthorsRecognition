He takes the rapier MISTRESS QUICKLY Good master, be content. CAIUS Wherefore shall I be content-a? MISTRESS QUICKLY The young man is an honest man. CAIUS What shall de honest man do in my closet? Dere is no honest man dat shall come in my closet. MISTRESS QUICKLY I beseech you, be not so phlegmatic. Hear the truth of it. He came of an errand to me from Parson Hugh.
