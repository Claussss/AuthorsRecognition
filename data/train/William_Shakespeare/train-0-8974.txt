You that are honest, by being what you are
Make them best seen and known. PAINTER He and myself Have travelled in the great show’r of your gifts,
And sweetly felt it. TIMON Ay, you are honest men. We are hither come to offer you our service. Most honest men. Why, how shall I requite you?
