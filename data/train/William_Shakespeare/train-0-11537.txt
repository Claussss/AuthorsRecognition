STEFANO Trinculo, run into no further danger. Interrupt the monster one word further, and, by this hand, I’ll turn my mercy out o’doors and make a stockfish of thee. TRINCULO Why, what did I? I did nothing. I’ll go farther off. STEFANO Didst thou not say he lied? ARIEL Thou liest. STEFANO Do I so? (Striking Trinculo) Take thou that. As you like this, give me the lie another time.
