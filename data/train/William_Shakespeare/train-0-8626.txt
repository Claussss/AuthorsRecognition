EDGAR Bless thy five wits. KENT (to Lear) O pity! Sir, where is the patience now
That you so oft have boasted to retain? EDGAR (aside) My tears begin to take his part so much
They’ll mar my counterfeiting. LEAR The little dogs and all, Tray, Blanch, and Sweetheart—see, they bark at me. EDGAR Tom will throw his head at them.—Avaunt, you curs!
