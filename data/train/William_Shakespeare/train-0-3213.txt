Dumaine and Maria talk apart What, was your visor made without a tongue? LONGUEVILLE (taking Catherine for Maria) I know the reason, lady, why you ask. O, for your reason ! Quickly, sir, I long. LONGUEVILLE You have a double tongue within your mask,
And would afford my speechless visor half. ‘Veal’, quoth the Dutchman. Is not veal a calf?
