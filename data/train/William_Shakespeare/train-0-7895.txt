ESCALUS So. (To Pompey) What trade are you of, sir? POMPEY A tapster, a poor widow’s tapster. ESCALUS Your mistress’s name? POMPEY Mistress Overdone. ESCALUS Hath she had any more than one husband? POMPEY Nine, sir—Overdone by the last. ESCALUS Nine?—Come hither to me, Master Froth. Master Froth, I would not have you acquainted with tapsters. They will draw you, Master Froth, and you will hang them. Get you gone, and let me hear no more of you.
