Villain, thy father was a plasterer
And thou thyself a shearman, art thou not? And Adam was a gardener. STAFFORD’S BROTHER And what of that? Marry, this: Edmund Mortimer, Earl of March,
Married the Duke of Clarence’ daughter, did he not? STAFFORD Ay, sir. By her he had two children at one birth. STAFFORD’S BROTHER That’s false.
