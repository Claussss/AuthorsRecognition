COUNTESS Well, sir? LAVATCH No, madam, ’tis not so well that I am poor, though many of the rich are damned. But if I may have your ladyship’s good will to go to the world, Isbel the woman and I will do as we may. COUNTESS Wilt thou needs be a beggar? LAVATCH I do beg your good will in this case.
