SIR JOHN By the Lord, thou sayst true, lad; and is not my Hostess of the tavern a most sweet wench? PRINCE HARRY As the honey of Hybla, my old lad of the castle; and is not a buff jerkin a most sweet robe of durance? SIR JOHN How now, how now, mad wag? What, in thy quips and thy quiddities? What a plague have I to do with a buff jerkin?
