ALBANY A herald, ho! EDMUND A herald, ho, a herald! Trust to thy single virtue, for thy soldiers,
All levied in my name, have in my name
Took their discharge. REGAN This sickness grows upon me. She is not well. Convey her to my tent.
Exit one or more with Regan [Enter a Herald and a trumpeter] Come hither, herald. Let the trumpet sound,
And read out this.
