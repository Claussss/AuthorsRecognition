Ay, ay. O, lay me by my mistress’ side! GRAZIANO He’s gone, but his wife’s killed. ‘Tis a notorious villain. Take you this weapon
Which I have here recovered from the Moor.
Come, guard the door without. Let him not pass,
But kill him rather. I’ll after that same villain,
For ’tis a damned slave. Exeunt Montano and Graziano
