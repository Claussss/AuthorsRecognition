ARMADO And therefore ‘apt’ because quick. MOTE Speak you this in my praise, master? ARMADO In thy condign praise. MOTE I will praise an eel with the same praise. ARMADO What—that an eel is ingenious? MOTE That an eel is quick. ARMADO I do say thou art quick in answers. Thou heatest my blood. MOTE I am answered, sir.
