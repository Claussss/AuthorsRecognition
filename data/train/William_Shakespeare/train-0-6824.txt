FESTE By’r Lady, sir, and some dogs will catch well. SIR ANDREW Most certain. Let our catch be ‘Thou knave’. FESTE ‘Hold thy peace, thou knave’, knight. I shall be constrained in’t to call thee knave, knight. SIR ANDREW ‘Tis not the first time I have constrained one to call me knave. Begin, fool. It begins ‘Hold thy peace’.
