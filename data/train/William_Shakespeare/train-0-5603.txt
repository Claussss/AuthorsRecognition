CLAUDIO For the which she wept heartily and said she cared not. DON PEDRO Yea, that she did. But yet for all that, an if she did not hate him deadly she would love him dearly. The old man’s daughter told us all. CLAUDIO All, all. And moreover, God saw him when he was hid in the garden. DON PEDRO But when shall we set the savage bull’s horns on the sensible Benedick’s head?
