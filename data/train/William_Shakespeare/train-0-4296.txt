1.3
Enter Bassanio with Shylock the Jew SHYLOCK Three thousand ducats. Well. BASSANIO Ay, sir, for three months. SHYLOCK For three months. Well. BASSANIO For the which, as I told you, Antonio shall be bound. SHYLOCK Antonio shall become bound. Well. BASSANIO May you stead me? Will you pleasure me? Shall I know your answer?
