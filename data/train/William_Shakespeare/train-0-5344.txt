DAVY I hope to see London once ere I die. BARDOLPH An I might see you there, Davy! SHALLOW By the mass, you’ll crack a quart together, ha, will you not, Master Bardolph? BARDOLPH Yea, sir, in a pottle-pot. SHALLOW By God’s liggens, I thank thee. The knave will stick by thee, I can assure thee that; a will not out; ’tis true-bred.
