Good madam. KING CLAUDIUS Gertrude, do not drink. QUEEN GERTRUDE I will, my lord, I pray you pardon me. She drinks, then offers the cup to Hamlet KING CLAUDIUS (aside) It is the poisoned cup; it is too late. I dare not drink yet, madam; by and by. QUEEN GERTRUDE (to Hamlet) Come, let me wipe thy face.
