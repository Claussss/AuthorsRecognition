Enter Nell within the Phoenix NELL (within the Phoenix) What a coil is there, Dromio? Who are those at the gate? DROMIO OF EPHESUS Let my master in, Nell. NELL (within the Phoenix) Faith no, he comes too late; And so tell your master. DROMIO OF EPHESUS O Lord, I must laugh. Have at you with a proverb: ‘Shall I set in my staff?’
