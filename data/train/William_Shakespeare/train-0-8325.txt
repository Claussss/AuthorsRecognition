CASSIO (laughing) Alas, poor caitiff! OTHELLO (aside) Look how he laughs already. I never knew a woman love man so. Alas, poor rogue! I think i’faith she loves me. OTHELLO (aside) Now he denies it faintly, and laughs it out. Do you hear, Cassio? OTHELLO (aside) Now he importunes him To tell it o’er. Go to, well said, well said.
