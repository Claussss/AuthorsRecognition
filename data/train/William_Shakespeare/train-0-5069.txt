Enter Master Slender SLENDER Whoa, ho, ho, father Page! PAGE Son, how now? How now, son? Have you dispatched? SLENDER Dispatched? I’ll make the best in Gloucestershire know on’t; would I were hanged, la, else. PAGE Of what, son? SLENDER I came yonder at Eton to marry Mistress Anne Page, and she’s a great lubberly boy. If it had not been i‘th’ church, I would have swinged him, or he should have swinged me. If I did not think it had been Anne Page, would I might never stir; and ’tis a postmaster’s boy.
