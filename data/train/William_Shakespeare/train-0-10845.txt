KENT I will not sleep, my lord, till I have delivered your letter. Exit FOOL If a man’s brains were in’s heels, were’t not in danger of kibes? LEAR Ay, boy. FOOL Then, I prithee, be merry: thy wit shall not go slipshod. LEAR Ha, ha, ha! FOOL Shalt see thy other daughter will use thee kindly, for though she’s as like this as a crab’s like an apple, yet I can tell what I can tell.
