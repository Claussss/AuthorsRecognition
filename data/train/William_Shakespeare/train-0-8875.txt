3.4
Enter Varro’s two Servants, meeting others, all Servants of Timon’s creditors, to wait for his coming out. Then enter Servants of Lucius, Titus, and Hortensius Well met; good morrow, Titus and Hortensius. TITUS’ SERVANT The like to you, kind Varro. HORTENSIUS’ SERVANT Lucius, what, do we meet together?
