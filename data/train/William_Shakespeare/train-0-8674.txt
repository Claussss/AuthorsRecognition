Sc. 19
Enter Regan and Oswald, Gonoril’s steward But are my brother’s powers set forth? OSWALD Ay, madam. Himself in person? OSWALD Madam, with much ado. Your sister is the better soldier. Lord Edmund spake not with your lord at home? OSWALD No, madam. What might import my sister’s letters to him? OSWALD I know not, lady.
