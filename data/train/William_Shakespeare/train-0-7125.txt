NESTOR (aside to Diomedes) O this is well. He rubs the vein of him. DIOMEDES (aside to Nestor) And how his silence drinks up this applause. If I go to him, with my armed fist
I’ll pash him o’er the face. AGAMEMNON O no, you shall not go. An a be proud with me, I’ll feeze his pride.
Let me go to him.
