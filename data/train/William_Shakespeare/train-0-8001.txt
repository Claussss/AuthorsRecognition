DUKE ’Tis well borne up. I have not yet made known to Mariana
A word of this.—What ho, within! Come forth! Enter Mariana (To Mariana) I pray you be acquainted with this maid.
She comes to do you good. ISABELLA I do desire the like. DUKE (to Mariana) Do you persuade yourself that I respect you? Good friar, I know you do, and so have found it.
