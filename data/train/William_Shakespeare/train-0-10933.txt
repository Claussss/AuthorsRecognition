Swithin footed thrice the wold,
A met the night mare and her nine foal,
Bid her alight
And her troth plight,
And aroint thee, witch, aroint thee! KENT (to Lear) How fares your grace? LEAR What’s he? KENT (to Gloucester) Who’s there? What is’t you seek? GLOUCESTER What are you there? Your names?
