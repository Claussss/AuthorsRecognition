PRINCE HARRY Ay, and mark thee too, Jack. SIR JOHN Do so, for it is worth the listening to. These nine in buckram that I told thee of— PRINCE HARRY (aside to Poins) So, two more already. SIR JOHN Their points being broken— POINS aside to the Prince SIR JOHN Began to give me ground. But I followed me close, came in foot and hand, and, with a thought, seven of the eleven I paid.
