SIR ANDREW Faith, so they say, but I think it rather consists of eating and drinking. SIR TOBY Thou’rt a scholar; let us therefore eat and drink. Marian, I say, a stoup of wine. Enter Feste, the clown SIR ANDREW Here comes the fool, i’faith. FESTE How now, my hearts. Did you never see the picture of ‘we three’?
