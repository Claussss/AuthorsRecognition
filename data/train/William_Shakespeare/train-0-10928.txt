Storm still Has his daughters brought him to this pass?
(To Edgar) Couldst thou save nothing? Wouldst thou
give ’em all? FOOL Nay, he reserved a blanket, else we had been all shamed. LEAR (to Edgar) Now all the plagues that in the pendulous air
Hang fated o’er men’s faults light on thy daughters! KENT He hath no daughters, sir.
