NORTHUMBERLAND If I be not, heavens be revenged on me. The hope thereof makes Clifford mourn in steel. WESTMORLAND What, shall we suffer this? Let’s pluck him down.
My heart for anger burns—I cannot brook it. Be patient, gentle Earl of Westmorland. Patience is for poltroons, such as he (indicating York).
He durst not sit there had your father lived.
My gracious lord, here in the Parliament
Let us assail the family of York.
