Enter Apemantus and Fool Stay, stay, here comes the fool with Apemantus.
Let’s ha’ some sport with ’em. VARRO’S SERVANT Hang him, he’ll abuse us. ISIDORE’S SERVANT A plague upon him, dog! VARRO’S SERVANT How dost, fool? APEMANTUS Dost dialogue with thy shadow? VARRO’S SERVANT I speak not to thee. APEMANTUS No, ’tis to thyself, (To Fool) Come away.
