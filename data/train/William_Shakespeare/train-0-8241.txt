CLOWN Why, masters, ha’ your instruments been in Naples, that they speak i’th’ nose thus? MUSICIAN HOW, sir, how? CLOWN Are these, I pray you, wind instruments? MUSICIAN Ay, marry are they, sir. CLOWN O, thereby hangs a tail. MUSICIAN Whereby hangs a tale, sir? CLOWN Marry, sir, by many a wind instrument that I know. But masters, here’s money for you, and the general so likes your music that he desires you, for love’s sake, to make no more noise with it.
