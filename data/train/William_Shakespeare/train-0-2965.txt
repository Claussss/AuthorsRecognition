O husband, God doth know you dined at home,
Where would you had remained until this time,
Free from these slanders and this open shame. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Dined at home?
(To Dromio) Thou villain, what sayst thou? DROMIO OF EPHESUS Sir, sooth to say, you did not dine at home. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS
