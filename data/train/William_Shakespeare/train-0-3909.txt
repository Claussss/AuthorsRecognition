Enter Bottom again, with the ass-head BOTTOM Why do they run away? This is a knavery of them to make me afeard. Enter Snout SNOUT O Bottom, thou art changed. What do I see on thee? BOTTOM What do you see? You see an ass-head of your own, do you? Exit Snout Enter Quince QUINCE Bless thee, Bottom, bless thee. Thou art translated. Exit
