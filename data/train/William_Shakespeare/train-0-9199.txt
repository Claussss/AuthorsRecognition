Near Birnam Wood Shall we well meet them. That way are they coming. Who knows if Donalbain be with his brother? For certain, sir, he is not. I have a file
Of all the gentry. There is Siward’s son,
And many unrough youths that even now 10
Protest their first of manhood. What does the tyrant? Great Dunsinane he strongly fortifies.
Some say he’s mad, others that lesser hate him
Do call it valiant fury; but for certain
He cannot buckle his distempered cause
Within the belt of rule.
