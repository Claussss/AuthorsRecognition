KING EDWARD Huntsman, what sayst thou? Wilt thou go along? Better do so than tarry and be hanged. RICHARD OF GLOUCESTER Come then, away—let’s have no more ado. KING EDWARD Bishop, farewell—shield thee from Warwick’s frown,
And pray that I may repossess the crown. Exeunt 4.7
