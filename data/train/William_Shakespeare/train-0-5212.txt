PRINCE HARRY By heaven, Poins, I feel me much to blame
So idly to profane the precious time,
When tempest of commotion, like the south
Borne with black vapour, doth begin to melt
And drop upon our bare unarmed heads.—
Give me my sword and cloak.—Falstaff, good night. Exeunt Prince Harry and Poins SIR JOHN Now comes in the sweetest morsel of the night,
