mine,

Word of denial in thy labras here,
Word of denial: froth and scum, thou liest. SLENDER (pointing to Nim) By these gloves, then, ’twas he. NIM Be advised, sir, and pass good humours. I will say ’marry, trap with you’ if you run the nuthook’s humour on me. That is the very note of it.
