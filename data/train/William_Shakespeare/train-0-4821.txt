PISTOL He hath studied her well, and translated her will: out of honesty, into English. NIM The anchor is deep. Will that humour pass? SIR JOHN Now, the report goes, she has all the rule of her husband’s purse; he hath a legion of angels. As many devils entertain, and ‘To her, boy!’ say I. NIM The humour rises; it is good. Humour me the angels!
