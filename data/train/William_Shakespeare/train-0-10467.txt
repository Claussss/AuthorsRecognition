ALL THE LORDS You are most welcome home. AUFIDIUS I have not deserved it. But, worthy lords, have you with heed perused
What I have written to you? ALL THE LORDS And grieve to hear’t. What faults he made before the last, I think
Might have found easy fines. But there to end
Where he was to begin, and give away
The benefit of our levies, answering us
With our own charge, making a treaty where
There was a yielding—this admits no excuse.
