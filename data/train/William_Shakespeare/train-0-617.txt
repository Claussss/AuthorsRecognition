TAILOR This is true that I say. An I had thee in place where, thou shouldst know it. GRUMIO I am for thee straight. Take thou the bill, give me thy mete-yard, and spare not me. HORTENSIO Godamercy, Grumio, then he shall have no odds. Well, sir, in brief, the gown is not for me. GRUMIO You are i‘th’ right, sir. ‘Tis for my mistress.
