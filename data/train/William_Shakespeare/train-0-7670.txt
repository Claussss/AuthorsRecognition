Nay then, let’s talk with him; pray call him in. Enter Falkner and Officers FALKNER Bless your honour: a new man, my lord. MORE Why sure this’ not he. FALKNER An your lordship will, the barber shall give you a sample of my head. I am he, in faith, my lord, I am ipse. Why, now thy face is like an honest man’s.
Thou hast played well at this new-cut and won.
