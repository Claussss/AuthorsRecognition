They fight. Douglas kills Blunt. Then enter Hotspur O Douglas, hadst thou fought at Holmedon thus,
I never had triumphed upon a Scot. All’s done, all’s won: here breathless lies the King. HOTSPUR Where? DOUGLAS Here. This, Douglas? No, I know this face full well. A gallant knight he was; his name was Blunt—
Semblably furnished like the King himself.
