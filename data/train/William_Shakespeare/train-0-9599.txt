Enter the Countess, Reynaldo her steward, and behindLavatch her clown 
COUNTESS I will now hear. What say you of this gentlewoman? REYNALDO Madam, the care I have had to even your content I wish might be found in the calendar of my past endeavours, for then we wound our modesty and make foul the clearness of our deservings, when of ourselves we publish them.
