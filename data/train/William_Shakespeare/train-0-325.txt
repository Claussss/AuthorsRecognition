LANCE It’s no matter for that, so she sleep not in her talk. SPEED ‘Item, she is slow in words.’ LANCE O villain, that set this down among her vices! To be slow in words is a woman’s only virtue. I pray thee out with’t, and place it for her chief virtue. SPEED ‘Item, she is proud.’ LANCE Out with that, too. It was Eve’s legacy, and cannot be ta’en from her.
