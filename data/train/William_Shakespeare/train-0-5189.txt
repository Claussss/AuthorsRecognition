MISTRESS QUICKLY By my troth, captain, these are very bitter words. BARDOLPH Be gone, good ensign; this will grow to a brawl anon. Die men like dogs! Give crowns like pins!
Have we not Hiren here? MISTRESS QUICKLY O’ my word, captain, there’s none such here. What the goodyear, do you think I would deny her? For God’s sake, be quiet.
