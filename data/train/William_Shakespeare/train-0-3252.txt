BOYET To make Judas hang himself. HOLOFERNES Begin, sir. You are my elder. BIRON Well followed—Judas was hanged on an elder. HOLOFERNES I will not be put out of countenance. 601 BIRON Because thou hast no face. HOLOFERNES What is this? BOYET A cittern-head. DUMAINE The head of a bodkin. BIRON A death’s face in a ring.
