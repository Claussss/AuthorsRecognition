SECOND GENTLEMAN I do not think he fears death. FIRST GENTLEMAN Sure he does not. He never was so womanish. The cause
He may a little grieve at. SECOND GENTLEMAN The Cardinal is the end of this. FIRST GENTLEMAN ’Tis likely By all conjectures: first, Kildare’s attainder,
Then deputy of Ireland, who, removed,
Earl Surrey was sent thither—and in haste, too,
Lest he should help his father.
