FENTON (within) Who’s within there, ho! MISTRESS QUICKLY Who’s there, I trow?—Come near the house, I pray you. Enter Master Fenton FENTON How now, good woman, how dost thou? MISTRESS QUICKLY The better that it pleases your good worship to ask. FENTON What news? How does pretty Mistress Anne? MISTRESS QUICKLY In truth, sir, and she is pretty, and honest, and gentle, and one that is your friend. I can tell you that by the way, I praise heaven for it.
