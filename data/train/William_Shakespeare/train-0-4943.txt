MISTRESS FORD Why, what have you to do whither they bear it? You were best meddle with buck-washing! FORD Buck? I would I could wash myself of the buck! Buck, buck, buck? Ay, buck, I warrant you, buck. And of the season too, it shall appear. Exeunt John and Robert, with the basket
Gentlemen, I have dreamt tonight. I’ll tell you my dream. Here, here, here be my keys. Ascend my chambers, search, seek, find out. I’ll warrant we’ll unkennel the fox. Let me stop this way first.
He locks the door
