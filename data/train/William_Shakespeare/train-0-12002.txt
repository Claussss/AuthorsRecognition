Enter four Country People, one of whom carries a garland before them. Arcite stands apart FIRST COUNTRYMAN My masters, I’ll be there—that’s certain. SECOND COUNTRYMAN And I’ll be there. THIRD COUNTRYMAN And I. FOURTH COUNTRYMAN Why then, have with ye, boys! ’Tis but a chiding—
Let the plough play today, I’ll tickle’t out
Of the jades’ tails tomorrow.
