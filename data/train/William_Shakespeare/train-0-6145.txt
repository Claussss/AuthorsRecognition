OLIVER Call him in. ’Twill be a good way. And tomorrow the wrestling is. Enter Charles CHARLES Good morrow to your worship. OLIVER Good Monsieur Charles—what’s the new news at the new court? CHARLES There’s no news at the court, sir, but the old news: that is, the old Duke is banished by his younger brother, the new Duke, and three or four loving lords have put themselves into voluntary exile with him, whose lands and revenues enrich the new Duke; therefore he gives them good leave to wander.
