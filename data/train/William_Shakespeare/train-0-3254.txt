HOLOFERNES You have put me out of countenance. BIRON False, we have given thee faces. HOLOFERNES But you have outfaced them all. An thou wert a lion, we would do so. Therefore, as he is an ass, let him go.
And so adieu, sweet Jude. Nay, why dost thou stay? DUMAINE For the latter end of his name. For the ass to the Jude. Give it him. Jud-as, away.
