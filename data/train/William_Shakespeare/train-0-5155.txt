POINS The reason? PRINCE HARRY What wouldst thou think of me if I should weep? POINS I would think thee a most princely hypocrite. PRINCE HARRY It would be every man’s thought, and thou art a blessed fellow to think as every man thinks. Never a man’s thought in the world keeps the roadway better than thine. Every man would think me an hypocrite indeed. And what accites your most worshipful thought to think so?
