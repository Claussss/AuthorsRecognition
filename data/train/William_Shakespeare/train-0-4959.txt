ANNE Good Master Shallow, let him woo for himself. SHALLOW Marry, I thank you for it, I thank you for that good comfort.—She calls you, coz. I’ll leave you. He stands aside ANNE Now, Master Slender. SLENDER Now, good Mistress Anne. ANNE What is your will? SLENDER My will? ‘Od’s heartlings, that’s a pretty jest indeed! I ne’er made my will yet, I thank God; I am not such a sickly creature, I give God praise.
