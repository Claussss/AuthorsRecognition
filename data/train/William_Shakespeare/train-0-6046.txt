FIRST PLEBEIAN Tear him to pieces! He’s a conspirator. CINNA I am Cinna the poet, I am Cinna the poet. FOURTH PLEBEIAN Tear him for his bad verses, tear him for his bad verses. CINNA I am not Cinna the conspirator. FOURTH PLEBEIAN It is no matter, his name’s Cinna. Pluck but his name out of his heart, and turn him going.
