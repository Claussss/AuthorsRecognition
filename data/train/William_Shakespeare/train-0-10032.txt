PANDER Three or four thousand chequins were as pretty a proportion to live quietly, and so give over. BAWD Why to give over, I pray you? Is it a shame to get when we are old? PANDER O, our credit comes not in like the commodity, nor the commodity wages not with the danger. Therefore if in our youths we could pick up some pretty estate, ‘twere not amiss to keep our door hatched. Besides, the sore terms we stand upon with the gods will be strong with us for giving o’er.
