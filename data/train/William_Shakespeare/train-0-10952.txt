Exit Oswald GONERIL Farewell, sweet lord, and sister. Edmond, farewell. Exeunt Goneril and Edmond (To Servants) Go seek the traitor Gloucester.
Pinion him like a thief; bring him before us. Exeunt other Servants Though well we may not pass upon his life
Without the form of justice, yet our power
Shall do a curtsy to our wrath, which men
May blame but not control.
