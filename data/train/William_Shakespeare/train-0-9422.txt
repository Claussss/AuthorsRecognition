He sits down. Enter Cleopatra led by Charmian, Iras, and Eros Nay, gentle madam, to him. Comfort him. IRAS Do, most dear Queen. CHARMIAN Do. Why, what else? CLEOPATRA Let me sit down. O Juno! She sits down ANTONY No, no, no, no, no. EROS (to Antony) See you here, sir? ANTONY O fie, fie, fie!
