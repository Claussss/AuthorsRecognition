Here is thy fee: arrest him, officer.
I would not spare my brother in this case
If he should scorn me so apparently. OFFICER (to Antipholus) I do arrest you, sir. You hear the suit. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS I do obey thee till I give thee bail.
(To
Angelo) But, sirrah, you shall buy this sport as dear
As all the metal in your shop will answer.
