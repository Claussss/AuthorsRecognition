HAMLET My ex’llent good friends. How dost thou, Guildenstern? Ah, Rosencrantz—good lads, how do ye both? ROSENCRANTZ As the indifferent children of the earth. GUILDENSTERN Happy in that we are not over-happy,
On Fortune’s cap we are not the very button. HAMLET Nor the soles of her shoe? ROSENCRANTZ Neither, my lord.
