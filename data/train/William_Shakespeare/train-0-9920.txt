PERICLES (aside) Simonides? THIRD FISHERMAN We would purge the land of these drones that rob the bee of her honey. PERICLES (aside) How from the finny subject of the sea
These fishers tell th’infirmities of men,
And from their wat’ry empire recollect
All that may men approve or men detect!
Coming forward Peace be at your labour, honest
fishermen.
