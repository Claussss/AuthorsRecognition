THIRD LORD Will’t hold, will’t hold? SECOND LORD It does; but time will—and so— THIRD LORD I do conceive. 64 TIMON Each man to his stool with that spur as he would to the lip of his mistress. Your diet shall be in all places alike. Make not a city feast of it, to let the meat cool ere we can agree upon the first place. Sit, sit. The gods require our thanks.
