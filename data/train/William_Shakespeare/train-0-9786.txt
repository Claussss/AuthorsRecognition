INTERPRETER Well, is this captain in the Duke of Florence’s camp? PAROLES Upon my knowledge he is, and lousy. 199 FIRST LORD DUMAINE (aside) Nay, look not so upon me: we shall hear of your lordship anon. INTERPRETER What is his reputation with the Duke? PAROLES The Duke knows him for no other but a poor officer of mine, and writ to me this other day to turn him out o’th’ band. I think I have his letter in my pocket. 206 INTERPRETER Marry, we’ll search.
