Come, quick, quick, that I may lay my head in thy lap. LADY PERCY (sitting) Go, ye giddy goose! Hotspur sits, resting his head on Lady Percy’s lap. The music plays Now I perceive the devil understands Welsh; And ’tis no marvel, he is so humorous. By’r Lady, he’s a good musician. Then should you be nothing but musical,
For you are altogether governed by humours.
Lie still, ye thief, and hear the lady sing in Welsh.
