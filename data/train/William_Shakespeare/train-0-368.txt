Enter Proteus, with Julia dressed as a page-boy PROTEUS (to Julia) Sebastian is thy name? I like thee well,
And will employ thee in some service presently. In what you please. I’ll do what I can. I hope thou wilt.—How now, you whoreson peasant,
Where have you been these two days loitering? LANCE Marry, sir, I carried Mistress Silvia the dog you bade me.
