THALIART My lord, ’tis done. ANTIOCHUS Enough. Enter a Messenger hastily Let your breath cool yourself, telling your haste. Your majesty, Prince Pericles is fled. Exit ANTIOCHUS (to Thaliart) As thou wilt live, fly after; like an arrow
Shot from a well-experienced archer hits
The mark his eye doth level at, so thou
Never return unless it be to say
‘Your majesty, Prince Pericles is dead.’
