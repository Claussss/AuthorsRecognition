PATROCLUS Why, thou damnable box of envy thou, what mean’st thou to curse thus? THERSITES Do I curse thee? PATROCLUS Why, no, you ruinous butt, you whoreson indistinguishable cur, no. THERSITES No? Why art thou then exasperate? Thou idle immaterial skein of sleave-silk, thou green sarsenet flap for a sore eye, thou tassel of a prodigal’s purse, thou! Ah, how the poor world is pestered with such waterflies! Diminutives of nature.
