HECTOR Is this Achilles? ACHILLES I am Achilles. Stand fair, I pray thee, let me look on thee. Behold thy fill. HECTOR Nay, I have done already. Thou art too brief. I will the second time,
As I would buy thee, view thee limb by limb. O, like a book of sport thou‘lt read me o’er.
But there’s more in me than thou understand’st.
Why dost thou so oppress me with thine eye?
