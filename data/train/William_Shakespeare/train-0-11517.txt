STEFANO I prithee now, lead the way without any more talking.—Trinculo, the King and all our company else being drowned, we will inherit here.—Here, bear my bottte.—Fettow Trinculo, we’ll fill him by and by again. CALIBAN (sings drunkenly) Farewell, master, farewell, farewell! TRINCULO A howling monster, a drunken monster!
