Not her that chides, sir, at any hand, I pray. I love no chiders, sir. Biondello, let’s away. LUCENTIO (aside) Well begun, Tranio. HORTENSIO Sir, a word ere you go. Are you a suitor to the maid you talk of—yea or no? And if I be, sir, is it any offence? No, if without more words you will get you hence.
