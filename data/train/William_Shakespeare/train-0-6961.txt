MALVOLIO Sir Topas, Sir Topas! SIR TOBY My most exquisite Sir Topas. FESTE Nay, I am for all waters. MARIA Thou mightst have done this without thy beard and gown, he sees thee not. SIR TOBY (to Feste) To him in thine own voice, and bring me word how thou findest him. I would we were well rid of this knavery. If he may be conveniently delivered, I would he were, for I am now so far in offence with my niece that I cannot pursue with any safety this sport to the upshot. To Maria
