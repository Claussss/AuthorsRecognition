SIR TOBY What, what! MALVOLIO ‘You must amend your drunkenness.’ SIR TOBY Out, scab. FABIAN Nay, patience, or we break the sinews of our plot. MALVOLIO ‘Besides, you waste the treasure of your time with a foolish knight’— SIR ANDREW That’s me, I warrant you. MALVOLIO ‘One Sir Andrew.’ SIR ANDREW I knew ’twas I, for many do call me fool.
