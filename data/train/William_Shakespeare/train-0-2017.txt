I’d weigh it lightly, were it heavier. RICHARD GLOUCESTER What, would you have my weapon, little lord? I would, that I might thank you as you call me. RICHARD GLOUCESTER HOW? YORK Little. PRINCE EDWARD My lord of York will still be cross in talk.—
Uncle, your grace knows how to bear with him. You mean to bear me, not to bear with me.—
Uncle, my brother mocks both you and me.
Because that I am little like an ape,
He thinks that you should bear me on your shoulders.
