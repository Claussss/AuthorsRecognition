OLD SHEPHERD Nay, but hear me. CLOWN Nay, but hear me. OLD SHEPHERD Go to, then. CLOWN She being none of your flesh and blood, your flesh and blood has not offended the King, and so your flesh and blood is not to be punished by him. Show those things you found about her, those secret things, all but what she has with her. This being done, let the law go whistle, I warrant you.
