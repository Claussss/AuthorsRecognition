APEMANTUS Ay. TIMON Wherefore? APEMANTUS That I had no augury but to be a lord.-Art not thou a merchant? MERCHANT Ay, Apemantus. Traffic confound thee, if the gods will not! MERCHANT If traffic do it, the gods do it. Traffic’s thy god, and thy god confound thee! Trumpet sounds. Enter a Messenger TIMON What trumpet’s that?
