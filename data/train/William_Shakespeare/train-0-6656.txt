SECOND CLOWN ‘Who builds stronger than a mason, a shipwright, or a carpenter?’ FIRST CLOWN Ay, tell me that, and unyoke. SECOND CLOWN Marry, now I can tell. FIRST CLOWN To’t. SECOND CLOWN Mass, I cannot tell. Enter Prince Hamlet and Horatio afar off FIRST CLOWN Cudgel thy brains no more about it, for your dull ass will not mend his pace with beating; and when you are asked this question next, say ‘a grave-maker’; the houses that he makes lasts till doomsday. Go, get thee to Johan. Fetch me a stoup of liquor.
