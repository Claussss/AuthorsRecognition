ARMADO And so farewell. JAQUENETTA Fair weather after you. Exeunt Dull and Jaquenetta ARMADO Villain, thou shalt fast for thy offences ere thou be pardoned. COSTARD Well, sir, I hope when I do it I shall do it on a full stomach. ARMADO Thou shalt be heavily punished. COSTARD I am more bound to you than your fellows, for they are but lightly rewarded.
