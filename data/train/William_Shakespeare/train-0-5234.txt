SIR JOHN Go to, peace, Mouldy. You shall go, Mouldy; it is time you were spent. MOULDY Spent? SHALLOW Peace, fellow, peace. Stand aside; know you where you are? 119 Mouldy stands aside For th‘other, Sir John, let me see: ‘Simon Shadow’— SIR JOHN Yea, marry, let me have him to sit under. He’s like to be a cold soldier.
