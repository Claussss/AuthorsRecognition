Vernon, and Exeter My lord of York, I promise you, the King
Prettily, methought, did play the orator. RICHARD DUKE OF YORK And so he did; but yet I like it not
In that he wears the badge of Somerset. Tush, that was but his fancy; blame him not.
I dare presume, sweet Prince, he thought no harm.
