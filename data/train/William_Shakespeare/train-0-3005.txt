DROMIO OF EPHESUS (pointing out the Courtesan) Sir, he dined with her there, at the Porcupine. He did, and from my finger snatched that ring. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS ’Tis true, my liege, this ring I had of her. DUKE (to Courtesan) Saw’st thou him enter at the abbey here? As sure, my liege, as I do see your grace.
