HOLOFERNES Quis, quis, thou consonant? MOTE The last of the five vowels if you repeat them, or the fifth if I. HOLOFERNES I will repeat them: a, e, i— MOTE The sheep. The other two concludes it: o, u. ARMADO Now by the salt wave of the Mediterraneum a sweet touch, a quick venue of wit; snip, snap, quick, and home. It rejoiceth my intellect—true wit.
