SIR JOHN Health and long life to you, Master Silence! He drinks SILENCE Fill the cup and let it come. I’ll pledge you a mile to th’ bottom. SHALLOW Honest Bardolph, welcome! If thou want’st anything and wilt not call, beshrew thy heart! (To the Page) Welcome, my little tiny thief, and welcome indeed, too!-I’ll drink to Master Bardolph, and to all the cavalieros about London.
