Flourish. Alarums  (To the women) Either be patient and entreat me fair,
Or with the clamorous report of war
Thus will I drown your exclamations. DUCHESS OF YORK Art thou my son? KING RICHARD Ay, I thank God, my father, and yourself. DUCHESS OF YORK Then patiently hear my impatience. KING RICHARD Madam, I have a touch of your condition,
That cannot brook the accent of reproof.
