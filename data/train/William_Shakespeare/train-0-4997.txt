MISTRESS FORD But is my husband coming? MISTRESS PAGE Ay, in good sadness is he, and talks of the basket too, howsoever he hath had intelligence. MISTRESS FORD We’ll try that, for I’ll appoint my men to carry the basket again, to meet him at the door with it as they did last time. MISTRESS PAGE Nay, but he’ll be here presently. Let’s go dress him like the witch of Brentford.
