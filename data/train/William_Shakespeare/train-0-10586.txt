How, ‘gone’? Apollo’s angry, and the heavens themselves
Do strike at my injustice. Hermione falls to the ground How now there? This news is mortal to the Queen. Look down
And see what death is doing. Take her hence. Her heart is but o’ercharged. She will recover.
I have too much believed mine own suspicion.
Beseech you, tenderly apply to her
Some remedies for life.
