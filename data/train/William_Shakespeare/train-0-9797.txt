INTERPRETER If your life be saved will you undertake to betray the Florentine? 295 PAROLES Ay, and the captain of his horse, Count Roussillon. INTERPRETER I’ll whisper with the general and know his pleasure. PAROLES I’ll no more drumming. A plague of all drums! Only to seem to deserve well, and to beguile the supposition of that lascivious young boy, the Count, have I run into this danger. Yet who would have suspected an ambush where I was taken?
