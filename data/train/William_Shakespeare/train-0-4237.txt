5.4 Alarum.Enter the Earls of Salisbury and Pembroke, and Lord Bigot I did not think the King so stored with friends. Up once again; put spirit in the French.
If they miscarry, we miscarry too. That misbegotten devil Falconbridge,
In spite of spite, alone upholds the day. They say King John, sore sick, hath left the field.
