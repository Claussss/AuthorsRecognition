ROBIN (aside) What hempen homespuns have we swagg’ring here
So near the cradle of the Fairy Queen?
What, a play toward? I’ll be an auditor—
An actor, too, perhaps, if I see cause. QUINCE Speak, Pyramus. Thisbe, stand forth. BOTTOM (as Pyramus) Thisbe, the flowers of odious savours sweet. QUINCE Odours, odours.
