VARRO’S SERVANT Thou art not altogether a fool. FOOL Nor thou altogether a wise man. As much foolery as I have, so much wit thou lack ’st. APEMANTUS That answer might have become Apemantus. Enter Timon and Flavius ALL SERVANTS Aside, aside, here comes Lord Timon. APEMANTUS Come with me, fool, come. FOOL I do not always follow lover, elder brother, and woman: sometime the philosopher.
