TOUCHSTONE (to Corin) Holla, you clown! ROSALIND Peace, fool, he’s not thy kinsman. CORIN Who calls? TOUCHSTONE Your betters, sir. CORIN Else are they very wretched. ROSALIND (to Touchstone) Peace, I say. (To Corin) Good even to you, friend. And to you, gentle sir, and to you all. I prithee, shepherd, if that love or gold
Can in this desert place buy entertainment,
Bring us where we may rest ourselves, and feed.
Here’s a young maid with travel much oppressed,
And faints for succour.
