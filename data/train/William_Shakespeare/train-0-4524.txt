PRINCE HARRY Where shall we take a purse tomorrow, Jack? SIR JOHN Zounds, where thou wilt, lad! I’ll make one; an I do not, call me villain and baffle me. PRINCE HARRY I see a good amendment of life in thee, from praying to purse-taking. SIR JOHN Why, Hal, ‘tis my vocation, Hal. ’Tis no sin for a man to labour in his vocation.
