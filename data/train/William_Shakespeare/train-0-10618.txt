CLOWN Out upon him! Prig, for my life, prig! He haunts wakes, fairs, and bear-baitings. AUTOLYCUS Very true, sir. He, sir, he. That’s the rogue that put me into this apparel. CLOWN Not a more cowardly rogue in all Bohemia. If you had but looked big and spit at him, he’d have run. AUTOLYCUS I must confess to you, sir, I am no fighter. I am false of heart that way, and that he knew, I warrant him.
