LUCIO But after all this fooling, I would not have it so. Art thou sure of this? MISTRESS OVERDONE I am too sure of it, and it is for getting Madame Julietta with child. LUCIO Believe me, this may be. He promised to meet me two hours since and he was ever precise in promise-keeping.
