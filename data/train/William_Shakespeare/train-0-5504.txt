CLAUDIO You must hang it first and draw it afterwards. DON PEDRO What? Sigh for the toothache? LEONATO Where is but a humour or a worm. BENEDICK Well, everyone can master a grief but he that has it. CLAUDIO Yet say I he is in love. DON PEDRO There is no appearance of fancy in him, unless it be a fancy that he hath to strange disguises, as to be a Dutchman today, a Frenchman tomorrow, or in the shape of two countries at once, as a German from the waist downward, all slops, and a Spaniard from the hip upward, no doublet. Unless he have a fancy to this foolery, as it appears he hath, he is no fool for fancy, as you would have it appear he is.
