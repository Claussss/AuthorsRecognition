STEFANO Drink, servant monster, when I bid thee. Thy eyes are almost set in thy head. TRINCULO Where should they be set else? He were a brave monster indeed if they were set in his tail. STEFANO My man-monster hath drowned his tongue in sack. For my part, the sea cannot drown me. I swam, ere I could recover the shore, five and thirty leagues, off and on. By this light, thou shalt be my lieutenant, monster, or my standard.
