EVANS But that is not the question. The question is concerning your marriage. SHALLOW Ay, there’s the point, sir. EVANS Marry, is it, the very point of it—to Mistress Anne Page. SLENDER Why, if it be so, I will marry her upon any reasonable demands. EVANS But can you affection the ’oman ? Let us command to know that of your mouth, or of your lips—for divers philosophers hold that the lips is parcel of the mouth. Therefore, precisely, can you carry your good will to the maid?
