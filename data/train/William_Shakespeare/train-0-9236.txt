Flourish. Enter Antony, Cleopatra, her ladies, the train, with eunuchs fanning her Look where they come. Take but good note, and you shall see in him
The triple pillar of the world transformed
Into a strumpet’s fool. Behold and see. CLEOPATRA (to Antony) If it be love indeed, tell me how much. There’s beggary in the love that can be reckoned.
