But my deeds, Suffolk, soon shall stay thy rage. I go of message from the Queen to France—
I charge thee, waft me safely cross the Channel! CAPTAIN Walter— Come, Suffolk, I must waft thee to thy death. Paene gelidus timor occupat artus—
It is thee I fear. Thou shalt have cause to fear before I leave thee.
What, are ye daunted now? Now will ye stoop?
