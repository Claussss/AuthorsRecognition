Enter Cominius Here is Cominius. I have been i‘th’ market-place; and, sir, ’tis fit
You make strong party, or defend yourself
By calmness or by absence. All’s in anger. Only fair speech. COMINIUS I think ’twill serve, if he Can thereto frame his spirit. VOLUMNIA He must, and will. Prithee now, say you will, and go about it.
