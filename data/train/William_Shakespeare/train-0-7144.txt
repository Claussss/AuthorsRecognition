HELEN She shall have it, my lord—if it be not my lord Paris. PANDARUS He? No, she’ll none of him. They two are twain. HELEN Falling in, after falling out, may make them three. PANDARUS Come, come, I’ll hear no more of this. I’ll sing you a song now. HELEN Ay, ay, prithee. Now by my troth, sweet lord, thou hast a fine forehead.
