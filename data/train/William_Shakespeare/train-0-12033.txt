Enter Arcite with a bundle containing meat, wine, and
files I should be near the place. Ho, cousin Patamon! Enter Palamon as from the bush The same. I have brought you food and files. Come forth and fear not, here’s no Theseus. Nor none so honest, Arcite. That’s no matter—We’ll argue that hereafter. Come, take courage—
You shall not die thus beastly. Here, sir, drink;
I know you are faint. Then I’ll talk further with you.
