EDGAR Hail, gentle sir. Do you hear aught, sir, of a battle toward? Most sure and vulgar, everyone hears that
That can distinguish sound. EDGAR But, by your favour, How near’s the other army? Near and on speedy foot. The main descry
Stands in the hourly thought. I thank you, sir. That’s all. Though that the Queen on special cause is here,
Her army is moved on.
