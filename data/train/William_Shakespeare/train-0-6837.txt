SIR ANDREW Before me, she’s a good wench. SIR TOBY She’s a beagle true bred, and one that adores me. What o’ that? SIR ANDREW I was adored once, too. SIR TOBY Let’s to bed, knight. Thou hadst need send for more money. SIR ANDREW If I cannot recover your niece, I am a foul way out. SIR TOBY Send for money, knight. If thou hast her not i’th’ end, call me cut.
