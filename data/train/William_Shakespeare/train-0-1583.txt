5.6
Enter Richard Duke of York, the Earl of Warwick, and a Shepherd 
RICHARD DUKE OF YORK Bring forth that sorceress condemned to burn. Enter Joan la Pucelle guarded Ah, Joan, this kills thy father’s heart outright.
Have I sought every country far and near,
And now it is my chance to find thee out
Must I behold thy timeless cruel death?
Ah Joan, sweet daughter Joan, I’ll die with thee.
