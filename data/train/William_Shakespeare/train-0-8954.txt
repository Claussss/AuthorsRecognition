APEMANTUS Would ’twere so, But not till I am dead. I’ll say thou’st gold.
Thou wilt be thronged to shortly. TIMON Thronged to? APEMANTUS Ay. Thy back, I prithee. APEMANTUS Live, and love thy misery. Long live so, and so die. I am quit. Enter the Banditti, thieves More things like men. Eat, Timon, and abhor them.
