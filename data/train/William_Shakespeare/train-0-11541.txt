STEFANO Give me thy hand. I am sorry I beat thee. But while thou liv’st, keep a good tongue in thy head. Within this half hour will he be asleep.
Wilt thou destroy him then? STEFANO Ay, on mine honour. ARIEL (aside) This will I tell my master. Thou mak’st me merry; I am full of pleasure.
Let us be jocund. Will you troll the catch
You taught me but while-ere?
