PETER That my master was ? No, forsooth, my master said that he was and that the King was an usurer. QUEEN MARGARET An usurper thou wouldst say. PETER Ay, forsooth—an usurper. SUFFOLK (calling within) Who is there? Enter a servant Take this fellow in and send for his master with a pursuivant presently. (To Peter) We’ll hear more of your matter before the King. Exit the servant with Peter
