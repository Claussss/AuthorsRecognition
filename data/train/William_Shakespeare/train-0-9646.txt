COUNTESS To be young again, if we could! I will be a fool in question, hoping to be the wiser by your answer. I pray you, sir, are you a courtier? LAVATCH O Lord, sir!—There’s a simple putting off. More, more, a hundred of them. COUNTESS Sir, I am a poor friend of yours that loves you. LAVATCH O Lord, sir!—Thick, thick, spare not me.
