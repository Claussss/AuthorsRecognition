FIRST GENTLEMAN Never greater, Nor, I’ll assure you, better taken, sir. SECOND GENTLEMAN May I be bold to ask what that contains,
That paper in your hand? FIRST GENTLEMAN Yes, ’tis the list Of those that claim their offices this day
By custom of the coronation.
The Duke of Suffolk is the first, and claims
To be High Steward; next, the Duke of Norfolk,
He to be Earl Marshal. You may read the rest.
