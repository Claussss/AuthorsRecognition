LADY MACDUFF Why, the honest men. MACDUFF’S SON Then the liars and swearers are fools, for there are liars and swearers enough to beat the honest men and hang up them. LADY MACDUFF Now God help thee, poor monkey! But how wilt thou do for a father? MACDUFF’S SON If he were dead you’d weep for him. If you would not, it were a good sign that I should quickly have a new father.
