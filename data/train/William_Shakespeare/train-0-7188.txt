PATROCLUS (to Thersites) Jove bless great Ajax! THERSITES H’m. PATROCLUS I come from the worthy Achilles— THERSITES Ha? PATROCLUS Who most humbly desires you to invite Hector to his tent— THERSITES H’m! PATROCLUS And to procure safe-conduct from Agamemnon. THERSITES Agamemnon? PATROCLUS Ay, my lord.
