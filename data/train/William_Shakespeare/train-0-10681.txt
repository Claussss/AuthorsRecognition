AUTOLYCUS I am a poor fellow, sir. (Aside) I know ye well enough. CAMILLO Nay prithee, dispatch—the gentleman is half flayed already. AUTOLYCUS Are you in earnest, sir? (Aside) I smell the trick on’t. FLORIZEL Dispatch, I prithee. AUTOLYCUS Indeed, I have had earnest, but I cannot with conscience take it.
