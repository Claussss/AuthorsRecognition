1.1
Enter Samson and Gregory, of the house of Capulet, with swords and
bucklers 
SAMSON Gregory, on my word, we’ll not carry coals. GREGORY No, for then we should be colliers. SAMSON I mean an we be in choler, we’ll draw. GREGORY Ay, while you live, draw your neck out of collar. SAMSON I strike quickly, being moved.
