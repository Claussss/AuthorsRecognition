ACHILLES Why, but he is not in this tune, is he? THERSITES No, but he’s out o’ tune thus. What music will be in him when Hector has knocked out his brains, I know not. But I am feared none, unless the fiddler Apollo get his sinews to make catlings on. Come, thou shalt bear a letter to him straight. THERSITES Let me carry another to his horse, for that’s the more capable creature.
