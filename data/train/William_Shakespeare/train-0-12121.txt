(Sings) ‘I will be true, my stars, my fate . . .’ Exit Daughter JAILER (to the Doctor) What think you of her, sir? DOCTOR I think she has a perturbed mind, which I cannot minister to. JAILER Alas, what then? DOCTOR Understand you she ever affected any man ere she beheld Palamon? JAILER I was once, sir, in great hope she had fixed her liking on this gentleman, my friend.
