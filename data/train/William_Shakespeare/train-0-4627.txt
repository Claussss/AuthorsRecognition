PRINCE HARRY (bowing) Well, here is my leg. SIR JOHN And here is my speech. (To Harvey, Poins, and Gadshill) Stand aside, nobility. HOSTESS O Jesu, this is excellent sport, i’faith. SIR JOHN Weep not, sweet Queen, for trickling tears are vain. HOSTESS O the Father, how he holds his countenance! For God’s sake, lords, convey my tristful Queen,
For tears do stop the floodgates of her eyes.
