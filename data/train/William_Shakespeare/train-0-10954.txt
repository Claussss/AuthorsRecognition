Unmerciful lady as you are, I’m none. CORNWALL (to Servants) To this chair bind him. (To Gloucester) Villain, thou shalt find— Regan plucks Gloucester’s beard By the kind gods, ’tis most ignobly done,
To pluck me by the beard. REGAN So white, and such a traitor? GLOUCESTER Naughty lady, These hairs which thou dost ravish from my chin
Will quicken and accuse thee. I am your host.
With robbers’ hands my hospitable favours
You should not ruffle thus. What will you do?
