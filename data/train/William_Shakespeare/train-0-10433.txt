SECOND WATCHMAN ’Tis a spell, you see, of much power. You know the way home again. FIRST WATCHMAN Do you hear how we are shent for keeping your greatness back? SECOND WATCHMAN What cause do you think I have to swoon? MENENIUS I neither care for th’ world nor your general. For such things as you, I can scarce think there’s any, you’re so slight. He that hath a will to die by himself fears it not from another. Let your general do his worst. For you, be that you are long, and your misery increase with your age. I say to you as I was said to, ‘Away!’
