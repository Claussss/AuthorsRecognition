CORNWALL Get horses for your mistress. Exit Oswald GONORIL Farewell, sweet lord, and sister. Edmund, farewell. Exeunt Gonoril and Edmund
(To Servants) Go seek the traitor Gloucester.
Pinion him like a thief; bring him before us. Exeunt other Servants Though we may not pass upon his life
Without the form of justice, yet our power
Shall do a curtsy to our wrath, which men
May blame but not control. Who’s there—the traitor?
