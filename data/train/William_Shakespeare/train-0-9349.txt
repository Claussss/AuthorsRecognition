CAESAR, ANTONY, and LEPIDUS Show’s the way, sir. Exeunt all but Enobarbus and Menas MENAS (aside) Thy father, Pompey, would ne’er have made this
treaty.
(To Enobarbus) You and I have known, sir. ENOBARBUS At sea, I think. MENAS We have, sir. ENOBARBUS You have done well by water. MENAS And you by land.
