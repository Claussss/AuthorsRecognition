CLAUDIO If he be, he knows how to turn his girdle. BENEDICK (aside to Claudio) Shall I speak a word in your ear? CLAUDIO God bless me from a challenge. BENEDICK You are a villain. I jest not. I will make it good how you dare, with what you dare, and when you dare. Do me right, or I will protest your cowardice. You have killed a sweet lady, and her death shall fall heavy on you. Let me hear from you. 149
