She curtsies to the King and begins to depart CARDINAL CAMPEIUS The Queen is obstinate, Stubborn to justice, apt to accuse it, and
Disdainful to be tried by’t. ’Tis not well.
She’s going away. KING HENRY (to the Crier) Call her again. Katherine, Queen of England, come into the court. GRIFFITH (to the Queen) Madam, you are called back.
