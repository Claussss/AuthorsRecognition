POSTHUMUS You are a great deal abused in too bold a persuasion, and I doubt not you sustain what you’re worthy of by your attempt. GIACOMO What’s that? POSTHUMUS A repulse; though your attempt, as you call it, deserve more—a punishment, too. FILARIO Gentlemen, enough of this. It came in too suddenly. Let it die as it was born; and, I pray you, be better acquainted.
