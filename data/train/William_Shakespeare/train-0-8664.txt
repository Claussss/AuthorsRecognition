Enter the Earl of Kent disguised, and FirstGentleman 
KENT Why the King of France is so suddenly gone back know you no reason? Something he left imperfect in the state
Which, since his coming forth, is thought of; which
Imports to the kingdom so much fear and danger
That his personal return was most required
And necessary.
