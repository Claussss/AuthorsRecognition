Sc. 15
Enter Butler, Brewer, Porter, and Horse-keeper, several ways 
BUTLER Robin Brewer, how now, man? What cheer, what cheer? BREWER Faith, Ned Butler, sick of thy disease, and these our other fellows here, Ralph Horse-keeper and Giles Porter: sad, sad. They say my lord goes to his trial today. HORSE-KEEPER To it, man? Why, he is now at it. God send him well to speed!
