KING SIMONIDES Thou liest like a traitor. PERICLES Traitor? KING SIMONIDES Ay, traitor, That thus disguised art stol’n into my court
With witchcraft of thy actions to bewitch
The yielding spirit of my tender child. 50 PERICLES rising Who calls me traitor, unless it be the King,
Ev’n in his bosom I will write the lie.
