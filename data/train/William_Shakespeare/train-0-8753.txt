KENT I am come To bid my king and master aye good night.
Is he not here? ALBANY Great thing of us forgot!— Speak, Edmund; where’s the King, and where’s
Cordelia? The bodies of Gonoril and Regan are brought in Seest thou this object, Kent? KENT Alack, why thus? EDMUND Yet Edmund was beloved. The one the other poisoned for my sake,
