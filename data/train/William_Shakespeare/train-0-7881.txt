ESCALUS How know you that? ELBOW My wife, sir, whom I detest before heaven and your honour— ESCALUS How, thy wife? ELBOW Ay, sir, whom I thank heaven is an honest woman— ESCALUS Dost thou detest her therefor? ELBOW I say, sir, I will detest myself also, as well as she, that this house, if it be not a bawd’s house, it is pity of her life, for it is a naughty house.
