LEAR Because they are not eight. FOOL Yes, indeed, thou wouldst make a good fool. To take’t again perforce—monster ingratitude! FOOL If thou wert my fool, nuncle, I’d have thee beaten for being old before thy time. LEAR How’s that? FOOL Thou shouldst not have been old till thou hadst been wise. O, let me not be mad, not mad, sweet heaven!
Keep me in temper. I would not be mad.
