Methinks the ground is even. EDGAR Horrible steep. Hark, do you hear the sea? GLOUCESTER No, truly. Why, then your other senses grow imperfect
By your eyes’ anguish. GLOUCESTER So may it be indeed. Methinks thy voice is altered, and thou speak’st
With better phrase and matter than thou didst. You’re much deceived. In nothing am I changed
But in my garments.
