SLENDER How does your fallow greyhound, sir? I heard say he was outrun on Cotswold. PAGE It could not be judged, sir. SLENDER You’ll not confess, you’ll not confess. SHALLOW That he will not. ‘Tis your fault, ’tis your fault. (To Page) ’Tis a good dog. PAGE A cur, sir. SHALLOW Sir, he’s a good dog and a fair dog. Can there be more said? He is good and fair. Is Sir John Falstaff here?
