HOSTESS Nay, my lord, he called you ’jack’ and said he would cudgel you. SIR JOHN Did I, Russell? RUSSELL Indeed, Sir John, you said so. SIR JOHN Yea, if he said my ring was copper. PRINCE HARRY I say ’tis copper; darest thou be as good as thy word now? SIR JOHN Why, Hal, thou knowest as thou art but man I dare, but as thou art prince, I fear thee as I fear the roaring of the lion’s whelp.
