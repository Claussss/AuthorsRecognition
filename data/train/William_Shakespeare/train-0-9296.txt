’Tis spoken well. Were we before our armies, and to fight,
I should do thus. Antony and Caesar embrace. CAESAR Welcome to Rome. ANTONY Thank you. CAESAR Sit. ANTONY Sit, sir. CAESAR Nay then. I learn you take things ill which are not so,
Or being, concern you not. I must be laughed at If or for nothing or a little I
Should say myself offended, and with you
Chiefly i’th’ world; more laughed at that I should
Once name you derogately, when to sound your name
It not concerned me.
