
3.12
Enter Caesar, Agrippa,Thidias, and Dolabella, with others Let him appear that’s come from Antony.
Know you him? Caesar, ’tis his schoolmaster; An argument that he is plucked, when hither
He sends so poor a pinion of his wing,
Which had superfluous kings for messengers
Not many moons gone by. Enter Ambassador from Antony
