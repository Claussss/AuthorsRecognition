TRINCULO Your lieutenant if you list; he’s no standard. STEFANO We’ll not run, Monsieur Monster. TRINCULO Nor go neither; but you’ll lie like dogs, and yet say nothing neither. STEFANO Moon-calf, speak once in thy life, if thou beest a good moon-calf. How does thy honour? Let me lick thy shoe.
I’ll not serve him; he is not valiant.
