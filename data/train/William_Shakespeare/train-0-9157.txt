MACDUFF’S SON With what I get, I mean, and so do they. LADY MACDUFF Poor bird, thou’dst never fear the net nor lime,
The pitfall nor the gin. MACDUFF’S SON Why should I, mother? Poor birds they are not set for.
My father is not dead, for all your saying. LADY MACDUFF Yes, he is dead. How wilt thou do for a father?
