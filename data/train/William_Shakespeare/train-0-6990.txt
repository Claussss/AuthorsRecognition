I hate a drunken rogue. Away with him! Who hath made this havoc with them? SIR ANDREW I’ll help you, Sir Toby, because we’ll be dressed together. SIR TOBY Will you help—an ass-head, and a coxcomb, and a knave; a thin-faced knave, a gull? Get him to bed, and let his hurt be looked to. Exeunt Sir Toby, Sir Andrew, Feste, and Fabian
