See, our best elders. FIRST SENATOR Martius, ’tis true that you have lately told us.
The Volsces are in arms. MARTIUS They have a leader, Tullus Aufidius, that will put you to’t.
I sin in envying his nobility,
And were I anything but what I am,
I would wish me only he. You have fought together! Were half to half the world by th’ ears and he
Upon my party, I’d revolt to make
Only my wars with him. He is a lion
That I am proud to hunt.
