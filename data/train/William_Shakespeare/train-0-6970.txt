FABIAN Anything. FESTE Do not desire to see this letter. FABIAN This is to give a dog, and in recompense desire my dog again. Enter the Duke, Viola as Cesario, Curio, and lords Belong you to the Lady Olivia, friends? FESTE Ay, sir, we are some of her trappings. I know thee well. How dost thou, my good fellow? FESTE Truly, sir, the better for my foes and the worse for my friends.
