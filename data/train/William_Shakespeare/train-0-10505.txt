Business, my lord? I think most understand
Bohemia stays here longer. LEONTES Ha? CAMILLO Stays here longer. LEONTES Ay, but why? To satisfy your highness, and the entreaties
Of our most gracious mistress. LEONTES Satisfy? Th‘entreaties of your mistress? Satisfy?
Let that suffice. I have trusted thee, Camillo,
With all the near’st things to my heart, as well
My chamber-counsels, wherein, priest-like, thou
Hast cleansed my bosom, I from thee departed
Thy penitent reformed. But we have been
Deceived in thy integrity, deceived
In that which seems so.
