DIONYZA I have one myself, Who shall not be more dear to my respect
Than yours, my lord. PERICLES Madam, my thanks and prayers. We’ll bring your grace e‘en to the edge o’th’ shore,
Then give you up to th’ masted Neptune and
The gentlest winds of heaven. I will embrace your offer.—Come, dear’st madam.—
O, no tears, Lychorida, no tears.
Look to your little mistress, on whose grace
You may depend hereafter.—Come, my lord. Exeunt
