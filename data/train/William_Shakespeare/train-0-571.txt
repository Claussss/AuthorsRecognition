CURTIS I prithee, good Grumio, tell me—how goes the world? GRUMIO A cold world, Curtis, in every office but thine. And therefore fire, do thy duty, and have thy duty, for my master and mistress are almost frozen to death. CURTIS There’s fire ready, and therefore, good Grumio, the news. GRUMIO Why, ‘Jack boy, ho boy!’, and as much news as wilt thou.
