Venezia, Venezia, Chi non ti vede, chi non ti prezia. Old Mantuan, old Mantuan—who understandeth thee not, loves thee not. (He sings) Ut, re, Sol, la, mi, fa. (To Nathaniel) Under pardon, sir, what are the contents? Or rather, as Horace says in his—what, my soul—verses? NATHANIEL Ay, sir, and very learned.
