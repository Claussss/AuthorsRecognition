KING EDWARD But you will take exceptions to my boon. No, gracious lord, except I cannot do it. KING EDWARD Ay, but thou canst do what I mean to ask. Why, then, I will do what your grace commands. RICHARD OF GLOUCESTER (to George) He plies her hard, and much rain wears the marble. GEORGE OF CLARENCE As red as fire! Nay, then her wax must melt.
