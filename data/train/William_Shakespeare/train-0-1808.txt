These are my ministers, and come with me. Are they thy ministers? What are they called? Rape and Murder, therefore called so
‘Cause they take vengeance of such kind of men. Good Lord, how like the Empress’ sons they are,
And you the Empress! But we worldly men
Have miserable, mad, mistaking eyes.
O sweet Revenge, now do I come to thee,
And if one arm’s embracement will content thee,
I will embrace thee in it by and by. Exit aloft
