TRANIO Gentlemen, God save you. If I may be bold, tell me, I beseech you, which is the readiest way to the house of Signor Baptista Minola? BIONDELLO He that has the two fair daughters—is’t he you mean? TRANIO Even he, Biondello. Hark you, sir, you mean not her to— Perhaps him and her, sir. What have you to do?
