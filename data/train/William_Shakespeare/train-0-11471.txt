GONZALO Not since widow Dido’s time. ANTONIO (to Sebastian) Widow? A pox o‘that! How came that ‘widow’ in? Widow Dido! SEBASTIAN What if he had said ‘widower Aeneas’ too? Good Lord, how you take it! ADRIAN (to Gonzalo) ‘Widow Dido’ said you? You make me study of that: she was of Carthage, not of Tunis.
