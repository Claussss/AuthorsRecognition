MISTRESS PAGE (to John and Robert) You will do it? MISTRESS FORD I ha’ told them over and over; they lack no direction.—Be gone, and come when you are called. Exeunt John and Robert Enter Robin MISTRESS PAGE Here comes little Robin. MISTRESS FORD How now, my eyas-musket, what news with you? ROBIN My master Sir John is come in at your back door, Mistress Ford, and requests your company.
