MERCHANT (to Jeweller) O, ’tis a worthy lord! JEWELLER Nay, that’s most fixed. A most incomparable man, breathed, as it were,
To an untirable and continuate goodness.
He passes. JEWELLER (showing a jewel) I have a jewel here. O, pray, let’s see’t. For the Lord Timon, sir? If he will touch the estimate. But for that—
