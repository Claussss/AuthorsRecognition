DUCHESS OF YORK O let me speak! KING RICHARD Do, then; but I’ll not hear. DUCHESS OF YORK I will be mild and gentle in my words. KING RICHARD And brief, good mother, for I am in haste. DUCHESS OF YORK Art thou so hasty? I have stayed for thee,
God knows, in torment and in agony— KING RICHARD And came I not at last to comfort you?
