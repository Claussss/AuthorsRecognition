Enter the Lord Scales upon the Tower, walking. Enter three or four Citizens below 
SCALES How now? Is Jack Cade slain? FIRST CITIZEN No, my lord Scales, nor likely to be slain, for he and his men have won the bridge, killing all those that did withstand them. The Lord Mayor craveth aid of your honour from the Tower to defend the city from the rebels.
