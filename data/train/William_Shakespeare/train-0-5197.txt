DOLL TEARSHEET I‘faith, and thou followed’st him like a church. Thou whoreson little tidy Bartholomew boar-pig, when wilt thou leave fighting o‘days, and foining o’nights, and begin to patch up thine old body for heaven? Enter Prince Harry and Poins, disguised as drawers SIR JOHN Peace, good Doll, do not speak like a death’s-head, do not bid me remember mine end.
