Biron did swear himself out of all suit. Dumaine was at my service, and his sword. ‘Non point,’ quoth I. My servant straight was mute. Lord Longueville said I came o’er his heart,
And trow you what he called me? ‘Qualm’, perhaps. Yes, in good faith. Go, sickness as thou art. Well, better wits have worn plain statute-caps.
But will you hear? The King is my love sworn.
