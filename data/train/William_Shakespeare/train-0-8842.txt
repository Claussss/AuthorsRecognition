APEMANTUS Canst not read? APEMANTUS There will little learning die then that day thou art hanged. This is to Lord Timon, this to Alcibiades. Go, thou wast born a bastard, and thou’lt die a bawd. PAGE Thou wast whelped a dog, and thou shalt famish a dog’s death. Answer not; I am gone. Exit APEMANTUS E‘en so thou outrunn’st grace. Fool, I will go with you to Lord Timon’s.
