Enter old Gobbo, blind,with a basket GOBBO Master young man, you, I pray you, which is the way to Master Jew’s ? LANCELOT (aside) O heavens, this is my true-begotten father who, being more than sand-blind—high-gravel-blind—knows me not. I will try confusions with him. GOBBO Master young gentleman, I pray you which is the way to Master Jew’s ?
