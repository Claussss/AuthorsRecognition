(Gesturing towards Gardiner) That good fellow, If I command him, follows my appointment.
I will have none so near else. Learn this, brother:
We live not to be griped by meaner persons. KING HENRY (to Gardiner) Deliver this with modesty to th’ Queen. Exit Gardiner The most convenient place that I can think of
For such receipt of learning is Blackfriars;
There ye shall meet about this weighty business.
My Wolsey, see it furnished. O, my lord,
Would it not grieve an able man to leave
So sweet a bedfellow? But conscience, conscience—
O, ’tis a tender place, and I must leave her.
