4.2
Enter Othello and Emilia 
OTHELLO You have seen nothing then? Nor ever heard, nor ever did suspect. Yes, you have seen Cassio and she together. But then I saw no harm, and then I heard
Each syllable that breath made up between ’em. OTHELLO What, did they never whisper? EMILIA Never, my lord. OTHELLO Nor send you out o’th’ way?
