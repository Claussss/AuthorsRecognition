ESCALUS I will go darkly to work with her. LUCIO That’s the way, for women are light at midnight. Enter Isabella, guarded ESCALUS (to Isabella) Come on, mistress, here’s a gentlewoman denies all that you have said. Enter the Duke, disguised as a friar, hooded, and the Provost LUCIO My lord, here comes the rascal I spoke of, here with the Provost.
