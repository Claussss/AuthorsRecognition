DROMIO OF SYRACUSE Nothing, sir, but that I am beaten. ANTIPHOLUS OF SYRACUSE Shall I tell you why? DROMIO OF SYRACUSE Ay, sir, and wherefore; for they say every why hath a wherefore. ANTIPHOLUS OF SYRACUSE ‘Why’ first: for flouting me; and then ‘wherefore’:
For urging it the second time to me. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE
