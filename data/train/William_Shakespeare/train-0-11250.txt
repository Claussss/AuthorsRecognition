Enter Pisanio with Posthumus’ suit Be those the garments? Ay, my noble lord. How long is’t since she went to Milford Haven? PISANIO She can scarce be there yet. CLOTEN Bring this apparel to my chamber. That is the second thing that I have commanded thee. The third is that thou wilt be a voluntary mute to my design. Be but duteous, and true preferment shall tender itself to thee. My revenge is now at Milford. Would I had wings to follow it. Come, and be true. Exit
