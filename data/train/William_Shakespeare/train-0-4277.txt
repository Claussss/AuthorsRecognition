Thanks, i’faith, for silence is only commendable
In a neat’s tongue dried and a maid not vendible. Exeunt Graziano and Lorenzo ANTONIO Yet is that anything now? BASSANIO Graziano speaks an infinite deal of nothing,
more than any man in all Venice. His reasons are as
two grains of wheat hid in two bushels of chaff: you
shall seek all day ere you find them, and when you
have them they are not worth the search.
