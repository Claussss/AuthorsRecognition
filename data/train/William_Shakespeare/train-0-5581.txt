CONRAD Marry, sir, we say we are none. DOGBERRY A marvellous witty fellow, I assure you, but I will go about with him. Come you hither, sirrah. A word in your ear, sir. I say to you it is thought you are false knaves. BORACHIO Sir, I say to you we are none. DOGBERRY Well, stand aside. Fore God, they are both in a tale. Have you writ down that they are none?
