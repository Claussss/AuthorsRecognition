Enter Sir John Oldcastle, with sword and buckler, Russell, Harvey, and Gadshill, followed byFrancis, with wine POINS Welcome, Jack. Where hast thou been? SIR JOHN A plague of all cowards, I say, and a vengeance too, marry and amen!—Give me a cup of sack, boy.—Ere I lead this life long, I’ll sew netherstocks, and mend them and foot them too. A plague of all cowards!—Give me a cup of sack, rogue. Is there no virtue extant?
