GOBBO Your worship’s friend, and Lancelot, sir. LANCELOT But I pray you, ergo old man, ergo I beseech you, talk you of young Master Lancelot? GOBBO Of Lancelot, an’t please your mastership. LANCELOT Ergo Master Lancelot. Talk not of Master Lancelot, father, for the young gentleman, according to fates and destinies and such odd sayings—the sisters three and such branches of learning—is indeed deceased; or, as you would say in plain terms, gone to heaven.
