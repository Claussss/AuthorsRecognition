PAROLES Save you, fair queen. HELEN And you, monarch. PAROLES No. HELEN And no. PAROLES Are you meditating on virginity? HELEN Ay. You have some stain of soldier in you, let me ask you a question. Man is enemy to virginity: how may we barricado it against him? in PAROLES Keep him out. HELEN But he assails, and our virginity, though valiant in the defence, yet is weak. Unfold to us some warlike resistance.
