Enter Sir Toby and Sir Andrew SIR TOBY Save you, gentleman. VIOLA And you, sir. SIR ANDREW Dieu vous garde, monsieur. VIOLA Et vous aussi, votre serviteur. SIR ANDREW I hope, sir, you are, and I am yours. SIR TOBY Will you encounter the house? My niece is desirous you should enter if your trade be to her.
