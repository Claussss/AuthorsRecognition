SIR JOHN Good maid, then. MISTRESS QUICKLY I’ll be sworn: as my mother was the first hour I was born. SIR JOHN I do believe the swearer. What with me? MISTRESS QUICKLY Shall I vouchsafe your worship a word or two? SIR JOHN Two thousand, fair woman, and I’ll vouchsafe thee the hearing. MISTRESS QUICKLY There is one Mistress Ford, sir—I pray come a little nearer this ways.
