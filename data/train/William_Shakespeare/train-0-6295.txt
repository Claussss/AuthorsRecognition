
ROSALIND Never talk to me. I will weep. CELIA Do, I prithee, but yet have the grace to consider that tears do not become a man. ROSALIND But have I not cause to weep? CELIA As good cause as one would desire; therefore weep. ROSALIND His very hair is of the dissembling colour. CELIA Something browner than Judas’s. Marry, his kisses are Judas’s own children.
