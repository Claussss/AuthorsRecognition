[Addition VI (Heywood)] Enter a Servingman to the players, with a reward SERVINGMAN Where be these players? ALL THE PLAYERS SERVINGMAN My lord is sent for to the court, And all the guests do after supper part;
And, for he will not trouble you again,
By me for your reward a sends eight angels,
With many thanks. But sup before you go.
It is his will you should be fairly entreated.
Follow, I pray ye.
