HORTENSIO Content. What’s the wager? LUCFNTIO Twenty crowns. PETRUCCIO Twenty crowns! I’ll venture so much of my hawk or hound,
But twenty times so much upon my wife. LUCENTIO A hundred, then. HORTENSIO Content. PETRUCCIO A match, ‘tis done. HORTENSIO Who shall begin? LUCENTIO That will I. Go, Biondello, bid your mistress come to me.
