They talk apart HOST (to Ford) Hast thou no suit against my knight, my guest cavaliero? HOST My hand, bully. Thou shalt have egress and regress—said I well?—and thy name shall be Brooke. It is a merry knight. (To Shallow and Page) Will you go, mijn’heers? SHALLOW Have with you, mine Host. PAGE I have heard the Frenchman hath good skill in his rapier.
