FOOL (to Lear) Prithee, nuncle, tell me whether a madman be a gentleman or a yeoman. A king, a king! To have a thousand With red burning spits come hissing in upon them! EDGAR The foul fiend bites my back. FOOL (to Lear) He’s mad that trusts in the tameness of a wolf, a horse’s health, a boy’s love, or a whore’s oath.
