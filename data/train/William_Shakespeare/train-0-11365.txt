On, speak to him. My boon is that this gentleman may render
Of whom he had this ring. POSTHUMUS (aside) What’s that to him? CYMBELINE (to Giacomo) That diamond upon your finger, say,
How came it yours? Thou’lt torture me to leave unspoken that
Which to be spoke would torture thee. I am glad to be constrained to utter that
Torments me to conceal. By villainy
I got this ring; ‘twas Leonatus’ jewel,
Whom thou didst banish; and, which more may
grieve thee,
As it doth me, a nobler sir ne’er lived
’Twixt sky and ground. Wilt thou hear more, my lord?
