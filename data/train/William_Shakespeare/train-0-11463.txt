Prithee, peace. SEBASTIAN (to Antonio) He receives comfort like cold porridge. ANTONIO The visitor will not give him o’er so. SEBASTIAN Look, he’s winding up the watch of his wit. By and by it will strike. GONZALO (to Alonso) Sir— SEBASTIAN (to Antonio) One: tell. GONZALO (to Alonso) When every grief is entertained that’s offered,
