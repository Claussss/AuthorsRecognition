There is expectance here from both the sides
What further you will do. HECTOR We’ll answer it: The issue is embracement.—Ajax, farewell. If I might in entreaties find success,
As seld I have the chance, I would desire
My famous cousin to our Grecian tents. ’Tis Agamemnon’s wish—and great Achilles
Doth long to see unarmed the valiant Hector.
