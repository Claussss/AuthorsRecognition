ROSALIND Do you not know I am a woman? When I think, I must speak.—Sweet, say on. Enter Orlando and Jaques CELIA You bring me out. Soft, comes he not here? ROSALIND ’Tis he. Slink by, and note him. Rosalind and Celia stand aside JAQUES (to Orlando) I thank you for your company, but, good faith, I had as lief have been myself alone.
