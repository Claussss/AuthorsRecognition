WARWICK aside Alas, I am not cooped here for defence.
(To King Edward)
I will away towards Barnet presently,
And bid thee battle, Edward, if thou dar’st. KING EDWARD Yes, Warwick—Edward dares, and leads the way.
Lords, to the Held—Saint George and victory! Exeunt below King Edward and his company.
