APEMANTUS Then I repent not. JEWELLER You know me, Apemantus? Thou know’st I do. I called thee by thy name. TIMON Thou art proud, Apemantus! APEMANTUS Of nothing so much as that I am not like Timon. TIMON Whither art going? APEMANTUS To knock out an honest Athenian’s brains. TIMON That’s a deed thou’It die for.
