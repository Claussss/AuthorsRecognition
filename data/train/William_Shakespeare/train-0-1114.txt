What sayst thou, Henry, wilt thou yield the crown? QUEEN MARGARET Why, how now, long-tongued Warwick, dare you
speak?
When you and I met at Saint Albans last,
Your legs did better service than your hands. Then ‘twas my turn to fly—and now ‘tis thine. You said so much before, and yet you fled. ’Twas not your valour, Clifford, drove me thence.
