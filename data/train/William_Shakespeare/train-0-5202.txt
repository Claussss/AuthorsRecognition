SIR JOHN I am old, I am old. DOLL TEARSHEET I love thee better than I love e’er a scurvy young boy of them all. SIR JOHN What stuff wilt have a kirtle of? I shall receive money o’Thursday; shalt have a cap tomorrow.—A merry song! The music plays again Come, it grows late; we’ll to bed. Thou’lt forget me when I am gone.
