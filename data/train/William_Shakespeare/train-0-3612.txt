Exeunt Capulet, his Wife,and his Cousin. The guests, gentlewomen, masquers, musicians, and servingmen begin to leave Come hither, Nurse. What is yon gentleman? The son and heir of old Tiberio. What’s he that now is going out of door? Marry, that, I think, be young Petruccio. What’s he that follows here, that would not dance?
