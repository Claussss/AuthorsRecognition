Enter Aeneas AENEAS My lord, is the lady ready? TROILUS (to Cressida) Hark, you are called. Some say the genius so
Cries ‘Come!’ to him that instantly must die.
To Pandarus
anon. PANDARUS Where are my tears? Rain, to lay this wind, or my heart will be blown up by the root. Exit with Aeneas I must then to the Grecians.
