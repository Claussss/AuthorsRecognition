Enter the Plebeians FIRST PLEBEIAN What is your name? SECOND PLEBEIAN Whither are you going? THIRD PLEBEIAN Where do you dwell? FOURTH PLEBEIAN Are you a married man or a bachelor? SECOND PLEBEIAN Answer every man directly. FIRST PLEBEIAN Ay, and briefly. FOURTH PLEBEIAN Ay, and wisely. THIRD PLEBEIAN Ay, and truly, you were best.
