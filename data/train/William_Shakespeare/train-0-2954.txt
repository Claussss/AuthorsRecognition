COURTESAN (to Antipholus) I pray you, sir, my ring, or else the chain.
I hope you do not mean to cheat me so? ANTIPHOLUS OF SYRACUSE Avaunt, thou witch !—Come, Dromio, let us go. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE ‘Fly pride’ says the peacock. Mistress, that you know. Exeunt Antipholus of Syracuse and Dromio of Syracuse
