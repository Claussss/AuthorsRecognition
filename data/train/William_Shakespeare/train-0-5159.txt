PAGE Away, you rascally Althea’s dream, away! PRINCE HARRY Instruct us, boy; what dream, boy? PAGE Marry, my lord, Althea dreamt she was delivered of a firebrand, and therefore I call him her dream. PRINCE HARRY (giving him money) A crown‘s-worth of good interpretation! There ’tis, boy. POINS O, that this good blossom could be kept from cankers! (Giving the Page money) Well, there is sixpence to preserve thee.
