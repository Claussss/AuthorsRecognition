Alas, he is betrayed, and I undone. Out, strumpet! Weep’st thou for him to my face? O, banish me, my lord, but kill me not. OTHELLO (attacking her) Down, strumpet. Kill me tomorrow; let me live tonight. OTHELLO Nay, an you strive— DESDEMONA But half an hour. OTHELLO Being done, there is no pause. DESDEMONA But while I say one prayer.
