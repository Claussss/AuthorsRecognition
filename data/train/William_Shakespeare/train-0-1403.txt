Coward of France! How much he wrongs his fame,
Despairing of his own arms’ fortitude,
To join with witches and the help of hell. Traitors have never other company.
But what’s that ‘Pucelle’ whom they term so pure? A maid, they say. BEDFORD A maid? And be so martial? Pray God she prove not masculine ere long.
