So we must. Is’t said this war’s afoot? Or it shall be
On fail of some condition? ’Tis in motion, The intelligence of state came in the instant
With the defier. Let’s to the King, who, were he A quarter carrier of that honour which
His enemy come in, the blood we venture
Should be as for our health, which were not spent,
Rather laid out for purchase. But, alas,
Our hands advanced before our hearts, what will
The fall o’th’ stroke do damage?
