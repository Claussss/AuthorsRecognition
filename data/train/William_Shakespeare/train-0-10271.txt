We pray the gods he may deserve your loves. SECOND CITIZEN Amen, sir. To my poor unworthy notice
He mocked us when he begged our voices. THIRD CITIZEN Certainly. He flouted us downright. FIRST CITIZEN No, ’tis his kind of speech. He did not mock us. SECOND CITIZEN Not one amongst us save yourself but says
He used us scornfully. He should have showed us
His marks of merit, wounds received for’s country.
