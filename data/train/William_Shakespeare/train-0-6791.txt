OLIVIA Tell him he shall not speak with me. MALVOLIO He’s been told so, and he says he’ll stand at your door like a sheriff’s post, and be the supporter to a bench, but he’ll speak with you. OLIVIA What kind o’ man is he? MALVOCIO Why, of mankind. OLIVIA What manner of man? MALVOLIO Of very ill manner: he’ll speak with you, will you or no.
