PERICLES (to Lysimachus) I embrace you, sir.— Give me my robes. He is attired in fresh robes I am wild in my beholding. O heavens, bless my girl! Celestial music But hark, what music? Tell Helicanus, my Marina, tell him
O’er point by point, for yet he seems to doubt,
How sure you are my daughter. But what music?
