FALKNER to Officers SHERIFF We’ll christen you, sirrah.—Bring him forward. MORE to Falkner FALKNER The azured heavens protect my noble Lord Chancellor! MORE to Sheriff SHERIFF A ruffian, my lord, that hath set half the city in an uproar. FALKNER My lord— SHERIFF There was a fray in Paternoster Row, and because they would not be parted the street was choked up with carts.
