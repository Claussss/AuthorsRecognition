1.3
Alarums, and then enter the young Earl of Rutland and his Tutor, a chaplain Ah, whither shall I fly to scape their hands? Enter Lord Clifford with soldiers Ah, tutor, look where bloody Clifford comes. CLIFFORD (to the Tutor) Chaplain, away—thy priesthood saves thy life.
As for the brat of this accursed duke,
Whose father slew my father—he shall die.
