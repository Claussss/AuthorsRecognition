ADRIANA Why, man, what is the matter? DROMIO OF SYRACUSE I do not know the matter, he is ’rested on the case. What, is he arrested? Tell me at whose suit. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE I know not at whose suit he is arrested well,
But is in a suit of buff which ’rested him, that can I tell.
Will you send him, mistress, redemption—the money in his desk?
