COMINIUS (to Sicinius) Aged sir, hands off. CORIOLANUS (to Sicinius) Hence, rotten thing, or I shall shake thy bones
Out of thy garments. Help, ye citizens! Enter a rabble of Plebeians, with the Aediles On both sides more respect. That would take from you all your power. Seize him, aediles. Down with him, down with him!
