JAQUENETTA God give you good-morrow, Master Parson. HOLOFERNES Master Parson, quasi ‘pierce one’ ? And if one should be pierced, which is the one? COSTARD Marry, Master Schoolmaster, he that is likeliest to a hogshead. HOLOFERNES ‘Of piercing a hogshead’—a good lustre of conceit in a turf of earth, fire enough for a flint, pearl enough for a swine—‘tis pretty, it is well.
