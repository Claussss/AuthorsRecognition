BEATRICE With a good leg and a good foot, uncle, and money enough in his purse—such a man would win any woman in the world, if a could get her good will. LEONATO By my troth, niece, thou wilt never get thee a husband if thou be so shrewd of thy tongue. ANTONIO In faith, she’s too curst. BEATRICE Too curst is more than curst. I shall lessen God’s sending that way, for it is said God sends a curst cow short horns, but to a cow too curst he sends none.
