Enter Thurio with Musicians How now, Sir Proteus, are you crept before us? Ay, gentle Thurio, for you know that love
Will creep in service where it cannot go. Ay, but I hope, sir, that you love not here. Sir, but I do, or else I would be hence. Who, Silvia? Ay, Silvia—for your sake. I thank you for your own. Now, gentlemen,
Let’s tune, and to it lustily awhile.
