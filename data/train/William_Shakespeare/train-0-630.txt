BIONDELLO His daughter is to be brought by you to the supper. LUCENTIO And then? BIONDELLO The old priest at Saint Luke’s church is at your command at all hours. LUCENTIO And what of all this? BIONDELLO I cannot tell, except they are busied about a counterfeit assurance. Take you assurance of her cum privilegio ad imprimendum solum—to th’ church take the priest, clerk, and some sufficient honest witnesses. If this be not that you look for, I have no more to say, But bid Bianca farewell for ever and a day.
