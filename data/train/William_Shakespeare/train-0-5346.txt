DAVY An’t please your worship, there’s one Pistol come from the court with news. SIR JOHN From the court? Let him come in. Enter Pistol How now, Pistol? PISTOL Sir John, God save you. SIR JOHN What wind blew you hither, Pistol? Not the ill wind which blows no man to good.
Sweet knight, thou art now one of the greatest men in
this realm.
