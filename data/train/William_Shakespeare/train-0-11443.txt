CALIBAN (within) There’s wood enough within. Come forth, I say! There’s other business for thee.
Come, thou tortoise! When? Enter Ariel, like a water-nymph Fine apparition! My quaint Ariel,
Hark in thine ear. He whispers My lord, it shall be done. Exit Thou poisonous slave, got by the devil himself
Upon thy wicked dam, come forth!
