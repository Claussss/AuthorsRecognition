TROILUS (aside) Wert thou the devil and wor’st it on thy horn,
It should be challenged. Well, well, ‘tis done, ’tis past—and yet it is not.
I will not keep my word. DIOMEDES Why then, farewell. Thou never shalt mock Diomed again. You shall not go. One cannot speak a word
But it straight starts you. DIOMEDES I do not like this fooling.
