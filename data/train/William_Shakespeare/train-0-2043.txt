Is all things ready for that solemn time? It is, and wants but nomination. BISHOP OF ELY Tomorrow, then, I judge a happy day. Who knows the Lord Protector’s mind herein?
Who is most inward with the noble Duke? BISHOP OF ELY Your grace, methinks, should soonest know his mind. We know each other’s faces. For our hearts,
He knows no more of mine than I of yours,
Or I of his, my lord, than you of mine.—
Lord Hastings, you and he are near in love.
