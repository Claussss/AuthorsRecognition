I have a little yet to say. Look there, my lords— He shows the King’s ring By virtue of that ring I take my cause
Out of the grips of cruel men, and give it
To a most noble judge, the King my master. LORD CHAMBERLAIN This is the King’s ring. ’Tis no counterfeit. ’Tis the right ring, by heav’n. I told ye all
When we first put this dangerous stone a-rolling
’Twould fall upon ourselves.
