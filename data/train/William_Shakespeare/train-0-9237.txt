I’ll set a bourn how far to be beloved. Then must thou needs find out new heaven, new earth. Enter a Messenger MESSENGER News, my good lord, from Rome. ANTONY Grates me: the sum. CLEOPATRA Nay, hear them, Antony. Fulvia perchance is angry; or who knows
If the scarce-bearded Caesar have not sent
His powerful mandate to you: ‘Do this, or this,
Take in that kingdom and enfranchise that.
Perform’t, or else we damn thee.’
