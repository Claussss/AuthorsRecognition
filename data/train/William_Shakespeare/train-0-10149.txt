FIRST SENATOR Then, worthy Martius, Attend upon Cominius to these wars. COMINIUS (to Martius) It is your former promise. MARTIUS Sir, it is, And I am constant. Titus Lartius, thou
Shalt see me once more strike at Tullus’ face.
What, art thou stiff? Stand’st out? LARTIUS No, Caius Martius. I’ll lean upon one crutch and fight with th’other
Ere stay behind this business.
