SEXTON But which are the offenders that are to be examined? Let them come before Master Constable. DOGBERRY Yea, marry, let them come before me. What is your name, friend? BORACHIO Borachio. DOGBERRY (to the Sexton) Pray write down ‘Borachio’. (To Conrad) Yours, sirrah? CONRAD I am a gentleman, sir, and my name is Conrad.
