JULIET How now, who calls? NURSE Your mother. Madam, I am here. What is your will? CAPULET’S WIFE This is the matter.—Nurse, give leave a while.
We must talk in secret.—Nurse, come back again.
I have remembered me, thou s’ hear our counsel.
Thou knowest my daughter’s of a pretty age. Faith, I can tell her age unto an hour.
