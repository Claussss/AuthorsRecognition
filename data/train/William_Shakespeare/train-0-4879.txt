SIR JOHN Fare thee well. Commend me to them both. There’s my purse; I am yet thy debtor.—Boy, go along with this woman. Exeunt Mistress Quickly and Robin (Aside) This news distracts me. PISTOL (aside) This punk is one of Cupid’s carriers. Clap on more sails! Pursue! Up with your sights!
Give fire! She is my prize, or ocean whelm them all!
