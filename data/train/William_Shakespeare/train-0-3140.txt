JAQUENETTA Good Costard, go with me.—Sir, God save your life. CUSTARD Have with thee, my girl. Exit with Jaquenetta NATHANIEL Sir, you have done this in the fear of God very religiously, and, as a certain father saith— HOLOFERNES Sir, tell not me of the father; I do fear colourable colours. But to return to the verses—did they please you, Sir Nathaniel?
