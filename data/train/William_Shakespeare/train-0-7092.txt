ACHILLES What, with me too, Thersites? THERSITES There’s Ulysses and old Nestor, whose wit was mouldy ere your grandsires had nails on their toes, yoke you like draught oxen and make you plough up the war. ACHILLES What? What? THERSITES Yes, good sooth. To Achilles! To, Ajax, to— AJAX I shall cut out your tongue.
