
MENENIUS The augurer tells me we shall have news tonight. BRUTUS Good or bad? MENENIUS Not according to the prayer of the people, for they love not Martius. SICINIUS Nature teaches beasts to know their friends. MENENIUS Pray you, who does the wolf love? SICINIUS The lamb. MENENIUS Ay, to devour him, as the hungry plebeians would the noble Martius.
