Enter old Queen Margaret, unseen behind them  Small joy have I in being England’s queen. QUEEN MARGARET (aside) And lessened be that small, God I beseech him.
Thy honour, state, and seat is due to me. RICHARD GLOUCESTER (to Elizabeth) What? Threat you me with telling of the King?
Tell him, and spare not. Look what I have said,
I will avouch’t in presence of the King.
I dare adventure to be sent to th’ Tower.
‘Tis time to speak; my pains are quite forgot.
