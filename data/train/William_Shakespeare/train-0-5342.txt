Enter Davy With a dish of apples DAVY There’s a dish of leather-coats for you. SHALLOW Davy! DAVY Your worship! ITo Sir John SILENCE sings A cup of wine
That’s brisk and fine,
And drink unto thee, leman mine,
And a merry heart lives long-a. SIR JOHN Well said, Master Silence. SILENCE And we shall be merry; now comes in the sweet o’th’ night.
