SIR TOBY O ay, make up that, he is now at a cold scent. FABIAN Sowter will cry upon’t for all this, though it be as rank as a fox. MALVOLIO ‘M.’ Malvolio—‘M’—why, that begins my name. FABIAN Did not I say he would work it out? The cur is excellent at faults. MALVOLIO ‘M’ But then there is no consonancy in the sequel. That suffers under probation. ‘A’ should follow, but ‘O’ does.
