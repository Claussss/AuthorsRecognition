Sennet. Exeunt Caesar and his train. Brutus, Cassius, and Casca remain CASCA (to Brutus) You pulled me by the cloak. Would you speak with me? Ay, Casca. Tell us what hath chanced today,
That Caesar looks so sad. CASCA Why, you were with him, were you not? I should not then ask Casca what had chanced.
