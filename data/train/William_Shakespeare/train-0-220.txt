Pardon, dear madam, ‘tis a passing shame
That I, unworthy body as I am,
Should censure thus on lovely gentlemen. Why not on Proteus, as of all the rest? Then thus: of many good, I think him best. Your reason? I have no other but a woman’s reason:
I think him so because I think him so. And wouldst thou have me cast my love on him?
