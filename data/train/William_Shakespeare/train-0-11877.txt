 Enter King Henry and Doctor Butts at a window, above I’ll show your grace the strangest sight— What’s that, Butts? I think your highness saw this many a day. Body o’me, where is it? BUTTS (pointing at Cranmer, below) There, my lord. The high promotion of his grace of Canterbury,
Who holds his state at door, ’mongst pursuivants,
Pages, and footboys.
