Yonder comes news. A wager they have met. My horse to yours, no. MARTIUS ’Tis done. LARTIUS Agreed. MARTIUS (to the Messenger) Say, has our general met the enemy? They lie in view, but have not spoke as yet. So, the good horse is mine. MARTIUS I’ll buy him of you. No, I’ll nor sell nor give him. Lend you him I will,
For half a hundred years.
(To the trumpeter) Summon the town.
