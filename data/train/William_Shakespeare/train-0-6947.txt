I prithee, foolish Greek, depart from me.

I shall give worse payment. FESTE By my troth, thou hast an open hand. These wise men that give fools money get themselves a good report, after fourteen years’ purchase. Enter Sir Andrew, Sir Toby, and Fabian SIR ANDREW (to Sebastian) Now, sir, have I met you again?
