Am I, supposed dead and drowned. PERICLES taking Thaisa’s hand THAISA Now I know you better. When we with tears parted Pentapolis,
The King my father gave you such a ring. This, this! No more, you gods. Your present kindness
Makes my past miseries sports; you shall do well
That on the touching of her lips I may
Melt, and no more be seen.—O come, be buried
A second time within these arms.
