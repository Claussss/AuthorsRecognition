BENEDICK I have almost matter enough in me for such an embassage. And so I commit you— CLAUDIO To the tuition of God, from my house if I had it— DON PEDRO The sixth of July, Your loving friend, BENEDICK Nay, mock not, mock not. The body of your discourse is sometime guarded with fragments, and the guards are but slightly basted on neither. Ere you flout old ends any further, examine your conscience. And so I leave you. Exit
