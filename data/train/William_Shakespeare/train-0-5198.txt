DOLL TEARSHEET Sirrah, what humour’s the Prince of? SIR JOHN A good shallow young fellow. A would have made a good pantler; a would ha’ chipped bread well. DOLL TEARSHEET They say Poins has a good wit. SIR JOHN He a good wit? Hang him, baboon! His wit’s as thick as Tewkesbury mustard; there’s no more conceit in him than is in a mallet.
