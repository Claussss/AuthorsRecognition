Cornetts. A great cry and noise within, crying, ‘A Palamon’ I am not there. O better never born,
Than minister to such harm. Enter Servant What is the chance? SERVANT The cry’s ‘A Palamon’. Then he has won. ’Twas ever likely—
He looked all grace and success, and he is
Doubtless the prim’st of men. I prithee run
And tell me how it goes.
