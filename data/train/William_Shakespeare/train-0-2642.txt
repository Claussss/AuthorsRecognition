LODOWICK (reading) ‘More fair and chaste than is the queen of shades,
More bold in constancy’— KING EDWARD (staying him) In constancy than who? LODOWICK (reading) ‘...than Judith was.’ KING EDWARD O monstrous line! Put in the next a sword
And I shall woo her to cut off my head!
Blot, blot, good Lod’wick. Let us hear the next.
