ARMADO (aside) I do betray myself with blushing.—Maid. JAQUENETTA Man. ARMADO I will visit thee at the lodge. JAQUENETTA That’s hereby. ARMADO I know where it is situate. JAQUENETTA Lord, how wise you are! ARMADO I will tell thee wonders. JAQUENETTA With that face? ARMADO I love thee. JAQUENETTA So I heard you say.
