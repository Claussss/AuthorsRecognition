Enter Helicanus and Aeschines, with other lords You shall not need, my fellow peers of Tyre,
Further to question of your King’s departure.
His sealed commission left in trust with me
Does speak sufficiently he’s gone to travel. THALIART (aside) How? The King gone? If further yet you will be satisfied
Why, as it were unlicensed of your loves,
He would depart, I’ll give some light unto you.
