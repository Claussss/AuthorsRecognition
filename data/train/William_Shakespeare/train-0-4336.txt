BASSANIO Well, we shall see your bearing. Nay, but I bar tonight. You shall not gauge me
By what we do tonight. BASSANIO No, that were pity. I would entreat you rather to put on
Your boldest suit of mirth, for we have friends
That purpose merriment. But fare you well.
I have some business. And I must to Lorenzo and the rest.
But we will visit you at supper-time. Exeunt severally
