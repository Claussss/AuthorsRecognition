Enter King Harry, the Dukes of Gloucester,  
KING HARRY Where is my gracious lord of Canterbury? Not here in presence. KING HARRY Send for him, good uncle. WESTMORLAND Shall we call in th’ambassador, my liege? Not yet, my cousin. We would be resolved,
Before we hear him, of some things of weight
That task our thoughts, concerning us and France.
