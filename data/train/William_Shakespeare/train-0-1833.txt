Come, come, thou reverend man of Rome,
And bring our emperor gently in thy hand,
Lucius, our emperor—for well I know
The common voice do cry it shall be so. Lucius, all hail, Rome’s royal emperor! MARCUS (to attendants) Go, go into old Titus’ sorrowful house
And hither hale that misbelieving Moor
To be adjudged some direful slaught’ring death
As punishment for his most wicked life. Exeunt some
