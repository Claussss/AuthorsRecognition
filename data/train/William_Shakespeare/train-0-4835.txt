RUGBY ’Tis ready, sir, here in the porch. He fetches the rapier CAIUS By my trot, I tarry too long. ’Od’s me, qu Dere is some simples in my closet dat I vill not for the varld I shall leave behind. MISTRESS QUICKLY (aside) Ay me, he’ll find the young man there, and be mad. CAIUS (discovering Simple) O diable, diable! Vat is in my closet? Villainy, larron! Rugby, my rapier!
