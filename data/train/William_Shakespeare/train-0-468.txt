GRUMIO Knock, sir? Whom should I knock? Is there any man has rebused your worship? PETRUCCIO Villain, I say, knock me here soundly. GRUMIO Knock you here, sir? Why, sir, what am I, sir, that I should knock you here, sir? Villain, I say, knock me at this gate,
And rap me well or I’ll knock your knave’s pate.
