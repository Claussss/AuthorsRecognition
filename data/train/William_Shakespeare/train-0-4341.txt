GRAZIANO Love-news, in faith. LANCELOT to Lorenzo LORENZO Whither goest thou? LANCELOT Marry, sir, to bid my old master the Jew to sup tonight with my new master the Christian. Hold, here, take this. (Giving money) Tell gentle Jessica
I will not fail her. Speak it privately.
Go. Exit Lancelot
Gentlemen,
