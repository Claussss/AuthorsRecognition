Enter Doctor Caius CAIUS Vere is mine Host de Jarteer? HOST Here, Master Doctor, in perplexity and doubtful dilemma. CAIUS I cannot tell vat is dat, but it is tell-a me dat you make grand preparation for a duke de Jamany. By my trot, der is no duke that the court is know to come. I tell you for good will. Adieu. Exit
