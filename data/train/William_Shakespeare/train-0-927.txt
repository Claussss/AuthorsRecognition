STAFFORD (to Cade’s followers) And will you credit this base drudge’s words
That speaks he knows not what? ALL CADE’S FOLLOWERS Ay, marry, will we—therefore get ye gone. STAFFORD’S BROTHER Jack Cade, the Duke of York hath taught you this. CADE (aside) He lies, for I invented it myself.
(Aloud) Go to, sirrah—tell the King from me that for
his father’s sake, Henry the Fifth, in whose time boys
went to span-counter for French crowns, I am content
he shall reign; but I’ll be Protector over him.
