MESSENGER Hold! Hold! O, hold! Hold! Hold! Enter Pirithous in haste Hold, ho! It is a curs
If you have done so quickly! Noble Palamon,
The gods will show their glory in a life
That thou art yet to lead. Can that be,
 When Venus, I have said, is false? How do things
fare? Arise, great sir, and give the tidings ear
That are most rarely sweet and bitter.
