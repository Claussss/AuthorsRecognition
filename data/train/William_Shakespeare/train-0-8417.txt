Enter Montano, Graziano, and Iago What is the matter? How now, general? O, are you come, Iago? You have done well,
That men must lay their murders on your neck. GRAZIANO What is the matter? EMILIA (to Iago) Disprove this villain if thou beest a man.
He says thou told‘st him that his wife was false.
I know thou didst not. Thou’rt not such a villain.
Speak, for my heart is full.
