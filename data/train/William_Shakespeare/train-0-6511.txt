POLONIUS This is too long. HAMLET It shall to the barber’s, with your beard. (To First Player) Prithee, say on. He’s for a jig or a tale of bawdry, or he sleeps. Say on, come to Hecuba. FIRST PLAYER ‘But who, O who had seen the mobbled queen’— HAMLET ‘The mobbled queen’? POLONIUS That’s good; ‘mobbled queen’ is good.
