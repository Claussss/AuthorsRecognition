VALENTINE Wise. THURIO What instance of the contrary? VALENTINE Your folly. THURIO And how quote you my folly? VALENTINE I quote it in your jerkin. THURIO My ‘jerkin’ is a doublet. VALENTINE Well then, I’ll double your folly. THURIO How! SILVIA What, angry, Sir Thurio? Do you change colour? VALENTINE Give him leave, madam, he is a kind of chameleon.
