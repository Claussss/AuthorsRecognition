Enter Adriana, Luciana, Courtesan, and a schoolmaster called Pinch ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Come, go along: my wife is coming yonder. DROMIO OF EPHESUS (to Adriana) Mistress, respice finem respect your end—or rather, to prophesy like the parrot, ‘Beware the rope’s end’. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Wilt thou still talk?
