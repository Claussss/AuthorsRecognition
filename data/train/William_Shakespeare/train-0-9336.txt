I crave your highness’ pardon. He is married? Take no offence that I would not offend you.
To punish me for what you make me do
Seems much unequal. He’s married to Octavia. O that his fault should make a knave of thee,
That act not what thou‘rt sure of! Get thee hence.
The merchandise which thou hast brought from Rome
Are all too dear for me. Lie they upon thy hand,
And be undone by ’em.
