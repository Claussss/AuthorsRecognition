OLIVIA Well, sir, for want of other idleness I’ll bide your proof. FESTE Good madonna, why mournest thou? OLIVIA Good fool, for my brother’s death. FESTE I think his soul is in hell, madonna. OLIVIA I know his soul is in heaven, fool. FESTE The more fool, madonna, to mourn for your brother’s soul, being in heaven. Take away the fool, gentlemen.
