BENVOLIO An I were so apt to quarrel as thou art, any man should buy the fee-simple of my life for an hour and a quarter. MERCUTIO The fee simple? O, simple! Enter Tybalt, Petruccio, and others BENVOLIO By my head, here comes the Capulets. MERCUTIO By my heel, I care not. TYBALT (to Petruccio and the others)
