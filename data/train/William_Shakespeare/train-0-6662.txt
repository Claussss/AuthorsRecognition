HAMLET I think it be thine indeed, for thou liest in’t. FIRST CLOWN You lie out on‘t, sir, and therefore it is not yours. For my part, I do not lie in’t, and yet it is mine. HAMLET Thou dost lie in‘t, to be in’t and say ’tis thine. ’Tis for the dead, not for the quick; therefore thou liest. FIRST CLOWN ‘Tis a quick lie, sir, ’twill away again from me to you.
