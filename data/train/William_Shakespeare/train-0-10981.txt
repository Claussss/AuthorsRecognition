4.4
Enter Regan and Oswald the steward But are my brother’s powers set forth? Himself in person there? Madam, with much ado. Your sister is the better soldier. Lord Edmond spake not with your lord at home? OSWALD No, madam. What might import my sister’s letters to him? OSWALD I know not, lady. Faith, he is posted hence on serious matter.
It was great ignorance, Gloucester’s eyes being out,
To let him live. Where he arrives he moves
All hearts against us. Edmond, I think, is gone,
In pity of his misery, to dispatch
His ‘nighted life, moreover to descry
The strength o’th’ enemy.
