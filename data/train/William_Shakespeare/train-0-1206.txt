Therefore at last I firmly am resolved:
You shall have aid. QUEEN MARGARET Let me give humble thanks for all at once. KING LOUIS (to the Post) Then, England’s messenger, return in post
And tell false Edward, thy supposed king,
That Louis of France is sending over masquers
To revel it with him and his new bride.
Thou seest what’s passed, go fear thy king withal.
