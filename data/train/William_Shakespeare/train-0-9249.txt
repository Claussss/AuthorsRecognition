CHARMIAN E’s the o’erflowing Nilus presageth famine. IRAS Go, you wild bedfellow, you cannot soothsay. CHARMIAN Nay, if an oily palm be not a fruitful prognostication, I cannot scratch mine ear. (To the Soothsayer) Prithee, tell her but a workaday fortune. SOOTHSAYER Your fortunes are alike. IRAS But how, but how? Give me particulars.
