DOLL TEARSHEET I’ll tell you what, you thin man in a censer, I will have you as soundly swinged for this, you bluebottle rogue, you filthy famished correctioner! If you be not swinged, I’ll forswear half-kirtles. FIRST BEADLE Come, come, you she knight-errant, come! MISTRESS QUICKLY O God, that right should thus o’ercome might! Well, of sufferance comes ease.
