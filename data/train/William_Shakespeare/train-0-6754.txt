Enter Sir Toby Belch and Maria 
SIR TOBY What a plague means my niece to take the death of her brother thus? I am sure care’s an enemy to life. MARIA By my troth, Sir Toby, you must come in earlier o’ nights. Your cousin, my lady, takes great exceptions to your ill hours.
