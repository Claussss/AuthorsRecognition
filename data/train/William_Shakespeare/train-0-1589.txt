RICHARD DUKE OF YORK Now heaven forfend—the holy maid with child? WARWICK (to Joan) The greatest miracle that e’er ye wrought.
Is all your strict preciseness come to this? RICHARD DUKE OF YORK She and the Dauphin have been ingling.
