He embraces Vincentio But is this true, or is it else your pleasure
Like pleasant travellers to break a jest
Upon the company you overtake? I do assure thee, father, so it is. Come, go along, and see the truth hereof,
For our first merriment hath made thee jealous. Exeunt all but Hortensio Well, Petruccio, this has put me in heart.
Have to my widow, and if she be froward,
Then hast thou taught Hortensio to be untoward.
