ENOBARBUS (aside to Agrippa) That year indeed he was troubled with a rheum.
What willingly he did confound he wailed,
Believe’, till I wept too. No, sweet Octavia, You shall hear from me still. The time shall not
Outgo my thinking on you. Come, sir, come, I’ll wrestle with you in my strength of love.
Look, here I have you (embracing Caesar); thus I let
you go,
And give you to the gods.
