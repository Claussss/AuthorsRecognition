BARDOLPH Pray thee, go down, good ensign. SIR JOHN Hark thee hither, Mistress Doll. He takes her aside PISTOL Not II I tell thee what, Corporal Bardolph, I could tear her! I’ll be revenged of her. PAGE Pray thee, go down. PISTOL I’ll see her damned first To Pluto’s damned lake, by this hand,
To th’infernal deep,
Where Erebus, and tortures vile also.
‘Hold hook and line!’ say I.
Down, down, dogs; down, Fates.
Have we not Hiren here?
