But hear you, my lord. HOTSPUR What sayst thou, my lady? What is it carries you away? HOTSPUR Why, my horse, My love, my horse. LADY PERCY Out, you mad-headed ape! A weasel hath not such a deal of spleen
As you are tossed with.
