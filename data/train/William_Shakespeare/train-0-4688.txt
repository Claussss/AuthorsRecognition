PRINCE HARRY Thou sayst true, Hostess, and he slanders thee most grossly. HOSTESS So he doth you, my lord, and said this other day you owed him a thousand pound. PRINCE HARRY (to Sir John) Sirrah, do I owe you a thousand pound? SIR JOHN A thousand pound, Hal? A million! Thy love is worth a million; thou owest me thy love.
