TRANIO Call forth an officer. Enter an Officer Carry this mad knave to the jail. Father Baptista, I
charge you see that he be forthcoming. VINCENTIO Carry me to the jail? GREMIO Stay, officer, he shall not go to prison. BAPTISTA Talk not, Signor Gremio. I say he shall go to prison. GREMIO Take heed, Signor Baptista, lest you be cony-catched in this business. I dare swear this is the right Vincentio.
