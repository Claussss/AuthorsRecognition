MISTRESS PAGE (to Mistress Quickly) Prithee, hold thy peace. EVANS Show me now, William, some declensions of your pronouns. WILLIAM Forsooth, I have forgot. EVANS It is ‘qui, que, quod’. If you forget your ‘qui’s, your ‘que‘s, and your ‘quod’s, you must be preeches. Go your ways and play; go. MISTRESS PAGE He is a better scholar than I thought he was.
