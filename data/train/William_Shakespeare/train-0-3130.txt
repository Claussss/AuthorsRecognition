DULL ’Tis true, indeed, the collusion holds in the exchange. HOLOFERNES God comfort thy capacity, I say th’allusion holds in the exchange. DULL And I say the pollution holds in the exchange, for the moon is never but a month old—and I say beside that ’twas a pricket that the Princess killed. HOLOFERNES Sir Nathaniel, will you hear an extemporal epitaph on the death of the deer? And to humour the ignorant call I the deer the Princess killed a pricket.
