Alas, my father, it befits not me
Unto a stranger knight to be so bold.
He may my proffer take for an offence,
Since men take women’s gifts for impudence. KING SIMONIDES How? Do as I bid you, or you’ll move me else.
THAISA (aside)
Now, by the gods, he could not please me better. KING SIMONIDES Furthermore, tell him we desire to know
Of whence he is, his name and parentage.
Thaisa bears the cup to Pericles
