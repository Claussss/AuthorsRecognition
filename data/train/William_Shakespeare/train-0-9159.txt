MACDUFF’S SON What is a traitor? LADY MACDUFF Why, one that swears and lies. MACDUFF’S SON And be all traitors that do so? LADY MACDUFF Everyone that does so is a traitor, and must be hanged. MACDUFF’S SON And must they all be hanged that swear and lie? LADY MACDUFF Every one. MACDUFF’S SON Who must hang them?
