WILLIAMS My liege, this was my gtove—here is the fellow of it—and he that I gave it to in change promised to wear it in his cap. I promised to strike him, if he did. I met this man with my glove in his cap, and I have been as good as my word. FLUELLEN Your majesty hear now, saving your majesty KING HARRY Give me thy glove, soldier. Look, here is the fellow of it.
