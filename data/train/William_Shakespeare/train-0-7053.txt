CRESSIDA There is among the Greeks Achilles, a better man than Troilus. PANDARUS Achilles? A drayman, a porter, a very camel. CRESSIDA Well, well. PANDARUS Well, well? Why, have you any discretion? Have you any eyes? Do you know what a man is? Is not birth, beauty, good shape, discourse, manhood, learning, gentleness, virtue, youth, liberality, and so forth, the spice and salt that season a man?
