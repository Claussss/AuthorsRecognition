ANTONIO To tell you true, I counterfeit him. URSULA You could never do him so ill-well unless you were the very man. Here’s his dry hand up and down. You are he, you are he. ANTONIO At a word, I am not. URSULA Come, come, do you think I do not know you by your excellent wit? Can virtue hide itself? Go to, mum, you are he. Graces will appear, and there’s an end.
