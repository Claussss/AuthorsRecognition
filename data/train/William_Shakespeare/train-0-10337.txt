Enter Coriolanus, Menenius, and Cominius, with other Senators and Patricians SICINIUS Well, here he comes. MENENIUS (to Coriolanus) Calmly, I do beseech you. Ay, as an hostler that for th’ poorest piece
Will bear the knave by th’ volume.—Th‘honoured
gods
Keep Rome in safety and the chairs of justice
Supplied with worthy men, plant love among’s,
Throng our large temples with the shows of peace,
And not our streets with war!
