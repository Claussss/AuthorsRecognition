KING CLAUDIUS How do ye, pretty lady? OPHELIA Well, God’ield you. They say the owl was a baker’s daughter. Lord, we know what we are, but know not what we may be. God be at your table! KING CLAUDIUS (to Gertrude) Conceit upon her father. OPHELIA Pray you, let’s have no words of this, but when they ask you what it means, say you this. (Sings)
