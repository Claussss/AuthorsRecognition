BENEDICK Were you in doubt, sir, that you asked her? LEONATO Signor Benedick, no, for then were you a child. DON PEDRO You have it full, Benedick. We may guess by this what you are, being a man. Truly, the lady fathers herself. Be happy, lady, for you are like an honourable father. BENEDICK If Signor Leonato be her father, she would not have his head on her shoulders for all Messina, as like him as she is.
