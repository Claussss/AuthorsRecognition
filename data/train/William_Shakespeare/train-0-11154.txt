CLOTEN Whoreson dog! I give him satisfaction? Would he had been one of my rank. SECOND LORD (aside) To have smelled like a fool. CLOTEN I am not vexed more at anything in th‘earth. A pox on’t, I had rather not be so noble as I am. They dare not fight with me because of the Queen, my mother. Every jack-slave hath his bellyful of fighting, and I must go up and down like a cock that nobody can match.
