I will not hear thee speak.—This man, Aufidius,
Was my beloved in Rome; yet thou behold’st. AUFIDIUS You keep a constant temper. Exeunt Coriolanus and Aufidius FIRST WATCHMAN Now, sir, is your name Menenius?
