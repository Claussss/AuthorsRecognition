How does your grace? O Griffith, sick to death. My legs, like loaden branches, bow to th’ earth,
Willing to leave their burden. Reach a chair. A chair is brought to her. She sits So now, methinks, I feel a little ease.
Didst thou not tell me, Griffith, as thou led’st me,
That the great child of honour, Cardinal Wolsey,
Was dead?
