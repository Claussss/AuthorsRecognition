LEPIDUS What colour is it of? ANTONY Of it own colour, too. LEPIDUS ’Tis a strange serpent. ANTONY ’Tis so, and the tears of it are wet. CAESAR (to Antony) Will this description satisfy him? ANTONY With the health that Pompey gives him; else he is a very epicure. POMPEY (aside to Menas) Go hang, sir, hang! Tell me of that? Away,
Do as I bid you. (Aloud) Where’s this cup I called for?
