ORSINO (to Sir Toby) How now, gentleman? How is’t with you? SIR TOBY That’s all one, he’s hurt me, and there’s th’end on’t. (To Feste) Sot, didst see Dick Surgeon, sot? FESTE O, he’s drunk, Sir Toby, an hour agone. His eyes were set at eight i’th’ morning. SIR TOBY Then he’s a rogue, and a passy-measures pavan.
