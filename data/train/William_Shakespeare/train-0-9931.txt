MASTER Why, wilt thou tourney for the lady? I’ll show the virtue I have learned in arms. MASTER Why, d‘ye take it, and the gods give thee good on’t! SECOND FISHERMAN Ay, but hark you, my friend, ’twas we that made up this garment through the rough seams of the waters. There are certain condolements, certain vails. I hope, sir, if you thrive, you’ll remember from whence you had this.
