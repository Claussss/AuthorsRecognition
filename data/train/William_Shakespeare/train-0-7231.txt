Is not yond Diomed with Calchas’ daughter? ’Tis he. I ken the manner of his gait.
He rises on the toe: that spirit of his
In aspiration lifts him from the earth.
Enter Diomedes and Cressida AGAMEMNON (to Diomedes) Is this the Lady Cressid? DIOMEDES Even she. Most dearly welcome to the Greeks, sweet lady.
