Prince for striking him about Bardolph. SIR JOHN moving away LORD CHIEF JUSTICE (to his Servant) What’s he that goes there ? SERVANT Falstaff, an’t please your lordship. LORD CHIEF JUSTICE He that was in question for the robbery? SERVANT He, my lord; but he hath since done good service at Shrewsbury, and, as I hear, is now going with some charge to the Lord John of Lancaster.
