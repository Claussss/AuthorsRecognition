IAGO Ay, too gentle. OTHELLO Nay, that’s certain. But yet the pity of it, Iago. O, Iago, the pity of it, Iago! IAGO If you are so fond over her iniquity, give her patent to offend; for if it touch not you, it comes near nobody. OTHELLO I will chop her into messes. Cuckold me! IAGO O, ’tis foul in her.
