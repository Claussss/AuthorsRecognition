FROTH All Hallow Eve. POMPEY Why, very well. I hope here be truths. He, sir, sitting, as I say, in a lower chair, sir—’twas in the Bunch of Grapes, where indeed you have a delight to sit, have you not? FROTH I have so, because it is an open room, and good for winter. POMPEY Why, very well then. I hope here be truths.
