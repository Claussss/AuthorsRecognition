A peevish, self-willed harlotry it is. Enter Juliet See where she comes from shrift with merry look. CAPULET (to Juliet) How now, my headstrong, where have you been gadding ? Where I have learned me to repent the sin
Of disobedient opposition
To you and your behests, and am enjoined
By holy Laurence to fall prostrate here
To beg your pardon. (Kneeling) Pardon, I beseech you.
Henceforward I am ever ruled by you.
