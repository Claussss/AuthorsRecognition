DUMAINE O most divine Kate! BIRON (aside) O most profane coxcomb! By heaven, the wonder in a mortal eye! BIRON (aside) By earth, she is not, corporal; there you lie. Her amber hairs for foul hath amber quoted. BIRON (aside) An amber-coloured raven was well noted. As upright as the cedar. BIRON (aside) Stoop, I say.
