I prithee, Diomed, visit me no more. THERSITES (aside) Now she sharpens. Well said, whetstone. DIOMEDES I shall have it. CRESSIDA What, this? DIOMEDES Ay, that. O all you gods! O pretty pretty pledge!
Thy master now lies thinking on his bed
Of thee and me, and sighs, and takes my glove
And gives memorial dainty kisses to it—
