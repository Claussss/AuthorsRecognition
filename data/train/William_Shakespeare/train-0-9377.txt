’Tis a noble Lepidus. A very fine one. O, how he loves Caesar! Nay, but how dearly he adores Mark Antony! Caesar? Why, he’s the Jupiter of men. What’s Antony—the god of Jupiter? Spake you of Caesar? How, the nonpareil? O Antony, O thou Arabian bird! Would you praise Caesar, say ‘Caesar’; go no further.
