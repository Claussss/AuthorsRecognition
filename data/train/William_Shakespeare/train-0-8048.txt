5.1
Enter at one doorthe Duke, Varrius, and lords, at another doorAngelo, Escalus, Lucio, citizens, and officers DUKE (to Angelo) My very worthy cousin, fairly met.
(To Escalus) Our old and faithful friend, we are glad to
see you. ANGELO and ESCALUS Happy return be to your royal grace. Many and hearty thankings to you both.
We have made enquiry of you, and we hear
Such goodness of your justice that our soul
Cannot but yield you forth to public thanks,
Forerunning more requital.
