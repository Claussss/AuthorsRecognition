Enter Helenus passing by below CRESSIDA Who’s that? PANDARUS That’s Helenus. I marvel where Troilus is. That’s Helenus. I think he went not forth today. That’s Helenus. CRESSIDA Can Helenus fight, uncle? PANDARUS Helenus? No—yes, he’ll fight indifferent well. I marvel where Troilus is. Hark, do you not hear the people cry ‘Troilus’? Helenus is a priest.
