 2.1
Enter Brutus in his orchard 
BRUTUS What, Lucius, ho!— I cannot by the progress of the stars
Give guess how near to day.—Lucius, I say!—
I would it were my fault to sleep so soundty.—
When, Lucius, when? Awake, I say! What, Lucius! Enter Lucius LUCIUS Called you, my lord? Get me a taper in my study, Lucius. When it is lighted, come and call me here.
