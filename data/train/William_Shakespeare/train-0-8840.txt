APEMANTUS Asses. ALL SERVANTS Why? APEMANTUS That you ask me what you are, and do not know yourselves. Speak to ’em, fool. FOOL How do you, gentlemen? ALL SERVANTS Gramercies, good fool. How does your mistress? FOOL She’s e’en setting on water to scald such chickens as you are. Would we could see you at Corinth.
