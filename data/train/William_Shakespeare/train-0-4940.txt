MISTRESS FORD He’s too big to go in there. What shall I do? SIR JOHN (coming forward) Let me see’t, let me see’t, O let me see’t! I’ll in, I’ll in. Follow your friend’s counsel; I’ll in. MISTRESS PAGE What, Sir John Falstaff! (Aside to him) Are these your letters, knight? SIR JOHN (aside to Mistress Page) I love thee. Help me away. Let me creep in here.
