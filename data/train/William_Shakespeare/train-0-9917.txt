THIRD FISHERMAN What say you, master? MASTER Look how thou stirrest now. Come away, or I’ll fetch th’ with a wanion. THIRD FISHERMAN Faith, master, I am thinking of the poor men that were cast away before us even now. MASTER Alas, poor souls, it grieved my heart to hear what pitiful cries they made to us to help them when, well-a-day, we could scarce help ourselves.
