AMIENS Well, I’ll end the song.—Sirs, cover the while. Lords prepare food and drink The Duke will drink under this tree. (To Jaques) He hath been all this day to look you. JAQUES And I have been all this day to avoid him. He is too disputable for my company. I think of as many matters as he, but I give heaven thanks, and make no boast of them. Come, warble, come.
