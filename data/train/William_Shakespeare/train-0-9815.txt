LAFEU A scar nobly got, or a noble scar, is a good liv’ry of honour. So belike is that. LAVATCH But it is your carbonadoed face. LAFEU (to the Countess) Let us go see your son, I pray you. I long to talk with the young noble soldier. LAVATCH Faith, there’s a dozen of ’em, with delicate fine hats, and most courteous feathers, which bow the head and nod at every man.
