MORRIS I’ll hear no words, sir, fare you well. FALKNER ’Sblood, farewelll MORRIS Why dost thou follow me? FALKNER Because I’m an ass. Do you set your shavers upon me, and then cast me off? Must I condole? Have the Fates played the fools? (Weeps) Am I their cut? Now the poor sconce is taken, must Jack march with bag and baggage?
