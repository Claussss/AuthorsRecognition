THIRD PLEBEIAN Tear him, tear him! They set upon Cinna Come, brands, ho! Firebrands! To Brutus’, to Cassius’!
Burn all! Some to Decius’ house, and some to Casca’s;
some to Ligarius‘. Away, go! Exeunt all the Plebeians, with Cinna
