The west yet glimmers with some streaks of day.
Now spurs the lated traveller apace
To gain the timely inn, and near approaches
The subject of our watch. THIRD MURDERER Hark, I hear horses. BANQUO (within) Give us a light there, ho! SECOND MURDERER Then ’tis he. The rest That are within the note of expectation
Already are i’th’ court.
