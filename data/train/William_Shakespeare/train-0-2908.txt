DROMIO OF SYRACUSE Do you know me, sir? Am I Dromio? Am I your man? Am I myself? ANTIPHOLUS or SYRACUSE Thou art Dromio, thou art my man, thou art thyself. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE I am an ass, I am a woman’s man, and besides myself. ANTIPHOLUS or SYRACUSE What woman’s man? And how besides thyself? DROMIO OF SYRACUSE Marry, sir, besides myself I am due to a woman: one that claims me, one that haunts me, one that will have me.
