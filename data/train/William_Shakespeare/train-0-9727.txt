WIDOW So, now they come. That is Antonio, the Duke’s eldest son;
That, Escalus. Which is the Frenchman? That with the plume. ’Tis a most gallant fellow.
I would he loved his wife. If he were honester
He were much goodlier. Is’t not
A handsome gentleman? HELEN I like him well. DIANA ’Tis pity he is not honest.
