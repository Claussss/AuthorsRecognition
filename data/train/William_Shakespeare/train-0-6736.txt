OSRIC Your lordship speaks most infallibly of him. HAMLET The concernancy, sir? Why do we wrap the gentleman in our more rawer breath? HORATIO Is’t not possible to understand in another tongue? You will to’t, sir, rarely. HAMLET What imports the nomination of this gentleman? OSRIC Of Laertes? HORATIO (aside to Hamlet) His purse is empty already; all ’s golden words are spent.
