FIRST MURDERER (aside to Macbeth) ‘Tis Banquo’s, then. ’Tis better thee without than he within.
Is he dispatched? FIRST MURDERER My lord, his throat is cut. That I did for him. Thou art the best o’th’ cut-throats. Yet he’s good
That did the like for Fleance. If thou didst it,
Thou art the nonpareil.
