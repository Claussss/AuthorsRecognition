Some dungeon. RICHARD GLOUCESTER Your bedchamber. III rest betide the chamber where thou liest. RICHARD GLOUCESTER So will it, madam, till I lie with you. RICHARD GLOUCESTER I know so. But gentle Lady Anne, To leave this keen encounter of our wits
And fall something into a slower method,
Is not the causer of the timeless deaths
Of these Plantagenets, Henry and Edward,
As blameful as the executioner?
