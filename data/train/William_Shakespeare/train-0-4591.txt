POINS (within) Francis! FRANCIS Anon, anon, sir! PRINCE HARRY Five year! By’r Lady, a long lease for the clinking of pewter. But Francis, darest thou be so valiant as to play the coward with thy indenture, and show it a fair pair of heels, and run from it? FRANCIS O Lord, sir, I’ll be sworn upon all the books in England, I could find in my heart—
