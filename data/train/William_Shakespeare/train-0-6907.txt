OLIVIA ‘Cross-gartered’? MALVOLIO ‘Go to, thou art made, if thou desirest to be so.’ OLIVIA Am I made? MALVOLIO ‘If not, let me see thee a servant still.’ OLIVIA Why, this is very midsummer madness. Enter a Servant SERVANT Madam, the young gentleman of the Count Orsino’s is returned. I could hardly entreat him back. He attends your ladyship’s pleasure.
