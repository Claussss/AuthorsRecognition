PANDARUS Why, you know, ’tis dimpled. I think his smiling becomes him better than any man in all Phrygia. CRESSIDA O he smiles valiantly. PANDARUS Does he not? CRESSIDA O yes, an’t were a cloud in autumn. PANDARUS Why, go to then. But to prove to you that Helen loves Troilus— CRESSIDA Troilus will stand to the proof if you’ll prove it so.
