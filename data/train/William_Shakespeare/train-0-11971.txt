JAILER They are famed to be a pair of absolute men. JAILER’S DAUGHTER By my troth, I think fame but stammers ’em—they stand a grece above the reach of report. JAILER I heard them reported in the battle to be the only doers. JAILER’S DAUGHTER Nay, most likely, for they are noble sufferers. I marvel how they would have looked had they been victors, that with such a constant nobility enforce a freedom out of bondage, making misery their mirth, and affliction a toy to jest at.
