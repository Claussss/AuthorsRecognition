Enter Sir John Falstaff, Bardolph, and the Page
Yonder he comes, and that arrant malmsey-nose knave Bardolph with him. Do your offices, do your offices, Master Fang and Master Snare; do me, do me, do me your offices. SIR JOHN How now, whose mare’s dead? What’s the matter ? FANG Sir John, I arrest you at the suit of Mistress Quickly.
