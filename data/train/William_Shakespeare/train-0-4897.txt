SHALLOW God save you, Master Doctor Caius. PAGE Now, good Master Doctor. SLENDER Give you good morrow, sir. CAIUS Vat be all you, one, two, tree, four, come for? HOST To see thee fight, to see thee foin, to see thee traverse, to see thee here, to see thee there; to see thee pass thy punto, thy stock, thy reverse, thy distance, thy montant. Is he dead, my Ethiopian? Is he dead, my Francisco? Ha, bully? What says my Aesculapius, my Galen, my heart of elder, ha? Is he dead, bully stale? Is he dead?
