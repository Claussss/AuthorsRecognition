Flourish. Exeunt the King, carried, and Helen 
2.2
Enter the Countess and Lavatch the clown 
COUNTESS Come on, sir. I shall now put you to the height of your breeding. LAVATCH I will show myself highly fed and lowly taught. I know my business is but to the court. COUNTESS ‘To the court’? Why, what place make you special, when you put off that with such contempt? ‘But to the court’!
