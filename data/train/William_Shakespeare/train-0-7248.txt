I thank thee, most imperious Agamemnon. AGAMEMNON to Troilus My well-famed lord of Troy, no less to you. Let me confirm my princely brother’s greeting.
You brace of warlike brothers, welcome hither. He embraces Hector and Troilus HECTOR (to Aeneas) Who must we answer? AENEAS The noble Menelaus. O, you, my lord! By Mars his gauntlet, thanks.
Mock not that I affect th’untraded oath.
Your quondam wife swears still by Venus’ glove.
She’s well, but bade me not commend her to you.
