EDGAR (aside) How should this be? Bad is the trade that must play fool to sorrow,
Ang’ring itself and others. He comes forward Bless thee, master. Is that the naked fellow? Ay, my lord. Get thee away. If for my sake
Thou wilt o‘ertake us hence a mile or twain
I’th’ way toward Dover, do it for ancient love,
And bring some covering for this naked soul,
Which I’ll entreat to lead me.
