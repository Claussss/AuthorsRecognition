SECOND CITIZEN You shall ha’t, worthy sir. CORIOLANUS A match, sir. There’s in all two worthy voices begged. I have your alms. Adieu. THIRD CITIZEN (to the other Citizens) But this is something odd. SECOND CITIZEN An ‘twere to give again—but ’tis no matter. Exeunt Citizens Enter two other Citizens CORIOLANUS Pray you now, if it may stand with the tune of your voices that I may be consul, I have here the customary gown.
