BOLINGBROKE Go some of you and fetch a looking-glass. Exit one or more NORTHUMBERLAND Read o’er this paper while the glass doth come. Fiend, thou torment’st me ere I come to hell. BOLINGBROKE Urge it no more, my lord Northumberland. NORTHUMBERLAND The Commons will not then be satisfied. They shall be satisfied. I’ll read enough
When I do see the very book indeed
Where all my sins are writ, and that’s myself.
