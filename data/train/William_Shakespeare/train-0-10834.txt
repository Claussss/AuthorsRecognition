FOOL May not an ass know when the cart draws the horse? Sings Does any here know me? This is not Lear.
Does Lear walk thus, speak thus? Where are his eyes?
Either his notion weakens, his discernings
Are lethargied—ha, waking? ’Tis not so.
Who is it that can tell me who I am? FOOL Lear’s shadow. LEAR (to Goneril) Your name, fair gentlewoman?
