OLIVIA God comfort thee. Why dost thou smile so, and kiss thy hand so oft? MARIA How do you, Malvolio? MALVOLIO At your request?—yes, nightingales answer daws. MARIA Why appear you with this ridiculous boldness before my lady? MALVOLIO ‘Be not afraid of greatness’—’twas well writ. OLIVIA What meanest thou by that, Malvolio?
