CLOWN No, I hear not your honest friend, I hear you. CASSIO Prithee, keep up thy quillets. There’s a poor piece of gold for thee. If the gentlewoman that attends the general’s wife be stirring, tell her there’s one Cassio entreats her a little favour of speech. Wilt thou do this? CLOWN She is stirring, sir. If she will stir hither, I shall seem to notify unto her.
