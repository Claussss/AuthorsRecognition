BRUTUS He’s a lamb indeed that baas like a bear. MENENIUS He’s a bear indeed that lives like a lamb. You two are old men. Tell me one thing that I shall ask you. SICINIUS and BRUTUS Well, sir? MENENIUS In what enormity is Martius poor in that you two have not in abundance? BRUTUS He’s poor in no one fault, but stored with all. SICINIUS Especially in pride.
