I prithee, beat thy drum and get thee gone. I am thy friend, and pity thee, dear Timon. How dost thou pity him whom thou dost trouble?
I had rather be alone. ALCIBIADES Why, fare thee well. Here is some gold for thee. TIMON Keep it. I cannot eat it. When I have laid proud Athens on a heap— Warr‘st thou ’gainst Athens?
