Enter Menelaus and Paris, fighting, then 
THERSITES The cuckold and the cuckold-maker are at it.—Now, bull! Now, dog! ‘Loo, Paris, ’loo! Now, my double-horned Spartan! ’Loo, Paris, ’loo! The bull has the game. Ware horns, ho! Exit Menelaus driving in Enter Bastard behind BASTARD Turn, slave, and fight.
