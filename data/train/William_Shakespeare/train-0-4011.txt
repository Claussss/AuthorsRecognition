HIPPOLYTA Well shone, Moon.—Truly, the moon shines with a good grace. Lion worries Thisbe’s mantle THESEUS Well moused, Lion. DEMETRIUS And then came Pyramus. Enter Bottom as Pyramus LYSANDER And so the lion vanished. Exit Lion BOTTOM (as Pyramus) Sweet moon, I thank thee for thy sunny beams.
I thank thee, moon, for shining now so bright;
For by thy gracious, golden, glittering gleams
I trust to take of truest Thisbe sight.
