BEADLE I will, my lord. (To Simpcox) Come on, sirrah, off with your doublet quickly. SIMPCOX Alas, master, what shall I do ? I am not able to stand. After the Beadle hath hit him once, he leaps over the stool and runs away. Some of the townsmen follow and cry, ‘A miracle ! A miracle !’ O God, seest thou this and bear’st so long?
