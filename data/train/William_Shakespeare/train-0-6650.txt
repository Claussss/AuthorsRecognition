KING CLAUDIUS Let’s follow, Gertrude.
How much I had to do to calm his rage!
Now fear I this will give it start again;
Therefore let’s follow.  5.1
Enter two Clowns carrying a spade and a pickaxe FIRST CLOWN Is she to be buried in Christian burial that wilfully seeks her own salvation? SECOND CLOWN I tell thee she is, and therefore make her grave straight. The coroner hath sat on her, and finds it Christian burial.
