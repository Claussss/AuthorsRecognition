KING RICHARD Some light-foot friend post to the Duke of Norfolk.
Ratcliffe thyself, or Catesby—where is he? Here, my good lord. KING RICHARD Catesby, fly to the Duke. I will, my lord, with all convenient haste. KING RICHARD Ratcliffe, come hither. Post to Salisbury;
When thou com‘st thither—(to Catesby) dull, unmindful villain,
Why stay’st thou here, and goest not to the Duke?
