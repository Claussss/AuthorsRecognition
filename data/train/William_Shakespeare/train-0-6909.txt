Enter Sir Toby, Fabian, and Maria SIR TOBY Which way is he, in the name of sanctity? If all the devils of hell be drawn in little, and Legion himself possessed him, yet I’ll speak to him. FABIAN Here he is, here he is. (To Malvolio) How is’t with you, sir? How is’t with you, man? MALVOLIO Go off, I discard you. Let me enjoy my private. Go off.
