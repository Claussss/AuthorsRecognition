GRUMIO What say you to a neat’s foot? ‘Tis passing good. I prithee, let me have it. I fear it is too choleric a meat.
How say you to a fat tripe finely broiled? I like it well. Good Grumio, fetch it me. I cannot tell, I fear ’tis choleric.
What say you to a piece of beef, and mustard? A dish that I do love to feed upon.
