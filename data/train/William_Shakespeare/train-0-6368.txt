Enter Touchstone the clown and Audrey 
TOUCHSTONE Tomorrow is the joyful day, Audrey, tomorrow will we be married. AUDREY I do desire it with all my heart; and I hope it is no dishonest desire to desire to be a woman of the world. Here come two of the banished Duke’s pages.
