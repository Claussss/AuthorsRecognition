STEFANO This will prove a brave kingdom to me, where I shall have my music for nothing. CALIBAN When Prospero is destroyed. STEFANO That shall be by and by. I remember the story. Exit Ariel, playing music TRINCULO The sound is going away. Let’s follow it, and after do our work. STEFANO Lead, monster; we’ll follow.—I would I could see this taborer. He lays it on.
