Enter Othello (To Othello) Come, captain, will you go? OTHELLO Have with you. Here comes another troop to seek for you. It is Brabanzio. General, be advised.
He comes to bad intent. Holla, stand, there! RODERIGO (to Brabanzio) Signor, it is the Moor. Down with him, thief! IAGO (drawing his sword) You, Roderigo? Come, sir, I am for you.
