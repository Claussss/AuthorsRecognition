
4.7
Alarum. Enter Agrippa with drummers and trumpeters Retire! We have engaged our selves too far.
Caesar himself has work, and our oppression
Exceeds what we expected. 
4.8
Alarums. Enter Antony, and Scarus wounded O my brave Emperor, this is fought indeed!
Had we done so at first, we had droven them home
With clouts about their heads.
