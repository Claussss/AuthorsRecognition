DOLL TEARSHEET By my troth, thou‘lt set me a-weeping an thou sayst so. Prove that ever I dress myself handsome till thy return—well, hearken a’th’ end. SIR JOHN Some sack, Francis. PRINCE and POINS (coming forward) Anon, anon, sir. SIR JOHN Ha, a bastard son of the King’s !—And art not thou Poins his brother?
