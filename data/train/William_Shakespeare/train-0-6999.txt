OLIVIA Open’t and read it. FESTE Look then to be well edified when the fool delivers the madman. (Reads) ‘By the Lord, madam’— OLIVIA How now, art thou mad? FESTE No, madam, I do but read madness. An your ladyship will have it as it ought to be you must allow vox. OLIVIA Prithee, read i’thy right wits.
