This is the very false gallop of verses. Why do you infect yourself with them? ROSALIND Peace, you dull fool, I found them on a tree. TOUCHSTONE Truly, the tree yields bad fruit. ROSALIND I’ll graft it with you, and then I shall graft it with a medlar; then it will be the earliest fruit i’th’ country, for you’ll be rotten ere you be half-ripe, and that’s the right virtue of the medlar.
