CRESSIDA Ay, a minced man—and then to be baked with no date in the pie, for then the man’s date is out. PANDARUS You are such another woman! One knows not at what ward you lie. CRESSIDA Upon my back to defend my belly, upon my wit to defend my wiles, upon my secrecy to defend mine honesty, my mask to defend my beauty, and you to defend all these—and at all these wards I lie at a thousand watches.
