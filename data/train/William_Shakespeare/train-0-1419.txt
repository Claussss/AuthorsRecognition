Madam, I have been bold to trouble you.
But since your ladyship is not at leisure,
I’ll sort some other time to visit you. He is going COUNTESS (to Messenger) What means he now? Go ask him whither he goes. Stay, my Lord Talbot, for my lady craves
To know the cause of your abrupt departure. Marry, for that she’s in a wrong belief,
I go to certify her Talbot’s here.
