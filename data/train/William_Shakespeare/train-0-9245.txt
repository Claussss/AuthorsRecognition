CHARMIAN (to Soothsayer) Good sir, give me good fortune. SOOTHSAYER I make not, but foresee. Pray then, foresee me one. You shall be yet Far fairer than you are. CHARMIAN He means in flesh. No, you shall paint when you are old. Wrinkles forbid! Vex not his prescience. Be attentive. You shall be more beloving than beloved.
