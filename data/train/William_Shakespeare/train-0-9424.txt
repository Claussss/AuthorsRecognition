CLEOPATRA (rising,to Charmian and Iras) Ah, stand by. EROS The Queen, my lord, the Queen. IRAS Go to him, madam. Speak to him. He’s unqualitied
With very shame. CLEOPATRA Well then, sustain me. O! Most noble sir, arise. The Queen approaches.
Her head’s declined, and death will seize her but
Your comfort makes the rescue.
