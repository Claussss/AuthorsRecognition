Enter Boult Now, sir, hast thou cried her through the market? BOULT I have cried her almost to the number of her hairs. I have drawn her picture with my voice. BAWD And I prithee tell me, how dost thou find the inclination of the people, especially of the younger sort? BOULT Faith, they listened to me as they would have hearkened to their fathers’ testament. There was a Spaniard’s mouth watered as he went to bed to her very description.
