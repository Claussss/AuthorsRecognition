Lord Mayor and ladies and the rest, be patient.
The state hath sent, and I must needs be gone.
Lead on there.—What seek’st thou, fellow? PLAYER of WIT Your lordship sent us eight angels by your man, and I have lost one here amongst the rushes. MORE Eight angels? Who delivered it? I sent them ten. SERVINGMAN I, my lord, delivered it. Anon they shall have two more.
