Enter in skirmish, with bloody pates, Winchester’s Servingmen in tawny coats and Gloucester’s in blue coats We charge you, on allegiance to ourself,
To hold your slaught’ring hands and keep the peace. The skirmish ceases Pray, Uncle Gloucester, mitigate this strife. FIRST SERVINGMAN Nay, if we be forbidden stones, we’ll fall to it with our teeth.
