‘Farewell, dear heart, since I must needs be gone.’ MARIA Nay, good Sir Toby. ‘His eyes do show his days are almost done.’ MALVOLIO Is’t even so? ‘But I will never die.’ ‘Sir Toby, there you lie.’ MALVOLIO This is much credit to you. ‘Shall I bid him go?’ ‘What an if you do?’ ‘Shall I bid him go, and spare not?’
