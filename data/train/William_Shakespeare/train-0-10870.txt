KENT Thou whoreson Z, thou unnecessary letter—(to Cornwall) my lord, if you’ll give me leave I will tread this unbolted villain into mortar and daub the wall of a jakes with him. (To Oswald) Spare my grey beard, you wagtail? CORNWALL Peace, sirrah. You beastly knave, know you no reverence? Yes, sir, but anger hath a privilege.
