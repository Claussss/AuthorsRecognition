ACHILLES What? THERSITES I say, this Ajax— Ajax threatens to strike him ACHILLES Nay, good Ajax. THERSITES Has not so much wit— Ajax threatens to strike him ACHILLES (to Ajax) Nay, I must hold you. THERSITES As will stop the eye of Helen’s needle, for whom he comes to fight. ACHILLES Peace, fool. THERSITES I would have peace and quietness, but the fool will not. He, there, that he, look you there.
