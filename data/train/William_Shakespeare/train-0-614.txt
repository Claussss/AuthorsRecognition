TAILOR I have. GRUMIO Face not me. Thou hast braved many men. Brave not me. I will neither be faced nor braved. I say unto thee I bid thy master cut out the gown, but I did not bid him cut it to pieces. Ergo thou liest. TAILOR (showing a paper) Why, here is the note of the fashion, to testify. PETRUCCIO Read it.
