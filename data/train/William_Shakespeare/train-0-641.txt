5.1
Enter Biondello, Lucentio, and Bianca. Gremio is out before 
BIONDELLO Softly and swiftly, sir, for the priest is ready. LUCENTIO I fly, Biondello; but they may chance to need thee at home, therefore leave us. BIONDELLO Nay, faith, I’ll see the church a’ your back and then come back to my master’s as soon as I can.
