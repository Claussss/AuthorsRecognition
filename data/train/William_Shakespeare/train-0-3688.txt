Romeo beats down their points and rushes between them. Tybalt under Romeo’s arm thrusts Mercutio in Exeunt Tybalt, Petruccio, and their followers MERCUTIO I am hurt. A plague o’ both your houses. I am sped.
Is he gone, and hath nothing? BENVOLIO What, art thou hurt? Ay, ay, a scratch, a scratch; marry, ’tis enough.
