SECOND GENTLEMAN Who’s that, I pray thee? MISTRESS OVERDONE Marry sir, that’s Claudio, Signor Claudio. FIRST GENTLEMAN Claudio to prison? ’Tis not so. MISTRESS OVERDONE Nay, but I know ’tis so. I saw him arrested, saw him carried away; and, which is more, within these three days his head to be chopped off.
