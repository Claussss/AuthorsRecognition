KING RICHARD Cousin, farewell; and uncle, bid him so.
Six years we banish him, and he shall go. Flourish.Exeunt all but Aumerle, the Lord Marshal, John of Gaunt, and Bolingbroke AUMERLE (to Bolingbroke) Cousin, farewell. What presence must not know,
From where you do remain let paper show. LORD MARSHAL (to Bolingbroke)
