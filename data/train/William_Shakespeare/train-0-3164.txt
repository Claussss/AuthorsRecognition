To look like her are chimney-sweepers black. LONGUEVILLE And since her time are colliers counted bright. And Ethiops of their sweet complexion crack. Dark needs no candles now, for dark is light. Your mistresses dare never come in rain,
For fear their colours should be washed away. ‘Twere good yours did; for, sir, to tell you plain,
I’ll find a fairer face not washed today.
