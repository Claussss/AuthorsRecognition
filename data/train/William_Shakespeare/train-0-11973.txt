WOOER I never saw ’em. JAILER The Duke himself came privately in the night, Palamon and Arcite appear at a windowabove  and so did they. What the reason of it is I know not. Look, yonder they are. That’s Arcite looks out. JAILER’S DAUGHTER No, sir, no—that’s Palamon. Arcite is the lower of the twain—(pointing at Arcite) you may perceive a part of him.
