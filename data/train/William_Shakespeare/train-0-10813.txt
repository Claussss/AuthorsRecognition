Horns within OSWALD He’s coming, madam. I hear him. Put on what weary negligence you please,
You and your fellows. I’d have it come to question.
If he distaste it, let him to my sister,
Whose mind and mine I know in that are one.
Remember what I have said. OSWALD Well, madam. And let his knights have colder looks among you.
What grows of it, no matter. Advise your fellows so.
I’ll write straight to my sister to hold my course.
Prepare for dinner.
