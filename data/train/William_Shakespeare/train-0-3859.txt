QUINCE Robin Starveling, you must play Thisbe’s mother. Tom Snout, the tinker? SNOUT Here, Peter Quince. QUINCE You, Pyramus’ father; myself, Thisbe’s father. Snug the joiner, you the lion’s part; and I hope here is a play fitted. SNUG Have you the lion’s part written? Pray you, if it be, give it me; for I am slow of study.
