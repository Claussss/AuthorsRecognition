Thaisa returns to the King He thanks your grace, names himself Pericles,
A gentleman of Tyre, who, seeking adventures,
Was solely by misfortune of the seas
Bereft of ships and men, cast on this shore. KING SIMONIDES Now by the gods I pity his mishaps,
And will awake him from his melancholy. Simonides, rising from his state, goes forthwith and embraces Pericles
