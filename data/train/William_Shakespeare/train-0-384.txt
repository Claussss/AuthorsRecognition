What? That my leg is too long? No, that it is too little. I’ll wear a boot, to make it somewhat rounder. JULIA (aside) But love will not be spurred to what it loathes. What says she to my face? She says it is a fair one. Nay, then, the wanton lies. My face is black. But pearls are fair; and the old saying is,
‘Black men are pearls in beauteous ladies’ eyes’.
