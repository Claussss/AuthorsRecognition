Enter Bardolph followed bythe Page By the mass, here comes Bardolph. PRINCE HARRY And the boy that I gave Falstaff. A had him from me Christian, and look if the fat villain have not transformed him ape. BARDOLPH God save your grace! PRINCE HARRY And yours, most noble Bardolph! POINS (to Bardolph) Come, you virtuous ass, you bashful fool, must you be blushing? Wherefore blush you now? What a maidenly man at arms are you become! Is’t such a matter to get a pottle-pot’s maidenhead?
