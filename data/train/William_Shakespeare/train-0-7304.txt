I come to lose my arm or win my sleeve. Enter Pandarus PANDARUS Do you hear, my lord, do you hear? TROILUS What now? PANDARUS Here’s a letter come from yon poor girl. TROILUS Let me read. Troilus reads the letter PANDARUS A whoreson phthisic, a whoreson rascally phthisic so troubles me, and the foolish fortune of this girl, and what one thing, what another, that I shall leave you one o’ these days.
