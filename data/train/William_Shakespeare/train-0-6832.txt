SIR TOBY Possess us, possess us, tell us something of him. MARIA Marry, sir, sometimes he is a kind of puritan. SIR ANDREW O, if I thought that I’d beat him like a dog. SIR TOBY What, for being a puritan? Thy exquisite reason, dear knight. SIR ANDREW I have no exquisite reason for’t, but I have reason good enough.
