Alarum. Enter Aeneas How now, Prince Troilus? Wherefore not afield? Because not there. This woman’s answer sorts,
For womanish it is to be from thence.
What news, Aeneas, from the field today? That Paris is returned home, and hurt. By whom, Aeneas? AENEAS Troilus, by Menelaus. Let Paris bleed, ’tis but a scar to scorn:
Paris is gored with Menelaus’ horn.
