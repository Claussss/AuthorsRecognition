STEFANO Trinculo, if you trouble him any more in’s tale, by this hand, I will supplant some of your teeth. TRINCULO Why, I said nothing. STEFANO Mum, then, and no more. (To Caliban) Proceed. I say by sorcery he got this isle;
From me he got it. If thy greatness will
Revenge it on him—for I know thou dar’st,
But this thing dare not—
