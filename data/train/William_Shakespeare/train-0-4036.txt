What follows if we disallow of this? The proud control of fierce and bloody war,
To enforce these rights so forcibly withheld— Here have we war for war, and blood for blood,
Controlment for controlment: so answer France. Then take my king’s defiance from my mouth,
The farthest limit of my embassy. Bear mine to him, and so depart in peace.
Be thou as lightning in the eyes of France,
For ere thou canst report, I will be there; 25
The thunder of my cannon shall be heard.
So hence. Be thou the trumpet of our wrath,
And sullen presage of your own decay.—
