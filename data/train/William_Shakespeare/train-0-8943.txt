First mend my company: take away thyself. So I shall mend mine own by th’ lack of thine. ’Tis not well mended so, it is but botched;
If not, I would it were. APEMANTUS What wouldst thou have to Athens? Thee thither in a whirlwind. If thou wilt,
Tell them there I have gold. Look, so I have. Here is no use for gold.
