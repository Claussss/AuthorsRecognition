SCHOOLMASTER An eel and woman, A learned poet says, unless by th’ tail
And with thy teeth thou hold, will either fail—
In manners this was false position. FIRST COUNTRYMAN A fire-ill take her! Does she flinch now? THIRD COUNTRYMAN What Shall we determine, sir? SCHOOLMASTER Our business is become a nullity,
Yea, and a woeful and a piteous nullity.
