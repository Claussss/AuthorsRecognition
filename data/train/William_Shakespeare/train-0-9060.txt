LADY MACBETH I heard the owl scream and the crickets cry.
Did not you speak? MACBETH When? LADY MACBETH Now. MACBETH As I descended? LADY MACBETH MACBETH Hark!—Who lies i’th’ second chamber? LADY MACBETH MACBETH (looking at his hands) This is a sorry sight. LADY MACBETH A foolish thought, to say a sorry sight.
