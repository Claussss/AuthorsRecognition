(Singing) ’And down, down, adown-a’ (etc.) Enter Doctor Caius CAIUS Vat is you sing? I do not like dese toys. Pray you go and vetch me in my closet un bo vert—a box, a green-a box. Do intend vat I speak? A green-a box. MISTRESS QUICKLY Ay, forsooth, I’ll fetch it you. (Aside) I am glad he went not in himself. If he had found the young man, he would have been horn-mad.
