ROMEO Here’s goodly gear. MERCUTIO Two, two—a shirt and a smock. NURSE Peter. PETER Anon. NURSE My fan, Peter. MERCUTIO Good Peter, to hide her face, for her fan’s the fairer face. NURSE God ye good morrow, gentlemen. MERCUTIO God ye good e’en, fair gentlewoman. NURSE Is it good e’en? MERCUTIO ’Tis no less, I tell ye: for the bawdy hand of the dial is now upon the prick of noon.
