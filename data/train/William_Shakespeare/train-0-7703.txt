Beware, good Wit, you take not her to wife. INCLINATION [to Lady Vanity] What, Unknown Honesty, a word in your ear. [Lady Vanity] offers to depart You shall not be gone as yet, I swear.
Here’s none but your friends; you need not to fray.
This young gentleman loves ye, therefore you must stay.
