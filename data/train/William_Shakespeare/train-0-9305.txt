Thou art a soldier only. Speak no more. ENOBARBUS That truth should be silent I had almost forgot. You wrong this presence, therefore speak no more. ENOBARBUS Go to, then; your considerate stone. I do not much dislike the matter, but
The manner of his speech, for’t cannot be
We shall remain in friendship, our conditions
So diff‘ring in their acts. Yet if I knew
What hoop should hold us staunch, from edge to edge
O’th’ world I would pursue it.
