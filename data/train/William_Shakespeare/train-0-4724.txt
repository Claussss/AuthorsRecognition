BLUNT Pray God you do. Exeunt [Hotspur, Worcester, Douglas, and Vernon at one door, Blunt at another door 4.4
Enter the Archbishop of York, and Sir Michael 
ARCHBISHOP (giving letters) Hie, good Sir Michael, bear this sealed brief
With winged haste to the Lord Marshal,
This to my cousin Scrope, and all the rest
To whom they are directed. If you knew
How much they do import, you would make haste.
