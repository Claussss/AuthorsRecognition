SIR JOHN I would it were otherwise; I would my means were greater and my waist slenderer. LORD CHIEF JUSTICE You have misled the youthful Prince. SIR JOHN The young Prince hath misled me. I am the fellow with the great belly, and he my dog. LORD CHIEF JUSTICE Well, I am loath to gall a new-healed wound. Your day’s service at Shrewsbury hath a little gilded over your night’s exploit on Gads Hill. You may thank th‘unquiet time for your quiet o’erposting that action.
