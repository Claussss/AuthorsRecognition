AUTOLYCUS I hope so, sir, for I have about me many parcels of charge. CLOWN What hast here? Ballads? MOPSA Pray now, buy some. I love a ballad in print, alife, for then we are sure they are true. AUTOLYCUS Here’s one to a very doleful tune, how a usurer’s wife was brought to bed of twenty money-bags at a burden, and how she longed to eat adders’ heads and toads carbonadoed.
