COUNTESS I think, sir, you can eat none of this homely meat. LAVATCH O Lord, sir!—Nay, put me to’t, I warrant you. COUNTESS You were lately whipped, sir, as I think. LAVATCH O Lord, sir!—Spare not me. COUNTESS Do you cry ‘O Lord, sir!’ at your whipping, and ‘spare not me’? Indeed, your ‘O Lord, sir!’ is very sequent to your whipping. You would answer very well to a whipping, if you were but bound to’t.
