Call hither Barnardine and Claudio. Exeunt Abhorson and Pompey Th’one has my pity; not a jot the other,
Being a murderer, though he were my brother. Enter Claudio Look, here’s the warrant, Claudio, for thy death.
’Tis now dead midnight, and by eight tomorrow
Thou must be made immortal. Where’s Barnardine?
