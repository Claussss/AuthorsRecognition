ARMADO Men of peace, well encountered. HOLOFERNES Most military sir, salutation! MOTE (aside to Costard) They have been at a great feast of languages and stolen the scraps. COSTARD (aside to Mote) O, they have lived long on the alms-basket of words. I marvel thy master hath not eaten thee for a word, for thou art not so long by the head as honorificabilitudinitatibus. Thou art easier swallowed than a flapdragon.
