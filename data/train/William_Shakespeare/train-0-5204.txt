PRINCE HARRY Why, thou globe of sinful continents, what a life dost thou lead! SIR JOHN A better than thou: I am a gentleman, thou art a drawer. PRINCE HARRY Very true, sir, and I come to draw you out by the ears. MISTRESS QUICKLY O, the Lord preserve thy grace! By my troth, welcome to London! Now the Lord bless that sweet face of thine!
