[Addition IV (Dekker)]
MORRIS What ail’st thou? Art thou mad now? FALKNER Mad now? Nails, if loss of hair cannot mad a man—what can? I am deposed: my crown is taken from me. More had been better a’ scoured Moorditch than a’ notched me thus. Does he begin sheep-shearing with Jack Falkner? MORRIS Nay, an you feed this vein, sir, fare you well.
