
1.2
A flourish of cornetts. Enter the King of France with letters, the two Lords Dumaine, and divers attendants The Florentines and Sienese are by th’ears,
Have fought with equal fortune, and continue
A braving war. FIRST LORD DUMAINE So ’tis reported, sir. Nay, ’tis most credible: we here receive it
A certainty vouched from our cousin Austria,
With caution that the Florentine will move us
For speedy aid-wherein our dearest friend
Prejudicates the business, and would seem
To have us make denial.
