LEAR What art thou? KENT A very honest-hearted fellow, and as poor as the King. LEAR If thou be‘st as poor for a subject as he’s for a king, thou’rt poor enough. What wouldst thou? KENT Service. LEAR Who wouldst thou serve? LEAR Dost thou know me, fellow? KENT No, sir, but you have that in your countenance which I would fain call master.
