Enter Bardolph, muddy BARDOLPH O Lord, sir, cozenage, mere cozenagel HOST Where be my horses? Speak well of them, varletto. BARDOLPH Run away with the cozeners. For so soon as I came beyond Eton, they threw me off from behind one of them, in a slough of mire, and set spurs and away, like three German devils, three Doctor Faustuses.
