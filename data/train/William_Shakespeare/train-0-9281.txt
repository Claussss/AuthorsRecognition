CLEOPATRA (yawning) Ha, ha. Give me to drink mandragora. CHARMIAN Why, madam? That I might sleep out this great gap of time
My Antony is away. You think of him too much. O, ’tis treason! CHARMIAN Madam, I trust not so. Thou, eunuch Mardian! MARDIAN What’s your highness’ pleasure? Not now to hear thee sing. I take no pleasure
In aught an eunuch has. ’is well for thee
That, being unseminared, thy freer thoughts
May not fly forth of Egypt. Hast thou affections?
