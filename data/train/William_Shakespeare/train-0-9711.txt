SECOND LORD DUMAINE A servant only, and a gentleman
Which I have sometime known. COUNTESS Paroles, was it not? SECOND LORD DUMAINE Ay, my good lady, he. A very tainted fellow, and full of wickedness.
My son corrupts a well-deriv
With his inducement. SECOND LORD DUMAINE Indeed, good lady, The fellow has a deal of that too much,
Which holds him much to have.
