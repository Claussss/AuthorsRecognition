Enter Gadshill visored GADSHILL Stand! SIR JOHN So I do, against my will. POINS O, ’tis our setter, I know his voice. Gadshill, what news? SIR JOHN You lie, ye rogue, ’tis going to the King’s tavern. GADSHILL There’s enough to make us all. SIR JOHN To be hanged. They put on visors PRINCE HARRY Sirs, you four shall front them in the narrow lane. Ned Poins and I will walk lower. If they scape from your encounter, then they light on us.
