Exit Alexas Madam, methinks, if you did love him dearly,
You do not hold the method to enforce
The like from him. CLEOPATRA What should I do I do not? In each thing give him way; cross him in nothing. Thou teachest like a fool, the way to lose him. Tempt him not so too far. Iwis, forbear.
In time we hate that which we often fear.
