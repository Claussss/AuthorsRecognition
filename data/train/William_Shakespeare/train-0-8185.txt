Enter Desdemona, Iago, Emilia, and Roderigo O, behold,
The riches of the ship is come on shore!
You men of Cyprus, let her have your knees. Montano and the Gentlemen make curtsy to Desdemona Hail to thee, lady, and the grace of heaven
Before, behind thee, and on every hand
Enwheel thee round! I thank you, valiant Cassio.
