LORD CHIEF JUSTICE And so they are. PRINCE JOHN The King hath called his parliament, my lord. LORD CHIEF JUSTICE He hath. PRINCE JOHN I will lay odds that, ere this year expire,
We bear our civil swords and native fire
As far as France. I heard a bird so sing,
Whose music, to my thinking, pleased the King.
Come, will you hence? Exeunt
