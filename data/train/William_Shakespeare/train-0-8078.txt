ESCALUS In very good time. Speak not you to him till we call upon you. ESCALUS (to the Duke) Come, sir, did you set these women on to slander Lord Angelo? They have confessed you did. DUKE ’Tis false. ESCALUS How! Know you where you are? Respect to your great place, and let the devil
Be sometime honoured fore his burning throne.
Where is the Duke? ’Tis he should hear me speak.
