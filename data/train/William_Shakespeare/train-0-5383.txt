BEATRICE And a good soldier to a lady, but what is he to a lord? MESSENGER A lord to a lord, a man to a man, stuffed with all honourable virtues. BEATRICE It is so, indeed. He is no less than a stuffed man. But for the stuffing—well, we are all mortal. LEONATO You must not, sir, mistake my niece. There is a kind of merry war betwixt Signor Benedick and her. They never meet but there’s a skirmish of wit between them. 61
