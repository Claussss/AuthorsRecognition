OTHELLO Indeed! DESDEMONA My lord? OTHELLO (to Desdemona) I am glad to see you mad. DESDEMONA Why, sweet Othello! OTHELLO Devil! He strikes her DESDEMONA I have not deserved this. My lord, this would not be believed in Venice,
Though I should swear I saw’t. ’Tis very much.
Make her amends, she weeps.
