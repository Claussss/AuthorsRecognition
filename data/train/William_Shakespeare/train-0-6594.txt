QUEEN GERTRUDE Be thou assured, if words be made of breath,
And breath of life, I have no life to breathe
What thou hast said to me. I must to England.
You know that? QUEEN GERTRUDE Alack, I had forgot.
’Tis so concluded on. This man shall set me packing.
I’ll lug the guts into the neighbour room.
Mother, good night indeed. This counsellor
Is now most still, most secret, and most grave,
Who was in life a foolish prating knave.—
Come, sir, to draw toward an end with you.—
Good night, mother. Exit, tugging in Polonius
