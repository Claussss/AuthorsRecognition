But heavenly Rosalind! Exit 13. Enter Celia and Rosalind 
CELIA Why cousin, why Rosalind—Cupid have mercy, not a word? ROSALIND Not one to throw at a dog. CELIA No, thy words are too precious to be cast away upon curs. Throw some of them at me. Come, lame me with reasons.
