SIR JOHN Bardolph, follow him. A tapster is a good trade. An old cloak makes a new jerkin; a withered servingman a fresh tapster. Go; adieu. BARDOLPH It is a life that I have desired. I will thrive. O base Hungarian wight, wilt thou the spigot wield? NIM He was gotten in drink; his mind is not heroic. Is not the humour conceited?
