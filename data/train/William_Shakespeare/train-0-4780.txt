SHALLOW Ay, cousin Slender, and Custalorum. SLENDER Ay, and Ratolorum too; and a gentleman born, Master Parson, who writes himself ‘Armigero’ in any bill, warrant, quittance, or obligation: ‘Armigero’. SHALLOW Ay, that I do, and have done any time these three hundred years. SLENDER All his successors gone before him hath done’t, and all his ancestors that come after him may. They may give the dozen white luces in their coat.
