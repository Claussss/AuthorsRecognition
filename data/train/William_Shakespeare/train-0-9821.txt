LAVATCH Truly, Fortune’s displeasure is but sluttish if it smell so strongly as thou speakest of. I will henceforth eat no fish of Fortune’s butt’ring. Prithee allow the wind. PAROLES Nay, you need not to stop your nose, sir, I spake but by a metaphor. 11 LAVATCH Indeed, sir, if your metaphor stink I will stop my nose, or against any man’s metaphor.
