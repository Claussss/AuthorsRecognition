FIRST LORD DUMAINE (aside) He hath out-villained villainy so far that the rarity redeems him. BERTRAM (aside) A pox on him! He’s a cat still. INTERPRETER His qualities being at this poor price, I need not to ask you if gold will corrupt him to revolt. PAROLES Sir, for a quart d he will sell the fee-simple of his salvation, the inheritance of it, and cut th’entail from all remainders, and a perpetual succession for it perpetually.
