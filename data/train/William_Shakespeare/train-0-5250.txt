BARDOLPH (giving Wart a caliver) Hold, Wart. Traverse—thas, thas, thas! 269 Wart marches SIR JOHN (to Wart) Come, manage me your caliver. So; very well. Go to, very good, exceeding good. O, give me always a little, lean, old, chapped, bald shot! Well said, i‘faith, Wart; thou’rt a good scab. Hold; (giving a coin) there’s a tester for thee.
