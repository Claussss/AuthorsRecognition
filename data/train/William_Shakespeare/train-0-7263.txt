Thou art thought to be Achilles’ male varlet. PATROCLUS ‘Male varlet’, you rogue? What’s that? THERSITES Why, his masculine whore. Now the rotten diseases of the south, guts-griping, ruptures, catarrhs, loads o’ gravel i’th’ back, lethargies, cold palsies, and the like, take and take again such preposterous discoveries!
