Why hast thou slain thine only daughter thus? Not I, ‘twas Chiron and Demetrius.
They ravished her, and cut away her tongue,
And they, ’twas they, that did her all this wrong. Go, fetch them hither to us presently. TITUS revealing the heads Why, there they are, both baked in this pie,
Whereof their mother daintily hath fed,
Eating the flesh that she herself hath bred.
‘Tis true, ’tis true, witness my knife’s sharp point.
