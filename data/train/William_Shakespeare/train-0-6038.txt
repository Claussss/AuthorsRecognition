ALL THE PLEBEIANS We’ll mutiny. FIRST PLEBEIAN We’ll burn the house of Brutus. THIRD PLEBEIAN Away then! Come, seek the conspirators. Yet hear me, countrymen, yet hear me speak. ALL THE PLEBEIANS Peace, ho! Hear Antony, most noble Antony. Why, friends, you go to do you know not what.
Wherein hath Caesar thus deserved your loves?
Alas, you know not. I must tell you’ then.
You have forgot the will I told you of.
