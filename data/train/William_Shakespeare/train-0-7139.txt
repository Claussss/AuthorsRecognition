She tickles him PANDARUS Rude, in sooth, in good sooth very rude. PARIS Well said, my lord. Will you say so in fits? PANDARUS I have business to my lord, dear Queen.—My lord, will you vouchsafe me a word? HELEN Nay, this shall not hedge us out. We’ll hear you sing, certainly. PANDARUS Well, sweet Queen, you are pleasant with me.—But marry, thus, my lord: my dear lord and most esteemed friend, your brother Troilus—
