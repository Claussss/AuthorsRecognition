PRINCESS Thou shalt know her, fellow, by the rest that have no heads. COSTARD Which is the greatest lady, the highest? PRINCESS The thickest and the tallest. The thickest and the tallest—it is so, truth is truth.
An your waist, mistress, were as slender as my wit
One o’ these maids’ girdles for your waist should be fit.
Are not you the chief woman? You are the thickest here.
