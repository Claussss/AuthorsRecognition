SIR TOBY Did she see thee the while, old boy? Tell me that. SIR ANDREW As plain as I see you now. FABIAN This was a great argument of love in her toward you. SIR ANDREW ’Slight, will you make an ass o’ me? FABIAN I will prove it legitimate, sir, upon the oaths of judgement and reason. SIR TOBY And they have been grand-jurymen since before Noah was a sailor.
