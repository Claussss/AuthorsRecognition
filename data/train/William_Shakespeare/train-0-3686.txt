O calm, dishonourable, vile submission! 1
Alla stoccado carries it away.
Tybalt, you ratcatcher, come, will you walk? TYBALT What wouldst thou have with me? MERCUTIO Good King of Cats, nothing but one of your nine lives. That I mean to make bold withal, and, as you shall use me hereafter, dry-beat the rest of the eight. Will you pluck your sword out of his pilcher by the ears? Make haste, lest mine be about your ears ere it be out.
