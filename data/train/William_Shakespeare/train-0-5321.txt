DAVY Here, sir. SHALLOW Davy, Davy, Davy; let me see, Davy; let me see. William Cook—bid him come hither.—Sir John, you shall not be excused. DAVY Marry, sir, thus: those precepts cannot be served. And again, sir: shall we sow the headland with wheat? SHALLOW With red wheat, Davy. But for William Cook; are there no young pigeons?
