ROSENCRANTZ We think not so, my lord. HAMLET Why, then ’tis none to you, for there is nothing either good or bad but thinking makes it so. To me it is a prison. ROSENCRANTZ Why, then your ambition makes it one; ’tis too narrow for your mind. HAMLET O God, I could be bounded in a nutshell and count myself a king of infinite space, were it not that I have bad dreams.
