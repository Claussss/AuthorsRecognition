TYBALT (drawing) I am for you. Gentle Mercutio, put thy rapier up. MERCUTIO (to Tybalt) Come, sir, your passado. ROMEO drawing Draw, Benvolio. Beat down their weapons.
Gentlemen, for shame forbear this outrage.
Tybalt, Mercutio, the Prince expressly hath
Forbid this bandying in Verona streets.
Hold, Tybalt, good Mercutio.
