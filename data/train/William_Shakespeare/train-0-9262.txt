Enter Cleopatra, Charmian, Alexas, and Iras Where is he? CHARMIAN I did not see him since. CLEOPATRA to Alexas See where he is, who’s with him, what he does.
I did not send you. If you find him sad,
Say I am dancing; if in mirth, report
That I am sudden sick. Quick, and return.
