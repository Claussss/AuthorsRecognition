SIR ANDREW (to Sir Toby) ‘Odours’, ‘pregnant’, and ‘vouchsafed’—I’ll get ’em all three all ready. OLIVIA Let the garden door be shut, and leave me to my hearing. Exeunt Sir Toby, Sir Andrew, and Maria Give me your hand, sir. My duty, madam, and most humble service. OLIVIA What is your name? Cesario is your servant’s name, fair princess.
