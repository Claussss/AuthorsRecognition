DOLL TEARSHEET Better than I was.—Hem! MISTRESS QUICKLY Why, that’s well said! A good heart’s worth gold. Enter Sir John Falstaff Lo, here comes Sir John. SIR JOHN (sings) CallsSings) ‘And was a worthy king’—How now, Mistress Doll? MISTRESS QUICKLY Sick of a qualm, yea, good faith. SIR JOHN So is all her sect; an they be once in a calm, they are sick.
