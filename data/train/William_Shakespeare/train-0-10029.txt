Sc. 16 A brothel sign.] Enter the Pander, his wife the Bawd, and their man Boult 
PANDER Boult. PANDER Search the market narrowly. Mytilene is full of gallants. We lose too much money this mart by being wenchless. BAWD We were never so much out of creatures.
