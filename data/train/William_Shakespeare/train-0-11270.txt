We’ll not be long away. BELARIUS (to Innogen) Pray be not sick, For you must be our housewife. INNOGEN Well or ill, I am bound to you. BELARIUS And shalt be ever. This youth, howe’er distressed, appears hath had
Good ancestors. ARVIRAGUS How angel-like he sings! GUIDERIUS But his neat cookery! He cut our roots in characters,
And sauced our broths as Juno had been sick
And he her dieter.
