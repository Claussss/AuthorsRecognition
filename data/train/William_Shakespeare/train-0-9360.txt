ENOBARBUS Not till you have slept—I fear me you’ll be in till then. LEPIDUS Nay, certainly, I have heard the Ptolemies’ pyramises are very goodly things: without contradiction I have heard that. MENAS (aside to Pompey) Pompey, a word. POMPEY (aside to Menas) Say in mine ear; what is’t? MENAS (aside to Pompey)
