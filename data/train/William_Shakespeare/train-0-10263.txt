THIRD CITIZEN How not your own desire? CORIOLANUS No, sir, ’twas never my desire yet to trouble the poor with begging. THIRD CITIZEN You must think if we give you anything we hope to gain by you. CORIOLANUS Well then, I pray, your price o’th’ consulship? FIRST CITIZEN The price is to ask it kindly. CORIOLANUS Kindly, sir, I pray let me ha’t. I have wounds to show you which shall be yours in private. (To Second Citizen) Your good voice, sir. What say you?
