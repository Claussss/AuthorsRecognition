FLAVIUS O, let me stay And comfort you, my master. TIMON If thou hat’st curses, Stay not. Fly whilst thou art blest and free.
Ne’er see thou man, and let me ne’er see thee. Exeunt Timon into his cave, Flavius another way 5.1
