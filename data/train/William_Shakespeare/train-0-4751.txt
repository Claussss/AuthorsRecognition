PRINCE HARRY He is indeed, And living to kill thee. I prithee
Lend me thy sword. SIR JOHN Nay, before God, Hal, If Percy be alive thou gett’st not my sword; But take my pistol if thou wilt. PRINCE HARRY Give it me. What, is it in the case? SIR JOHN Ay, Hal; ’Tis hot, ’tis hot. There’s that will sack a city.
