SIMPLE May I be bold to say so, sir? SIR JOHN Ay, Sir Tike; who more bold? SIMPLE I thank your worship. I shall make my master glad with these tidings. Exit HOST Thou art clerkly, thou art clerkly, Sir John. Was there a wise woman with thee? SIR JOHN Ay, that there was, mine Host, one that hath taught me more wit than ever I learned before in my life. And I paid nothing for it, neither, but was paid for my learning.
