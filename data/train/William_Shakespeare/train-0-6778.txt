MARIA Peace, you rogue, no more o’ that. Here comes my lady. Make your excuse wisely, you were best. Enter Olivia, with Malvolio and attendants FESTE asideOlivia) God bless thee, lady. OLIVIA (to attendants) Take the fool away. FESTE Do you not hear, fellows? Take away the lady. OLIVIA Go to, you’re a dry fool. I’ll no more of you. Besides, you grow dishonest.
