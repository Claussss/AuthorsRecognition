Alarums. Excursions, whereinatthew Gough is slain, and all the rest of his men with him. Then enter Jack Cade with his company, among them the Butcher, the Weaver, and John, a rebel 
CADE So, sirs, now go some and pull down the Savoy; others to th’ Inns of Court—down with them all. BUTCHER I have a suit unto your lordship.
