NURSE I know not. Go ask his name. The Nurse goes  If he be married,
My grave is like to be my wedding bed. NURSE (returning) His name is Romeo, and a Montague,
The only son of your great enemy. JULIET aside My only love sprung from my only hate!
Too early seen unknown, and known too late!
Prodigious birth of love it is to me
That I must love a loathed enemy.
