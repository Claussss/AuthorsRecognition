Forsake thy seat, I do beseech thee, captain,
And hear me speak a word. POMPEY (aside to Menas) Forbear me till anon. (Aloud) This wine for Lepidus! Menas whispers in Pompey’s ear LEPIDUS What manner o’ thing is your crocodile? ANTONY It is shaped, sir, like itself; and it is as broad as it hath breadth. It is just so high as it is, and moves with it own organs. It lives by that which nourisheth it, and the elements once out of it, it transmigrates.
