Enter Ventidius O, come, Ventidius.
You must to Parthia, your commission’s ready.
Follow me, and receive’t. 
2.4
Enter Lepidus, Maecenas, and Agrippa Trouble yourselves no further. Pray you, hasten
Your generals after. Sir, Mark Antony Will e’en but kiss Octavia, and we’ll follow. Till I shall see you in your soldier’s dress,
Which will become you both, farewell.
