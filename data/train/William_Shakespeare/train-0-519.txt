KATHERINE What is your crest—a coxcomb? A combless cock, so Kate will be my hen. No cock of mine. You crow too like a craven. Nay, come, Kate, come. You must not look so sour. It is my fashion when I see a crab. Why, here’s no crab, and therefore look not sour. KATHERINE There is, there is. PETRUCCIO Then show it me. 230
