Exit Gloucester Enter King Lear, Edgar as a Bedlam beggar, and Lear’s Fool EDGAR Frateretto calls me, and tells me Nero is an angler in the lake of darkness. Pray, innocent, and beware the foul fiend. FOOL Prithee, nuncle, tell me whether a madman be a gentleman or a yeoman. LEAR A king, a king! FOOL No, he’s a yeoman that has a gentleman to his son; for he’s a mad yeoman that sees his son a gentleman before him.
