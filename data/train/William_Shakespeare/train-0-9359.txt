LEPIDUS You’ve strange serpents there? ANTONY Ay, Lepidus. LEPIDUS Your serpent of Egypt is bred now of your mud by the operation of your sun; so is your crocodile. ANTONY They are so. Sit, and some wine. A health to Lepidus! Antony, Pompey, and Lepidus sit LEPIDUS I am not so well as I should be, but I’ll ne’er out.
