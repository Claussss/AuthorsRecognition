The precedent whereof in Lucrece view,
Assailed by night with circumstances strong
Of present death, and shame that might ensue
By that her death, to do her husband wrong.
Such danger to resistance did belong That dying fear through all her body spread;
And who cannot abuse a body dead? By this, mild patience bid fair Lucrece speak
To the poor counterfeit of her complaining.
