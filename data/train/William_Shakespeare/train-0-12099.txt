JAILER’S DAUGHTER Yes, truly can I—I can sing ‘The Broom’
And ‘Bonny Robin’—are not you a tailor? JAILER’S BROTHER JAILER’S DAUGHTER Where’s my wedding gown? JAILER’s BROTHER I’ll bring it tomorrow. JAILER’S DAUGHTER Do, very rarely—I must be abroad else,
To call the maids and pay the minstrels,
For I must lose my maidenhead by cocklight,
‘Twill never thrive else. (Sings) ‘O fair, O sweet ...’
