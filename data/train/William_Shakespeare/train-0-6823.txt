SIR TOBY A contagious breath. SIR ANDREW Very sweet and contagious, i’faith. SIR TOBY To hear by the nose, it is dulcet in contagion. But shall we make the welkin dance indeed? Shall we rouse the night-owl in a catch that will draw three souls out of one weaver? Shall we do that? SIR ANDREW An you love me, let’s do’t. I am dog at a catch.
