SECOND REBEL The nobility think scorn to go in leather aprons. FIRST REBEL Nay more, the King’s Council are no good workmen. SECOND REBEL True; and yet it is said ‘Labour in thy vocation’; which is as much to say as ‘Let the magistrates be labouring men’; and therefore should we be magistrates. FIRST REBEL Thou hast hit it; for there’s no better sign of a brave mind than a hard hand.
