Enter Lodowick, with pen, ink and paper Hast thou pen, ink and paper ready, Lod’wick? LODOWICK Ready, my liege. KING EDWARD Then in the summer arbour sit by me;
Make it our council house or cabinet:
Since green our thoughts, green be the conventicle
Where we will ease us by disburd’ning them. They sit. Lodowick prepares to write
