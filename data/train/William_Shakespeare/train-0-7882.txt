ESCALUS How dost thou know that, constable? ELBOW Marry, sir, by my wife, who, if she had been a woman cardinally given, might have been accused in fornication, adultery, and all uncleanliness there. ESCALUS By the woman’s means? ELBOW Ay, sir, by Mistress Overdone’s means. But as she spit in his face, so she defied him.
