LUCIUS The strings, my lord, are false. He thinks he still is at his instrument.—
Lucius, awake! LUCIUS My lord. Didst thou dream, Lucius, that thou so cried’st out? My lord, I do not know that I did cry. Yes, that thou didst. Didst thou see anything? LUCIUS Nothing, my lord. Sleep again, Lucius.—Sirrah Claudio!
(To Varrus) Fellow,
Thou, awake!
