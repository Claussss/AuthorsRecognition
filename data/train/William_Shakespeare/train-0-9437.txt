Enter Thidias Caesar’s will? Hear it apart. None but friends; say boldly. So haply are they friends to Antony. He needs as many, sir, as Caesar has,
Or needs not us. If Caesar please, our master
Will leap to be his friend. For us, you know,
Whose he is, we are: and that is Caesar’s. So. (To Cleopatra) Thus, then, thou most renowned:
