And SO do I. Let’s briefly put on manly readiness,
And meet i’th’ hall together. Well contented. Exeunt all but Malcolm and Donalbain What will you do? Let’s not consort with them.
To show an unfelt sorrow is an office
Which the false man does easy. I’ll to England. To Ireland, I. Our separated fortune
Shall keep us both the safer. Where we are
There’s daggers in men’s smiles. The nea’er in blood,
The nearer bloody.
