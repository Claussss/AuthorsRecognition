Heavens bless my lord from fell Aufidius! He’ll beat Aufidius’ head below his knee
And tread upon his neck. Enter Valeria, with an usher and the Gentlewoman VALERIA My ladies both, good day to you. VOLUMNIA Sweet madam. VIRGILIA I am glad to see your ladyship. VALERIA How do you both? You are manifest housekeepers. What are you sewing here? A fine spot, in good faith. How does your little son?
