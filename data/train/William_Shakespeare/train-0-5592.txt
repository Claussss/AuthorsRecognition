My villainy? LEONATO Thine, Claudio, thine I say. You say not right, old man. LEONATO My lord, my lord, I’ll prove it on his body if he dare,
Despite his nice fence and his active practice,
His May of youth and bloom of lustihood. Away, I will not have to do with you. Canst thou so doff me? Thou hast killed my child.
If thou kill’st me, boy, thou shalt kill a man.
