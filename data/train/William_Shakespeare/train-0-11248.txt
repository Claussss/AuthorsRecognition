PISANIO Well, my good lord. CLOTEN Wilt thou serve me? For since patiently and constantly thou hast stuck to the bare fortune of that beggar Posthumus, thou canst not in the course of gratitude but be a diligent follower of mine. Wilt thou serve me? 121 PISANIO Sir, I will. CLOTEN Give me thy hand. Here’s my purse. Hast any of thy late master’s garments in thy possession?
