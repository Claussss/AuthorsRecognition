Enter a Servant How does the boy? He took good rest tonight. ’Tis hoped his sickness is discharged. LEONTES To see his nobleness! Conceiving the dishonour of his mother
He straight declined, drooped, took it deeply,
Fastened and fixed the shame on’t in himself;
Threw off his spirit, his appetite, his sleep,
And downright languished. Leave me solely. Go,
See how he fares.
