What miserable praise hast thou for her
That’s foul and foolish? There’s none so foul and foolish thereunto,
But does foul pranks which fair and wise ones do. DESDEMONA O heavy ignorance! Thou praisest the worst best. But what praise couldst thou bestow on a deserving woman indeed—one that, in the authority of her merit, did justly put on the vouch of very malice itself?
