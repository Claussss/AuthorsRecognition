SICINIUS Where is he, hear you? MENENIUS Nay, I hear nothing. His mother and his wife hear nothing from him. Enter three or four Citizens ALL THE CITIZENS (to the tribunes) The gods preserve you both. Good e’en, our neighbours. Good e‘en to you all, good e’en to you all. FIRST CITIZEN Ourselves, our wives and children, on our knees
Are bound to pray for you both.
