DOGBERRY Write down ‘Master Gentleman Conrad’.—Masters, do you serve God? CONRAD and BORACHIO Yea, sir, we hope. DOGBERRY Write down that they hope they serve God. And write ‘God’ first, for God defend but God should go before such villains. Masters, it is proved already that you are little better than false knaves, and it will go near to be thought so shortly. How answer you for yourselves?
