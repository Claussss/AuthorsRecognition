If so thou think’st, vex him with eager words. Clifford, ask mercy and obtain no grace. Clifford, repent in bootless penitence. Clifford, devise excuses for thy faults. While we devise fell tortures for thy faults. Thou didst love York, and I am son to York. Thou pitied’st Rutland—I will pity thee. Where’s Captain Margaret to fence you now?
