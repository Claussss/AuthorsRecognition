Enter two Murderers FIRST MURDERER Ho, who’s here? BRACKENBURY What wouldst thou, fellow? And how cam’st thou
hither? SECOND MURDERER I would speak with Clarence, and I came hither on my legs. BRACKENBURY What, so brief? FIRST MURDERER ‘Tis better, sir, than to be tedious. (To Second Murderer) Let him see our commission, and talk no more.
