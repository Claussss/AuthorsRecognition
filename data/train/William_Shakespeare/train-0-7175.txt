AGAMEMNON (to Nestor) What says Achilles? Would he aught with us? NESTOR (to Achilles) Would you, my lord, aught with the general? NESTOR (to Agamemnon) Nothing, my lord. The better. Exeunt Agamemnon and Nestor ACHILLES to Menelaus Good day, good day. MENELAUS How do you? How do you? ACHILLES (to Patroclus)
