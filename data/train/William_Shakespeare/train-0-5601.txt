CLAUDIO Well, I will meet you, so I may have good cheer. DON PEDRO What, a feast, a feast? CLAUDIO I’faith, I thank him, he hath bid me to a calf’s head and a capon, the which if I do not carve most curiously, say my knife’s naught. Shall I not find a woodcock too? 155 BENEDICK Sir, your wit ambles well, it goes easily.
