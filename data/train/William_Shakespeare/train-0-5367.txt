Take them away. Si fortuna me tormenta, spero me contenta. Exeunt all but Prince John and Lord Chief Justice PRINCE JOHN I like this fair proceeding of the King’s.
He hath intent his wonted followers
Shall all be very well provided for,
But all are banished till their conversations
Appear more wise and modest to the world.
