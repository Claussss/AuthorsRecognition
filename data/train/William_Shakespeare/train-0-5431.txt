LEONATO Cousin, you apprehend passing shrewdly. BEATRICE I have a good eye, uncle. I can see a church by daylight. LEONATO The revellers are entering, brother. Make good room. Enter Don Pedro, Claudio, Benedick, and Balthasar, all masked, Don John, and Borachio, with a drummer DON PEDRO (to Hero) Lady, will you walk a bout with your friend?
