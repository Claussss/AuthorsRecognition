GLOUCESTER Weapons? Arms? What’s the matter here? CORNWALL Keep peace, upon your lives. He dies that strikes again. What’s the matter? REGAN The messengers from our sister and the King. CORNWALL (to Kent and Oswald) What’s your difference? Speak. OSWALD I am scarce in breath, my lord. KENT No marvel, you have so bestirred your valour, you cowardly rascal. Nature disclaims in thee; a tailor made thee.
