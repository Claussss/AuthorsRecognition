MARIA That quaffing and drinking will undo you. I heard my lady talk of it yesterday, and of a foolish knight that you brought in one night here to be her wooer. SIR TOBY Who, Sir Andrew Aguecheek? MARIA Ay, he. SIR TOBY He’s as tall a man as any’s in Illyria. MARIA What’s that to th’ purpose? SIR TOBY Why, he has three thousand ducats a year.
