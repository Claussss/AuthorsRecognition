Exeunt King John and the Bastard at one door, Hubert and Arthur at another door 
3.3
Alarum; excursions; retreat. Enter King John, Queen Eleanor, Arthur Duke of Brittaine, the Bastard, Hubert, lords, with soldiers KING JOHN (to Queen Eleanor) So shall it be; your grace shall stay behind
So strongly guarded. (To Arthur) Cousin, look not sad;
Thy grandam loves thee, and thy uncle will
As dear be to thee as thy father was.
