PATROCLUS Why am I a fool? THERSITES Make that demand to the Creator. It suffices me thou art. Look you, who comes here? Enter Agamemnon, Ulysses, Nestor, Diomedes, Ajax, and Calchas ACHILLES Patroclus, I’ll speak with nobody.—Come in with me, Thersites. Exit THERSITES Here is such patchery, such juggling and such knavery. All the argument is a whore and a cuckold. A good quarrel to draw emulous factions and bleed to death upon. Now the dry serpigo on the subject, and war and lechery confound all. Exit
