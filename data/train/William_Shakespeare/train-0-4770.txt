JOHN OF LANCASTER This is the strangest tale that e’er I heard. PRINCE HARRY This is the strangest fellow, brother John.
(To Sir John) Come, bring your luggage nobly on your
back.
For my part, if a lie may do thee grace,
I’ll gild it with the happiest terms I have. A retreat is sounded The trumpet sounds retreat; the day is our.
Come, brother, let us to the highest of the field
To see what friends are living, who are dead.
