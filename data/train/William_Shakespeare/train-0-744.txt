KING HENRY (to Horner) Say, man, were these thy words? HORNER An’t shall please your majesty, I never said nor thought any such matter. God is my witness, I am falsely accused by the villain. PETER raising his hands Base dunghill villain and mechanical,
I’ll have thy head for this thy traitor’s speech !
(To King Henry) I do beseech your royal majesty,
Let him have all the rigour of the law.
