Enter abovethe Ghosts of Lord Rivers, Lord Gray, and Sir Thomas Vaughan GHOST OF RIVERS (to Richard) Let me sit heavy on thy soul tomorrow,
Rivers that died at Pomfret. Despair and die. GHOST OF GRAY (to Richard) Think upon Gray, and let thy soul despair. GHOST OF VAUGHAN (to Richard) Think upon Vaughan, and with guilty fear
Let fall thy pointless lance. Despair and die.
