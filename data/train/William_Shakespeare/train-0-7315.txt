They fight. Achilles is bested HECTOR Pause, if thou wilt. I do disdain thy courtesy, proud Trojan.
Be happy that my arms are out of use.
My rest and negligence befriends thee now;
But thou anon shalt here of me again.
Till when, go seek thy fortune. Exit HECTOR Fare thee well. I would have been much more a fresher man
Had I expected thee.
