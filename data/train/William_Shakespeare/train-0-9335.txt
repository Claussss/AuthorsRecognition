MESSENGER I have done my duty. CLEOPATRA Is he married? I cannot hate thee worser than I do
If thou again say ‘Yes’. He’s married, madam. The gods confound thee! Dost thou hold there still? Should I lie, madam? CLEOPATRA O, I would thou didst, So half my Egypt were submerged and made
A cistern for scaled snakes. Go, get thee hence.
Hadst thou Narcissus in thy face, to me
Thou wouldst appear most ugly. He is married?
