You, sir, what trade are you? COBBLER Truly, sir, in respect of a fine workman I am but, as you would say, a cobbler. MURELLUS But what trade art thou? Answer me directly. COBBLER A trade, sir, that I hope I may use with a safe conscience, which is indeed, sir, a mender of bad soles.
