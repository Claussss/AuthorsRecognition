THIRD PLEBEIAN Pluck down benches! Exeunt Plebeians with Caesar’s body Now let it work. Mischief, thou art afoot.
Take thou what course thou wilt.
Enter Octavius’Servant How now, fellow? Sir, Octavius is already come to Rome. ANTONY Where is he? He and Lepidus are at Caesar’s house. And thither will I straight to visit him.
He comes upon a wish. Fortune is merry,
And in this mood will give us anything.
