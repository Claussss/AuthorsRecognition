RICHARD GLOUCESTER Shall we hear from you, Catesby, ere we sleep? CATESBY You shall, my lord. RICHARD GLOUCESTER At Crosby House, there shall you find us both. Exit Catesby My lord, what shall we do if we perceive
Lord Hastings will not yield to our complots? RICHARD GLOUCESTER Chop off his head. Something we will determine.
And look when I am king, claim thou of me
The earldom of Hereford, and all the movables
Whereof the King my brother was possessed.
