BORACHIO (aside to Don John) And that is Claudio. I know him by his bearing. DON JOHN Are not you Signor Benedick? CLAUDIO You know me well. I am he. DON JOHN Signor, you are very near my brother in his love. He is enamoured on Hero. I pray you dissuade him from her. She is no equal for his birth. You may do the part of an honest man in it.
