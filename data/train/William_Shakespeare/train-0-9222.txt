Exeunt. Alarum 
5.10
Enter Macbeth Why should I play the Roman fool, and die
On mine own sword? Whiles I see lives, the gashes
Do better upon them. Enter Macduff Turn, hell-hound, turn. Of all men else I have avoided thee.
But get thee back. My soul is too much charged
With blood of thine already. I have no words;
