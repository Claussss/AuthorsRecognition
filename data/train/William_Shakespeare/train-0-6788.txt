SIR TOBY ’Tis a gentleman here. (He belches) A plague o’ these pickle herring! (To Feste) How now, sot? FESTE Good Sir Toby. OLIVIA Cousin, cousin, how have you come so early by this lethargy? SIR TOBY Lechery? I defy lechery. There’s one at the gate. OLIVIA Ay, marry, what is he? SIR TOBY Let him be the devil an he will, I care not. Give me faith, say I. Well, it’s all one.
