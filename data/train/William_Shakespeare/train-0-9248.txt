CHARMIAN Out, fool—I forgive thee for a witch. ALEXAS You think none but your sheets are privy to your wishes. CHARMIAN (to the Soothsayer) Nay, come, tell Iras hers. ALEXAS We’ll know all our fortunes. ENOBARBUS Mine, and most of our fortunes, tonight shall be drunk to bed. IRAS (showing her hand to the Soothsayer) There’s a palm presages chastity, if nothing else.
