KING CLAUDIUS O, ’tis too true. (Aside) How smart a lash that speech doth give my
conscience.
The harlot’s cheek, beautied with plast’ring art,
Is not more ugly to the thing that helps it
Than is my deed to my most painted word.
O heavy burden! I hear him coming. Let’s withdraw, my lord. Exeunt Claudius and Polonius
