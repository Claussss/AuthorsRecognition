HIPPOLYTA I am aweary of this moon. Would he would change. THESEUS It appears by his small light of discretion that he is in the wane; but yet in courtesy, in all reason, we must stay the time. LYSANDER Proceed, Moon. STARVELING All that I have to say is to tell you that the lantern is the moon, I the man i’th’ moon, this thorn bush my thorn bush, and this dog my dog.
