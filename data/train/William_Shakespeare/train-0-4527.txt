SIR JOHN Hear ye, Edward, if I tarry at home and go not, I’ll hang you for going. POINS You will, chops? SIR JOHN Hal, wilt thou make one? PRINCE HARRY Who, I rob? I a thief? Not I, by my faith. SIR JOHN There’s neither honesty, manhood, nor good fellowship in thee, nor thou earnest not of the blood royal, if thou darest not stand for ten shillings.
