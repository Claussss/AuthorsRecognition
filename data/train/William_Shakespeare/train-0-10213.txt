BRUTUS And topping all others in boasting. MENENIUS This is strange now. Do you two know how you are censured here in the city—I mean of us o’th’ right-hand file. Do you? SICINIUS and BRUTUS Why, how are we censured? MENENIUS Because—you talk of pride now—will you not be angry? SICINIUS and BRUTUS Well, well, sir, well?
