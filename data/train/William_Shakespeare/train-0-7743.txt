Farewell, my lord of Rochester. We’ll pray
or your release, and labour’t as we may. SHREWSBURY [to Rochester] Thereof assure yourself. So do we leave ye,
And to your happy private thoughts bequeath ye. Exeunt Lords Now, Master Lieutenant, on; i’ God’s name, go;
And with as glad a mind go I with you
As ever truant bade the school adieu.
