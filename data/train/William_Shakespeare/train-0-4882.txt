Enter Bardolph, and Master Ford disguised as Brooke FORD God bless you, sir. SIR JOHN And you, sir. Would you speak with me? FORD I make bold to press with so little preparation upon you. SIR JOHN You’re welcome. What’s your will? (To Bardolph) Give us leave, drawer. Exit Bardolph FORD Sir, I am a gentleman that have spent much. My name is Brooke.
