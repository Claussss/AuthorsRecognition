FABIAN (aside
to Sir Toby) He is as horribly conceited of him, and pants and looks pale as if a bear were at his heels. SIR TOBY (to Viola) There’s no remedy, sir, he will fight with you for’s oath’ sake. Marry, he hath better bethought him of his quarrel, and he finds that now scarce to be worth talking of. Therefore draw for the supportance of his vow, he protests he will not hurt you.
