What fashion, madam, shall I make your breeches? That fits as well as ‘Tell me, good my lord,
What compass will you wear your farthingale?’
Why, e’en what fashion thou best likes, Lucetta. You must needs have them with a codpiece, madam. Out, out, Lucetta. That will be ill-favoured. A round hose, madam, now’s not worth a pin
Unless you have a codpiece to stick pins on.
