Enter Edgar, armed, at the third sound, a trumpeter before him ALBANY (to the Herald) Ask him his purposes, why he appears
Upon this call o’th’ trumpet. HERALD (to Edgar) What are you? Your name and quality, and why you answer
This present summons? EDGAR O, know my name is lost, By treason’s tooth bare-gnawn and canker-bit.
Yet ere I move’t, where is the adversary
I come to cope withal?
