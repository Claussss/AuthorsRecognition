CORNWALL I’ll answer that. My sister may receive it much more worse
To have her gentlemen abused, assaulted,
For following her affairs. Put in his legs. They put Kent in the stocks Come, my good lord, away! Exeunt all but Gloucester and Kent I am sorry for thee, friend. ’Tis the Duke’s pleasure,
Whose disposition, all the world well knows,
Will not be rubbed nor stopped. I’ll entreat for thee.
