AGAMEMNON (to Hector) So now, fair prince of Troy, I bid good night.
Ajax commands the guard to tend on you. Thanks and good night to the Greeks’ general. Good night, my lord. HECTOR Good night, sweet Lord Menelaus. THERSITES (aside) Sweet draught! ‘Sweet’, quoth a? Sweet sink, sweet sewer. Good night and welcome both at once, to those
That go or tarry.
