SIR ANDREW Wherefore, sweetheart? What’s your metaphor? MARIA It’s dry, sir. SIR ANDREW Why, I think so. I am not such an ass but I can keep my hand dry. But what’s your jest? MARIA A dry jest, sir. SIR ANDREW Are you full of them? MARIA Ay, sir, I have them at my fingers’ ends. Marry, now I let go your hand I am barren. Exit
