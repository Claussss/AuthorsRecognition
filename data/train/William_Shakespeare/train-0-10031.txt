BAWD What else, man? The stuff we have, a strong wind will blow it to pieces, they are so pitifully sodden. PANDER Thou sayst true. They’re too unwholesome, o’ conscience. The poor Transylvanian is dead that lay with the little baggage. BOULT Ay, she quickly pooped him, she made him roast meat for worms. But I’ll go search the market. Exit
