THIRD SERVINGMAN Why, here’s he that was wont to thwack our general, Caius Martius. FIRST SERVINGMAN Why do you say ‘thwack our general’? THIRD SERVINGMAN I do not say ‘thwack our general’; but he was always good enough for him. SECOND SERVINGMAN Come, we are fellows and friends. He was ever too hard for him. I have heard him say so himself.
