5.1
Enter Achilles and Patroclus I’ll heat his blood with Greekish wine tonight,
Which with my scimitar I’ll cool tomorrow.
Patroclus, let us feast him to the height. Here comes Thersites. Enter Thersites ACHILLES How now, thou core of envy, Thou crusty botch of nature, what’s the news? THERSITES Why, thou picture of what thou seemest, and idol of idiot-worshippers, here’s a letter for thee.
