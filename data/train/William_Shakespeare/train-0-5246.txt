BARDOLPH taking the money Bullcalf stands aside MOULDY coming forward BARDOLPH Go to; stand aside. Mouldy stands aside FEEBLE By my troth, I care not. A man can die but once. We owe God a death. I’ll ne’er bear a base mind. An’t be my destiny, so; an’t be not, so. No man’s too good to serve’s prince. And let it go which way it will, he that dies this year is quit for the next.
