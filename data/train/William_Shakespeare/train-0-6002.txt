Liberty! Freedom! Tyranny is dead!
Run hence, proclaim, cry it about the streets. Some to the common pulpits, and cry out
‘Liberty, freedom, and enfranchisement!’ People and senators, be not affrighted. Exeunt in a tumult Lepidus, Popillius, other senators, Artemidorus, Soothsayer, and citizen Fly not! Stand still! Ambition’s debt is paid.
