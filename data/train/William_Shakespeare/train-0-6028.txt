Stay, ho, and let us hear Mark Antony. THIRD PLEBEIAN Let him go up into the public chair.
We’ll hear him. Noble Antony, go up. For Brutus’ sake I am beholden to you.
Antony ascends to the pulpit What does he say of Brutus? THIRD PLEBEIAN He says, for Brutus’ sake He finds himself beholden to us all.
