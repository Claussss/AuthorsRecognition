Enter Gadshill GADSHILL Good morrow, carriers. What’s o’clock? FIRST CARRIER I think it be two o’clock. GADSHILL I prithee lend me thy lantern to see my gelding in the stable. FIRST CARRIER Nay, by God, soft. I know a trick worth two of that, i’faith. GADSHILL (to Second Carrier) I pray thee, lend me thine.
