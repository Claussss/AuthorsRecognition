FESTE I will help you to’t. But tell me true, are you not mad indeed, or do you but counterfeit? MALVOLIO Believe me, I am not, I tell thee true. FESTE Nay, I’ll ne’er believe a madman till I see his brains. I will fetch you light, and paper, and ink. MALVOLIO Fool, I’ll requite it in the highest degree. I prithee, be gone.
