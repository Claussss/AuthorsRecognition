OPHELIA (sings) They bore him barefaced on the bier,
Hey non nony, nony, hey nony,
And on his grave rained many a tear— Fare you well, my dove. Hadst thou thy wits and didst persuade revenge,
It could not move thus. OPHELIA You must sing ‘Down, a-down’, and you, ‘Call him a-down-a’. O, how the wheel becomes it! It is the false steward that stole his master’s daughter. LAERTES This nothing’s more than matter.
