Enter Bassanio with Leonardo and followers  O rare fortune! Here comes the man. To him, father, for I am a Jew if I serve the Jew any longer. BASSANIO (toone
of his men) You may do so, but let it be so hasted that supper be ready at the farthest by five of the clock. See these letters delivered, put the liveries to making, and desire Graziano to come anon to my lodging. Exit one
