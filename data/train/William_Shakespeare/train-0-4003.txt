FLUTE (as Thisbe) Tide life, tide death, I come without delay. Exeunt Bottom and Flute severally SNOUT (as Wall) Thus have I, Wall, my part discharged so;
And being done, thus Wall away doth go. Exit THESEUS Now is the wall down between the two neighbours. DEMETRIUS No remedy, my lord, when walls are so wilful to hear without warning.
