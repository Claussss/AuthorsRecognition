VARRUS My lord. CLAUDIO My lord. Why did you so cry out, sirs, in your sleep? Did we, my lord? BRUTUS Ay. Saw you anything? No, my lord, I saw nothing. CLAUDIO Nor I, my lord. Go and commend me to my brother Cassius.
Bid him set on his powers betimes before,
And we will follow. BOTH It shall be done, my lord.
