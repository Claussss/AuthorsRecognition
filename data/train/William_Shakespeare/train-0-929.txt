O gross and miserable ignorance ! CADE Nay, answer if you can: the Frenchmen are our enemies; go to, then, I ask but this—can he that speaks with the tongue of an enemy be a good counsellor or no? ALL CADE’S FOLLOWERS No, no—and therefore we’ll have his head! STAFFORD’S BROTHER (to Stafford) Well, seeing gentle words will not prevail,
Assail them with the army of the King.
