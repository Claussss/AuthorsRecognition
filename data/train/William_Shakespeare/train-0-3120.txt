PRINCESS (to Costard) Thou, fellow, a word. Who gave thee this letter? COSTARD I told you—my lord. To whom shouldst thou give it? COSTARD From my lord to my lady. From which lord to which lady? From my lord Biron, a good master of mine,
To a lady of France that he called Rosaline. Thou hast mistaken his letter. Come, lords, away.
(To Rosaline, giving her the letter)
Here, sweet, put up this, ‘twill be thine another day.
