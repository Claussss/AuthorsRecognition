BOTH SENATORS ’Tis most nobly spoken. ALCIBIADES Descend, and keep your words. Trumpets sound. Exeunt Senators from the walls. Enter Soldier, with a tablet of wax My noble general, Timon is dead,
Entombed upon the very hem o’th’ sea;
And on his gravestone this insculpture, which
With wax I brought away, whose soft impression
Interprets for my poor ignorance.
