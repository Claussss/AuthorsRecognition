DROMIO OF SYRACUSE (within the Phoenix) Break any breaking here, and I’ll break your knave’s pate. DROMIO OF EPHESUS A man may break a word with you, sir, and words are but wind;
Ay, and break it in your face, so he break it not behind. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE (within the Phoenix) It seems thou want’st breaking. Out upon thee, hind!
