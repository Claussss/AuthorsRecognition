GREMIO They’re busy within. You were best knock louder. Vincentio knocks again. The Pedant looks out of the window PEDANT What’s he that knocks as he would beat down the gate? VINCENTIO Is Signor Lucentio within, sir? PEDANT He’s within, sir, but not to be spoken withal. VINCENTIO What if a man bring him a hundred pound or two to make merry withal?
