5.4
Enter Menenius and Sicinius 
MENENIUS See you yon coign o’th’ Capitol, yon corner-stone? SICINIUS Why, what of that? MENENIUS If it be possible for you to displace it with your little finger, there is some hope the ladies of Rome, especially his mother, may prevail with him. But I say there is no hope in’t, our throats are sentenced and stay upon execution.
