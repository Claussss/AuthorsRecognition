SIR JOHN I will answer it straight: I have done all this. That is now answered. SHALLOW The Council shall know this. SIR JOHN ’Twere better for you if it were known in counsel. You’ll be laughed at. EVANS Pauca verba, Sir John, good worts. SIR JOHN Good worts? Good cabbage!—Slender, I broke your head. What matter have you against me?
