Scorning advice, read the conclusion then, He angrily throws down the riddle Which read and not expounded, ’tis decreed,
As these before thee, thou thyself shalt bleed. DAUGHTER (to Pericles) Of all ‘sayed yet, mayst thou prove prosperous;
Of all ’sayed yet, I wish thee happiness. Like a bold champion I assume the lists,
Nor ask advice of any other thought
But faithfulness and courage.
