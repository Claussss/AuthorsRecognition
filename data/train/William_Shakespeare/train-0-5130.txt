FANG Sirrah!—Where’s Snare? MISTRESS QUICKLY O Lord, ay, good Master Snare. SNARE coming forward FANG Snare, we must arrest Sir John Falstaff. MISTRESS QUICKLY Yea, good Master Snare, I have entered him and all. SNARE It may chance cost some of us our lives, for he will stab. MISTRESS QUICKLY Alas the day, take heed of him; he stabbed me in mine own house, most beastly, in good faith. A cares not what mischief he does; if his weapon be out, he will foin like any devil, he will spare neither man, woman, nor child.
