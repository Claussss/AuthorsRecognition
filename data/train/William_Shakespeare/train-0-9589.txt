Enter a Page Monsieur Paroles, my lord calls for you. PAROLES Little Helen, farewell. If I can remember thee I will think of thee at court. HELEN Monsieur Paroles, you were born under a charitable star. PAROLES Under Mars, I. HELEN I especially think under Mars. PAROLES Why ‘under Mars’? HELEN The wars hath so kept you under that you must needs be born under Mars.
