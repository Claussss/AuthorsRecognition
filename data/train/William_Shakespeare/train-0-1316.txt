KING EDWARD Away with her—go, bear her hence perforce. QUEEN MARGARET Nay, never bear me hence—dispatch me here.
Here sheathe thy sword—I’ll pardon thee my death.
What? Wilt thou not? Then, Clarence, do it thou. GEORGE OF CLARENCE By heaven, I will not do thee so much ease. QUEEN MARGARET Good Clarence, do; sweet Clarence, do thou do it.
