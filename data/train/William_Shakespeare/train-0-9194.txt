DOCTOR Do you mark that? LADY MACBETH The Thane of Fife had a wife. Where is she now? What, will these hands ne’er be clean? No more o’ that, my lord, no more o’ that. You mar all with this starting. DOCTOR Go to, go to. You have known what you should not. GENTLEWOMAN She has spoke what she should not, I am sure of that. Heaven knows what she has known.
