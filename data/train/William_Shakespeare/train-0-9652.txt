PAROLES Why, there ’tis, so say I too. LAFEU Not to be helped. PAROLES Right, as ’twere a man assured of a— LAFEU Uncertain life and sure death. PAROLES Just, you say well, so would I have said. LAFEU I may truly say it is a novelty to the world. PAROLES It is indeed. If you will have it in showing, you shall read it in [pointing to the ballad] what-do-ye-call there.
