I fear no uncles dead. RICHARD GLOUCESTER Nor none that live, I hope. PRINCE EDWARD An if they live, I hope I need not fear.
(To York) But come, my lord, and with a heavy heart,
Thinking on them, go we unto the Tower. A Sennet. Exeunt all but Richard, Buckingham, and Catesby BUCKINGHAM (to Richard) Think you, my lord, this little prating York
Was not incensed by his subtle mother
To taunt and scorn you thus opprobriously?
