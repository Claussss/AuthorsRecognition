ORLANDO With no less religion than if thou wert indeed my Rosalind. So, adieu. ROSALIND Well, Time is the old justice that examines all such offenders; and let Time try. Adieu. Exit Orlando CELIA You have simply misused our sex in your love-prate. We must have your doublet and hose plucked over your head, and show the world what the bird hath done to her own nest.
