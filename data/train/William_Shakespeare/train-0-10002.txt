CERIMON What The sea tossed up upon our shore this chest. ’Tis off some wreck. CERIMON Set’t down. Let’s look upon’t. SECOND GENTLEMAN ’Tis like a coffin, sir. CERIMON Whate’er it be, ’Tis wondrous heavy.—Did the sea cast it up? I never saw so huge a billow, sir,
Or a more eager. CERIMON Wrench it open straight.
