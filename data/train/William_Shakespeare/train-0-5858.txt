PISTOL By this leek, I will most horribly revenge— Fluellen threatens him FLUELLEN Eat, I pray you. Will you have some more sauce to your leek? There is not enough leek to swear by. Quiet thy cudgel, thou dost see I eat. FLUELLEN Much good do you, scald knave, heartily. Nay, pray you throw none away. The skin is good for your broken coxcomb. When you take occasions to see leeks hereafter, I pray you mock at ’em, that is all.
