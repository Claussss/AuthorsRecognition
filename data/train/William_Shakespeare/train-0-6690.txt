Peace, who comes here? Enter young Osric, a courtier, taking off his hat Your lordship is right welcome back to Denmark. HAMLET I humbly thank you, sir. (To Horatio) Dost know this water-fly? HORATIO No, my good lord. HAMLET Thy state is the more gracious, for ‘tis a vice to know him. He hath much land, and fertile. Let a beast be lord of beasts, and his crib shall stand at the king’s mess. ’Tis a chuff, but, as I say, spacious in the possession of dirt.
