A MURDERER Where is your husband? LADY MACDUFF I hope in no place so unsanctified
Where such as thou mayst find him. He’s a traitor. MACDUFF’S SON Thou liest, thou shag-haired villain. A MURDERER (stabbing him) What, you egg! Young fry of treachery! MACDUFF’S SON He has killed me, mother. Run away, I pray you.
