MARGARET O illegitimate construction! I scorn that with my heels. BEATRICE (to Hero) ‘Tis almost five o’clock, cousin. ’Tis time you were ready. By my troth, I am exceeding ill. Heigh-ho! MARGARET For a hawk, a horse, or a husband? BEATRICE For the letter that begins them all—h. MARGARET Well, an you be not turned Turk, there’s no more sailing by the star.
