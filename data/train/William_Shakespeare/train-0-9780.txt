INTERPRETER Shall I set down your answer so? 139 PAROLES Do. I’ll take the sacrament on’t, how and which way you will.  (aside) All’s one to him. BERTRAM (aside) What a past-saving slave is this! FIRST LORD DUMAINE (aside) You’re deceived, my lord. This is Monsieur Paroles, the ‘gallant militarist’—that was his own phrase—that had the whole theoric of war in the knot of his scarf, and the practice in the chape of his dagger.
