Enter Theseus, Hippolyta, Pirithous, and attendants Bring ’em in
 Quickly, by any means, I long to see ’em. Exit one or more (To Emilia) Your two contending lovers are returned,
And with them their fair knights. Now, my fair sister,
You must love one of them. I had rather both,
 So neither for my sake should fall untimely.
