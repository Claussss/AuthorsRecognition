VERGES Well, give them their charge, neighbour Dogberry. DOGBERRY First, who think you the most desertless man to be constable? SECOND WATCHMAN Hugh Oatcake, sir, or George Seacoal, for they can write and read. DOGBERRY Come hither, neighbour Seacoal, God hath blest you with a good name. To be a well-favoured man is the gift of fortune, but to write and read comes by nature.
