PRINCESS What’s your will, sir? What’s your will? I have a letter from Monsieur Biron to one Lady Rosaline. O, thy letter, thy letter! (She takes it) He’s a good friend of mine.
(To Costard) Stand aside, good bearer. Boyet, you can carve.
Break up this capon. She gives the letter to Boyet BOYET I am bound to serve.
