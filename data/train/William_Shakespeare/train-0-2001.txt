QUEEN ELIZABETH (to York) Come, come, my boy, we will to sanctuary.—
Madam, farewell. DUCHESS OF YORK Stay, I will go with you. QUEEN ELIZABETH You have no cause. to Elizabeth) My gracious lady, go, And thither bear your treasure and your goods.
For my part, I’ll resign unto your grace
The seal I keep, and so betide to me
As well I tender you and all of yours.
Go, I’ll conduct you to the sanctuary. Exeunt
