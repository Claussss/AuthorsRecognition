She turned to folly, and she was a whore. Thou dost belie her, and thou art a devil. She was false as water. EMILIA Thou art rash as fire To say that she was false. O, she was heavenly true! Cassio did top her. Ask thy husband else.
O, I were damned beneath all depth in hell
But that I did proceed upon just grounds
To this extremity! Thy husband knew it all.
