SHALLOW Do you like him, Sir John? SIR JOHN Shadow will serve for summer. Prick him, for we have a number of shadows fill up the muster book. Shadow stands aside SHALLOW (calls) ‘Thomas Wart.’ SIR JOHN Where’s he? WART Here, sir. SIR JOHN Is thy name Wart? WART Yea, sir. SIR JOHN Thou art a very ragged wart.
