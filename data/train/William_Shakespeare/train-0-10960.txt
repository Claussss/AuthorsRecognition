Where is thy lustre now? All dark and comfortless. Where’s my son Edmond?
Edmond, enkindle all the sparks of nature
To quite this horrid act. Out, treacherous villain! Thou call’st on him that hates thee. It was he
That made the overture of thy treasons to us,
Who is too good to pity thee. O, my follies! Then Edgar was abused.
Kind gods, forgive me that, and prosper him!
