POMPEY Sir, she was respected with him before he married with her. ESCALUS Which is the wiser here, justice or iniquity? (To Elbow) Is this true? ELBOW (to Pompey) O thou caitiff, O thou varlet, O thou wicked Hanniball I respected with her before I was married to her? (To Escalus) If ever I was respected with her, or she with me, let not your worship think me the poor Duke’s officer. (To Pompey) Prove this, thou wicked Hannibal, or I’ll have mine action of battery on thee.
