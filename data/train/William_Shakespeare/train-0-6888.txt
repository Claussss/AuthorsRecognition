Exeunt
severally  3.2
Enter Sir Toby, Sir Andrew, and Fabian 
SIR ANDREW No, faith, I’ll not stay a jot longer. SIR TOBY Thy reason, dear venom, give thy reason. FABIAN You must needs yield your reason, Sir Andrew. SIR ANDREW Marry, I saw your niece do more favours to the Count’s servingman than ever she bestowed upon me. I saw’t i’th’ orchard.
