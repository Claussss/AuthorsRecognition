Enter at another doorSir Thomas Lovell, meeting them Good hour of night, Sir Thomasl Whither so late? Came you from the King, my lord? I did, Sir Thomas, and left him at primero
With the Duke of Suffolk. I must to him too, Before he go to bed. I’ll take my leave. Not yet, Sir Thomas Lovell—what’s the matter?
It seems you are in haste. An if there be
No great offence belongs to’t, give your friend
Some touch of your late business. Affairs that walk,
As they say spirits do, at midnight, have
In them a wilder nature than the business
That seeks dispatch by day.
