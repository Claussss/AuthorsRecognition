What, my lord? OTHELLO That he hath used thee. DESDEMONA How, unlawfully? OTHELLO Ay. DESDEMONA He will not say so. OTHELLONo, his mouth is stopped. Honest Iago hath ta’en order for’t. O, my fear interprets! What, is he dead? Had all his hairs been lives, my great revenge
Had stomach for em all. DESDEMONA (weeping)
