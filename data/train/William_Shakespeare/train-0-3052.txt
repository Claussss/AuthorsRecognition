KING Well, it was proclaimed ‘damsel’. COSTARD This was no damsel, neither, sir. She was a virgin. COSTARD If it were, I deny her virginity. I was taken with a maid. KING This ‘maid’ will not serve your turn, sir. COSTARD This maid will serve my turn, sir. KING Sir, I will pronounce your sentence. You shall fast a week with bran and water.
