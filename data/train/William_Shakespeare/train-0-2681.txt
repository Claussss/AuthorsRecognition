KING EDWARD Name them, fair Countess, and by heaven I will. COUNTESS OF SALISBURY It is their lives that stand between our love
That I would have choked up, my sovereign. KING EDWARD Whose lives, my lady? COUNTESS OF SALISBURY My thrice-loving liege, Your Queen and Salisbury, my wedded husband,
Who, living, have that title in our love
That we cannot bestow but by their death.
