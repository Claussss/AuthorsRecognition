PANDARUS No, nor Hector is not Troilus, in some degrees. ’Tis just to each of them: he is himself. PANDARUS Himself? Alas, poor Troilus, I would he were. CRESSIDA So he is. PANDARUS Condition I had gone barefoot to India. CRESSIDA He is not Hector. PANDARUS Himself ? No, he’s not himself. Would a were himself! Well, the gods are above, time must friend or end. Well, Troilus, well, I would my heart were in her body. No, Hector is not a better man than Troilus.
