ALL Speak, speak. FIRST CITIZEN You are all resolved rather to die than to famish? ALL Resolved, resolved. FIRST CITIZEN First, you know Caius Martius is chief enemy to the people. ALL We know’t, we know’t. FIRST CITIZEN Let us kill him, and we’ll have corn at our own price. Is’t a verdict? ALL No more talking on’t, let it be done. Away, away. SECOND CITIZEN One word, good citizens.
