OPHELIA I shall obey, my lord.  1.4
Enter Prince Hamlet, Horatio, and Marcellus The air bites shrewdly, it is very cold. It is a nipping and an eager air. HAMLET What hour now? HORATIO I think it lacks of twelve. MARCELLUS No, it is struck. Indeed? I heard it not. Then it draws near the season
Wherein the spirit held his wont to walk.
