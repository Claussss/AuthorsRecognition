But who comes here? The safer sense will ne’er accommodate
His master thus. LEAR No, they cannot touch me for crying. I am the King himself. EDGAR O thou side-piercing sight! LEAR Nature’s above art in that respect. There’s your press-money. That fellow handles his bow like a crow-keeper. Draw me a clothier’s yard. Look, look, a mouse! Peace, peace, this piece of toasted cheese will do’t. There’s my gauntlet. I’ll prove it on a giant. Bring up the brown bills. O, well flown, bird, i‘th’ clout, i’th’ clout! Whew! Give the word.
