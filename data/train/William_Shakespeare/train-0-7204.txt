CRESSIDA (to Troilus) Did not I tell you? Would he were knocked i’th’ head.
One knocks within
Who’s that at door?—Good uncle, go and see.—
My lord, come you again into my chamber.
You smile and mock me, as if I meant naughtily. TROILUS Ha ha! Come, you are deceived, I think of no such thing.
One knocks within
How earnestly they knock! Pray you come in.
I would not for half Troy have you seen here.
