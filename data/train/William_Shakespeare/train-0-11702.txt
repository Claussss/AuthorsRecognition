SECOND GENTLEMAN That trick of state Was a deep envious one. FIRST GENTLEMAN At his return No doubt he will requite it. This is noted,
And generally: whoever the King favours,
The Card’nal instantly will find employment—
And far enough from court, too. SECOND GENTLEMAN All the commons Hate him perniciously and, o’ my conscience,
Wish him ten fathom deep. This Duke as much
They love and dote on, call him ‘bounteous
Buckingham,
The mirror of all courtesy’—
