LUCIO O, thou damnable fellow! Did not I pluck thee by the nose for thy speeches? DUKE I protest I love the Duke as I love myself. ANGELO Hark how the villain would close now, after his treasonable abuses. ESCALUS Such a fellow is not to be talked withal. Away with him to prison. Where is the Provost? Away with him to prison. Lay bolts enough upon him. Let him speak no more. Away with those giglets too, and with the other confederate companion.
