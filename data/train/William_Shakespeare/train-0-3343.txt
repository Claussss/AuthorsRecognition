JOHN OF GAUNT Come, come, my son, I’ll bring thee on thy way.
Had I thy youth and cause, I would not stay. BOLINGBROKE Then England’s ground, farewell. Sweet soil, adieu,
My mother and my nurse that bears me yet!
Where’er I wander, boast of this I can:
Though banished, yet a trueborn Englishman.
