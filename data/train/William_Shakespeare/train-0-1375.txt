Enter, to the Lord Protector at the Tower gates, the Bishop of Winchester and his men in tawny coats How now, ambitious vizier! What means this? Peeled priest, dost thou command me to be shut out? I do, thou most usurping proditor,
And not ‘Protector’, of the King or realm. Stand back, thou manifest conspirator.
Thou that contrived’st to murder our dead lord,
Thou that giv’st whores indulgences to sin,
If thou proceed in this thy insolence—
