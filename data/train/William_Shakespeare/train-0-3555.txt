ABRAHAM Quarrel, sir? No, sir. SAMSON But if you do, sir, I am for you. I serve as good a man as you. ABRAHAM No better. SAMSON Well, sir. Enter Benvolio GREGORY Say ‘better’. Here comes one of my master’s kinsmen. SAMSON (to Abraham) Yes, better, sir. ABRAHAM You lie. SAMSON Draw, if you be men. Gregory, remember thy washing blow.
