PETRUCCIO (to the Tailor) Go, take it up unto thy master’s use. GRUMIO (to the Tailor) Villain, not for thy life. Take up my mistress’ gown for thy master’s use! PETRUCCIO Why, sir, what’s your conceit in that? GRUMIO O, sir, the conceit is deeper than you think for. ‘Take up my mistress’ gown to his master’s use’—O fie, fie, fie!
