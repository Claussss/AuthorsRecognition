HORATIO Half a share. HAMLET A whole one, I. For thou dost know, O Damon dear,
This realm dismantled was
Of Jove himself, and now reigns here
A very, very—pajock. HORATIO You might have rhymed. HAMLET O good Horatio, I’ll take the Ghost’s word for a thousand pound. Didst perceive? HORATIO Very well, my lord.
