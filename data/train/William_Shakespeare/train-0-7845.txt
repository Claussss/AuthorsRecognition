FIRST GENTLEMAN Well, there went but a pair of shears between us. LUCIO I grant—as there may between the lists and the velvet. Thou art the list. FIRST GENTLEMAN And thou the velvet. Thou art good velvet, thou’rt a three-piled piece, I warrant thee. I had as lief be a list of an English kersey as be piled as thou art pilled, for a French velvet. Do I speak feelingly now?
