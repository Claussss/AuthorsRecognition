SICINIUS O blessed heavens! More noble blows than ever thou wise words,
And for Rome’s good. I’ll tell thee what—yet go.
Nay, but thou shalt stay too. I would my son
Were in Arabia, and thy tribe before him,
His good sword in his hand. He’d make an end of thy posterity. VOLUMNIA Bastards and all. Good man, the wounds that he does bear for Rome
