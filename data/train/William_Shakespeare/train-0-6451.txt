MARCELLUS How is’t, my noble lord? HORATIO (to Hamlet) What news, my lord? HAMLET O wonderful! Good my lord, tell it. No, you’ll reveal it. Not I, my lord, by heaven. Nor I, my lord. How say you then, would heart of man once think it?
But you’ll be secret? HORATIO and MARCELLUS Ay, by heav’n, my lord.
