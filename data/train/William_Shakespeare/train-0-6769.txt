SIR ANDREW Ay, ’tis strong, and it does indifferent well in a divers-coloured stock. Shall we set about some revels? SIR TOBY What shall we do else—were we not born under Taurus? SIR ANDREW Taurus? That’s sides and heart. SIR TOBY No, sir, it is legs and thighs: let me see thee caper. Sir Andrew capers
