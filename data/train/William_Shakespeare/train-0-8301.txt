DESDEMONA I’faith, is’t true? Most veritable. Therefore look to’t well. Then would to God that I had never seen it! OTHELLO Ha, wherefore? Why do you speak so startingly and rash? Is’t lost? Is’t gone? Speak, is’t out o’th’ way? DESDEMONA Heaven bless us! OTHELLO Say you? It is not lost, but what an if it were?
