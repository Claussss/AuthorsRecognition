TRINCULO ‘Lord’ quoth he? That a monster should be such a natural! CALIBAN (to Stefano) Lo, lo, again! Bite him to death, I prithee. STEFANO Trinculo, keep a good tongue in your head. If you prove a mutineer, the next tree. The poor monster’s my subject, and he shall not suffer indignity. I thank my noble lord. Wilt thou be pleased
To hearken once again to the suit I made to thee?
