CORIOLANUS Away! SECOND SERVINGMAN Away? Get you away. CORIOLANUS Now thou’rt troublesome. SECOND SERVINGMAN Are you so brave? I’ll have you talked with anon. Enter Third Servingman. The First meets him THIRD SERVINGMAN What fellow’s this? FIRST SERVINGMAN A strange one as ever I looked on. I cannot get him out o’th’ house. Prithee, call my master to him.
