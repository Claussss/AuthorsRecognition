Doth he so seek his life? Has censured him already, And, as I hear, the Provost hath a warrant
For’s execution. Alas, what poor Ability’s in me to do him good? LUCIO Assay the power you have. ISABELLA My power? Alas, I doubt. LUCIO Our doubts are traitors, And makes us lose the good we oft might win,
By fearing to attempt. Go to Lord Angelo;
And let him learn to know, when maidens sue,
Men give like gods, but when they weep and kneel,
All their petitions are as freely theirs
As they themselves would owe them.
