LANCE Ask my dog. If he say ‘Ay’, it will. If he say ‘No’, it will. If he shake his tail and say nothing, it will. SPEED The conclusion is, then, that it will. LANCE Thou shalt never get such a secret from me but by a parable. SPEED ‘Tis well that I get it so. But Lance, how sayst thou that my master is become a notable lover?
