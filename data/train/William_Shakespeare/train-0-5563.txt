BENEDICK Sir, sir, be patient. For my part, I am so attired in wonder
I know not what to say. O, on my soul, my cousin is belied. Lady, were you her bedfellow last night? No, truly not, although until last night
I have this twelvemonth been her bedfellow. Confirmed, confirmed. O, that is stronger made
Which was before barred up with ribs of iron.
Would the two princes lie? And Claudio lie,
Who loved her so that, speaking of her foulness,
Washed it with tears? Hence from her, let her die.
