Enter Ratcliffe RATCLIFFE My lord? KING RICHARD ‘Swounds, who is there? My lord, ’tis I. The early village cock
Hath twice done salutation to the morn.
Your friends are up, and buckle on their armour. KING RICHARD O Ratcliffe, I have dreamed a fearful dream.
What thinkest thou, will all our friends prove true?
