Enter Mistress Ford MISTRESS FORD Mistress Page! By my faith, I was going to your house. MISTRESS PAGE And by my faith, I was coming to you. You look very ill. MISTRESS FORD Nay, I’ll ne’er believe that: I have to show to the contrary. MISTRESS PAGE Faith, but you do, in my mind. MISTRESS FORD Well, I do, then. Yet I say I could show you to the contrary. O Mistress Page, give me some counsel.
