KING HENRY to York Think’st thou that I will leave my kingly throne, B. The 1595 text abridges 5.4.82.1-5.5.17, and may reflect authorial revision. An edited text of the abridged passage follows: ALL THE LANCASTER PARTY Saint George for Lancaster! Alarums to the battle. The house ofYork flies, then the chambers are discharged. Then enter King Edward, George of Clarence, and Richard of Gloucester, and their followers: they make a great shout, and cry ‘For York! For York!’ Then Queen Margaret, Prince Edward, Oxford and Somerset are all taken prisoner. Flourish, and enter all again
