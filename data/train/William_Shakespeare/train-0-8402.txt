OTHELLO Peace, and be still. I will so. What’s the matter? OTHELLO That handkerchief Which I so loved and gave thee, thou gav’st to Cassio. No, by my life and soul. Send for the man
And ask him. Sweet soul, take heed, take heed of perjury.
Thou art on thy deathbed. DESDEMONA Ay, but not yet to die. OTHELLO Presently.
