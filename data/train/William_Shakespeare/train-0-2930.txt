ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Why, give it to my wife, and fetch your money. Come, come, you know I gave it you even now.
Either send the chain, or send me by some token. ANTIPHOLUS OF EPHESUS Fie, now you run this humour out of breath.
Come, where’s the chain? I pray you let me see it. SECOND MERCHANT My business cannot brook this dalliance.
