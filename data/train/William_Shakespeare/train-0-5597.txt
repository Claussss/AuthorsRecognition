CLAUDIO We had liked to have had our two noses snapped off with two old men without teeth. DON PEDRO Leonato and his brother. What thinkest thou? Had we fought, I doubt we should have been too young for them. BENEDICK In a false quarrel there is no true valour. I came to seek you both. CLAUDIO We have been up and down to seek thee, for we are high-proof melancholy and would fain have it beaten away. Wilt thou use thy wit?
