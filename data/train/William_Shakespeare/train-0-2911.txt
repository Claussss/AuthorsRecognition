ANTIPHOLUS or SYRACUSE What complexion is she of? DROMIO OF SYRACUSE Swart like my shoe, but her face nothing like so clean kept. For why?—She sweats a man may go overshoes in the grime of it. ANTIPHOLUS OF SYRACUSE That’s a fault that water will mend. DROMIO OF SYRACUSE No, sir, ’tis in grain. Noah’s flood could not do it.
