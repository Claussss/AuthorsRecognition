ORLANDO For these two hours, Rosalind, I will leave thee. ROSALIND Alas, dear love, I cannot lack thee two hours. ORLANDO I must attend the Duke at dinner. By two o’clock I will be with thee again. ROSALIND Ay, go your ways, go your ways. I knew what you would prove; my friends told me as much, and I thought no less. That flattering tongue of yours won me. ‘Tis but one cast away, and so, come, death! Two o’clock is your hour?
