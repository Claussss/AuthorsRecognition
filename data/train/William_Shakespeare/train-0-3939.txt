DEMETRIUS (to Helena) I say I love thee more than he can do. If thou say so, withdraw, and prove it too. Quick, come. HERMIA Lysander, whereto tends all this? She takes him by the arm Away, you Ethiope. DEMETRIUS No, no, sir, yield. Seem to break loose, take on as you would follow,
But yet come not. You are a tame man; go.
