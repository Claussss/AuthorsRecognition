MOPSA Is it true, think you? AUTOLYCUS Very true, and but a month old. DORCAS Bless me from marrying a usurer! AUTOLYCUS Here’s the midwife’s name to’t, one Mistress Tail-Porter, and five or six honest wives’ that were present. Why should I carry lies abroad? MOPSA (to Clown) Pray you now, buy it. CLOWN Come on, lay it by, and let’s first see more ballads. We’ll buy the other things anon.
