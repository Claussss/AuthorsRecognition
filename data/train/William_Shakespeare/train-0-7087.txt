THERSITES Nay, look upon him. ACHILLES So I do. What’s the matter? THERSITES Nay, but regard him well. ACHILLES ‘Well’? Why, I do so. THERSITES But yet you look not well upon him. For whosomever you take him to be, he is Ajax. ACHILLES I know that, fool. THERSITES Ay, but ‘that fool’ knows not himself.
