Exeunt severally 
3.1
Enter at one doorArtemidorus, the Soothsayer,
and citizens. Flourish. Enter at another doorCaesar, Brutus, Cassius, Casca, Decius, Metellus, Trebonius, Cinna, Ligarius,Antony, Lepidus, Publius, Popillius, and other senators CAESAR (to the Soothsayer) The ides of March are come.
