Come, let’s to the block. HANGMAN My lord, I pray ye put off your doublet. MORE No, my good friend, I have a great cold already, and I would be loath to take more. Point me meet the block, for I was ne’er here before. To the east side, my lord. MORE Then to the east. We go to sigh; that o’er, to sleep in rest.
No eye salute my trunk with a sad tear.
Our birth to heaven should be thus: void of fear. Exit
