SIR JOHN Will I live? To Bardolph and the Page MISTRESS QUICKLY, Will you have Doll Tearsheet meet you at supper? SIR JOHN No more words; let’s have her. Exeunt Mistress Quickly, Bardolph, the Page, Fang and Snare LORD CHIEF JUSTICE (to Gower) I have heard better news. SIR JOHN What’s the news, my good lord?
