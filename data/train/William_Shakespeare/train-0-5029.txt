SIR JOHN What are they? Let us know. HOST Ay, come, quick. HOST Conceal them, or thou diest. SIMPLE Why, sir, they were nothing but about Mistress Anne Page, to know if it were my master’s fortune to have her or no. SIR JOHN ’Tis, ’tis his fortune. SIMPLE What, sir? SIR JOHN To have her or no. Go say the woman told me SO.
