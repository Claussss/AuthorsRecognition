Enter Biondello I warrant you. But sir, here comes your boy.
’Twere good he were schooled. Fear you not him. Sirrah Biondello,
Now do your duty throughly, I advise you.
Imagine ’twere the right Vincentio. BIONDELLO Tut, fear not me. But hast thou done thy errand to Baptista? I told him that your father was at Venice
And that you looked for him this day in Padua.
