HAMLET Why he more than another? FIRST CLOWN Why, sir, his hide is so tanned with his trade that a will keep out water a great while, and your water is a sore decayer of your whoreson dead body. Here’s a skull, now. This skull has lain in the earth three-and-twenty years. HAMLET Whose was it? FIRST CLOWN A whoreson mad fellow’s it was. Whose do you think it was?
