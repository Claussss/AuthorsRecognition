Enter Lodovico and Graziano What ho, no watch, no passage? Murder, murder! ’Tis some mischance. The voice is very direful. CASSIO O, help! LODOVICO Hark. RODERIGO O wretched villain! Two or three groan. ’Tis heavy night.
These may be counterfeits. Let’s think’t unsafe
To come into the cry without more help.
