And I’ll be sworn upon’t that he loves her,
For here’s a paper written in his hand,
A halting sonnet of his own pure brain,
Fashioned to Beatrice. HERO And here’s another, Writ in my cousin’s hand, stol’n from her pocket,
Containing her affection unto Benedick. BENEDICK A miracle! Here’s our own hands against our hearts. Come, I will have thee, but by this light, I take thee for pity.
