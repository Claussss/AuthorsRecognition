Enter Lear’s Fool, and Edgar as a Bedlam beggar in the hovel Fathom and half! Fathom and half! Poor Tom! FOOL Come not in here, nuncle. Here’s a spirit. Help me, help me! KENT Give me thy hand. Who’s there? FOOL A spirit, a spirit. He says his name’s Poor Tom. What art thou that dost grumble there i’th’ straw?
Come forth.
