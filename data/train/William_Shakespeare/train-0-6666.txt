HAMLET Upon what ground? FIRST CLOWN Why, here in Denmark. I have been sexton here, man and boy, thirty years. HAMLET How long will a man lie i’th’ earth ere he rot? FIRST CLOWN I’faith, if a be not rotten before a die—as we have many pocky corpses nowadays, that will scarce hold the laying in—a will last you some eight year or nine year. A tanner will last you nine year.
