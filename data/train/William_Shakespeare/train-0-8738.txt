GONORIL Mean you to enjoy him, then? The let-alone lies not in your good will. Nor in thine, lord. ALBANY Half-blooded fellow, yes. Let the drum strike and prove my title good. Stay yet, hear reason. Edmund, I arrest thee
On capital treason, and in thine attaint
This gilded serpent. (To Regan) For your claim, fair
sister,
I bar it in the interest of my wife.
’Tis she is subcontracted to this lord,
And I, her husband, contradict the banns.
If you will marry, make your love to me.
My lady is bespoke.—Thou art armed, Gloucester.
