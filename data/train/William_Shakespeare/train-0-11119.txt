POSTHUMUS I will wage against your gold, gold to it; my ring I hold dear as my finger, ’tis part of it. GIACOMO You are a friend, and therein the wiser. If you buy ladies’ flesh at a million a dram, you cannot preserve it from tainting. But I see you have some religion in you, that you fear. POSTHUMUS This is but a custom in your tongue. You bear a graver purpose, I hope.
