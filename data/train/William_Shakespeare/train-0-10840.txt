Do you mark that? I cannot be so partial, Goneril,
To the great love I bear you— Pray you, content. What, Oswald, ho!—
You, sir, more knave than fool, after your master. Nuncle Lear, nuncle Lear,
Tarry, take the fool with thee.
A fox when one has caught her,
And such a daughter,
Should sure to the slaughter,
If my cap would buy a halter.
So, the fool follows after. Exit
