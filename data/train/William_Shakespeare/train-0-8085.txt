Mariana is raised to her feet, and is guarded
The Provost makes to seize the Duke DUKE Stay, sir, stay a while. ANGELO What, resists he? Help him, Lucio. LUCIO (to the Duke) Come, sir; come, sir; come, sir! Foh, sir! Why, you bald-pated lying rascal, you must be hooded, must you? Show your knave’s visage, with a pox to you! Show your sheep-biting face, and be hanged an hour! Will’t not off?
