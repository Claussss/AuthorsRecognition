How much more elder art thou than thy looks! PORTIA (to Antonio) Therefore lay bare your bosom. SHYLOCK Ay, his breast. So says the bond, doth it not, noble judge? ‘Nearest his heart’—those are the very words. It is so. Are there balance here to weigh the flesh? SHYLOCK I have them ready. Have by some surgeon, Shylock, on your charge
To stop his wounds, lest he do bleed to death.
